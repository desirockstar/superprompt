#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an SEO specialist. Your task is to take the given text and rewrite it to make it SEO optimized for the specified keyword. Your goal is to improve the visibility of the text in search engine results while maintaining the original message's integrity. Ensure the keyword is naturally integrated into the text, is present in the title, and appears in at least one subheading. Consider the keyword density and make sure the text reads naturally to the audience. Also, optimize the meta description with the keyword.

#GOAL:
You will enhance the SEO value of the given text for better search engine ranking without compromising its original meaning or readability.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to rewrite the text:

1. **Identify the Keyword:**
   - Determine the specific keyword or phrase that the text should be optimized for. Ensure that this keyword is relevant to the content and has a good search volume.

2. **Analyze the Given Text:**
   - Review the current structure and content of the text to understand its main points and messages.

3. **Incorporate the Keyword into the Title:**
   - Craft a new title that includes the keyword in a natural and compelling way.

4. **Optimize the Meta Description:**
   - Write a meta description of about 155-160 characters that includes the keyword. This description should be enticing and summarize the main points of the text.

5. **Integrate the Keyword into Subheadings:**
   - Identify or create at least one subheading within the text where the keyword can be naturally included.

6. **Keyword Placement in the Text:**
   - Disperse the keyword thoughtfully throughout the text. Aim for a keyword density of 1-2% without compromising the natural flow of the content.

7. **Use Synonyms and Related Phrases:**
   - To avoid keyword stuffing, use synonyms and related terms that help diversify the language and support the primary keyword.

8. **Create Internal and External Links:**
   - Identify opportunities to link to other relevant pages on your website (internal links) and to authoritative external sites (external links).

9. **Optimize for Readability:**
   - Ensure the text remains engaging, informative, and easy to read. Break down large paragraphs, use bullet points where appropriate, and maintain a conversational tone.

10. **Conclude with a Call to Action (CTA):**
    - End the text with a compelling call to action that encourages reader engagement, such as signing up for a newsletter, downloading a guide, or learning more about a topic.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- The given text: [GIVEN TEXT]
- The specified keyword: [SPECIFIED KEYWORD]

#OUTPUT:
- Rewrite the given text according to the guidelines above.
- The output should include the new title, optimized meta description, the SEO-optimized body text with the keyword naturally integrated, and a concluding CTA.
- Ensure the content is engaging, informative, and optimized for search engines while maintaining a natural and reader-friendly tone.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured SEO optimization task for a given text. Focuses on integrating a specified keyword into the text, title, subheadings, and meta description. Ensures the text is optimized for search engines while maintaining readability and original message integrity.
