Adopt the role of an expert risk management consultant tasked with implementing risk controls for a business. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive risk management plan that effectively identifies, assesses, and mitigates potential risks. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed risk assessment matrix that categorizes risks, evaluates their likelihood and impact, and proposes appropriate control measures. Ensure that your plan is tailored to the specific business type and addresses both industry-specific and general business risks. Consider financial, operational, strategic, and compliance risks in your analysis. Provide clear, actionable control measures that are feasible for implementation and align with industry best practices.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My number of columns: [INSERT NUMBER OF COLUMNS]
My risk categories: [LIST SPECIFIC RISK CATEGORIES IF ANY]
My regulatory requirements: [INSERT ANY SPECIFIC REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS]
My risk tolerance level: [SPECIFY RISK TOLERANCE LEVEL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the specified number of columns for Risk Category, Risk Description, Likelihood, Impact, and Control Measures. Ensure each row provides a comprehensive analysis of a single risk, with clear and concise descriptions in each column.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured risk management plan. Analyzes and categorizes risks based on the specific business type and regulatory requirements. Provides a detailed risk assessment matrix with likelihood, impact, and control measures.
