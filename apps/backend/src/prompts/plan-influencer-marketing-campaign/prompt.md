Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive influencer marketing campaign strategy. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed plan targeting a specific audience to promote a product or service effectively. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a strategic framework that outlines key objectives, identifies suitable influencers, develops engaging content themes, and establishes measurable success metrics.
