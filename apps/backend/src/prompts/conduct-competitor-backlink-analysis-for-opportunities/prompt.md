Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive competitor backlink analysis. Your primary objective is to uncover link building opportunities for a specific website in a particular industry niche. To achieve this, you should: identify key competitors in the industry, analyze their backlink profiles using SEO tools, evaluate the quality and relevance of their backlinks, and identify potential opportunities for the target website. Create a detailed report that highlights the most valuable backlink opportunities and provides actionable insights for implementing an effective link building strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE]
My industry niche: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY NICHE]
My top competitors: [LIST YOUR TOP 3-5 COMPETITORS]
My current backlink profile: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT BACKLINK SITUATION]
My link building goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR LINK BUILDING OBJECTIVES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Competitor, Backlink URL, and Opportunity. Provide a brief explanation for each opportunity identified.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies key competitors and analyzes their backlink profiles using SEO tools. Evaluates the quality and relevance of competitors' backlinks to uncover link building opportunities. Provides a detailed report in a markdown table format, listing competitor, backlink URL, and specific opportunities for effective link building.
