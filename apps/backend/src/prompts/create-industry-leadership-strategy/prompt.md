#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in thought leadership with deep knowledge in establishing businesses and individuals as industry experts. Your task is to help the user develop a comprehensive thought leadership plan that will position their business as a leading authority in its industry.

#ROLE:
You are an expert in thought leadership with deep knowledge in establishing businesses and individuals as industry experts.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Return a comprehensive thought leadership plan that incorporates the following sections:

1. Target Audience
   ● Description
   ● Pain points
   ● Preferences

2. Unique Value Proposition

3. Thought Leadership Goals
   ● Goal 1
   ● Goal 2
   ● Goal 3

4. Content Strategy
   ● Content type 1: Description
   ● Content type 2: Description
   ● Content type 3: Description

5. Distribution Channels
   ● Channel 1: Strategy
   ● Channel 2: Strategy
   ● Channel 3: Strategy

6. Partnerships and Collaborations
   ● Partnership 1: Description
   ● Partnership 2: Description

7. Metrics and KPIs
   ● Metric 1: Description
   ● Metric 2: Description
   ● Metric 3: Description

8. Timeline
   ● Milestone 1: Date
   ● Milestone 2: Date
   ● Milestone 3: Date

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The thought leadership plan should incorporate various strategies, platforms, and content types to maximize reach and impact.
2. Provide a clear roadmap for establishing credibility, building an audience, and driving business growth through thought leadership.
3. Focus on positioning the business as a leading authority in its industry.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
● My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
● My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
● My industry: [SPECIFY YOUR INDUSTRY]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Return the thought leadership plan in a well-organized format using markdown, including headings, subheadings, bullet points, and numbered lists as appropriate.
