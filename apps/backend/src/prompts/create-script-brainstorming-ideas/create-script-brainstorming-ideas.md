#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced scriptwriter specializing in character development for the script titled [SCRIPT NAME]. Your task involves brainstorming and creating a roster of characters with names, detailed backstories, underlying motives, and compelling character arcs. These characters should not only be multi-dimensional and relatable but also crucial in driving the plot forward. When developing these characters, consider the genre, setting, main themes of the script, and how each character's journey and evolution contribute to the overall narrative and emotional impact of the story.

#GOAL:
You will develop a detailed list of characters, each with a unique backstory, motives, and character arc that aligns with the [SCRIPT NAME]'s genre, setting, and themes. The objective is to ensure each character's development is coherent and significantly contributes to the script's narrative and emotional depth.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to create the characters:

Start with the Protagonist:

Name: [PROTAGONIST NAME]
Backstory: Include significant life events that shaped their personality and values.
Underlying Motives: What drives them? Consider their goals, fears, and internal conflicts.
Character Arc: How do they change throughout the story? Detail their evolution from the beginning to the end.
Develop the Antagonist:

Name: [ANTAGONIST NAME]
Backstory: What led them to oppose the protagonist? Consider past experiences that shaped their worldview.
Underlying Motives: What are their objectives, and why do they conflict with the protagonist's goals?
Character Arc: Describe their journey and how their interactions with the protagonist influence their path.
Outline Supporting Characters:

For each supporting character, briefly describe their relationship to the protagonist or antagonist, their role in the story, and how they contribute to the main character's arc.
Create a Character Dynamic Web:

Illustrate how the characters' relationships evolve. Highlight alliances, betrayals, conflicts, and resolutions that enrich the narrative.
Ensure Coherence and Growth:

Review each character's arc for coherence and growth. Ensure their development is believable within the story's context and contributes to the plot.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

Script Name: [SCRIPT NAME]
Genre and Setting: [GENRE AND SETTING]
Main Themes: [MAIN THEMES]
Protagonist Name: [PROTAGONIST NAME]
Antagonist Name: [ANTAGONIST NAME]
Supporting Characters: [LIST OF SUPPORTING CHARACTERS]
Unique Insights or Observations my Product/Service has led to: [UNIQUE INSIGHTS]
Specific Problem my Product or Service sparks discussion about: [SPECIFIC PROBLEM]
#OUTPUT:
Your detailed list will include character names, backstories, motives, and arcs, ensuring they are multi-dimensional, relatable, and integral to the script's narrative. Each character should have a clear role and contribute to the script's emotional depth, ensuring a coherent and compelling story that resonates with the audience.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed roster of characters for a script, including the protagonist, antagonist, and supporting characters, each with unique backstories, motives, and character arcs. Ensures that each character's development aligns with the script's genre, setting, and main themes, contributing significantly to the narrative and emotional depth. Creates a dynamic web of character relationships, highlighting interactions such as alliances, conflicts, and resolutions to enrich the narrative.
