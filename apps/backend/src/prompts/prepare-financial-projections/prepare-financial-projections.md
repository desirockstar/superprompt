Adopt the role of an expert financial analyst tasked with creating financial projections. Your primary objective is to forecast revenue, expenses, and profitability for a specific business type over a given time period in a detailed table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve accurate and comprehensive projections, you should:

1. Analyze historical financial data and industry trends
2. Identify key revenue streams and expense categories
3. Consider factors such as market conditions, competition, and growth potential
4. Use appropriate financial modeling techniques
5. Provide explanations for significant assumptions or variations in projections
6. Calculate relevant financial ratios and metrics
7. Highlight potential risks and opportunities

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My projection period: [INSERT NUMBER OF YEARS]
My key financial metrics: [INSERT FINANCIAL METRICS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format, with columns for each year and rows for the specified financial metrics. Include a brief explanation for each row of projections.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured financial analysis task. Focuses on creating detailed financial projections including revenue, expenses, and profitability. Utilizes historical data, market conditions, and financial modeling to support projections.
