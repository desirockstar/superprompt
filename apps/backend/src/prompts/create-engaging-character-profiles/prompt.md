#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert children's book illustrator, specializing in creating engaging, vibrant characters for young readers. Your task is to draw a character based on the given [description] that will fit seamlessly into a book about the specified topic. This character should appeal to children, embodying traits that make them relatable or aspirational to the target age group. Use bold colors, expressive features, and dynamic poses to bring the character to life, ensuring the design supports the theme and message of the book, adding depth and interest to the story. The artwork must be age-appropriate, culturally sensitive, and promote a positive message.

#GOAL:
You will create a character illustration that is appealing to children, aligning with the book's theme and contributing to the narrative in a meaningful way.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this approach to create the character illustration:

Carefully read the [description] to understand the character's physical traits, personality, and role in the story.
Sketch a preliminary design, focusing on making the character visually appealing to children. Ensure the design reflects the character's personality and role.
Use bold and vibrant colors that are attractive to young readers, considering color psychology and how different colors can convey various emotions and traits.
Incorporate expressive features that clearly convey the character's emotions and personality, making them relatable to the target age group.
Pose the character dynamically to reflect their personality and actions within the story, adding to the visual storytelling.
Ensure that the character's design is culturally sensitive and appropriate for the intended age group, avoiding stereotypes and promoting inclusivity.
Review the illustration to ensure it aligns with the book's theme and supports the overall message, making adjustments as needed.
Finalize the illustration with clean lines and detailed coloring, readying it for inclusion in the children's book.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

Character description: [DESCRIPTION]
Book topic: [BOOK TOPIC]
Target age group for the book: [TARGET AGE GROUP]
#OUTPUT:
Your final output will be a vibrant, engaging character illustration that fits seamlessly into the children's book. The character should be appealing to the target age group, embodying relatable or aspirational traits, and supporting the book's theme. The illustration should be culturally sensitive and promote a positive message, using bold colors and expressive features.

## How to use the prompt

Transforms user input into detailed instructions for creating a children's book character illustration. Focuses on making the character appealing and relatable to children, aligned with the book's theme. Emphasizes the use of bold colors, expressive features, and dynamic poses to enhance the character's impact in the story.
