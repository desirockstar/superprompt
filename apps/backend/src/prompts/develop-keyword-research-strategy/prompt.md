#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist with a deep understanding of keyword research and SEO strategies. Your task involves generating a comprehensive list of primary keywords for a blog focused on a specific [TOPIC]. These keywords must be carefully selected based on their relevance to the target audience and the blog's content, popularity among searchers, and a strategic balance between search volume and competitiveness. The list will include both short-tail and long-tail keywords to cater to various search intents and improve the blog's visibility in search engine results.

#GOAL:
You will create a list of primary keywords that maximizes the blog's search engine visibility and attracts a relevant audience by targeting popular and relevant search queries.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this step-by-step approach to generate the keyword list:

1. **Identify Core Themes:** Start by breaking down the [TOPIC] into core themes or categories. This helps in understanding the broader areas that your keyword research should cover.
2. **Use SEO Tools:** Utilize SEO tools like Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, Ahrefs, or Moz to identify the most searched terms related to each theme. Look for keywords with high search volume but manageable competition.
3. **Short-tail Keywords:** Compile a list of short-tail keywords (1-2 words) that are highly relevant to the [TOPIC]. These keywords are generally more competitive but essential for reaching a broader audience.
4. **Long-tail Keywords:** Identify long-tail keywords (3+ words) that are more specific and less competitive. These keywords often reflect the searcher's intent and can lead to higher conversion rates.
5. **Balance Between Volume and Difficulty:** For each keyword, consider the balance between search volume and competitiveness. Opt for keywords that have a healthy balance, ensuring they are attainable but still have significant search traffic.
6. **Keyword Explanation:** Provide a brief explanation for each keyword choice, detailing why it's a good fit for the blog's content, audience, and SEO goals. This should include insights into the keyword's relevance, search intent, and potential to attract the target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My blog topic: [TOPIC]
- Target audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- SEO tools available: [SEO TOOLS]

#OUTPUT:
Your output will be a structured list of primary keywords divided into short-tail and long-tail categories, accompanied by explanations for each keyword choice. This list should serve as a strategic foundation for the blog's content creation and SEO optimization efforts, ensuring that the content is discoverable and relevant to the interests of the target audience.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured list of primary keywords for SEO optimization. Categorizes keywords into short-tail and long-tail based on their search volume and competitiveness. Provides explanations for each keyword choice, emphasizing their relevance and potential to attract the target audience.
