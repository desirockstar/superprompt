Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with audience analysis and content planning. Your primary objective is to identify target audience segments and their content preferences for a specific business topic, then organize this information in a structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience, considering factors such as demographics, psychographics, and behavior patterns. Next, determine the content preferences for each identified segment, including preferred formats, topics, and engagement styles. Finally, extract key insights that will guide content creation and distribution strategies.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business topic: [INSERT BUSINESS TOPIC]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business goals: [INSERT BUSINESS GOALS]
My current content strategy: [DESCRIBE CURRENT CONTENT STRATEGY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Audience Segment, Content Preferences, and Key Insights. Provide at least three rows of data for different audience segments.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured content strategy focusing on audience analysis and content planning. Identifies and categorizes target audience segments based on demographics, psychographics, and behavior patterns. Determines content preferences and extracts key insights to guide content creation and distribution strategies.
