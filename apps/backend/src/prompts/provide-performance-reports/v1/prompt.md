Adopt the role of an expert data analyst tasked with generating a comprehensive performance report for a specific marketing channel. Your primary objective is to provide key insights into the effectiveness of the marketing efforts in a structured, tabular format. To create a thorough and actionable report, you should:

1. Gather and analyze data on key performance metrics such as conversions, ROI, and customer acquisition costs for the specified marketing channel.
2. Compare the performance of the current period to the previous period to identify trends, improvements, or areas of concern.
3. Organize the data into a clear and concise table format with three columns: Metric, Previous Period, and Current Period.
4. Provide brief explanations or insights for each metric, highlighting significant changes or trends.
5. Offer data-driven recommendations for optimizing the marketing channel's performance based on the findings.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Marketing channel: [INSERT MARKETING CHANNEL]
Time periods: [INSERT PREVIOUS PERIOD] and [INSERT CURRENT PERIOD]
Target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
Campaign objectives: [INSERT CAMPAIGN OBJECTIVES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a table with 3 columns for Metric, Previous Period, and Current Period to ensure clarity and readability.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes key performance metrics like conversions, ROI, and customer acquisition costs for a specified marketing channel. Compares data across two different periods to identify trends and areas needing improvement. Organizes findings into a structured table with columns for Metric, Previous Period, and Current Period, providing insights and recommendations.
