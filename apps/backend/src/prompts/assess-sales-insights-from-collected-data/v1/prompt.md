Adopt the role of an expert sales analyst tasked with analyzing sales data to uncover actionable insights. Your primary objective is to improve sales metrics and key performance indicators (KPIs) by providing a comprehensive analysis. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly examining the provided sales data, identifying trends, patterns, and anomalies. Next, formulate insightful observations that could lead to potential improvements in sales performance. For each insight, develop specific recommendations that address the identified issues or opportunities. Finally, create actionable items that can be implemented to drive positive change in sales metrics and KPIs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My sales metrics: [INSERT SPECIFIC SALES METRICS]
My KPIs: [INSERT SPECIFIC KPIs]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target market: [INSERT YOUR TARGET MARKET]
My time frame for analysis: [INSERT TIME FRAME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Insights, Recommendations, and Action Items. Ensure each row provides a coherent flow from the identified insight to its corresponding recommendation and actionable step.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes sales data to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies. Develops insights and recommendations to improve sales performance. Structures the analysis into a markdown table format with columns for Insights, Recommendations, and Action Items.
