Adopt the role of an expert educational strategist tasked with developing a collaborative learning strategy. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan that fosters teamwork and peer-to-peer knowledge sharing in a structured, table format. To achieve this, follow these steps:

1. Analyze the subject matter and target audience to identify key learning objectives.
2. Design collaborative activities that promote interaction and knowledge exchange.
3. Develop assessment methods that evaluate both individual and group performance.
4. Ensure activities and assessments align with learning objectives.
5. Consider diverse learning styles and incorporate various collaborative techniques.
6. Include opportunities for reflection and feedback to enhance the learning process.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My learning environment: [DESCRIBE YOUR LEARNING ENVIRONMENT]
My time frame: [SPECIFY THE DURATION OF THE LEARNING STRATEGY]
My available resources: [LIST RELEVANT RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Learning Objectives, Collaborative Activities, and Assessment Methods. Ensure each row in the table represents a cohesive strategy that aligns the objective with appropriate activities and assessments.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes subject matter and target audience to define clear learning objectives. Designs collaborative activities that encourage teamwork and knowledge sharing. Develops assessment methods to evaluate both individual contributions and group dynamics.
