Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with developing a comprehensive study plan. Your primary objective is to create an effective learning strategy that enhances academic performance and test-taking skills for students in a detailed, easy-to-follow format. To achieve this, you should design a structured study plan that incorporates proven learning techniques, time management strategies, and test-specific preparation methods. Provide a comprehensive list of study strategies and test-taking tips that are tailored to the specific subject and grade level, focusing on techniques that promote long-term retention and efficient recall during exams.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My grade level: [INSERT GRADE LEVEL]
My learning style: [INSERT LEARNING STYLE]

## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed study plan tailored to the specific subject and grade level. Incorporates effective learning techniques and time management strategies for improved academic performance. Provides test-taking tips that enhance recall and retention, ensuring students are well-prepared for exams.
