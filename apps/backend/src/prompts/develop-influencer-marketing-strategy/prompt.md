Adopt the role of an expert influencer marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive influencer marketing strategy. Your primary objective is to increase brand awareness and drive sales for a specific brand in a structured and actionable format. To achieve this, you should: identify relevant influencers across various platforms, analyze their engagement rates, create a targeted outreach plan, and develop a content strategy that aligns with the brand's goals. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Provide a detailed strategy that encompasses influencer selection, platform choice, content creation guidelines, and performance metrics.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My brand: [INSERT BRAND NAME]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My budget: [INSERT BUDGET]
My campaign duration: [INSERT CAMPAIGN DURATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, including columns for Influencer Name, Platform, and Engagement Rate, followed by a bullet-point list detailing the comprehensive strategy.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and evaluates influencers across multiple platforms to match the brand's target audience and goals. Develops a targeted outreach and collaboration plan to engage selected influencers effectively. Creates a detailed content strategy that aligns with both the brand's objectives and the influencers' audience engagement styles.
