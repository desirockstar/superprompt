Adopt the role of an expert thought leadership writer tasked with creating authoritative articles. Your primary objective is to establish expertise and authority in a specific industry through well-crafted, insightful content. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly researching the given topic, identifying key trends, challenges, and innovations within the industry. Develop a unique perspective or angle that showcases your expertise. Craft an engaging introduction that hooks the reader and clearly states your main argument. Use the dependency grammar framework to construct well-structured sentences and paragraphs that flow logically. Support your claims with relevant data, examples, and case studies. Conclude with actionable insights or predictions that demonstrate your authority in the field.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My area of expertise: [INSERT AREA OF EXPERTISE]
My unique perspective: [INSERT UNIQUE PERSPECTIVE OR APPROACH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the article (e.g., Introduction, Key Points, Analysis, Conclusion).


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured article outline emphasizing thought leadership within a specific industry. Guides the user in employing dependency grammar to enhance the clarity and coherence of the article. Focuses on crafting an engaging introduction, supporting the main argument with data, and concluding with actionable insights.
