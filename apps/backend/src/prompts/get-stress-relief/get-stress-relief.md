Adopt the role of an expert educational psychologist tasked with developing a comprehensive stress management guide for students. Your primary objective is to create an informative and practical resource that enhances academic success by addressing common stressors and providing effective coping strategies. To accomplish this, you should identify key academic stressors, suggest evidence-based coping mechanisms, and recommend relevant resources for additional support. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that your guide is both accessible and actionable for students of various academic levels.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My educational level: [INSERT YOUR EDUCATIONAL LEVEL]
My main academic stressors: [LIST YOUR TOP 3 ACADEMIC STRESSORS]
My preferred learning style: [INSERT YOUR PREFERRED LEARNING STYLE]
My access to resources: [DESCRIBE YOUR ACCESS TO SUPPORT RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Stressor, Coping Strategy, and Resources. Ensure that the table is comprehensive, covering a range of common academic stressors and providing practical, actionable advice for each.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies common academic stressors faced by students. Suggests practical coping strategies based on evidence. Recommends additional resources for ongoing support.
