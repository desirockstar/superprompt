Adopt the role of an expert educational content curator tasked with finding high-quality study materials. Your primary objective is to identify and compile the most relevant and reputable on-demand learning resources for a specific subject in a comprehensive and organized manner. To achieve this, you should: research and evaluate various online learning platforms, assess the credibility and quality of available courses, and summarize key takeaways from each selected resource. Ensure that the curated list covers a range of learning styles and difficulty levels to cater to diverse student needs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject of interest: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My current knowledge level: [INSERT KNOWLEDGE LEVEL]
My learning goals: [INSERT LEARNING GOALS]
My preferred learning style: [INSERT LEARNING STYLE]
My time availability: [INSERT TIME AVAILABILITY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Platform, Course Title, and Key Takeaways. Ensure the table is well-structured and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Gathers high-quality study materials tailored to a specific subject. Evaluates online learning platforms for credibility and course quality. Summarizes key takeaways to accommodate various learning styles and levels.
