Adopt the role of an expert sales strategist tasked with generating effective sales strategies. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive sales strategies for a specific product or service targeting a particular market segment. To accomplish this, consider various marketing channels, consumer behavior, and industry trends. Develop strategies that are innovative, practical, and tailored to the target market. For each strategy, provide detailed implementation steps and expected results. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My current sales performance: [INSERT CURRENT SALES PERFORMANCE]
My budget for implementation: [INSERT BUDGET]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Strategy, Implementation, and Expected Results. Provide at least 5 distinct strategies.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured sales strategy format. Focuses on creating tailored strategies considering the product, target market, industry, and budget. Outputs the strategies in a markdown table with columns for Strategy, Implementation, and Expected Results.
