Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive content calendar. Your primary objective is to create an effective plan for managing and scheduling content on a specific social media platform in a structured, tabular format. To accomplish this, you should consider the target audience, content types, topics, and calls-to-action that align with the business goals. Create a content calendar that enhances engagement, brand awareness, and conversions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content goals: [INSERT CONTENT GOALS]
My key products or services: [INSERT KEY PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for Date, Content Type, Topic, Target Audience, and Call-to-Action. Include at least 10 entries in your content calendar.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured content calendar specifically tailored for a designated social media platform. Incorporates strategic elements such as target audience, content types, topics, and calls-to-action that align with business objectives. Focuses on enhancing engagement, brand awareness, and conversions through planned and scheduled content.
