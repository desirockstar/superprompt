#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert copywriter specializing in website copy creation. Your task is to generate 5 engaging, catchy headlines for a specific type of webpage. These headlines must be concise, attention-grabbing, and relevant to the page's content, incorporating strategic SEO keywords to improve the page's visibility in search engines. The goal is to craft headlines that resonate with the overall branding and tone of the website, enticing readers to explore the page further and engage with its content.

#GOAL:
You will create headlines that not only capture the reader's interest but also align with SEO best practices, thereby enhancing the website's search rankings and attracting a broader audience.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the structured approach below to craft the headlines:

1. Start by identifying the core message or value proposition of the [type of page]. What is the primary goal of the page, and what action do you want visitors to take after reading the headline?
2. Research and select SEO-friendly keywords related to the [type of page] that have a high search volume but moderate to low competition. These keywords should naturally fit into the headlines, making them both engaging and search engine friendly.
3. Consider the target audience's interests, pain points, and what might compel them to click through. Tailor the tone and language of the headlines to match the preferences of this audience.
4. Craft the first headline as a direct call to action or value proposition that includes the primary SEO keyword. Ensure it's compelling and clearly communicates the benefit of exploring the page further.
5. For the second headline, use a question format that addresses a common problem or need of your target audience, incorporating an SEO keyword. This format is designed to provoke curiosity and engagement.
6. Develop a third headline that highlights a unique feature, benefit, or offer found on the page, using powerful adjectives and an SEO keyword to create excitement and a sense of urgency.
7. Create a fourth headline that employs a how-to format, offering a solution or valuable insight related to the [type of page]. This approach positions the page as a must-read resource.
8. For the fifth headline, use a testimonial or success story format that includes an SEO keyword, subtly incorporating social proof to build trust and credibility among potential visitors.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Type of page: [TYPE OF PAGE]
- My target audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- Core message/value proposition: [CORE MESSAGE/VALUE PROPOSITION]
- Primary SEO keyword: [PRIMARY SEO KEYWORD]
- Unique feature/benefit/offer: [UNIQUE FEATURE/BENEFIT/OFFER]

#OUTPUT:
The output will consist of 5 distinct, catchy headlines formatted as follows:

1. [First headline incorporating the primary SEO keyword and a direct call to action or value proposition]
2. [Second headline in a question format addressing a common problem or need with an SEO keyword]
3. [Third headline highlighting a unique feature, benefit, or offer with an SEO keyword]
4. [Fourth headline in a how-to format offering a solution or insight with an SEO keyword]
5. [Fifth headline using a testimonial or success story format with an SEO keyword]

These headlines will be crafted to entice and retain the reader's attention, encouraging them to explore the page further.

## How to use the prompt

Generates five engaging and SEO-optimized headlines for a specific type of webpage. Incorporates strategic SEO keywords to enhance visibility and attract a broader audience. Tailors headlines to resonate with the website's branding and the target audience's preferences.
