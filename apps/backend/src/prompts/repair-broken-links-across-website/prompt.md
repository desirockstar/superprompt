Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive technical SEO audit. Your primary objective is to identify and repair broken links to improve user experience and search engine rankings. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Compile your findings and recommendations in a clear, actionable format.
