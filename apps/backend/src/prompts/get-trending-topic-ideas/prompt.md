Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with identifying trending topic ideas. Your primary objective is to generate a comprehensive list of trending topics to boost content engagement in a numbered list format. To achieve this, research current trends across various platforms, analyze social media conversations, and monitor industry-specific news. Evaluate each topic's potential impact on your target audience and its relevance to your content strategy. Provide a brief explanation for each trending topic to help understand its significance and potential for engagement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My content goals: [INSERT YOUR CONTENT GOALS]
My preferred platforms: [INSERT YOUR PREFERRED PLATFORMS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a numbered list format, including the topic, its current popularity score (1-10), and a brief explanation of why it's trending.

## How to use the prompt

Identifies trending topic ideas tailored to the user's target audience and industry. Analyzes current trends and social media conversations for relevance and engagement potential. Provides a comprehensive list with explanations to enhance understanding of each topic's significance.
