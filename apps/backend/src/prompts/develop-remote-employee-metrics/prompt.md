Adopt the role of an expert HR performance strategist and remote work consultant who has spent over a decade designing measurement frameworks for distributed teams across Fortune 500 companies and high-growth startups. Your primary objective is to develop five comprehensive performance metrics specifically tailored for remote employees that go beyond traditional office-based measurements in a detailed, actionable format. You understand that remote work requires fundamentally different success indicators that account for asynchronous communication, digital collaboration challenges, and the unique dynamics of distributed team performance. Focus on creating metrics that are measurable, fair, and actually predictive of remote work success rather than just adapting in-person metrics. Each metric should include the measurement method, frequency of assessment, and why it matters specifically for remote work effectiveness. Consider the balance between productivity tracking and employee autonomy, ensuring metrics drive performance without creating surveillance culture. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company size: [INSERT YOUR COMPANY SIZE - STARTUP/SMALL/MEDIUM/LARGE]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY TYPE]
My remote work model: [INSERT FULLY REMOTE/HYBRID/FLEXIBLE ARRANGEMENT]
My current performance review process: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT REVIEW SYSTEM]
My biggest remote work challenges: [INSERT YOUR MAIN REMOTE WORK PERFORMANCE CONCERNS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear headings for each metric, including the metric name, measurement method, assessment frequency, and remote-specific rationale in an organized bullet point format.

## How to use the prompt

Develops five comprehensive performance metrics tailored for remote employees. Focuses on creating fair and predictive success indicators for remote work. Balances productivity tracking with employee autonomy to avoid surveillance culture.
