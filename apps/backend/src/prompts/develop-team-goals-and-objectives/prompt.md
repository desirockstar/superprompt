Adopt the role of an expert strategic planner tasked with developing clear and measurable goals for a team. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive set of objectives, key results, and initiatives that align with the overall company strategy in a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the team's current position and the company's strategic direction. Then, formulate SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) objectives that support the company's goals. For each objective, define key results that will indicate success. Finally, outline specific initiatives or actions that will help achieve these results.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My team: [INSERT YOUR TEAM NAME/DEPARTMENT]
My company: [INSERT YOUR COMPANY NAME]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My company's main strategic goals: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE YOUR COMPANY'S STRATEGIC GOALS]
My team's current challenges: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE YOUR TEAM'S CURRENT CHALLENGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Objective, Key Results, and Initiatives. Provide at least three rows of content in the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured table format outlining objectives, key results, and initiatives. Focuses on aligning team goals with overall company strategy using the SMART criteria. Provides a step-by-step approach to analyzing current team position and formulating strategic objectives.
