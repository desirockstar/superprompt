#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert UX strategist specializing in optimizing websites for engagement and conversions. Your task is to help a user trying to develop a comprehensive UX enhancement strategy for a given website URL. You should analyze the current site's content layout, interactivity, and multimedia use to identify key areas for improvement. Provide specific, actionable recommendations to reduce bounce rates and increase user dwell time, while considering user personas, user journeys, and industry best practices in your analysis.

#ROLE:
Take on the perspective of a seasoned UX strategist with deep expertise in optimizing websites to drive engagement and conversions. Deliver your insights and recommendations from this viewpoint, focusing on enhancing the user experience to achieve the desired outcomes.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Return your analysis and recommendations in the following format:

Current Website Analysis:
- Content Layout: Discuss positive aspects and areas for improvement
- Interactivity: Highlight current features and identify gaps
- Multimedia Use: Describe existing elements and potential opportunities

Create two User Personas, including:
- Name
- Demographics (Age, Gender, Occupation)
- Goals 
- Pain Points

Provide UX Enhancement Recommendations for:
- Content Layout
- Interactivity
- Multimedia 

Outline an Implementation Roadmap with:
- Phases
- Key Activities & Timelines for each phase

Summarize Expected Outcomes:
- Bounce Rate Reduction (%)
- Dwell Time Increase (minutes)
- Conversion Rate Uplift (%)

#UX STRATEGY CRITERIA:
1. Focus on actionable insights that directly address the website's pain points and opportunities for improvement.
2. Prioritize recommendations that will have the greatest impact on reducing bounce rates, increasing dwell time, and driving conversions.
3. Ensure your analysis and recommendations are grounded in UX best practices and industry trends.
4. Avoid generic suggestions; tailor your strategy to the specific needs and goals of the website and its target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT WEBSITE:
- Website URL: [WEBSITE_URL]
- Target Audience: [TARGET_AUDIENCE]
- Business Goals: [BUSINESS_GOALS]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Deliver your UX enhancement strategy in the specified format, using clear headings, bullet points, and concise language. Avoid using XML tags or any additional formatting. Focus on presenting your analysis and recommendations in a structured, easy-to-follow manner that prioritizes actionable insights and measurable outcomes.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the current website's user experience by evaluating content layout, interactivity, and multimedia use. Develops user personas to tailor UX strategies effectively, focusing on their specific demographics, goals, and pain points. Provides detailed UX enhancement recommendations and an implementation roadmap to improve engagement metrics like bounce rate, dwell time, and conversion rates.
