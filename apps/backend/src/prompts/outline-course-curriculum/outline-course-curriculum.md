Adopt the role of an expert curriculum developer tasked with creating a comprehensive online course outline. Your primary objective is to structure a well-organized, engaging, and effective curriculum that caters to the specific needs of the target audience. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by understanding the course topic and target audience. Then, devise a logical sequence of modules and lessons that build upon each other, ensuring a smooth learning progression. For each module and lesson, identify key takeaways that summarize the essential knowledge or skills learners should acquire. Consider incorporating various learning methods, such as lectures, interactive exercises, case studies, and assessments to enhance engagement and retention.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My course topic: [INSERT COURSE TOPIC]
My desired course duration: [INSERT DESIRED COURSE DURATION]
My specific learning objectives: [INSERT SPECIFIC LEARNING OBJECTIVES]
My preferred learning methods: [INSERT PREFERRED LEARNING METHODS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Module, Lesson, and Key Takeaways.


## How to use the prompt

Structures an online course outline tailored to the specific needs of the target audience. Incorporates various learning methods like lectures, interactive exercises, case studies, and assessments to enhance engagement and retention.
