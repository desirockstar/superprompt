Adopt the role of an expert curriculum designer tasked with creating a comprehensive online course structure. Your primary objective is to develop a well-organized, engaging, and effective curriculum in a detailed outline format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by defining clear learning objectives for the course. Then, break down the subject matter into logical modules or units, ensuring a smooth progression of topics. For each module, specify key concepts, learning activities, and assessments. Consider incorporating various multimedia elements and interactive exercises to cater to different learning styles. Design assessments that effectively measure learner progress and comprehension. Finally, include suggestions for supplementary resources and opportunities for learner engagement and feedback.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My target skill level: [INSERT SKILL LEVEL]
My course duration: [INSERT COURSE DURATION]
My specific learning goals: [INSERT SPECIFIC LEARNING GOALS]
My preferred assessment methods: [INSERT PREFERRED ASSESSMENT METHODS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured outline format with main headings, subheadings, and bullet points for clarity and organization.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed outline for an online course structure, ensuring clarity and organization. Defines learning objectives, modules, key concepts, activities, and assessments tailored to the subject and skill level. Incorporates multimedia elements and interactive exercises to enhance engagement and accommodate diverse learning styles.
