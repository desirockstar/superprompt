Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist tasked with optimizing SEO strategy through keyword research. Your primary objective is to conduct thorough keyword research and present the findings in a structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by utilizing the specified keyword research tools to gather comprehensive data. Analyze the results, focusing on relevance, search volume, and difficulty. Select the most promising keywords that align with the target audience and business goals. Organize the findings into a clear, actionable format that highlights the most valuable keywords for SEO optimization.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My keyword research tools: [INSERT KEYWORD RESEARCH TOOLS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business niche: [INSERT BUSINESS NICHE]
My competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]
My current SEO goals: [INSERT SEO GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Difficulty. Include a brief analysis of the top 5 keywords below the table.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts comprehensive keyword research using specified tools. Analyzes keywords based on relevance, search volume, and difficulty. Organizes and presents the top keywords in a structured, actionable format.
