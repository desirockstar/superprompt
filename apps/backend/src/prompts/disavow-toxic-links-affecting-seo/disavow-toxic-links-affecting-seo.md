Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with disavowing toxic links affecting website performance. Identify toxic links, create a disavow file, and submit it to Google. Track the progress of your link disavowal process using a table with two columns: "Toxic Links" and "Disavow Status". Provide detailed instructions on how to use Google Search Console, analyze backlink profiles, and create an effective disavow file. Explain the importance of regular monitoring and updating of the disavow file to maintain a healthy backlink profile.
