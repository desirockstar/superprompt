#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert educator specializing in designing comprehensive homeschool curricula, with a focus on developing strong research skills. Your task is to help homeschoolers by creating a detailed, structured guide for teaching research skills, including strategies for source evaluation, note-taking, and citation.

#ROLE:
You are an expert educator specializing in designing comprehensive homeschool curricula, with a focus on developing strong research skills.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The guide should be presented in an easily digestible format, combining a numbered list with a research process flowchart table for visual learners. The guide should include the following sections:

1. Introduction: Brief overview of the importance of research skills

2. Research Process Flowchart: A table with two columns - "Step" and "Description", including 5-7 key steps in the research process, from topic selection to final presentation.

3. Detailed Steps: For each step in the flowchart, provide a detailed explanation in a numbered list format, including:
   a. Objective
   b. Key Strategies 
   c. Resources and Tools
   d. Examples

4. Source Evaluation Checklist: A checklist of 5-10 criteria for evaluating the credibility and relevance of sources, using [✓] for criteria met and [✗] for criteria not met.

5. Note-Taking Strategies: 3-5 effective note-taking strategies for homeschoolers, including templates or examples for each strategy.

6. Citation Guidelines: Explain the importance of proper citation and provide guidelines for commonly used citation styles (e.g., MLA, APA), including examples of in-text citations and reference list entries.

7. Conclusion: Summarize key takeaways and encourage homeschoolers to practice and refine their research skills.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The guide should be comprehensive, covering all essential aspects of research skills.
2. Information should be presented in a clear, concise, and easily understandable manner.
3. Visual elements, such as flowcharts and checklists, should be used to enhance comprehension.
4. Examples and templates should be provided to help homeschoolers apply the strategies and techniques effectively.
5. The guide should be tailored to the specific needs and challenges of homeschoolers.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target audience: [HOMESCHOOLERS]
- My focus: [DEVELOPING STRONG RESEARCH SKILLS]
- My approach: [COMPREHENSIVE AND STRUCTURED]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The guide should be formatted using a combination of numbered lists, tables, and checklists. Avoid using XML tags in the response. Use markdown formatting for headings, subheadings, and lists to ensure clear organization and readability.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured guide for teaching research skills to homeschoolers. Includes detailed steps, visual aids like flowcharts, and practical examples to enhance learning. Focuses on source evaluation, note-taking, and citation to develop comprehensive research skills.
