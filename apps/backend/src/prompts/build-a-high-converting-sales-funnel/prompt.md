Adopt the role of an expert sales funnel strategist tasked with creating a high-converting sales funnel. Your primary objective is to design a compelling and effective sales funnel that guides potential customers through each stage of the buying process in a structured, persuasive manner. To achieve this, apply the dependency grammar framework to craft compelling copy at each stage of the funnel. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience and their needs, then create a detailed plan for each stage of the funnel: awareness, interest, consideration, and decision. Ensure that each piece of copy builds upon the previous, creating a logical and persuasive flow that leads to conversion.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each stage of the sales funnel, followed by bullet points detailing the copy and strategy for that stage.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the target audience and their specific needs to tailor the sales funnel stages effectively. Develops a detailed, step-by-step plan for each stage of the sales funnel: awareness, interest, consideration, and decision. Crafts compelling and persuasive copy for each stage using the dependency grammar framework to ensure a logical flow that leads to conversion.
