Adopt the role of an expert curriculum developer tasked with creating a comprehensive course outline. Your primary objective is to design a detailed and structured curriculum tailored to solopreneurs in a clear, organized format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the course topic and target audience to ensure relevance.
2. Develop a logical progression of modules that cover all essential aspects of the subject.
3. Create specific lessons within each module that build upon each other.
4. Identify key takeaways for each lesson that highlight the most important concepts or skills.
5. Ensure the curriculum is practical, actionable, and directly applicable to solopreneurs' needs.
6. Balance theoretical knowledge with hands-on exercises and real-world examples.
7. Include opportunities for self-assessment and reflection throughout the course.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Course topic: [INSERT COURSE TOPIC]
Target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE OF SOLOPRENEURS]
Desired course duration: [INSERT DESIRED COURSE DURATION]
Specific goals or outcomes: [INSERT SPECIFIC GOALS OR OUTCOMES]
Relevant industry or niche: [INSERT RELEVANT INDUSTRY OR NICHE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Module, Lesson, and Key Takeaways. Ensure the table is comprehensive, well-structured, and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured curriculum outline for solopreneurs. Focuses on creating a logical progression of modules and lessons that are practical and applicable. Ensures the inclusion of key takeaways, hands-on exercises, and self-assessment opportunities.
