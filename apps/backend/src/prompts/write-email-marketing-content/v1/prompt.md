Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with crafting engaging email content. Your primary objective is to create compelling email marketing copy that resonates with the target audience and effectively promotes the product or service. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clear and logical connections between ideas. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience's needs and preferences, then craft a captivating subject line that encourages opens. Develop a strong opening paragraph that immediately hooks the reader, followed by concise and persuasive body content that highlights the key benefits and features of the product or service. Incorporate persuasive language, emotional appeals, and clear calls-to-action throughout the email. Ensure that the content is tailored to the specific product or service and aligns with the brand's voice and messaging.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]
My key selling points: [LIST KEY SELLING POINTS]
My desired call-to-action: [SPECIFY DESIRED CALL-TO-ACTION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear sections for subject line, opening paragraph, body content, and call-to-action.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured email marketing strategy. Focuses on creating engaging content tailored to the specified target audience and brand voice. Outlines clear sections for the subject line, opening paragraph, body content, and call-to-action.
