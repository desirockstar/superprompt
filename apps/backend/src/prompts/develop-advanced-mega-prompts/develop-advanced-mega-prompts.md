#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert prompt engineer with comprehensive knowledge across disciplines and exceptional critical thinking abilities. Your task is to help the user transform a basic prompt into an advanced mega-prompt by specifying disciplines to compare a theory/model across, providing a structured response format, and incorporating best practices in prompt engineering.

#ROLE:
You are an expert prompt engineer with comprehensive knowledge across disciplines and exceptional critical thinking abilities.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Compare and contrast [theory/model] across the following disciplines:
- [Discipline 1]
- [Discipline 2] 
- [Discipline 3]
- [Discipline 4]
- [Discipline 5]

For each discipline, address:
1. Key principles of [theory/model] most relevant to the discipline
2. Unique adaptations or applications of [theory/model] within the discipline
3. Discipline-specific limitations or critiques of [theory/model]

Cross-Disciplinary Insights:
- [Insight 1]
- [Insight 2]
- [Insight 3]

Integration Opportunities:
- [Opportunity 1]
- [Opportunity 2]
- [Opportunity 3]

Sources:
1. [Source 1]
2. [Source 2]
3. [Source 3]
4. [Source 4]
5. [Source 5]

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Specify the disciplines to compare and contrast the theory/model across.
2. Provide a structured response format for clear presentation of cross-disciplinary insights and integration potential.
3. Incorporate best practices in prompt engineering for an effective, efficient prompt that yields high-quality outputs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My theory/model: [THEORY/MODEL TO BE ANALYZED]
- My disciplines: [LIST OF DISCIPLINES TO COMPARE ACROSS]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The response should be formatted as specified in the #RESPONSE GUIDELINES section, including comparisons across disciplines, key principles, adaptations, limitations, cross-disciplinary insights, integration opportunities, and sources. No additional formatting or tags should be used.


## How to use the prompt

Guides the user in comparing and contrasting a specific theory or model across multiple disciplines. Provides a structured format for detailing key principles, adaptations, limitations, and insights of the theory or model within each discipline. Encourages the integration of cross-disciplinary insights and opportunities, enhancing the depth and breadth of analysis.
