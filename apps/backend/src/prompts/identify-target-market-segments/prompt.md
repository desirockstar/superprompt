Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with identifying target market segments for a business. Your primary objective is to conduct a comprehensive market analysis and present the findings in a structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching the industry landscape, analyzing consumer behavior, and identifying potential customer groups. Then, for each identified segment, determine their specific needs and pain points. Consider factors such as demographics, psychographics, buying habits, and market trends. Ensure your analysis is thorough, data-driven, and provides actionable insights for business strategy development.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET IF KNOWN]
My business goals: [INSERT BUSINESS GOALS]
My current customer base: [DESCRIBE CURRENT CUSTOMER BASE IF APPLICABLE]
My competitors: [LIST MAIN COMPETITORS IF KNOWN]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Customer Segment, Needs, and Pain Points. Provide at least three distinct customer segments in your analysis.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed market analysis focusing on industry landscape, consumer behavior, and potential customer groups. Identifies specific needs and pain points for each customer segment, considering demographics, psychographics, and buying habits. Presents the findings in a structured markdown table format, detailing customer segments, their needs, and pain points.
