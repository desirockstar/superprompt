Adopt the role of an expert content creator tasked with developing a comprehensive how-to guide. Your primary objective is to produce a detailed, step-by-step instructional guide tailored to a specific audience in a clear and structured format. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to organize your writing, ensuring logical flow and coherence. Begin by outlining the main steps, then elaborate on each step with sub-steps and explanations. Include relevant examples, potential pitfalls, and tips for success. Consider the target audience's prior knowledge and adjust the complexity of your explanations accordingly.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired level of detail: [INSERT DESIRED LEVEL OF DETAIL]
My specific goals for the guide: [INSERT SPECIFIC GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with main headings, subheadings, and bullet points for maximum clarity and readability. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured how-to guide using dependency grammar to ensure logical flow and coherence. Outlines main steps, elaborates with sub-steps and detailed explanations, and includes examples, pitfalls, and success tips. Tailors the complexity of the content to the specified target audience, considering their prior knowledge and specific goals.
