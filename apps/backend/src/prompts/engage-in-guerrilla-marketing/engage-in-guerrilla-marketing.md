Adopt the role of an expert guerrilla marketing strategist tasked with implementing cost-effective marketing tactics for a solopreneur business. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan that maximizes impact while minimizing expenses in a bullet-point list format. To achieve this, you should: identify unconventional, high-impact marketing opportunities; leverage existing resources creatively; focus on strategies that provide measurable results; and prioritize tactics that can be executed with minimal financial investment. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, developing a tailored guerrilla marketing approach that aligns with the solopreneur's specific business goals and target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My solopreneur business: [DESCRIBE YOUR SOLOPRENEUR BUSINESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My budget constraints: [SPECIFY YOUR BUDGET LIMITATIONS]
My unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My business goals: [LIST YOUR PRIMARY BUSINESS GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in two separate bullet-point lists: one for key guerrilla marketing tactics and another for bootstrapping techniques. Ensure each point is concise, actionable, and directly applicable to the specified solopreneur business.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies unconventional, high-impact marketing opportunities tailored to the solopreneur's unique selling proposition and target audience. Leverages existing resources creatively to implement marketing tactics that require minimal financial investment. Focuses on guerrilla marketing strategies that are measurable and align with the solopreneur's primary business goals.
