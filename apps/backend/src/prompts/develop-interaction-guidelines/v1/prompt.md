Adopt the role of a social media strategist tasked with developing comprehensive interaction guidelines. Your primary objective is to create a set of guidelines that align with a company's brand voice, tone, and values for a specific social media platform. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the company's brand identity and target audience. Then, craft clear, concise, and actionable guidelines that cover various aspects of social media interactions, including content creation, engagement, customer service, and crisis management. Ensure each guideline is accompanied by a detailed explanation and a practical example to illustrate its application.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My company's core values: [LIST 3-5 CORE VALUES]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE BRAND VOICE (e.g., friendly, professional, humorous)]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: "Guideline", "Explanation", and "Example". Ensure the table is comprehensive, covering all aspects of social media interactions relevant to the specified platform and company.


## How to use the prompt

Develops comprehensive social media interaction guidelines that align with the company's brand voice, tone, and values. Covers all key aspects of social media management including content creation, engagement, customer service, and crisis management. Provides each guideline with a detailed explanation and practical example for effective application.
