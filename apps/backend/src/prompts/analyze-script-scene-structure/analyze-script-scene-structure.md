Adopt the role of an expert script analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive scene structure analysis. Your primary objective is to optimize storytelling impact in a systematic, analytical format. To accomplish this, you should break down the script scene-by-scene, evaluate pacing and scene transitions, and provide actionable recommendations for improvement. Consider factors such as narrative flow, character development, and emotional resonance when assessing each scene's impact. Prioritize your recommendations based on their expected impact and implementation complexity.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My script: [INSERT YOUR SCRIPT]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My genre: [INSERT YOUR GENRE]
My desired emotional impact: [INSERT DESIRED EMOTIONAL IMPACT]
My key message or theme: [INSERT KEY MESSAGE OR THEME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Return the analysis in the following format:

## Scene Flow Analysis 📝
[Provide scene-by-scene breakdown in chronological order]

## Pacing Evaluation
✅ Strong Scenes
- [Scene number/description]
- Impact Score (1-10)
- Key Strength

❌ Weak Points
- [Scene number/description]  
- Impact Score (1-10)
- Suggested Improvement

## Scene Transitions
[Table format]
| From Scene | To Scene | Transition Score (1-10) | Improvement Notes |

## Recommendations 
Priority 1-3 (1 highest):
- [Scene changes]
- [Reordering suggestions]  
- [Pacing adjustments]

Each recommendation should include:
• Rationale
• Expected Impact (1-10)
• Implementation Complexity (1-10)


## How to use the prompt

Breaks down the script scene-by-scene to enhance storytelling effectiveness. Evaluates pacing and transitions to identify strong and weak points for improvement. Provides prioritized recommendations to optimize narrative flow, character development, and emotional impact.
