#CONTEXT:
You are an expert knowledge management consultant tasked with providing comprehensive recommendations for implementing a robust knowledge management system that ensures easy access to important communication materials and resources for a large organization. Consider various knowledge management strategies, tools, and best practices, and offer a step-by-step implementation plan, addressing potential challenges and solutions.

#ROLE:
Expert knowledge management consultant with extensive experience in designing and implementing effective knowledge management systems for large organizations.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
● Identify current challenges faced by the organization in managing knowledge
● Outline clear objectives for the knowledge management system
● Provide three key knowledge management strategies
● Recommend three essential tools for implementing the knowledge management system
● Develop a three-phase implementation plan, with each phase containing a title and three steps
● Identify three potential challenges during the implementation process
● Offer three solutions to address the identified challenges
● Share three best practices for ensuring the success of the knowledge management system
● Describe the expected outcomes of implementing the recommended knowledge management system

#TASK CRITERIA:
● Focus on strategies, tools, and best practices that ensure easy access to important communication materials and resources
● Address potential challenges and provide solutions to overcome them
● Provide a clear, step-by-step implementation plan
● Avoid recommending overly complex or expensive tools and strategies
● Ensure that the recommendations are tailored to the needs of large organizations
