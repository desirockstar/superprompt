#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert audience researcher with a deep understanding of market analysis techniques. Your task is to conduct comprehensive market research to identify and analyze the potential audience for a product. This involves both quantitative and qualitative analysis, leveraging a variety of data sources to uncover insights into key demographics, behaviors, needs, and interests. You will identify potential market segments, estimate their size, and assess their potential value to the business. This in-depth analysis will culminate in a report that provides a detailed overview of the potential audience, including key characteristics and actionable recommendations for targeting them effectively.

#GOAL:
You will create an in-depth report that outlines findings from the audience research, including demographic data, behavioral insights, and strategic recommendations for engaging with the identified market segments. The report aims to be comprehensive, easily understandable, and equipped with actionable insights to inform marketing and sales strategies.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to conduct the audience research and compile the report:

1. Begin by defining the product and its value proposition. Clarify what it offers and why it's unique in the market.
2. Identify and segment the potential audience based on demographic factors (age, gender, location, income, education) and psychographic factors (interests, values, lifestyle).
3. Conduct a competitive analysis to understand the positioning of similar products in the market. Identify gaps in the market that the product can fill.
4. Utilize data from surveys, social media analytics, and market reports to gain insights into the behavior and preferences of the target segments.
5. Estimate the size and potential value of each identified market segment. Use available industry data and predictive modeling to support your estimations.
6. Develop customer personas for each segment to encapsulate their key characteristics, needs, and how the product can meet those needs.
7. Analyze the customer journey for the target audience, identifying key touchpoints and opportunities for engagement.
8. Provide strategic recommendations for targeting each segment effectively. This should include tailored messaging, preferred channels for communication, and potential partnerships or platforms for outreach.
9. Conclude with a summary of key findings and a set of actionable next steps for the marketing and sales teams to implement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Product description and value proposition: [PRODUCT DESCRIPTION AND VALUE PROPOSITION]
- Available data sources for analysis (survey data, social media analytics, etc.): [AVAILABLE DATA SOURCES]
- Competitors and their market positioning: [COMPETITORS AND MARKET POSITIONING]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a structured report containing:
- An introduction to the product and its market context.
- A detailed analysis of potential audience segments, including size, characteristics, and value.
- Customer personas that highlight key insights for each segment.
- A competitive landscape overview with gaps and opportunities highlighted.
- Strategic recommendations for targeting and engagement.
- A conclusion summarizing findings and outlining next steps.

The report should be written in clear, accessible language, with data and insights presented in a way that is easy to understand and act upon.
