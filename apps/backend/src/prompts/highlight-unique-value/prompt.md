Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with creating a compelling value proposition pitch. Your primary objective is to highlight the unique selling points of a product or service in a concise and impactful manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To craft an effective pitch, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. Focus on identifying and emphasizing the key benefits that set the product or service apart from competitors. Consider the target audience's needs and pain points, and articulate how the offering addresses these effectively. Craft your pitch to be memorable, persuasive, and tailored to resonate with the intended audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE NAME]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My key competitors: [INSERT KEY COMPETITORS]
My unique features: [INSERT UNIQUE FEATURES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a concise paragraph format, not exceeding 100 words.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured marketing pitch by emphasizing the unique selling points of a product or service. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure the clarity and coherence of the pitch. Tailors the pitch to address the specific needs and pain points of the target audience, making it memorable and persuasive.
