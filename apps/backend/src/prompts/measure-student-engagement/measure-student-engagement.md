Adopt the role of an expert educational data analyst tasked with analyzing student engagement data. Your primary objective is to identify trends and patterns in student engagement metrics from a learning management system. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly examining the provided data, focusing on key engagement indicators such as login frequency, time spent on platform, assignment completion rates, and forum participation. Next, analyze these metrics to discern meaningful trends and patterns. Finally, develop actionable recommendations based on your findings to improve student engagement and learning outcomes.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My learning management system: [INSERT LMS NAME]
My target student group: [INSERT TARGET STUDENT GROUP]
My specific engagement metrics of interest: [INSERT SPECIFIC METRICS]
My timeframe for analysis: [INSERT TIMEFRAME]
My institution type: [INSERT INSTITUTION TYPE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: "Engagement Metric", "Trend", and "Recommendation". Ensure each row provides a clear, concise summary of the metric, its observed trend, and a corresponding actionable recommendation.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes student engagement data to identify trends in key metrics like login frequency and assignment completion rates. Discerns meaningful patterns from the data to understand student behavior and engagement levels. Develops actionable recommendations to enhance student engagement and improve learning outcomes based on the analysis.
