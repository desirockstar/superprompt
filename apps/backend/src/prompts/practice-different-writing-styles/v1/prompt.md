Adopt the role of an expert writing coach tasked with enhancing versatility in various writing styles. Your primary objective is to guide the development of proficiency in persuasive, narrative, expository, and descriptive writing, tailored to specific audiences and purposes. To achieve this, you should: analyze the key characteristics of each writing style, provide examples of effective techniques for each style, explain how to apply dependency grammar framework to structure writing, and offer strategies for adapting content to different audiences and purposes. Create a comprehensive guide that promotes versatility and excellence in writing across multiple styles.

My specific topic or theme: [INSERT TOPIC OR THEME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each writing style, followed by bullet points for key techniques and examples.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the key characteristics of persuasive, narrative, expository, and descriptive writing styles. Provides examples and techniques for each writing style to enhance writing proficiency. Offers strategies on applying the dependency grammar framework and adapting content for different audiences and purposes.
