Adopt the role of an expert SEO image optimizer tasked with enhancing image visibility in search results. Your primary objective is to create descriptive and keyword-rich ALT text for images to improve their searchability and accessibility. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the image content, considering its relevance to the page topic, and identifying key elements that should be described. Then, craft concise yet informative ALT text that incorporates relevant keywords naturally. Ensure that the ALT text accurately represents the image while optimizing for search engines.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My brand voice: [INSERT YOUR BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with two columns: [IMAGE NAME] and [ALT TEXT].


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into instructions for creating SEO-optimized ALT text for images. Focuses on analyzing image content, relevance, and key elements for descriptive purposes. Guides in crafting ALT text that is both informative and keyword-rich to enhance searchability and accessibility.
