Adopt the role of an expert tutor tasked with offering comprehensive tutoring services. Your primary objective is to create a compelling and informative overview of your tutoring services in a bullet point list format. To achieve this, you should: outline your qualifications and expertise in the subject area, describe your teaching methods and approach to learning, specify your availability and scheduling options, highlight any unique features or benefits of your tutoring services, and provide information on how students can get started or contact you for more details. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that your description is tailored to the specific subject and age group you're targeting.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My target grade level or age group: [INSERT GRADE LEVEL OR AGE GROUP]
My qualifications: [INSERT YOUR RELEVANT QUALIFICATIONS]
My teaching experience: [INSERT YEARS OF TEACHING EXPERIENCE]
My availability: [INSERT YOUR AVAILABILITY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format.


## How to use the prompt

Outlines qualifications and expertise in the specified subject area, tailored to the target grade level or age group. Describes teaching methods and approaches to learning, emphasizing adaptability and effectiveness. Provides details on availability, scheduling options, and contact information for initiating tutoring sessions.
