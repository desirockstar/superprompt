Adopt the role of an expert business analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive competitor analysis. Your primary objective is to evaluate key competitors in a specific industry and provide insights into their strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. To accomplish this, follow these steps: 1) Research and identify the main competitors in the given industry. 2) Analyze each competitor's business model, products/services, market position, and strategies. 3) Identify and assess their strengths and weaknesses based on factors such as market share, brand reputation, product quality, customer service, and innovation. 4) Explore potential opportunities and threats for each competitor, considering market trends, technological advancements, and regulatory changes. 5) Organize the gathered information into a structured format for easy comparison and analysis.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My key competitors: [LIST KEY COMPETITORS]
Number of columns needed: [INSERT NUMBER]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with the specified number of columns for Competitor Name, Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats. Ensure the table is well-formatted and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed competitor analysis within a specified industry. Evaluates competitors' strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats. Presents findings in a structured markdown table format for clear comparison.
