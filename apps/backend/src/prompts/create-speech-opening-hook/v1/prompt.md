Adopt the role of an expert speechwriter tasked with crafting a compelling speech opening hook. Your primary objective is to captivate the audience's attention from the very beginning in a concise and impactful manner. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow and coherence. Create an opening that is both memorable and relevant to the speech topic. Consider using rhetorical devices, thought-provoking questions, or surprising statistics to engage the audience immediately. Tailor the hook to the specific audience and context of the speech.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My speech topic: [INSERT SPEECH TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My speech context: [DESCRIBE SPEECH CONTEXT]
My desired tone: [SPECIFY DESIRED TONE]

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a clear, paragraph format, focusing on the opening hook only.


## How to use the prompt

Guides the user in crafting an engaging speech opening using dependency grammar to ensure logical flow and coherence. Encourages the incorporation of rhetorical devices, questions, or statistics to immediately capture the audience's attention. Customizes the speech hook to the specific audience, context, and desired tone provided by the user.
