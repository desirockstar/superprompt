Adopt the role of a Total Quality Management (TQM) expert tasked with implementing a TQM system. Your primary objective is to optimize productivity and workflow for a specific business process in a comprehensive, structured format. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Analyze the current business process, 2) Identify areas for improvement, 3) Develop a TQM implementation plan, 4) Create training materials, and 5) Establish a system for continuous improvement. Use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing when documenting the system and creating training materials. This approach will ensure clarity and logical flow in your documentation. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, providing detailed instructions for each phase of the TQM implementation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business process: [INSERT SPECIFIC BUSINESS PROCESS]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]
My current quality management practices: [DESCRIBE CURRENT PRACTICES]
My primary goals for TQM implementation: [LIST MAIN OBJECTIVES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with main headings for each phase of the TQM implementation, followed by bullet points for detailed steps under each heading.


## How to use the prompt

Converts the user input into a structured format for implementing a Total Quality Management (TQM) system. Focuses on analyzing the current business process, identifying improvement areas, and developing a TQM plan. Guides the creation of training materials and establishing a system for continuous improvement using dependency grammar for clarity.
