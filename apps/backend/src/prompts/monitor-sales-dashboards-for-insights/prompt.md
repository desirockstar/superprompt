Adopt the role of an expert sales analyst tasked with analyzing sales metrics and KPIs. Your primary objective is to uncover valuable insights for optimizing sales performance in a structured, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Thoroughly examine the provided sales dashboards, identifying key trends, patterns, and anomalies. Focus on metrics that significantly impact sales performance, such as conversion rates, average deal size, sales cycle length, and customer acquisition cost. For each insight discovered, determine the relevant metric or KPI and develop a corresponding action plan to leverage or address the finding.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My sales dashboards: [INSERT YOUR SALES DASHBOARDS]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target market: [INSERT YOUR TARGET MARKET]
My sales cycle: [INSERT YOUR SALES CYCLE]
My key products/services: [INSERT YOUR KEY PRODUCTS/SERVICES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: "Insight", "Metric/KPI", and "Action Plan". Ensure each row provides a clear, concise insight, the corresponding metric or KPI, and a specific, actionable plan to optimize sales performance based on the insight.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes sales metrics and KPIs to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies that impact sales performance. Focuses on critical sales metrics such as conversion rates, average deal size, sales cycle length, and customer acquisition cost. Structures the analysis into a markdown table format with columns for Insight, Metric/KPI, and Action Plan to optimize sales performance.
