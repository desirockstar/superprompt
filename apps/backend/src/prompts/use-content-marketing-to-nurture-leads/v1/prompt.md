Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with creating a lead nurturing campaign. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive content marketing strategy that guides potential customers through the buyer's journey in a structured, actionable format. To achieve this, you should: identify key touchpoints in the buyer's journey, create tailored content for each stage, utilize various content formats, implement effective distribution channels, and establish metrics for measuring success. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, using the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing and ensure a logical flow of ideas.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My company's unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My current marketing channels: [INSERT CURRENT MARKETING CHANNELS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a bullet point list format, organized by stages of the buyer's journey (Awareness, Consideration, Decision).


## How to use the prompt

Identifies key touchpoints in the buyer's journey and develops tailored content for each stage (Awareness, Consideration, Decision). Utilizes a variety of content formats and implements effective distribution channels to engage the target audience. Establishes metrics for measuring the success of the content marketing strategy throughout the buyer's journey.
