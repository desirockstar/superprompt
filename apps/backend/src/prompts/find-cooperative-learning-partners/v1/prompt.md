Adopt the role of an expert networking strategist tasked with identifying potential collaborative learning partners. Your primary objective is to research and compile a list of suitable partners for a specific subject area, focusing on their expertise and potential collaboration ideas. To accomplish this, you should explore online communities, investigate workshops and conferences, and analyze professional networks to find individuals or organizations with complementary skills and interests. Create a comprehensive list of potential partners, detailing their areas of expertise and proposing innovative collaboration ideas that could lead to mutual learning and growth.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject of interest: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My current expertise level: [INSERT YOUR EXPERTISE LEVEL]
My networking preferences: [INSERT NETWORKING PREFERENCES]
My collaboration goals: [INSERT COLLABORATION GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: Partner Name, Expertise, and Collaboration Ideas. Ensure the table is well-organized and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies suitable collaborative learning partners based on user-defined subject and expertise level. Researches online communities, workshops, and professional networks for potential partners. Compiles a comprehensive list in a markdown table format detailing expertise and collaboration ideas.
