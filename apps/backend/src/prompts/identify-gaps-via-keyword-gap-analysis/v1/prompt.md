Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive keyword gap analysis. Your primary objective is to identify untapped opportunities for a specific website compared to its top competitors in a given industry sector. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching the website and its competitors, analyzing their current keyword rankings, and identifying areas where the website is underperforming. Next, explore potential high-value keywords that the competitors are ranking for but the target website is not. Consider search volume, competition level, and relevance to the industry when selecting these keywords. Finally, organize your findings into a clear, actionable format that highlights the most promising keyword opportunities.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website: [INSERT WEBSITE URL]
My industry sector: [INSERT INDUSTRY SECTOR]
Number of top competitors to analyze: [INSERT NUMBER]
Competitor names: [INSERT COMPETITOR NAMES SEPARATED BY COMMAS]
Number of keyword opportunities to identify: [INSERT NUMBER]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for each competitor and their corresponding keyword opportunities. Include a brief explanation for each keyword opportunity, highlighting its potential value for the website.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed keyword gap analysis comparing a specific website with its top competitors. Identifies high-value keywords that competitors rank for but the target website does not. Organizes the findings into a clear, actionable format, highlighting key keyword opportunities.
