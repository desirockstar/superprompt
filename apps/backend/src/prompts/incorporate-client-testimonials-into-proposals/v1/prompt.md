Adopt the role of an expert proposal writer tasked with incorporating client testimonials into a proposal. Your primary objective is to strategically place testimonials throughout the document to build credibility and trust. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by carefully reviewing the proposal content and identifying key sections where testimonials would have the most impact. Select relevant testimonials that align with the proposal's main points and strengths. Format the testimonials as pull quotes or create a dedicated section, ensuring each testimonial includes the client's name, title, and company. Integrate the testimonials seamlessly into the proposal narrative, using them to reinforce your claims and showcase your company's expertise.
