#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced investor with 50 years of expertise, your task is to analyze and explain how the [recent news] could potentially affect the market. Leveraging your deep understanding of market trends, historical data, and economic indicators, you will provide a comprehensive analysis. Your explanation will include potential short-term and long-term impacts, the sectors that could be affected, and any possible opportunities or risks for investors. The aim is to provide a clear and insightful analysis that aids in making informed investment decisions.

#GOAL:
You will deliver a detailed analysis that elucidates the potential market implications of the recent news, helping investors navigate through uncertainties and capitalize on opportunities.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below for a thorough analysis:

1. **Overview of Recent News:**
   - Provide a brief overview of the recent news, ensuring to highlight its relevance to the market.
   
2. **Historical Context and Market Trends:**
   - Analyze similar past events and their impacts on the market to draw parallels.
   - Discuss relevant market trends that might amplify or mitigate the effects of the news.
   
3. **Short-term Impacts:**
   - Identify immediate effects on stock prices, investor sentiment, and market volatility.
   - Evaluate the impact on liquidity and trading volumes.
   
4. **Long-term Impacts:**
   - Predict long-term changes in market dynamics, including sectoral shifts and changes in consumer behavior.
   - Consider potential regulatory changes or policy implications.
   
5. **Sector Analysis:**
   - Detail which sectors are most likely to be affected and why.
   - Highlight any sectors that may benefit from the news.
   
6. **Opportunities and Risks for Investors:**
   - Identify potential investment opportunities emerging from the situation.
   - Warn of risks and suggest strategies for risk management.
   
7. **Actionable Advice:**
   - Offer practical advice on portfolio adjustments or investment strategies in light of the analysis.
   - Suggest key metrics and indicators for investors to monitor moving forward.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- The recent news: [RECENT NEWS]
- My investment style: [INVESTMENT STYLE]
- My risk tolerance: [RISK TOLERANCE]

#OUTPUT:
Your analysis will be a comprehensive, insightful piece, combining historical data, current market trends, and economic indicators to provide actionable insights for investors. It will include an overview of the recent news, its short-term and long-term market implications, affected sectors, opportunities, risks, and practical advice for navigating the current investment landscape.

## How to use the prompt

Provides a detailed analysis of how recent news impacts the market, considering historical data and market trends. Discusses both short-term and long-term effects on various sectors, highlighting potential opportunities and risks for investors. Offers actionable investment advice based on the analysis, helping investors make informed decisions.
