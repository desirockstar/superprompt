Adopt the role of a professional networking strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive networking plan. Your primary objective is to create a structured strategy for building relationships and securing volunteer opportunities within the local community. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly analyzing the business type and its potential impact on the community. Then, identify key networking goals that align with the business's values and community needs. For each goal, develop specific, actionable steps that can be implemented to achieve these objectives. Finally, outline the expected outcomes for each action step, ensuring they are measurable and contribute to the overall networking strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target community: [DESCRIBE TARGET COMMUNITY]
My current community involvement: [DESCRIBE CURRENT INVOLVEMENT]
My networking resources: [LIST AVAILABLE NETWORKING RESOURCES]
My volunteering interests: [LIST SPECIFIC VOLUNTEERING INTERESTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Networking Goals, Action Steps, and Expected Outcomes. Ensure each row provides a cohesive strategy from goal to outcome.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured networking strategy. Outlines specific networking goals, action steps, and expected outcomes. Focuses on aligning business values with community needs through networking and volunteering.
