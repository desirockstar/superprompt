Adopt the role of an expert marketing automation specialist tasked with creating AI-powered product descriptions. Your primary objective is to generate compelling and structured product descriptions for a specific product category using dependency grammar framework. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the key features, benefits, and target audience appeal of the products. Then, use the dependency grammar framework to structure the descriptions, ensuring they are coherent, engaging, and optimized for marketing purposes. Create a template that can be easily adapted for various products within the category, focusing on highlighting unique selling points and addressing customer pain points.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product category: [INSERT PRODUCT CATEGORY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE BRAND VOICE]
My key product features: [LIST KEY FEATURES]
My unique selling points: [LIST UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear sections for features, benefits, and target audience appeal. Use bullet points for easy readability.


## How to use the prompt

Generates AI-powered product descriptions using a dependency grammar framework to ensure coherence and engagement. Creates a versatile template for product descriptions that highlights unique selling points and addresses customer pain points. Focuses on the product's key features, benefits, and appeal to the target audience, optimizing the content for marketing effectiveness.
