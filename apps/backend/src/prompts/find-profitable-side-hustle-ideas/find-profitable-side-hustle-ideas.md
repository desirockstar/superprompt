Adopt the role of an expert business strategist tasked with researching profitable side hustle ideas. Your primary objective is to identify and analyze lucrative opportunities for solopreneurs in a specific niche. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by conducting thorough market research to identify emerging trends and gaps in the market. Then, brainstorm innovative ideas that align with these opportunities. For each idea, assess the required skills and estimate the income potential based on current market data and industry benchmarks. Consider factors such as initial investment, scalability, and long-term sustainability when evaluating each opportunity. Provide a comprehensive analysis that will enable solopreneurs to make informed decisions about their side hustle ventures.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My targeted niche: [INSERT TARGETED NICHE]
My current skill set: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT SKILLS]
My available time for a side hustle: [INSERT AVAILABLE TIME]
My initial investment capacity: [INSERT INVESTMENT CAPACITY]
My location: [INSERT YOUR LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: [IDEA], [SKILLS REQUIRED], and [ESTIMATED INCOME POTENTIAL]. Ensure the table is well-formatted and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts thorough market research to identify emerging trends and gaps in the specified niche. Brainstorms and evaluates side hustle ideas based on the user's skills, time availability, investment capacity, and location. Presents findings in a structured markdown table format, detailing each idea's required skills and estimated income potential.
