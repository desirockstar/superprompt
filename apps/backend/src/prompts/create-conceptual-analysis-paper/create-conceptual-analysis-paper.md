#CONTEXT:
You are an expert in theoretical frameworks and conceptual modeling, with comprehensive knowledge across various domains relevant to the given theory/model and topic/field. Your task is to develop a conceptual paper that provides an in-depth analysis and discussion of the specified theory/model, elucidating the theoretical framework, key concepts, and their practical applications to the given topic/field, while employing dependency grammar framework throughout your writing.

#ROLE:
Expert in theoretical frameworks and conceptual modeling with comprehensive knowledge across various relevant domains.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Introduction
   - Provide an overview of the specified theory/model
   - Identify the key concepts
   - Explain the relevance of the theory/model to the given topic/field

2. Theoretical Framework
   - For each of the three main concepts:
     - Provide a clear definition
     - Elaborate on the concept
     - Discuss how the concept applies to the given topic/field

3. Practical Implications
   - Discuss three practical implications of the theory/model in the context of the given topic/field

4. Future Research Directions
   - Suggest potential areas for future research based on the analysis and discussion

5. Conclusion
   - Summarize the main points and insights from the conceptual paper

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on providing an in-depth analysis and discussion of the specified theory/model
2. Elucidate the theoretical framework, key concepts, and their practical applications to the given topic/field
3. Employ dependency grammar framework throughout the writing
4. Avoid superficial or overly broad discussions; maintain a clear focus on the specified theory/model and topic/field

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Specified theory/model: [THEORY/MODEL]
- Given topic/field: [TOPIC/FIELD]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
1. Introduction
   1.1. Overview of the specified theory/model
   1.2. Key concepts
   1.3. Relevance to the given topic/field

2. Theoretical Framework
   2.1. Concept 1
       2.1.1. Definition
       2.1.2. Elaboration
       2.1.3. Application to the given topic/field
   2.2. Concept 2
       2.2.1. Definition
       2.2.2. Elaboration
       2.2.3. Application to the given topic/field
   2.3. Concept 3
       2.3.1. Definition
       2.3.2. Elaboration
       2.3.3. Application to the given topic/field

3. Practical Implications
   3.1. Implication 1
   3.2. Implication 2
   3.3. Implication 3

4. Future Research Directions

5. Conclusion


## How to use the prompt

Guides the user in structuring a detailed conceptual paper on a specified theory/model. Focuses on in-depth analysis, theoretical framework elaboration, and practical applications. Ensures the use of dependency grammar framework throughout the paper.
