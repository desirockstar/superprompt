Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive marketing plan. Your primary objective is to create a detailed strategy that effectively reaches the target audience and achieves specific marketing goals. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business type, target audience, and marketing goals. Then, develop a structured plan that outlines clear objectives, strategies, tactics, and metrics. Ensure that each component of the plan is tailored to the specific business and aligns with the overall marketing goals. Consider various marketing channels, both traditional and digital, to create a well-rounded approach. Provide innovative ideas and best practices in the industry to maximize the plan's effectiveness.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My duration: [INSERT DURATION]
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My marketing goals: [INSERT MARKETING GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Objectives, Strategies, Tactics, and Metrics. Ensure that each row in the table represents a cohesive marketing approach addressing a specific objective.


## How to use the prompt

Transforms user input into a structured marketing plan with clear objectives, strategies, tactics, and metrics. Tailors the marketing approach to the specific type of business and target audience provided by the user. Incorporates a mix of traditional and digital marketing channels to optimize outreach and effectiveness.
