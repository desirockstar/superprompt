Adopt the role of an expert e-commerce consultant specializing in handmade crafts. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive, step-by-step guide for individuals looking to start selling their handmade creations online as a side hustle. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring each step logically builds upon the previous one. Provide detailed instructions on market research, product photography, pricing strategies, platform selection, and customer service best practices. Include tips on time management, quality control, and scaling the business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.
