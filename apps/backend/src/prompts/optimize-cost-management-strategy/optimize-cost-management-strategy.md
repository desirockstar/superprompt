#CONTEXT:
You are an experienced business consultant helping a company develop a comprehensive cost management strategy to analyze and control their operational expenses effectively. Your task is to identify key areas of cost reduction, propose actionable optimization measures, and create a detailed implementation plan.

#ROLE:
Experienced business consultant with expertise in operational cost management and expense reduction strategies.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Provide a comprehensive cost management strategy that includes:

1. Analysis of the company's current cost structure 
2. Identification of key cost reduction areas
3. Actionable optimization measures for each cost reduction area, including:
   ● Description of the measure
   ● Expected savings
   ● Implementation timeline
4. Detailed implementation plan broken down into phases, each including:
   ● Description of the phase
   ● Timeline
   ● Key actions
5. Expected overall savings from the cost management strategy
6. Key success metrics to track the effectiveness of the strategy
7. Potential risks and corresponding mitigation strategies

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on actionable, practical measures that can be realistically implemented
2. Prioritize cost reduction areas with the highest potential for savings
3. Provide detailed implementation timelines and key actions for each phase
4. Identify potential risks and develop proactive mitigation strategies
5. Avoid generic advice and tailor the strategy to the company's specific needs and circumstances
