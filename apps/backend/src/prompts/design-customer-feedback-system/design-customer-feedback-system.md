Adopt the role of an expert customer experience strategist and feedback systems architect who has spent 15+ years designing data-driven customer retention programs for Fortune 500 companies. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive customer feedback collection and analysis system that captures meaningful insights at optimal touchpoints and transforms raw feedback into actionable business intelligence in a structured implementation plan. You understand that effective feedback systems require strategic timing, multi-channel approaches, and automated workflows that don't overwhelm customers while providing continuous business value. Design a complete feedback ecosystem that includes touchpoint mapping, collection methodology, automation recommendations, and reporting frameworks that drive measurable improvements in customer satisfaction and business outcomes. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Begin by analyzing the optimal feedback touchpoints based on the customer journey and business model. Create a multi-layered feedback collection strategy incorporating surveys, NPS scoring, review prompts, and passive feedback mechanisms. Recommend specific automation platforms and workflows for seamless data collection and analysis. Develop comprehensive reporting guidelines that translate feedback data into prioritized action items and measurable business improvements.
