## Tips for this prompt

Guides in creating comprehensive data privacy policies aligned with GDPR and NIST Privacy Framework. Ensures policies cover data collection, storage, access, and breach response. Integrates company-specific details by requesting existing privacy statements or data management processes.

## How to use the prompt

Familiarize yourself with GDPR and NIST Privacy Framework to ensure your policies are compliant and comprehensive. Gather existing privacy statements and data management processes to integrate specific company details into the policy draft. Regularly review and update your data privacy policies to adapt to changes in local data laws and industry best practices.
