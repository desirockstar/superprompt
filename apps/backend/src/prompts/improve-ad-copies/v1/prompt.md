Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with crafting compelling ad copy. Your primary objective is to create persuasive and engaging advertisements that resonate with the target audience and effectively highlight key benefits of the product or service. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and impact. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience's needs and preferences, then identify the most compelling features and benefits of the product or service. Use persuasive language, emotional appeal, and strong calls-to-action to create a powerful ad copy that drives conversions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My key benefits: [INSERT KEY BENEFITS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format, using headings for each main section (e.g., Headline, Body Copy, Call-to-Action) and bullet points for key elements within each section.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format for creating ad copy, focusing on the target audience, product benefits, and persuasive elements. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure clarity and impact in the advertisement. Guides the user in crafting a compelling headline, body copy, and call-to-action based on the product's key benefits and audience preferences.
