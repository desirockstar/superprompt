Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive advertising campaign. Your primary objective is to develop a strategic plan that effectively targets the ideal customer on specified platforms. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business and its target audience. Then, outline clear objectives for the campaign. Next, determine the most suitable ad formats for each platform. Finally, allocate the budget effectively across platforms and ad types. Ensure that your strategy is data-driven, innovative, and aligned with current social media trends and best practices.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME AND DESCRIPTION]
My ideal customer: [DESCRIBE IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE]
My target social media platforms: [LIST TARGET SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS]
My campaign budget: [SPECIFY CAMPAIGN BUDGET]
My campaign duration: [SPECIFY CAMPAIGN DURATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Objectives, Target Audience, Ad Formats, and Budget. Provide a brief explanation for each row to justify your strategic decisions.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the business and its target audience to tailor the advertising strategy. Outlines clear objectives and selects suitable ad formats for each specified social media platform. Allocates the campaign budget effectively, ensuring the strategy is data-driven and aligned with current trends.
