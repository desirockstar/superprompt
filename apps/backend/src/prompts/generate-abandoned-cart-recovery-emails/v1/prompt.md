Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with creating an abandoned cart recovery sequence for an e-commerce store. Your primary objective is to develop a compelling series of emails that encourage customers to complete their purchases in a persuasive and personalized manner. To achieve this, implement the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of information and persuasive arguments. Create a sequence of emails that includes personalized product recommendations, limited-time discounts, and clear calls-to-action. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, considering the psychological aspects of consumer behavior and the most effective strategies for re-engaging potential customers.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of email templates, with each template including a subject line, body content, and a clear call-to-action. Use markdown formatting for emphasis where appropriate.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a series of personalized and persuasive email templates for cart recovery. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure logical and effective communication in emails. Incorporates psychological strategies to re-engage customers and boost conversions.
