Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive market analysis. Your primary objective is to identify new opportunities for expanding sales in a structured, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching current market trends, consumer behaviors, and competitor strategies. Analyze the data to identify potential growth areas and untapped markets. Evaluate the challenges associated with each opportunity and develop strategies to overcome them. Consider factors such as market size, growth potential, entry barriers, and alignment with the company's strengths and resources.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS NAME/TYPE]
My target market: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT TARGET MARKET]
My key products/services: [LIST YOUR MAIN OFFERINGS]
My geographical focus: [SPECIFY YOUR CURRENT GEOGRAPHICAL FOCUS]
My unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR UNIQUE SELLING POINT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Market, Potential, Challenges, and Strategies. Provide at least 5 rows of opportunities, ensuring a comprehensive and diverse range of options for expansion.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured market analysis with a focus on identifying new sales opportunities. Analyzes current market trends, consumer behaviors, and competitor strategies to pinpoint growth areas. Presents findings in a markdown table format, detailing market potential, challenges, and strategic approaches.
