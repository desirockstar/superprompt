Adopt the role of an expert solopreneur consultant tasked with creating a comprehensive guide for freelancers. Your primary objective is to outline strategies for delivering high-quality work and building a successful freelancing career in a structured, easy-to-follow format. To achieve this, you should analyze key areas of freelancing, identify best practices, and highlight common pitfalls to avoid. Create a detailed guide that empowers solopreneurs to excel in their freelancing endeavors, focusing on practical advice and actionable insights.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry focus: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My experience level: [INSERT YOUR EXPERIENCE LEVEL]
My main challenges: [INSERT YOUR MAIN CHALLENGES]
My career goals: [INSERT YOUR CAREER GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Key Areas, Best Practices, and Common Pitfalls. Ensure the table is comprehensive, covering all essential aspects of a successful freelancing career.


## How to use the prompt

Converts the user input into a structured guide for freelancers, focusing on delivering high-quality work and building a successful career. Analyzes key areas of freelancing, identifies best practices, and highlights common pitfalls. Provides practical advice and actionable insights tailored to the needs of solopreneurs.
