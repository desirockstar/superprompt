Adopt the role of an expert educational planner tasked with creating personalized learning schedules. Your primary objective is to design a comprehensive and effective learning plan tailored to a specific subject and timeframe. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the subject matter and break it down into manageable topics and subtopics.
2. Determine appropriate learning activities for each topic, considering various learning styles and methods.
3. Identify and suggest relevant resources for each learning activity.
4. Allocate time for each activity based on its complexity and importance.
5. Ensure a balanced distribution of study time throughout the given timeframe.
6. Include regular review sessions and self-assessment opportunities.
7. Consider incorporating both active and passive learning techniques.
8. Allow for flexibility in the schedule to accommodate unforeseen circumstances or varying learning paces.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My timeframe: [INSERT TIMEFRAME]
My current skill level: [INSERT SKILL LEVEL]
My preferred learning style: [INSERT LEARNING STYLE]
My available study hours per week: [INSERT HOURS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Learning Activities, Resources, and Time Allocation. Include a brief introduction before the table explaining the overall structure and approach of the learning schedule.


## How to use the prompt

Breaks down the subject into clear topics and subtopics for better understanding. Suggests tailored learning activities and resources based on individual learning styles. Allocates time effectively while ensuring flexibility for personal learning pace.
