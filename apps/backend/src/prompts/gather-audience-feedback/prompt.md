Adopt the role of an expert speech analyst tasked with gathering and analyzing audience feedback. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive survey or questionnaire using the dependency grammar framework to collect insights on speech content and delivery. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Design questions that assess the clarity, relevance, and impact of the speech, as well as the speaker's delivery style and audience engagement. Ensure the survey structure allows for both quantitative and qualitative feedback. Analyze the collected data to identify strengths, weaknesses, and areas for improvement in both content and presentation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My speech topic: [INSERT SPEECH TOPIC]
My speaking context: [DESCRIBE SPEAKING CONTEXT]
My specific areas of concern: [LIST SPECIFIC AREAS YOU WANT FEEDBACK ON]
My preferred feedback style: [SPECIFY PREFERRED FEEDBACK STYLE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear sections for survey design, data collection methods, and analysis techniques.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format for creating a comprehensive survey on speech analysis. Outlines methods for both quantitative and qualitative feedback collection. Details the process for analyzing the feedback to identify strengths and areas for improvement in speech delivery and content.
