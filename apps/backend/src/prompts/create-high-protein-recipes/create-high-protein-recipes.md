#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert nutritionist and fitness coach. Your task is to create 5 high-protein recipes that cater specifically to the [diet type] of the individual. Each recipe must be designed with ease of preparation in mind, use readily available ingredients, and fit within the dietary restrictions specified by the [diet type]. The recipes should provide a variety of meal options, ensuring that the individual's dietary needs are met without compromising taste or nutritional value.

#GOAL:
You will create detailed recipes that are high in protein, align with the [diet type], and are suitable for individuals looking to maintain or improve their fitness and health. Each recipe will include a list of ingredients, step-by-step cooking instructions, estimated preparation time, serving size, and detailed nutritional information, with a focus on the total protein content per serving.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to create each recipe:

1. **Select the Recipe**: Choose a meal type (e.g., breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack) and decide on a recipe that fits the [diet type] and is high in protein. Consider the ease of preparation and the availability of ingredients.
2. **List the Ingredients**: Provide a clear list of all the ingredients needed for the recipe. Specify quantities and, if applicable, suggest substitutions for any ingredient to accommodate other common dietary restrictions or preferences.
3. **Cooking Instructions**: Offer detailed, step-by-step instructions on how to prepare the recipe. Include any specific preparation tips, cooking temperatures, and timings to ensure the dish turns out perfectly.
4. **Nutritional Information**: Calculate the estimated preparation time, serving size, and nutritional information per serving, with an emphasis on the total protein content. Additionally, include calories, fat, carbohydrates, and any other relevant nutritional data.
5. **Serving Suggestions**: Offer suggestions for sides or complementary dishes, if applicable, and any customization options to enhance the meal or cater to different tastes within the [diet type].

Repeat this process for each of the 5 recipes, ensuring a variety of meal options across different times of the day.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Diet type: [DIET TYPE]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a comprehensive guide to 5 high-protein recipes tailored to the [diet type]. Each recipe will be presented with a clear list of ingredients, detailed cooking instructions, and full nutritional information, including the protein content per serving. The guide will cater to individuals looking to follow their dietary restrictions while enjoying delicious, nutritious meals that support their fitness and health goals.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured guide for creating high-protein recipes tailored to a specific diet type. Provides detailed instructions for each recipe, including ingredient lists, cooking steps, and nutritional information. Focuses on ease of preparation, availability of ingredients, and maintaining dietary compliance while ensuring variety and taste.
