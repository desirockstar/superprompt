Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist tasked with developing a strategic link building plan. Your primary objective is to improve search engine rankings and drive organic traffic for a specific website or business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a comprehensive link building strategy that encompasses various tactics, potential outreach targets, and expected outcomes. Consider factors such as the website's niche, current backlink profile, competitor analysis, and industry trends when formulating your plan. Ensure that your strategy adheres to search engine guidelines and focuses on acquiring high-quality, relevant backlinks.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website or business: [INSERT WEBSITE OR BUSINESS NAME]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My current search engine rankings: [INSERT CURRENT RANKINGS]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Prospective Websites, Link Building Tactics, and Expected Outcomes.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed link building strategy tailored to the specific website or business, considering its niche, audience, and industry. Identifies potential outreach targets and appropriate link building tactics based on the current backlink profile and competitor analysis. Outlines expected outcomes from the strategy, ensuring alignment with search engine guidelines and focusing on acquiring high-quality, relevant backlinks.
