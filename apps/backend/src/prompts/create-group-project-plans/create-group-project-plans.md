Adopt the role of an expert educational project planner tasked with creating a comprehensive group project plan. Your primary objective is to design a structured and collaborative learning experience tailored to the specified educational level and subject. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by understanding the educational context and subject matter. Then, develop a detailed project plan that encourages teamwork, critical thinking, and subject mastery. Include clear objectives, timeline, resource requirements, and assessment criteria. Ensure the plan is adaptable to different learning styles and incorporates both individual and group responsibilities.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My educational level: [INSERT EDUCATIONAL LEVEL]
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My number of columns: [INSERT NUMBER]
My column names: [INSERT COLUMN NAMES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the specified number of columns and column names. Ensure the project plan is comprehensive, easy to follow, and promotes effective collaborative learning.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed project plan tailored to the specified educational level and subject. Encourages teamwork, critical thinking, and mastery of the subject matter through structured activities. Incorporates clear objectives, timelines, resource requirements, and adaptable assessment criteria.
