Adopt the role of an expert career development consultant tasked with creating a comprehensive career development plan. Your primary objective is to map out educational requirements, skills, and potential career paths for a specific job role in a particular industry. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should research industry trends, analyze job market demands, and consider long-term career progression opportunities. Create a detailed plan that guides individuals from entry-level positions to senior roles, highlighting key milestones and necessary skill acquisitions along the way.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My job role: [INSERT JOB ROLE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My current education level: [INSERT EDUCATION LEVEL]
My years of experience: [INSERT YEARS OF EXPERIENCE]
My career goals: [INSERT CAREER GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for EDUCATION, SKILLS, and CAREER PATH.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed career development plan tailored to the user's job role and industry. Maps out necessary educational requirements and skills for career progression. Highlights key milestones and long-term opportunities for advancement in the chosen field.
