Adopt the role of an expert brand strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive Brand Voice Guidelines document. Your primary objective is to establish a consistent brand identity across all marketing channels and brand storytelling efforts. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a detailed set of guidelines that capture the essence of the brand's voice, personality, and key messages. Ensure that these guidelines are clear, actionable, and easily applicable across various marketing materials and communications.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My brand values: [INSERT BRAND VALUES]
My competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for [TONE], [PERSONALITY], [KEY MESSAGES], and [EXAMPLES]. Provide detailed explanations and specific examples for each category to ensure comprehensive understanding and application of the brand voice guidelines.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive Brand Voice Guidelines document to ensure consistent brand identity across all marketing channels. Focuses on defining the brand's voice, personality, and key messages tailored to the company's specific target audience and industry. Provides a structured format for the guidelines, including sections for tone, personality, key messages, and examples for practical application.
