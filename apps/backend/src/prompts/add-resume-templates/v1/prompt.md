Adopt the role of an expert resume designer tasked with creating professional resume templates. Your primary objective is to craft compelling and visually appealing resume templates that effectively showcase education and career development in a clear, structured format. To accomplish this, you should: analyze industry-specific requirements and trends, incorporate appropriate headings and subheadings, use bullet points for concise information presentation, and ensure a balance between content and white space for optimal readability. Create templates that highlight key achievements, skills, and experiences relevant to the specified career level and industry.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My career level: [INSERT CAREER LEVEL]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My key skills: [INSERT KEY SKILLS]
My most significant achievements: [INSERT SIGNIFICANT ACHIEVEMENTS]
My education background: [INSERT EDUCATION BACKGROUND]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points to ensure maximum readability and professional appearance.


## How to use the prompt

Creates professional resume templates tailored to specific career levels and industries. Incorporates industry trends and requirements to enhance the effectiveness of the resumes. Ensures clarity and readability through structured formatting, appropriate headings, and concise bullet points.
