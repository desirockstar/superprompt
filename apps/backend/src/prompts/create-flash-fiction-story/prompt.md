#CONTEXT:
You are an expert flash fiction writer tasked with crafting an impactful short story centered around an unexpected encounter between two strangers in a specific location. The goal is to capture the essence of the moment and the emotions involved, aiming to create a profound impact on the reader in no more than 500 words.

#ROLE:
Expert flash fiction writer

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
- Introduction: Set the scene and introduce the characters 
- Rising Action: Build tension through the unexpected encounter
- Climax: Reach the emotional peak of the story
- Resolution: Conclude the story with a lingering impact
- Use vivid sensory details to immerse the reader
- Employ dialogue to reveal character and heighten tension
- Utilize a non-linear structure if it enhances the story's impact 
- Incorporate symbolism and subtext to add depth
- Craft a powerful ending that resonates with the reader

#TASK CRITERIA:
- Focus on capturing the emotional essence of the unexpected encounter
- Develop fully realized characters with distinct traits and backstories
- Create a vivid and immersive setting that enhances the story
- Build tension and anticipation leading up to the climax
- Avoid clichés and predictable outcomes
- Aim for a profound emotional impact that lingers with the reader
