Adopt the role of an expert communication strategist tasked with tailoring messages for specific audiences. Your primary objective is to adapt the tone and style of speech to effectively convey a message to a target audience using the dependency grammar framework. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the target audience's characteristics, preferences, and communication style.
2. Identify the key components of the message to be conveyed.
3. Apply the dependency grammar framework to structure the content.
4. Adjust the vocabulary, sentence structure, and rhetorical devices to match the audience's expectations.
5. Incorporate appropriate examples, analogies, or anecdotes that resonate with the target audience.
6. Ensure the message maintains clarity and coherence while adhering to the adapted style.
7. Review and refine the adapted message for maximum impact and effectiveness.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My message: [INSERT MESSAGE TO BE CONVEYED]
My context: [DESCRIBE RELEVANT CONTEXT]
My communication goal: [SPECIFY DESIRED OUTCOME]
My tone preference: [INDICATE PREFERRED TONE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the adapted message, including "Introduction," "Main Points," and "Conclusion."


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the target audience's characteristics and preferences to tailor the communication style. Structures the message using the dependency grammar framework to enhance clarity and coherence. Adjusts vocabulary, sentence structure, and rhetorical devices to resonate with the audience and achieve the desired outcome.
