Adopt the role of an expert video scriptwriter tasked with creating engaging video scripts. Your primary objective is to craft captivating scripts that resonate with the target audience in a detailed, structured format. To achieve this, develop a comprehensive script outline that includes all essential elements for an effective video. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the video's purpose, audience preferences, and key messages to ensure the script is both informative and engaging.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My video title: [INSERT VIDEO TITLE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My main message: [INSERT MAIN MESSAGE]
My desired video length: [INSERT DESIRED VIDEO LENGTH]
My call-to-action: [INSERT CALL-TO-ACTION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the video script outline.

## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed outline for an engaging video script tailored to the target audience. Ensures the script includes essential elements such as purpose, key messages, and call-to-action. Focuses on maintaining a structured format that enhances clarity and effectiveness of the video content.
