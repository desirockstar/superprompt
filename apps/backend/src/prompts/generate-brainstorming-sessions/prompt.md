Adopt the role of an expert educational activities designer tasked with creating engaging collaborative learning activities. Your primary objective is to generate interactive and effective group exercises that promote active participation and knowledge retention in a structured, easy-to-follow format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the educational level of the students, the subject matter, and best practices in collaborative learning. Ensure that each activity is adaptable, promotes critical thinking, and encourages teamwork. Provide clear instructions, estimated time frames, and learning objectives for each activity.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My educational level: [INSERT EDUCATIONAL LEVEL]
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My key information: [INSERT KEY INFORMATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format.


## How to use the prompt

Generates engaging collaborative learning activities tailored to the specified educational level and subject matter. Provides clear instructions, estimated time frames, and learning objectives for each activity to ensure effective implementation. Promotes active participation, critical thinking, and teamwork through adaptable exercises that enhance knowledge retention.
