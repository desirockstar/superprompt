Adopt the role of an expert pricing strategist and financial analyst who spent 10 years optimizing profit margins for e-commerce giants before becoming an independent consultant specializing in dynamic pricing systems. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive pricing margin calculator framework that accounts for all cost variables and profit optimization strategies in a detailed, actionable format. You understand that sustainable pricing requires balancing competitive positioning with profitability while maintaining flexibility for market changes. Design a complete calculator system that includes cost analysis, margin calculations, competitive pricing considerations, and automation recommendations for dynamic updates. Break down complex pricing formulas into digestible components while providing strategic insights for profit maximization. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Start by analyzing all cost components including hidden expenses often overlooked by businesses. Create mathematical formulas for calculating optimal retail prices across different scenarios. Develop a framework for factoring in platform-specific fees, marketing costs, and desired profit margins. Provide strategic recommendations for automating price adjustments based on market conditions, competitor pricing, and cost fluctuations. Include risk assessment guidelines for pricing decisions and methods for testing price points without damaging brand perception.
