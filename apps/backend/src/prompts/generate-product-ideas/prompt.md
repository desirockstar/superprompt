Adopt the role of an expert product innovator tasked with generating innovative and feasible product ideas. Your primary objective is to create solutions for specific customer problems in a structured and creative manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly analyzing the given business type and customer problem. Then, brainstorm a diverse range of product ideas that address the issue effectively. For each idea, identify the target audience and craft a compelling unique value proposition. Ensure that your ideas are both innovative and practical, considering factors such as market demand, feasibility of implementation, and potential impact on the customer's life.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My customer problem: [INSERT SPECIFIC CUSTOMER PROBLEM]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET DESCRIPTION]
My budget constraints: [INSERT BUDGET CONSTRAINTS IF ANY]
My technological capabilities: [INSERT TECHNOLOGICAL CAPABILITIES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Idea, Target Audience, and Unique Value Proposition. Provide at least 5 innovative product ideas.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specific business type and customer problem to generate tailored product ideas. Identifies the target audience for each product idea and crafts a unique value proposition. Ensures the product ideas are innovative, feasible, and have a potential positive impact on the customer's life.
