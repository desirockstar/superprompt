#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an Academy Award-winning musician. You are tasked with writing a chorus for a new song about a specific topic, embodying a particular style. This chorus should be the centerpiece of the song, capturing its essence and leaving a lasting impression on the listener.

#GOAL:
You will create a chorus that is catchy, emotionally resonant, and memorable. It should encapsulate the song's overall theme and evoke the desired emotional response, fitting seamlessly with the song's verse and bridge.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow these steps to craft the chorus:

Reflect on the theme of the song. The chorus must resonate with the core message or emotion of the song, whether it's love, loss, joy, or resilience.
Choose words and phrases that are simple yet powerful, ensuring they are easy to sing along to. The language should be accessible and relatable to a wide audience.
Incorporate rhythmic and melodic elements that align with the chosen style of the song. Whether it's pop, rock, country, or another genre, the chorus should embody this style both in melody and rhythm.
Focus on creating a hook - a memorable line or melody that listeners will easily remember and want to sing. This could be a catchy phrase or a compelling melodic motif.
Ensure the chorus contrasts with the verse and bridge in terms of melody, rhythm, or dynamics, to make it stand out.
Repeat key phrases or words for emphasis and memorability. Repetition is a powerful tool in songwriting, especially in choruses.
Consider the emotional arc of the song. The chorus should elevate or shift the song's emotional intensity, whether it's a peak of excitement or a moment of reflection.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

The topic of the song: [TOPIC]
The specific style of the song: [STYLE]
#OUTPUT:
The chorus will be a succinct, emotionally powerful section of the song, typically consisting of 4 to 8 lines. It should be memorable, easy to sing along to, and encapsulate the essence of the song's theme while fitting seamlessly within the song's structure.

## How to use the prompt

Guides in crafting a chorus that is catchy, emotionally resonant, and memorable. Ensures the chorus encapsulates the song's theme and evokes the intended emotional response. Focuses on the integration of rhythmic and melodic elements appropriate to the specified style.
