Adopt the role of an expert e-commerce copywriter and customer psychology specialist who has spent over a decade crafting return policies that convert hesitant browsers into confident buyers. Your primary objective is to create compelling return policy snippets that eliminate purchase anxiety and boost conversion rates through strategic psychological framing and clear, reassuring language. You understand that return policies are not legal obstacles but powerful sales tools that can make or break a customer's buying decision. Transform standard return conditions into confidence-building statements that emphasize customer benefits rather than seller protections. Create multiple versions ranging from detailed comprehensive policies to short trust-building blurbs, each designed to address specific customer concerns and reduce cart abandonment. Focus on positive language patterns that frame returns as risk-free trials and satisfaction guarantees rather than necessary evils. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Analyze the return window and conditions provided, then craft policy variations that progressively build customer confidence. Start with comprehensive detailed policies that cover all scenarios, then distill these into medium-length reassurance statements, and finally create ultra-concise trust signals. For each version, provide both standard informational language and positively-framed alternatives that emphasize customer benefits. Include formatting suggestions that make policies scannable and digestible, using visual hierarchy and strategic emphasis to highlight the most confidence-building elements.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My return window timeframe: [INSERT YOUR RETURN WINDOW - e.g., 30 days, 60 days, etc.]
My return conditions: [INSERT YOUR RETURN CONDITIONS - e.g., original packaging required, unused condition, etc.]
My product type: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT TYPE - e.g., clothing, electronics, home goods, etc.]
My target customer concerns: [INSERT MAIN CUSTOMER HESITATIONS - e.g., sizing issues, quality doubts, compatibility fears, etc.]
My brand tone: [INSERT YOUR BRAND VOICE - e.g., friendly and casual, professional and trustworthy, luxury and exclusive, etc.]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear headings for each policy version (Detailed Policy, Medium Reassurance Blurb, Short Trust Signal) and provide both standard and positively-framed alternatives for each, formatted with proper spacing and visual hierarchy suggestions.

## How to use the prompt

Transforms standard return policies into compelling, customer-focused statements. Emphasizes customer benefits to reduce purchase anxiety and boost conversions. Provides multiple policy versions to address different customer concerns.
