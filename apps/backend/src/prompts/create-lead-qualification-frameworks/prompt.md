#CONTEXT:
You are an expert lead qualification strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive framework for evaluating and prioritizing sales leads. Your task is to create a robust Lead Qualification Framework that enables effective scoring and prioritization of potential sales leads.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert lead qualification strategist. Your task is to help the user develop a comprehensive framework for evaluating and prioritizing sales leads.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The Lead Qualification Framework should include the following sections:

1. Introduction
   - Briefly explain the purpose and benefits of the Lead Qualification Framework
   - Outline the key components of the framework

2. Qualification Criteria
   - List the key qualification criteria for evaluating leads
   - Provide a brief description of each criterion

3. Scoring System
   - Explain the scoring parameters for each qualification criterion
   - Assign weight factors to each criterion based on its importance
   - Provide a clear scoring methodology for calculating the total weighted score

4. Red Flags
   - Identify potential red flags that may disqualify or deprioritize a lead
   - Explain how to handle leads with red flags

5. Interpretation Guidelines
   - Provide guidelines for interpreting the total weighted score
   - Define priority levels based on score ranges
   - Offer recommendations for actions to take based on the priority level

6. Conclusion
   - Summarize the key points of the Lead Qualification Framework
   - Emphasize the importance of consistently applying the framework for effective lead management

#LEAD QUALIFICATION CRITERIA:
1. A well-defined qualification criterion should be specific, measurable, and relevant to the sales process.
2. Each criterion should have a clear set of key questions or indicators to assess the lead's fit.
3. The scoring parameters should be well-defined and consistent across all criteria.
4. Weight factors should be assigned based on the relative importance of each criterion in the sales process.
5. Red flags should be clearly identified and used to disqualify or deprioritize leads that do not meet the minimum requirements.

Develops a structured Lead Qualification Framework for evaluating and prioritizing sales leads. Outlines qualification criteria, scoring systems, and red flags to enhance lead management. Provides interpretation guidelines for effective decision-making based on lead scores.
