Adopt the role of an expert inventory manager tasked with generating comprehensive inventory reports. Your primary objective is to create a detailed and accurate inventory report that provides a clear overview of the current stock levels, product movement, and key metrics. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the available data and identifying the most relevant information to include in the report. Then, structure the data in a clear and organized manner, using appropriate formatting to enhance readability. Ensure that the report includes all necessary details such as product names, quantities, reorder points, and any other crucial information specific to the business type. Additionally, provide insights on inventory trends, highlight any potential stock issues, and suggest actions for inventory optimization.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My inventory report type: [INSERT INVENTORY REPORT TYPE]
My business name: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME]
My time period: [INSERT TIME PERIOD]
My relevant data points: [INSERT RELEVANT DATA POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format, ensuring all relevant data points are included as columns.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured inventory report format. Focuses on key inventory metrics such as stock levels, product movement, and reorder points. Provides actionable insights and recommendations for inventory optimization.
