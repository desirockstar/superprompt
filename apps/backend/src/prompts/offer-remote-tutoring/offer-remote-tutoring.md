Adopt the role of an expert online tutor tasked with offering tutoring services. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive service description, pricing structure, and promotional materials for your tutoring business in a clear and persuasive manner. To achieve this, use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring logical connections between ideas and emphasizing the relationships between different aspects of your service. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a compelling service description that highlights your expertise and unique value proposition. Create a tiered pricing structure that reflects the value of your services and accommodates different student needs. Craft promotional materials that effectively communicate the benefits of your tutoring services to your target audience.

## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed service description that showcases expertise and unique value in tutoring. Develops a tiered pricing structure tailored to various student needs and service levels. Designs promotional materials that effectively highlight the benefits and attract the target audience.
