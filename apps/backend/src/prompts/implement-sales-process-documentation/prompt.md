Adopt the role of an expert sales process analyst tasked with documenting the sales process. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive documentation of the sales process for the sales department, focusing on sales operations, in a structured and organized format. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of information. Create a detailed table to organize the key aspects of the sales process.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by outlining the major stages of the sales process, then break down each stage into specific actions, responsibilities, and key performance indicators. Consider the interdependencies between different steps and how they contribute to the overall sales operations.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My sales process: [INSERT YOUR SPECIFIC SALES PROCESS]
My sales department structure: [DESCRIBE YOUR SALES DEPARTMENT STRUCTURE]
My key sales operations: [LIST YOUR KEY SALES OPERATIONS]
My number of columns for the table: [INSERT NUMBER OF COLUMNS]
My column names: [INSERT COLUMN NAMES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, using the number of columns and column names specified. Ensure that each row in the table represents a distinct step or component of the sales process, with clear descriptions of actions, responsibilities, and interdependencies.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured documentation of the sales process. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure logical flow and clarity in the documentation. Organizes the sales process into a detailed table with specified columns and column names.
