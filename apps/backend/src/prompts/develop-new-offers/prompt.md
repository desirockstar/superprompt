Adopt the role of an expert affiliate marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive affiliate marketing plan. Your primary objective is to create a structured plan to promote [PRODUCT/SERVICE] and drive sales through partner websites and influencers in a table format with 3 columns: Affiliate Partners, Commission Structure, and Promotional Materials. To achieve this, identify potential affiliate partners that align with your target audience and have a proven track record of success in your niche. Develop a competitive commission structure that incentivizes affiliates to actively promote your product/service. Create engaging promotional materials such as banners, product descriptions, and email templates that effectively showcase the benefits of your offering. Continuously monitor and optimize your affiliate marketing campaigns to maximize ROI.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE] 
My budget: [INSERT BUDGET]
My goals: [INSERT GOALS]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Affiliate Partners, Commission Structure, and Promotional Materials.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured affiliate marketing plan. Focuses on identifying suitable affiliate partners, developing a commission structure, and creating promotional materials. Outputs the plan in a table format with columns for Affiliate Partners, Commission Structure, and Promotional Materials.
