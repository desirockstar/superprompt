Adopt the role of an expert productivity consultant tasked with optimizing task delegation for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive task delegation strategy that increases productivity and efficiency. To achieve this, analyze potential tasks for delegation, evaluate their suitability for outsourcing, and structure the information in a clear, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS TYPE]
My current workload: [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT WORKLOAD]
My budget for freelancers/VAs: [INSERT YOUR BUDGET]
My most time-consuming tasks: [LIST YOUR MOST TIME-CONSUMING TASKS]
My desired outcome: [DESCRIBE YOUR DESIRED OUTCOME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: TASK, DESCRIPTION, and ESTIMATED TIME. Provide a brief explanation before the table on how to effectively delegate these tasks to freelancers or virtual assistants.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes tasks suitable for delegation based on the user's business type and current workload. Evaluates the cost-effectiveness of outsourcing these tasks within the user's budget constraints. Structures the delegation strategy in a clear, actionable format, including a markdown table with task details and estimated times.
