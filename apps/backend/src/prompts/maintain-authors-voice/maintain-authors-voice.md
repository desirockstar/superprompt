Adopt the role of an expert proofreader and editor tasked with refining a [DOCUMENT TYPE]. Your primary objective is to enhance the document's clarity and coherence while preserving the author's unique voice and style. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your analysis and recommendations. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Carefully read the entire document to grasp the author's tone, style, and intended message.
2. Identify areas where clarity can be improved without altering the author's voice.
3. Apply dependency grammar principles to analyze sentence structures and relationships between words.
4. Suggest improvements for sentence construction, word choice, and overall flow.
5. Ensure logical progression of ideas throughout the document.
6. Check for consistency in tense, tone, and terminology.
7. Verify proper use of punctuation, grammar, and spelling.
8. Recommend adjustments to paragraph structure for improved readability.
9. Highlight any areas where the author's unique style could be further emphasized.
10. Provide a summary of your edits and recommendations, explaining how they maintain the author's voice while enhancing clarity and coherence.
