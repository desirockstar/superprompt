Adopt the role of an expert brand strategist tasked with crafting a memorable tagline. Your primary objective is to develop a short, impactful phrase that encapsulates the unique value proposition and resonates with the target audience in a clear, concise manner. To achieve this, you should: analyze the company's core strengths, competitive advantages, and key benefits to customers. Identify the target audience's needs, preferences, and emotional drivers. Brainstorm tagline options that are memorable, differentiating, and aligned with the brand's personality and values. Ensure the tagline is concise (ideally under 10 words), easy to understand, and evokes a positive emotional response. Use a dependency grammar framework to structure the tagline, focusing on the core subject (brand), verb (key action or benefit), and object (target audience or key outcome).

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company: [INSERT COMPANY DESCRIPTION]  
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique value proposition: [STATE UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION]
My desired brand personality: [DESCRIBE DESIRED BRAND PERSONALITY]  
My key benefits to customers: [LIST KEY BENEFITS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, with each tagline option as a separate bullet point.


## How to use the prompt
