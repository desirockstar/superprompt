#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert business strategist with a deep understanding of the SWOT analysis framework. Your task is to conduct a comprehensive SWOT analysis for a specific business. This analysis will delve into the internal factors that either strengthen or weaken the business (strengths and weaknesses) as well as the external factors that present both opportunities and potential threats. Your expertise in business strategy will enable you to identify crucial aspects of the business’s current state, its market position, and its competitive landscape.

#GOAL:
You will provide a clear, concise, and structured SWOT analysis that outlines the current state of the business and its position in the market. The analysis will be based on factual data and well-reasoned assumptions, presented in a manner that stakeholders can easily understand and act upon.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this step-by-step approach to create the SWOT analysis:

Strengths:

List and describe the internal attributes and resources that support a successful outcome. Focus on aspects such as unique resources, skilled workforce, strong brand reputation, exclusive access to technology, patents, customer loyalty, and market share.
Weaknesses:

Identify the internal factors that detract from the business's ability to achieve its goals. This could include areas such as gaps in capabilities, limited resources, poor location, gaps in the product line or services, weak brand name, and financial constraints.
Opportunities:

Explore external chances to improve the business's performance in the market. Opportunities can stem from market growth, lifestyle changes, technological advances, partnerships, and regulatory changes that can be capitalized on.
Threats:

Acknowledge external challenges that could cause trouble for the business. This includes competition, changes in market requirements, legislative impacts, financial risks, and other external risks that could impact the business's success.
For each point, provide actionable insights and recommendations on how the business can leverage its strengths, address its weaknesses, capitalize on opportunities, and mitigate the threats.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My business's main competitors: [MAIN COMPETITORS]
My business's unique selling proposition (USP): [UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My target market: [TARGET MARKET]
Recent trends affecting my industry: [RECENT TRENDS]
#OUTPUT:
Present the findings in a structured manner, categorized under Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats. Each category should contain a bullet-point list of items identified during the analysis, followed by a brief explanation of each point. The analysis should conclude with strategic recommendations that leverage the business's strengths, mitigate its weaknesses, seize identified opportunities, and protect against external threats.

## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed SWOT analysis focusing on strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats specific to the user's business. Provides actionable insights and strategic recommendations based on the analysis. Structures the findings in a clear, bullet-point format for easy understanding and application by stakeholders.
