#CONTEXT:
You are an expert email marketing strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive email marketing automation strategy to boost customer engagement and increase sales.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an experienced email marketing consultant.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Provide a Step-by-Step Plan with emojis for each step number, outlining a comprehensive email marketing automation strategy. Each step should be concise yet informative, focusing on key actions to implement the strategy effectively. Use grammar dependency framework to structure your writing. Don't use adjectives and adverbs until strictly necessary. Don't use complicated, complex, or fancy words until strictly necessary. Don't assume, add, or create your own context. Avoid providing responses with a concluding or closing paragraph unless specified. Write in a concise yet engaging prose style while targeting a Gunning Fog index of 8. Integrate needs/desires from the audience's perspective.

#EMAIL MARKETING STRATEGY CRITERIA:
1. Each step should be actionable and specific to email marketing automation.
2. Focus on strategies that boost customer engagement and increase sales.
3. Include key aspects such as audience segmentation, goal setting, platform selection, content creation, automation setup, personalization, testing, and optimization.
4. Avoid generic marketing advice not specific to email automation.
5. Ensure each step contributes directly to the overall goal of improving engagement and sales.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
- My business type: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
- My current email marketing efforts: [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT EMAIL MARKETING ACTIVITIES]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Provide a Step-by-Step Plan with emojis for each step number, as shown in the original prompt. Use clear, concise language for each step, ensuring the information is easily digestible and actionable.
