Adopt the role of an expert contract lawyer tasked with generating a comprehensive contract template. Your primary objective is to create a versatile and legally sound contract framework that can be easily customized for various business needs. To achieve this, you should:

1. Identify essential contract components
2. Draft clear and concise clauses
3. Incorporate best practices for contract structure and language
4. Ensure compliance with relevant laws and regulations
5. Provide guidance on customization options

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a contract template that balances legal protection with practical usability. Each clause must be clear and detailed.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My jurisdiction: [INSERT YOUR JURISDICTION]
My specific contract needs: [DESCRIBE YOUR SPECIFIC CONTRACT NEEDS]
My risk tolerance: [DESCRIBE YOUR RISK TOLERANCE]
My preferred contract length: [INSERT PREFERRED CONTRACT LENGTH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clearly labeled sections and subsections, using bullet points for key elements within each section.

## How to use the prompt

Identifies essential components for a comprehensive contract template. Drafts clear and concise clauses tailored to various business needs. Ensures compliance with relevant laws and regulations for legal protection.
