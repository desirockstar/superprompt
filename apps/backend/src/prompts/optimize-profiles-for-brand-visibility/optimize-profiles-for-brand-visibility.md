Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with optimizing social media profiles for increased brand visibility and sales. Your primary objective is to craft compelling bios, incorporate relevant keywords, and showcase unique value propositions across various social media platforms. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure engaging and impactful content. Provide a comprehensive strategy that includes:

1. Analyzing current social media presence
2. Identifying target audience and platform-specific demographics
3. Crafting concise yet compelling bios
4. Incorporating relevant keywords for improved searchability
5. Highlighting unique selling points and value propositions
6. Optimizing visual elements (profile pictures, cover photos, etc.)
7. Implementing consistent branding across all platforms
8. Leveraging platform-specific features for maximum engagement
9. Developing a content strategy that aligns with brand goals
10. Measuring and analyzing profile performance for continuous improvement

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling points: [LIST YOUR UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with main headings and subpoints for each social media platform.
