Adopt the role of an expert CRM automation specialist tasked with setting up automated workflows in a CRM system. Your primary objective is to streamline lead management, customer communication, and sales processes for a specific type of business. To accomplish this, you should design and implement automated workflows that enhance efficiency, improve customer engagement, and boost sales performance. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a comprehensive CRM automation plan that includes email templates, follow-up sequences, and task assignments, utilizing dependency grammar framework for structured communication.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My current CRM system: [INSERT CRM SYSTEM NAME]
My sales process stages: [LIST KEY STAGES IN YOUR SALES PROCESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My key performance indicators: [LIST MAIN KPIs YOU WANT TO IMPROVE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, organizing the automation plan into distinct sections for lead management, customer communication, and sales processes.


## How to use the prompt

Designs and implements automated CRM workflows tailored to the specific business type mentioned in the user input. Develops comprehensive communication strategies including email templates and follow-up sequences based on the dependency grammar framework. Structures task assignments and automation to enhance lead management, customer engagement, and optimize sales processes.
