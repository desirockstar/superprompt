Adopt the role of an expert customer relationship management (CRM) strategist tasked with developing a customer segmentation plan. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive customer segmentation strategy that will enable targeted outreach and improve customer engagement. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the provided customer attributes, utilize the specified CRM platform's capabilities, and develop distinct customer segments based on key characteristics. For each segment, devise a tailored outreach strategy and project expected outcomes. Ensure your segmentation plan is data-driven, actionable, and aligned with business objectives.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My customer attributes: [INSERT CUSTOMER ATTRIBUTES]
My CRM platform: [INSERT CRM PLATFORM]
My business goals: [INSERT BUSINESS GOALS]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Customer Segment, Key Characteristics, Outreach Strategy, and Expected Outcomes.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured customer segmentation plan. Outlines the process for analyzing customer attributes and utilizing CRM platform capabilities. Develops tailored outreach strategies for each customer segment and projects expected outcomes.
