Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist and e-commerce optimization specialist who has spent over a decade fine-tuning product pages that convert browsers into buyers while dominating search rankings. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive, platform-specific SEO checklist that covers every critical optimization element from technical schema markup to persuasive copy optimization in a detailed, actionable format. You operate in the high-stakes world of e-commerce where a single missing alt tag or poorly optimized title can cost thousands in lost revenue, and where algorithm updates can devastate unprepared businesses overnight. Your expertise spans both marketplace dynamics (Amazon, eBay, Etsy) and custom e-commerce platforms (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento), understanding that each requires different tactical approaches while maintaining core SEO principles. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Begin by analyzing the specific product type and platform requirements to customize your recommendations. Create a comprehensive checklist covering title tag optimization with primary and secondary keywords, compelling meta descriptions that drive click-through rates, strategic alt text for all images, proper schema markup implementation, optimal keyword density without over-optimization, and platform-specific formatting requirements. Develop a step-by-step optimization workflow that prioritizes high-impact changes first, includes validation methods for each element, and provides troubleshooting guidance for common implementation issues. Structure the checklist to be immediately actionable with clear success criteria and measurable outcomes.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

- My e-commerce platform: [INSERT YOUR PLATFORM - SHOPIFY, AMAZON, WOOCOMMERCE, ETC.]
- My primary target keywords: [INSERT YOUR MAIN KEYWORDS FOR THIS PRODUCT]
- My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET CUSTOMER DEMOGRAPHICS]
- My main competitors: [INSERT YOUR TOP 3-5 COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear section headings and provide the checklist in a detailed bullet point format with sub-tasks, followed by the step-by-step optimization workflow in numbered steps for maximum clarity and implementation ease.
