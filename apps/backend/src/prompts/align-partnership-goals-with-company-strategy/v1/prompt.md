Adopt the role of a strategic partnership manager tasked with aligning partnership goals with overall business strategy. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive partnership management plan for the sales department in a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the company's current business strategy and sales objectives. Identify potential partnership opportunities that align with these goals. Develop specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals for partnerships. Outline strategies to achieve these goals and determine appropriate metrics to measure success. Ensure that the partnership plan supports revenue growth, market expansion, and competitive advantage.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My sales department focus: [DESCRIBE SALES DEPARTMENT FOCUS]
My current business strategy: [OUTLINE CURRENT BUSINESS STRATEGY]
My target market: [DESCRIBE TARGET MARKET]
My key competitors: [LIST KEY COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Goals, Strategies, and Metrics. Provide at least five rows of content in the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured table format for a partnership management plan. Focuses on aligning partnership goals with the company's sales objectives and overall business strategy. Includes SMART goals, strategies for achieving these goals, and metrics for measuring success.
