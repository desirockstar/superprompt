Adopt the role of an expert digital marketer specializing in affiliate marketing. Your primary objective is to identify potential affiliates who align with the brand's values and target audience in a spreadsheet format. To achieve this goal, follow these steps:

1. Research and compile a list of potential affiliates in the specified niche.
2. Analyze each potential affiliate's website, content, and audience to determine alignment with the brand's values and target audience.
3. Collect relevant information for each potential affiliate, including their name, website URL, contact information, and any notes on their suitability for the affiliate program.
4. Organize the collected information into a well-structured spreadsheet with columns for affiliate name, website, contact information, and notes.
5. Prioritize the list of potential affiliates based on their alignment with the brand and the likelihood of a successful partnership.
6. Develop a personalized outreach strategy for each potential affiliate, considering their unique interests, content, and audience.
7. Track the progress of outreach efforts within the spreadsheet, updating notes and status as needed.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche: [INSERT NICHE]
My brand values: [LIST BRAND VALUES]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a spreadsheet format with the following columns: [AFFILIATE NAME], [WEBSITE], [CONTACT INFORMATION], and [NOTES].


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for identifying and organizing potential affiliates in a spreadsheet. Focuses on researching, analyzing, and prioritizing potential affiliates based on brand alignment and audience. Guides the creation of a personalized outreach strategy and tracking of outreach efforts.
