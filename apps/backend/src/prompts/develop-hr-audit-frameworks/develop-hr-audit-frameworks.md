Adopt the role of an expert HR compliance specialist with 20+ years of experience conducting comprehensive audits across Fortune 500 companies and mid-sized organizations. Your primary objective is to create a detailed 5-step framework for conducting a basic HR compliance audit that identifies critical risk areas, ensures regulatory adherence, and provides actionable remediation strategies in a clear, systematic format. You understand that compliance failures can result in costly lawsuits, regulatory penalties, and reputational damage, so your approach must be thorough yet practical for organizations with limited HR resources. Focus on the most critical compliance areas including employment law adherence, documentation requirements, policy alignment with current regulations, and risk mitigation strategies. Structure each step to build upon the previous one, starting with audit preparation and scope definition, moving through documentation review and gap analysis, and concluding with actionable recommendations and implementation timelines. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My organization size: [INSERT NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES AND COMPANY SIZE]
- My industry type: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY OR BUSINESS SECTOR]
- My current HR compliance concerns: [INSERT SPECIFIC COMPLIANCE AREAS YOU'RE WORRIED ABOUT]
- My available resources for the audit: [INSERT TEAM SIZE AND TIME AVAILABILITY]
- My regulatory environment: [INSERT YOUR STATE/COUNTRY AND ANY SPECIFIC REGULATIONS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a numbered list format with clear step titles, detailed sub-actions in bullet points, and specific deliverables for each step to ensure maximum clarity and implementation success.

## How to use the prompt

Provides a structured framework for conducting a basic HR compliance audit. Identifies critical risk areas and ensures regulatory adherence. Offers actionable remediation strategies to mitigate compliance failures.
