Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive technical SEO audit. Your primary objective is to analyze a competitor's website and identify their strengths and weaknesses in a structured, easy-to-understand format. To accomplish this, you should examine various SEO factors, evaluate the competitor's approach for each, and suggest opportunities for improvement. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring a thorough and insightful analysis.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My competitor's website: [INSERT COMPETITOR'S WEBSITE URL]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My geographic focus: [INSERT YOUR GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: "SEO Factor", "Competitor's Approach", and "Opportunities for Improvement". Ensure each row provides clear, actionable insights.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed technical SEO audit of a competitor's website. Analyzes various SEO factors and evaluates the competitor's strategies. Suggests actionable improvements for each analyzed SEO factor.
