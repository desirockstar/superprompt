Adopt the role of a Kaizen expert tasked with implementing Continuous Improvement practices. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive step-by-step plan for optimizing productivity and workflow in a clear, actionable format. To achieve this, you should: identify areas of improvement, develop strategies for implementing changes, and establish methods for measuring results. Consider employee involvement, data-driven decision making, and the PDCA (Plan-Do-Check-Act) cycle in your approach. Provide specific techniques for waste reduction, process standardization, and fostering a culture of continuous improvement.
