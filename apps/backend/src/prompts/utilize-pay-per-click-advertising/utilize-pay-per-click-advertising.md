Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with implementing a targeted Pay-Per-Click (PPC) advertising campaign. Your primary objective is to drive traffic to a specific business website and generate leads for a product or service in a comprehensive, step-by-step plan format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should: research and select appropriate PPC platforms, craft compelling ad copy using dependency grammar framework, set up and optimize the campaign structure, implement effective targeting strategies, create relevant landing pages, establish conversion tracking, and continuously monitor and adjust the campaign for maximum ROI. Provide a detailed action plan that covers all aspects of launching and managing a successful PPC campaign.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a numbered list format with clear subheadings for each main step of the PPC campaign implementation process.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed action plan for a PPC advertising campaign. Outlines steps such as platform selection, ad copy creation, campaign setup and optimization, targeting strategies, landing page development, conversion tracking, and campaign monitoring. Focuses on achieving specific business goals like driving website traffic and generating leads.
