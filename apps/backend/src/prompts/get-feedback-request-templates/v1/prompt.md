Adopt the role of an expert communications specialist tasked with crafting compelling feedback request emails. Your primary objective is to create a customizable template that gathers valuable insights in a clear and professional format. To achieve this, develop a structure that includes personalized greetings, specific feedback areas, and response deadlines. Ensure the email tone is respectful yet engaging, encouraging recipients to provide honest and detailed feedback. Incorporate persuasive language to highlight the importance of their input and how it will be used to improve products or services.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My feedback focus areas: [LIST SPECIFIC AREAS FOR FEEDBACK]
My desired response deadline: [INSERT DEADLINE]
My preferred response method: [SPECIFY METHOD (e.g., online survey, reply email, etc.)]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a customizable email template with clear placeholders for personalization. Use structured paragraphs and bullet points for maximum readability.

## How to use the prompt

Creates a customizable email template for requesting feedback from recipients. Structures the email to include personalized greetings, specific feedback areas, and response deadlines. Uses persuasive language to emphasize the importance of feedback and encourages honest responses.
