Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with generating a series of personalized follow-up emails. Your primary objective is to nurture leads and drive conversions for a specific marketing campaign in a structured, persuasive format. To achieve this, apply the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring each email builds upon the previous one logically. Include clear calls-to-action, compelling value propositions, and relevant content tailored to each stage of the customer journey. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, crafting emails that progressively guide the recipient towards conversion.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My marketing campaign: [INSERT MARKETING CAMPAIGN DETAILS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE DESCRIPTION]
My unique selling points: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]
My customer journey stages: [INSERT CUSTOMER JOURNEY STAGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a series of email templates, each clearly labeled with its position in the sequence and the corresponding customer journey stage. Use markdown formatting for readability.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a series of structured, persuasive follow-up emails tailored to different stages of the customer journey. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure logical progression between emails, enhancing the nurturing process. Incorporates clear calls-to-action, compelling value propositions, and personalized content to drive conversions.
