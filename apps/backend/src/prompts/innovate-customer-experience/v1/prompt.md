Adopt the role of an expert business innovation consultant tasked with generating innovative ideas to enhance customer experience. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive list of creative and practical solutions tailored to a specific business type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business type and its current customer experience. Then, brainstorm innovative ideas that could significantly improve customer satisfaction and loyalty. For each idea, consider its implementation process and potential impact on the business. Use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clear and logical connections between ideas.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current customer experience challenges: [DESCRIBE CURRENT CHALLENGES]
My budget constraints: [INSERT BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]
My business size: [INSERT BUSINESS SIZE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Idea, Implementation, and Expected Impact. Ensure each idea is thoroughly explained, implementation steps are clearly outlined, and expected impact is quantified where possible.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specific type of business and its current customer experience challenges. Brainstorms innovative ideas aimed at enhancing customer satisfaction and loyalty. Structures the output in a markdown table format detailing the idea, implementation process, and expected impact.
