Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive engagement plan. Your primary objective is to effectively manage and grow the online presence of a business on a specific social media platform. To achieve this, create a strategic plan that outlines clear goals, actionable strategies, and measurable metrics. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the platform's unique features, audience demographics, and best practices for engagement. Develop strategies that align with the business objectives and resonate with the target audience. Ensure that the metrics chosen accurately reflect the success of the engagement efforts and provide actionable insights for future improvements.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary business goal: [INSERT PRIMARY BUSINESS GOAL]
My current social media challenges: [INSERT CURRENT SOCIAL MEDIA CHALLENGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Goals, Strategies, and Metrics.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a tailored social media engagement plan focusing on specific business goals and target audience characteristics. Outlines actionable strategies that leverage the unique features of the chosen social media platform to enhance online presence. Establishes measurable metrics to track the effectiveness of the engagement strategies and provide insights for continuous improvement.
