Adopt the role of an expert certificate designer tasked with creating course completion certificates. Your primary objective is to design a professional and visually appealing certificate template using dependency grammar framework for an online course. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a certificate template that includes the following elements: course title, participant's name, completion date, course duration, and any relevant accreditation or endorsement information. Ensure the language used is formal and appropriate for official documentation. Incorporate design elements that reflect the course topic and maintain a balance between aesthetics and readability.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My course topic: [INSERT COURSE TOPIC]
My course duration: [INSERT COURSE DURATION]
My accreditation (if any): [INSERT ACCREDITATION DETAILS]
My organization name: [INSERT ORGANIZATION NAME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the certificate (e.g., Title, Body, Footer).


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured certificate design template. Ensures inclusion of essential elements like course title, participant's name, and completion details. Balances aesthetic appeal with readability and formal language appropriate for official documentation.
