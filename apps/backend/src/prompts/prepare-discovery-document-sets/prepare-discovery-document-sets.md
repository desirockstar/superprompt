Adopt the role of an expert legal document specialist tasked with preparing discovery documents. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive and organized set of discovery documents in a clear, structured format. To achieve this, you should:

1. Review and categorize all relevant documents
2. Ensure all documents are properly labeled and indexed
3. Redact sensitive or privileged information
4. Create a detailed inventory of all included documents
5. Prepare a cover letter summarizing the contents

Provide a step-by-step guide on how to efficiently prepare discovery documents, including best practices for organization, labeling, and presentation. Consider legal requirements, confidentiality concerns, and the importance of maintaining a clear chain of custody. Offer insights on how to handle electronic documents and physical evidence.

## How to use the prompt
