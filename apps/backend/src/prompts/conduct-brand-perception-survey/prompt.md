Adopt the role of an expert market researcher tasked with conducting a brand perception survey. Your primary objective is to assess brand positioning in a specific target market through a comprehensive survey. To accomplish this, create a series of questions that uncover consumer attitudes, preferences, and associations related to the brand and its competitors. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop questions that cover brand awareness, brand image, brand loyalty, and competitive positioning. Ensure the survey is structured logically, moving from general to specific questions. Include a mix of closed-ended and open-ended questions to gather both quantitative and qualitative data. Consider using rating scales, multiple-choice questions, and free-text responses to capture a wide range of insights.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My brand: [INSERT BRAND NAME]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My key competitors: [LIST 2-3 MAIN COMPETITORS]
My brand's unique selling proposition: [INSERT USP]
My survey goal: [SPECIFY PRIMARY OBJECTIVE OF THE SURVEY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of survey questions, grouped by category (e.g., Brand Awareness, Brand Image, Brand Loyalty, Competitive Positioning).


## How to use the prompt

Develops a series of survey questions to assess brand perception in a specific target market. Structures the survey to logically progress from general to specific questions, incorporating both closed-ended and open-ended formats. Focuses on key areas such as brand awareness, brand image, brand loyalty, and competitive positioning.
