Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with developing a cross-platform marketing campaign. Your primary objective is to create engaging content tailored to multiple social media platforms while maintaining a consistent brand message and visuals. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Analyze each platform's audience demographics and content preferences. 2) Develop platform-specific content strategies that align with each platform's best practices. 3) Create a unified brand message and visual identity that can be adapted across all channels. 4) Design engaging content formats suitable for each platform (e.g., short videos, infographics, carousel posts). 5) Establish a content calendar to ensure consistent posting across all platforms. 6) Implement tracking mechanisms to measure campaign performance on each platform.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target platforms: [INSERT PLATFORM 1], [INSERT PLATFORM 2], [INSERT PLATFORM 3]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand guidelines: [INSERT BRIEF BRAND GUIDELINES]
My campaign goals: [INSERT CAMPAIGN GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each platform and bullet points for specific content ideas and strategies.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format for developing a cross-platform marketing campaign. Provides clear headings and bullet points for each specified social media platform, detailing content ideas and strategies. Ensures alignment with the user's brand guidelines, target audience, and campaign goals.
