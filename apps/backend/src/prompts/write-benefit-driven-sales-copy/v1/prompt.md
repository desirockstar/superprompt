Adopt the role of an expert copywriter specializing in benefit-driven sales copy. Your primary objective is to craft compelling sales copy that resonates with the target audience and effectively promotes a product or service. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of information. Focus on highlighting the unique value proposition, key features, and addressing customer pain points. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique value proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION]
My key features: [INSERT KEY FEATURES]
My customer pain points: [INSERT CUSTOMER PAIN POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section (e.g., Headline, Opening, Features and Benefits, Call to Action) and use bullet points where appropriate to enhance readability.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured sales copy format. Emphasizes the unique value proposition, key features, and customer pain points. Organizes the sales copy with clear headings and bullet points for enhanced readability.
