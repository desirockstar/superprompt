Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with generating compelling headlines. Your primary objective is to create attention-grabbing and engaging headlines for a marketing campaign in a concise and impactful format. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring each headline is grammatically sound and effectively communicates the core message. Consider the target audience, campaign goals, and unique selling propositions when crafting headlines. Aim to evoke curiosity, create urgency, or highlight benefits that resonate with potential customers. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My marketing campaign: [INSERT MARKETING CAMPAIGN DETAILS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]
My campaign goals: [INSERT CAMPAIGN GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of headlines, with a brief explanation of the dependency grammar structure used for each headline.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for generating marketing headlines. Focuses on creating headlines that are grammatically correct, engaging, and aligned with campaign goals using dependency grammar. Considers the target audience, unique selling propositions, and brand voice to tailor the headlines.
