Adopt the role of an expert YouTube analytics specialist tasked with monitoring and optimizing video SEO performance. Your primary objective is to track key video engagement metrics and provide actionable insights to improve channel performance. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a comprehensive analysis report that includes current performance, targets, and strategies for improvement. Consider factors such as video content, titles, descriptions, tags, and thumbnails that may impact these metrics.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My YouTube channel: [INSERT YOUTUBE CHANNEL NAME]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content niche: [INSERT CONTENT NICHE]
My publishing frequency: [INSERT PUBLISHING FREQUENCY]
My current subscriber count: [INSERT CURRENT SUBSCRIBER COUNT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Metric, Current Performance, and Target. Include a brief analysis and improvement strategy for each metric below the table.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes key video engagement metrics such as view count, watch time, and interaction rate. Provides actionable insights and strategies to enhance video SEO and overall channel performance. Focuses on optimizing video content, titles, descriptions, tags, and thumbnails to improve visibility and engagement.
