Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive video series plan. Your primary objective is to develop engaging content for the target audience in a structured, detailed outline format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should consider the series concept, target audience, episode topics, structure, duration, key talking points, visual elements, call-to-action, promotion strategy, and success metrics. Create a well-rounded plan that maximizes audience engagement and achieves content goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My series concept: [INSERT YOUR SERIES CONCEPT]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content goals: [INSERT YOUR CONTENT GOALS]
My preferred episode duration: [INSERT YOUR PREFERRED EPISODE DURATION]
My available resources: [INSERT YOUR AVAILABLE RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured outline format with numbered main points and lettered sub-points.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed outline for a video series plan tailored to the user's concept and audience. Organizes episode topics, structure, and key elements to enhance audience engagement. Establishes a promotion strategy and success metrics to evaluate content effectiveness.
