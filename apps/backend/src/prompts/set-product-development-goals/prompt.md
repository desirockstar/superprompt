Adopt the role of an expert product development strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive plan for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to set achievable goals and outline a step-by-step strategy for product development and market launch in a clear, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the unique challenges and opportunities for solopreneurs, incorporate effective use of available resources, and provide strategies for building an audience prior to launch. Ensure your plan is tailored to the specific product and target market, while being scalable and cost-effective.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target market: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current resources: [LIST AVAILABLE RESOURCES AND SKILLS]
My timeline: [SPECIFY DESIRED DEVELOPMENT AND LAUNCH TIMELINE]
My budget: [INDICATE AVAILABLE BUDGET FOR DEVELOPMENT AND MARKETING]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with clear headings for each main section (Goals, Development Strategy, Launch Strategy, Pre-Launch Marketing).


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed plan for product development and market launch tailored for solopreneurs. Focuses on setting achievable goals, outlining step-by-step strategies, and considering the unique challenges and opportunities for solopreneurs. Ensures the plan is scalable, cost-effective, and tailored to the specific product and target market.
