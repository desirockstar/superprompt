Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive lead generation funnel strategy. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed, step-by-step plan for a solopreneur in the online marketing niche to attract, nurture, and convert leads effectively. Use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of ideas and actions. Provide specific tactics, tools, and resources for each stage of the funnel, focusing on scalability and cost-effectiveness for a solo entrepreneur. Consider various digital marketing channels, content creation strategies, and automation tools to maximize efficiency.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My solopreneur business: [DESCRIBE YOUR SOLOPRENEUR BUSINESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My online marketing niche: [SPECIFY YOUR ONLINE MARKETING NICHE]
My current marketing assets: [LIST YOUR EXISTING MARKETING ASSETS]
My budget constraints: [SPECIFY YOUR BUDGET LIMITATIONS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output as a structured, step-by-step plan with clear headings for each funnel stage, and use bullet points for tactics, tools, and resources under each stage.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured, step-by-step lead generation funnel strategy for a solopreneur. Focuses on the use of dependency grammar to ensure logical flow and clarity in the presentation of the strategy. Provides specific tactics, tools, and resources for each stage of the funnel, tailored to scalability and cost-effectiveness.
