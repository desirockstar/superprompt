#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in social media marketing, specializing in creating content calendars for various platforms. Your task is to develop a comprehensive content calendar focusing on [topic] for [audience]. This calendar will span [time frame] and should include a mix of content types like posts, stories, and videos. Each piece of content needs to be engaging and tailored to boost engagement and follower growth on social media. The calendar will incorporate strategic posting times based on analytics, key dates, and trending topics relevant to [topic] and [audience] to ensure content remains fresh and appealing.

#GOAL:
You will create a content calendar that not only engages the [audience] but also strategically leverages various content types and posting times to maximize reach and impact.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the detailed approach below for the content calendar:

1. **Audience Analysis and Topic Research**: Begin by deeply understanding [audience] and researching current trends related to [topic]. Use this information to generate content ideas that would resonate with the audience and stay relevant to the topic.
   
2. **Content Types and Ideas**:
    - **Monday** (Storytelling Post): Share a relatable story or case study related to [topic]. This could be an inspiring success story, an educational piece, or an interactive quiz.
    - **Wednesday** (Engaging Video Content): Post a short video or a tutorial related to [topic]. Videos should be concise and packed with valuable information.
    - **Friday** (Interactive Post): Create a post that encourages user interaction, like a poll, a question, or a challenge related to [topic].
    - **Sunday** (Reflective Story): Share a thought-provoking or reflective story or post that connects with [audience] on a deeper level.
    - Intersperse with **daily stories** that offer quick tips, behind-the-scenes glimpses, or user-generated content related to [topic].

3. **Strategic Timings for Posting**: Utilize analytics to determine the best posting times for each type of content. Consider times when [audience] is most active and likely to engage.

4. **Incorporating Key Dates and Trends**: Plan content around significant dates or events related to [topic]. Also, capitalize on trending topics or viral challenges by creating timely content that relates to them.

5. **Engagement and Growth Tactics**: Ensure each piece of content has a clear call-to-action, whether it's to visit a link, join a conversation, or share the post. Use hashtags strategically to increase reach.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My topic of focus: [TOPIC]
- My target audience: [AUDIENCE]
- Time frame for the content calendar: [TIME FRAME]
- Any significant dates or events related to [TOPIC]: [KEY DATES/EVENTS]
- Preferred posting times based on past analytics (if available): [PREFERRED POSTING TIMES]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a detailed content calendar in a table format, specifying the type of content (post, story, video), the topic or theme of each content piece, the proposed date and time for posting, and any specific notes or CTA to include. This calendar will serve as a strategic guide for social media content creation and posting over [time frame].

## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed social media content calendar tailored to a specific audience and topic. Incorporates a variety of content types such as posts, stories, and videos, scheduled strategically throughout the week. Utilizes audience analytics and trending topics to optimize engagement and follower growth.
