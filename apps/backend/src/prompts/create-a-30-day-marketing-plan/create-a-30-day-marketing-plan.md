#CONTEXT:
Assuming the role of an experienced digital marketing manager for a business described in #INFORMATION ABOUT ME section, selling a specific product/service to specified target audience. You are tasked with developing a focused 30-day marketing plan that concentrates on utilizing existing assets like the website, landing page, and email list effectively. The plan will primarily involve strategizing lead generation and digital marketing campaigns for a specific social media platform (specified in #INFORMATION ABOUT ME section), aiming at amplifying reach, engagement, and conversions without the need to create new foundational digital assets.

#GOAL:
You will create a detailed 30-day marketing strategy focusing on lead generation and maximizing the use of the specified social media platform to engage with target audience, enhance brand visibility, and drive sales. This plan assumes all foundational digital assets like websites and landing pages are already optimized and in place.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the outlined approach below to construct the marketing plan:

1. Audience Engagement Strategy:
● Analyze the existing data on the target audience to refine target personas for more personalized marketing.
● Plan daily engagement tactics, such as interactive posts, polls, and live sessions focused on the specified target audience, to foster community and brand loyalty.

2. Content Marketing:
● Develop a content calendar that aligns with key themes, product launches, or industry trends relevant to the specified target audience.
● Incorporate a variety of content types (videos, blogs, infographics) to share on speicifed social media platform and via email newsletters to keep the audience informed and engaged.

3. Promotional Campaigns:
● Outline weekly promotional campaigns that highlight different aspects of product/service, including special offers, customer testimonials, and product demos.
● Use targeted advertising on specified social media platform to reach broader segments of the target audience, leveraging existing analytics for targeting precision.

4. Lead Generation Initiatives:
● Plan strategic calls-to-action within content and social media posts to encourage email list sign-ups, leveraging existing offers or content as incentives.
● Organize a virtual event or webinar that requires email sign-up, promoting it heavily on specified social media platform and through email marketing.

5. Measurement and Optimization:
● Establish specific, measurable goals for each campaign, including engagement rates, lead generation numbers, and conversion rates from the social media platform.
● Prompt the user to schedule weekly analysis sessions to review campaign performance against KPIs, making adjustments as necessary to improve results.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
● My Business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
● My Product/Service: [PRODUCT/SERVICE]
● My Target Audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE + AGE]
● Social Media Platform: [SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
● My Competitors: [URL OF COMPETITORS]

#OUTPUT:
Present the 30-day marketing plan in a table format with the following columns:
● Day, Task,   Objective,  Platform, KPIs,   Notes

This table is a template; specific tasks, objectives, platforms, KPIs, and notes should be tailored based on the detailed steps outlined in the response guidelines, considering the details described in the #INFORMATION ABOUT ME section.
