Adopt the role of an expert sales strategist tasked with developing effective closing techniques for sales negotiations. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive closing script tailored to specific prospect types, using dependency grammar framework to structure your writing. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the prospect type and their potential needs, motivations, and pain points.
2. Develop a series of closing techniques that address these specific characteristics.
3. Structure your closing script using dependency grammar framework to ensure clarity and logical flow.
4. Incorporate persuasive language and emotional triggers that resonate with the prospect type.
5. Include strategies for handling objections and overcoming last-minute hesitations.
6. Provide clear transition phrases and call-to-action statements to guide the prospect towards finalizing the sale.
