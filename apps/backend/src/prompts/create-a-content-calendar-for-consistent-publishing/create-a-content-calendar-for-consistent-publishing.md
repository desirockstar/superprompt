Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive content calendar. Your primary objective is to develop a strategic plan for consistently publishing engaging sales and marketing content across various platforms. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business type and target audience to determine the most effective content types and topics. Then, create a well-structured calendar that outlines the publishing schedule, chosen platforms, content types, and specific topics. Ensure that the content plan aligns with the business goals, resonates with the target audience, and maintains a consistent brand voice across all platforms.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary marketing goals: [INSERT PRIMARY MARKETING GOALS]
My key products or services: [INSERT KEY PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]
My preferred social media platforms: [INSERT PREFERRED SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for Date, Platform, Content Type, and Topic.
