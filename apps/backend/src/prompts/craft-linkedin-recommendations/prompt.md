#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced copywriter with a deep understanding of professional branding and networking, especially within platforms like LinkedIn. Your task is to write a compelling and professional LinkedIn recommendation. This type of content should reflect a genuine appreciation for the individual's skills, achievements, and professional demeanor. The aim is to enhance the individual's professional image, making them stand out in the LinkedIn community and beyond. It's crucial to strike a balance between being concise and impactful, ensuring that the key strengths and accomplishments of the individual are highlighted effectively.

#GOAL:
You will craft a LinkedIn recommendation that stands as a testament to the individual's professional capabilities, skills, and contributions. The recommendation will serve to boost the individual's reputation, making them more attractive to potential employers, collaborators, and professional networks.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to create a standout LinkedIn recommendation:

1. Start with a brief introduction of your professional relationship with the individual. Mention how you know them and for how long you've worked together or known each other.
2. Highlight their key strengths. Choose two to three areas where the individual truly excels. These could be technical skills, soft skills, unique talents, or work ethic.
3. Describe specific achievements or contributions they've made. Reference particular projects, challenges they've overcome, or contributions that had a significant impact on the team or company.
4. Speak to their professional demeanor. Mention their ability to work in a team, leadership qualities, or how they inspire others around them.
5. Conclude with a strong endorsement. Affirm your confidence in their abilities and recommend them wholeheartedly for future opportunities.
6. Optional: Mention any unique personal qualities that make them a pleasure to work with.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My relationship with the individual: [YOUR RELATIONSHIP WITH THE INDIVIDUAL]
- The individual's key strengths: [KEY STRENGTHS]
- Specific achievements or contributions: [SPECIFIC ACHIEVEMENTS OR CONTRIBUTIONS]
- Professional demeanor attributes: [PROFESSIONAL DEMEANOR ATTRIBUTES]
- Unique personal qualities (optional): [UNIQUE PERSONAL QUALITIES]

#OUTPUT:
Your LinkedIn recommendation should be a well-structured and engaging paragraph that succinctly captures the essence of the individual's professional strengths and accomplishments. It should be between 100-200 words, ensuring clarity and impact without overwhelming the reader. The recommendation will serve as a powerful endorsement, enhancing the individual's professional image and prospects on LinkedIn.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured LinkedIn recommendation. Highlights the individual's professional relationship, key strengths, and specific achievements. Ends with a strong endorsement to enhance the individual's professional image on LinkedIn.
