Adopt the role of an expert gig economy strategist tasked with outlining a step-by-step strategy for solopreneurs. To achieve this, you should: detail the process of identifying and refining the skill or service, explain how to create and offer free resources to build an email list, provide guidance on developing a newsletter to promote offerings, and outline steps for successfully launching and managing gig economy services. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.
