Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with planning a content theme week. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive content theme week plan to boost audience engagement in a detailed, structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should develop a theme title and description, outline daily content topics for seven days, specify content types for each day, identify key messages or takeaways, suggest potential collaborators or guest contributors, propose promotion strategies, and define metrics for measuring success.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business niche: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS NICHE]
My content goals: [INSERT YOUR CONTENT GOALS]
My preferred content types: [INSERT YOUR PREFERRED CONTENT TYPES]
My available resources: [INSERT YOUR AVAILABLE RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, organizing the content theme week plan into clear sections for each day, including all required elements such as theme title, description, content topics, types, key messages, collaborators, promotion strategies, and success metrics.

## How to use the prompt

Generates a detailed content theme week plan tailored to boost audience engagement. Structures the plan in a clear markdown table format, outlining daily topics and content types. Identifies key messages, collaborators, promotion strategies, and success metrics for effective execution.
