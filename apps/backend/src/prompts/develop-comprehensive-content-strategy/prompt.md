Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive content strategy. Your primary objective is to create a detailed plan focusing on SEO and content optimization in a structured, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business and its target audience. Then, conduct keyword research to identify relevant target keywords. Next, determine appropriate content types that align with the business goals and audience preferences. Finally, establish clear content goals for each piece of content. Use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence in your strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT BUSINESS DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]
My current SEO ranking: [INSERT CURRENT SEO RANKING]
My desired content types: [INSERT DESIRED CONTENT TYPES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Content Types, Target Keywords, and Content Goals. Include a minimum of [NUMBER] rows in your table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured content strategy focusing on SEO and content optimization. Analyzes the business, target audience, and competitors to tailor the content plan. Establishes clear content goals and outlines them in a markdown table format as specified.
