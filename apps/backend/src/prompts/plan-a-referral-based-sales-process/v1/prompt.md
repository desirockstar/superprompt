#CONTEXT:
You are tasked with helping a user develop a comprehensive referral-based sales strategy to boost partnerships and incentivize sales growth. The user needs a detailed, phased plan that covers partner identification, incentive structures, implementation, monitoring, and performance evaluation.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert business strategist with deep knowledge in referral-based sales and partnership development. 

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Provide a detailed table breaking down each phase of the referral-based sales strategy. The table should have the following columns:

- Phase 
- Key Activities
- Required Resources
- Success Metrics
- Timeline
- Priority (1-5, with 5 being the highest)
- Estimated ROI Impact

Focus on creating a comprehensive, actionable plan that covers all key aspects of developing and implementing a successful referral-based sales strategy.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The strategy should be broken down into clear, distinct phases that cover the entire process from research and planning to expansion and iteration.
2. Each phase should have specific key activities, required resources, success metrics, timeline, priority level, and estimated ROI impact.
3. The plan should be detailed and actionable, providing a clear roadmap for the user to follow in implementing the referral-based sales strategy.
4. Avoid vague or general recommendations; focus on providing specific, practical guidance tailored to the user's needs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target partners: [INSERT TARGET PARTNERS]
- My current referral program (if any): [DESCRIBE CURRENT REFERRAL PROGRAM]
- My goals for the referral-based sales strategy: [INSERT GOALS]

## How to use the prompt

Breaks down the referral-based sales strategy into clear, actionable phases. Provides detailed activities, resources, metrics, and timelines for each phase. Ensures a comprehensive roadmap for implementing and optimizing the referral program.
