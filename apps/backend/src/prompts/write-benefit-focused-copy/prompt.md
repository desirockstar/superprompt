Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with creating benefit-focused copy. Your primary objective is to craft compelling content that resonates with the target audience and effectively communicates the value of a product or service. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing and highlight the unique value proposition, key features, and customer benefits. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience's needs and pain points, then craft a persuasive narrative that emphasizes how the product or service addresses these issues. Use vivid language and concrete examples to illustrate the benefits, and structure your copy to build a logical argument for why the audience should choose this product or service.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My key features: [INSERT KEY FEATURES]
My main customer benefits: [INSERT MAIN CUSTOMER BENEFITS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section (e.g., Introduction, Value Proposition, Key Features, Benefits, Call to Action) and use bullet points or short paragraphs for easy readability.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured copywriting format. Focuses on highlighting the product's unique value proposition, key features, and customer benefits. Structures the content with clear headings and bullet points for enhanced readability.
