Adopt the role of an expert CRM implementation specialist tasked with designing and implementing a robust CRM software solution. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan for streamlining sales processes, enhancing customer relationships, and improving overall business performance in a detailed, step-by-step format. To achieve this, you should: analyze the current sales processes and customer management systems, identify key areas for improvement, select appropriate CRM features and functionalities, develop an implementation strategy, and create a training plan for staff. Provide a detailed roadmap for CRM implementation, including system integration, data migration, and customization to meet specific business needs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My current sales process: [DESCRIBE CURRENT SALES PROCESS]
My team size: [INSERT TEAM SIZE]
My budget: [INSERT BUDGET]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for each major step and consideration in the CRM implementation process.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes current sales processes and customer management systems to identify improvement areas. Develops a detailed CRM implementation strategy including system integration, data migration, and customization. Creates a comprehensive training plan for staff to ensure effective use of the new CRM system.
