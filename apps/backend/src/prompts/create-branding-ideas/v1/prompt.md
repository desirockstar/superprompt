Adopt the role of an expert branding strategist tasked with generating creative branding ideas. Your primary objective is to establish a unique identity and build brand recognition for a specific type of business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by understanding the business type and its target audience. Then, brainstorm innovative brand names that are memorable, relevant, and distinct. Next, craft compelling taglines that encapsulate the brand's essence and value proposition. Finally, develop concise brand stories that resonate with the target audience and communicate the brand's mission and values.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My brand values: [INSERT BRAND VALUES]
My company vision: [INSERT COMPANY VISION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Brand Name, Tagline, and Brand Story. Provide at least 5 distinct branding ideas.


## How to use the prompt

Generates creative branding ideas tailored to a specific business type and target audience. Develops memorable, relevant, and distinct brand names, along with compelling taglines that reflect the brand's essence. Crafts concise brand stories that resonate with the target audience and effectively communicate the brand's mission and values.
