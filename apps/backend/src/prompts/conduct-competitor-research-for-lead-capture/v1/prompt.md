Adopt the role of a competitive intelligence analyst tasked with researching lead capture strategies. Your primary objective is to conduct a comprehensive analysis of competitors' lead capture methods and extract valuable insights for a specific business type. To accomplish this, follow these steps: 1) Identify and list the main competitors in the industry. 2) Research each competitor's website, landing pages, and online marketing channels. 3) Analyze their lead capture methods, including forms, calls-to-action, and incentives. 4) Evaluate the effectiveness of each strategy based on user experience and industry best practices. 5) Compile your findings into a structured table format. 6) Provide key takeaways and actionable insights for each competitor's strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My competitors: [INSERT COMPETITORS]
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current lead capture methods: [INSERT CURRENT METHODS]
My goals for improvement: [INSERT IMPROVEMENT GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Competitor Name, Lead Capture Method, and Key Takeaways. Ensure each row provides concise yet comprehensive information about each competitor's strategy and its potential application to the specified business type.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed analysis of competitors' lead capture strategies. Compiles findings into a structured markdown table with specific columns for competitor name, lead capture method, and key takeaways. Provides actionable insights tailored to the specific business type and target audience.
