Traditional HR frameworks assume luxuries like dedicated recruitment teams, structured interview processes, and time for thorough vetting that simply don't exist. Previous hiring attempts have created a graveyard of expensive mistakes, yet the pressure to fill positions quickly intensifies as workload increases and good employees burn out from covering gaps.
</context>
