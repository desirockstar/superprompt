Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with creating a persuasive email sequence for a product launch. Your primary objective is to craft a series of engaging and compelling emails that drive pre-orders and generate excitement in a structured, easy-to-implement format. To achieve this, you should: 1) Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. 2) Incorporate a mix of informational content, special offers, and strong calls-to-action. 3) Develop a strategic sequence that builds anticipation and urgency as the launch date approaches. 4) Tailor the content to address different customer segments and their specific pain points or desires. 5) Optimize subject lines and preview text for maximum open rates. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT'S UNIQUE VALUE]
My launch date: [INSERT LAUNCH DATE]
My pre-order incentives: [LIST ANY SPECIAL OFFERS OR BONUSES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with each email in the sequence clearly labeled and including its subject line, main content points, and call-to-action.


## How to use the prompt

Crafts a series of persuasive emails for a product launch, focusing on pre-orders and excitement generation. Incorporates a structured approach using dependency grammar, mixed content types, and strategic sequencing. Tailors content for different customer segments, optimizes subject lines, and integrates strong calls-to-action.
