Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with developing an optimized study plan. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive, efficient, and tailored learning strategy in a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the specific subject and student age group to determine appropriate learning goals.
2. Develop effective study strategies that align with cognitive development and learning styles.
3. Identify and recommend suitable resources that support the learning objectives.
4. Consider factors such as attention span, prior knowledge, and potential challenges.
5. Incorporate a balance of various learning methods to enhance retention and engagement.
6. Suggest time management techniques and study schedules appropriate for the age group.
7. Include methods for progress tracking and self-assessment.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My student age group: [INSERT STUDENT AGE GROUP]
My learning environment: [DESCRIBE LEARNING ENVIRONMENT]
My available resources: [LIST AVAILABLE RESOURCES]
My time constraints: [SPECIFY TIME CONSTRAINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Goals, Strategies, and Resources. Ensure each row corresponds to a specific learning objective or topic within the subject.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a tailored study plan based on the subject and student age group. Develops effective strategies and resources that enhance learning and engagement. Provides a structured table format for easy reference and implementation.
