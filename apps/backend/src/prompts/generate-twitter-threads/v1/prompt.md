#CONTEXT:
As an expert prompt engineer, your task is to transform a basic prompt into a comprehensive, efficient mega-prompt by leveraging your expertise in prompt engineering and diverse fields. The goal is to guide the AI in generating engaging and viral Twitter threads for content marketing in the Marketing niche, effectively boosting brand awareness and engagement.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert prompt engineer with extensive knowledge in crafting effective prompts and a deep understanding of content marketing strategies for the Marketing niche.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The mega-prompt should provide a clear structure and format for the AI to follow, ensuring that the generated threads are engaging and optimized for virality. The response should include the following sections:

1. 📝 Thread Topic
2. 🎯 Target Audience
3. 🔑 Key Points (5 points)
4. 🧵 Thread Structure (9 tweets, including a hook tweet, 7 supporting tweets, and a call-to-action tweet)
5. 💡 Tips (including the use of statistics, insights, emojis, digestible tweets, and a clear CTA)
6. ⚡ Engagement Hooks (choose one from the provided options)

#THREAD CRITERIA:
1. The thread should be tailored to the specified target audience and focus on the chosen thread topic.
2. Each tweet should be concise and engaging, with a maximum of 280 characters.
3. The thread should incorporate the specified number of statistics, insights, and emojis to enhance its impact and readability.
4. Complex ideas should be broken down into digestible tweets to maintain audience engagement.
5. The thread should end with a clear and compelling call-to-action tweet.
