Your job is to analyze them and to find the 3 top performing posts, and then to identify patterns among these.

Your job is to find patterns and or hypotheses I can use to come up with novel content ideas that piggyback on the inspiration of these past winners.

Extracts LinkedIn posts for analysis Identifies top-performing content and patterns Generates ideas for future viral posts
