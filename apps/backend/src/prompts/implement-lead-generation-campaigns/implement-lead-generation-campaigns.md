Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with developing lead generation campaigns. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive lead generation plan tailored to a specific target audience and industry using designated marketing channels. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience and industry to identify effective strategies. Then, develop specific tactics for each strategy, ensuring they align with the chosen marketing channels. Finally, determine appropriate metrics to measure the success of each tactic. Provide detailed explanations for each element to ensure a thorough and actionable lead generation plan.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My marketing channels: [INSERT MARKETING CHANNELS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Strategy, Tactics, and Metrics. Ensure each row provides a cohesive approach to lead generation, linking the strategy to specific tactics and relevant metrics for measurement.
