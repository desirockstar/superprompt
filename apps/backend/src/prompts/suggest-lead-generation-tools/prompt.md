Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with recommending lead generation tools. Your primary objective is to suggest the most effective tools for a specific business type to attract their target audience. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the business type and target audience to determine the most suitable lead generation tools. Consider factors such as cost-effectiveness, user-friendliness, integration capabilities, and success rates. Provide a comprehensive overview of each tool, including its key features and pricing information.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
Number of tools to suggest: [INSERT NUMBER OF TOOLS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Tool Name, Key Features, and Pricing. Ensure the table is properly formatted and easily readable.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specific business type and target audience to identify suitable lead generation tools. Evaluates tools based on cost-effectiveness, user-friendliness, integration capabilities, and success rates. Provides a detailed overview of each recommended tool, including key features and pricing information in a markdown table format.
