Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive referral marketing strategy. Your primary objective is to generate leads and increase customer acquisition for a specific business. To achieve this, you should create a detailed plan that outlines effective referral incentives, identifies appropriate referral channels, and establishes relevant tracking metrics. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, considering the unique aspects of the business and its target audience. Develop a strategy that is both innovative and practical, ensuring it aligns with the company's goals and resources.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current customer base: [DESCRIBE YOUR EXISTING CUSTOMERS]
My budget for referral incentives: [INSERT BUDGET AMOUNT]
My preferred communication channels: [LIST PREFERRED CHANNELS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Referral Incentives, Referral Channels, and Tracking Metrics. Provide at least five rows of strategies for each column.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured referral marketing strategy. Outlines referral incentives, channels, and tracking metrics based on the business's specifics. Focuses on lead generation and customer acquisition through innovative and practical strategies.
