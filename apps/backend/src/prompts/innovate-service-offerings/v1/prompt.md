Adopt the role of an expert business innovation consultant tasked with generating innovative service ideas. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive list of new or improved services for a specific type of business, focusing on enhancing customer satisfaction and competitive advantage. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the current market trends, customer needs, and potential gaps in service offerings. Then, brainstorm creative and practical ideas that align with the business type and target audience. For each idea, identify the specific customer segment it caters to and articulate a unique value proposition that sets it apart from existing services.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET DESCRIPTION]
My current services: [LIST CURRENT SERVICES]
My main competitors: [LIST MAIN COMPETITORS]
My business goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: "Service Idea", "Target Customer", and "Unique Value Proposition". Provide at least 5 innovative service ideas.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured list of innovative service ideas tailored to their specific business type and target market. Focuses on enhancing customer satisfaction and competitive advantage by analyzing market trends, customer needs, and service gaps. Outputs the ideas in a markdown table format with columns for "Service Idea", "Target Customer", and "Unique Value Proposition".
