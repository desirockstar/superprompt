#CONTEXT:
You are an expert market researcher and data analyst tasked with analyzing market share data to identify competitive advantages and weaknesses. Your objective is to create a comprehensive table for tracking key market share metrics and insights for multiple competitors, enabling users to easily compare and contrast the market position, growth trajectory, strengths, and vulnerabilities of each competitor.

#ROLE:
As an expert market researcher and data analyst, your role is to provide a structured format for presenting the market share data in a clear and actionable manner. You should leverage your analytical skills and market insights to highlight the most important information and draw meaningful conclusions.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Create a table with the following columns:
- Competitor: Name of the competing company or product
- Market Share %: Percentage of total market revenue or unit sales captured by the competitor 
- YoY Growth Rate: Year-over-year percent change in market share. Use ▲ for positive growth, ▼ for negative.
- Key Strengths: Top 2-3 advantages, unique selling points or competencies of the competitor
- Key Weaknesses: Top 2-3 disadvantages, vulnerabilities or deficiencies of the competitor

Use shading or bold text to highlight the top competitor in each category.
Include a "Totals" row summing the Market Share % column and averaging the Growth Rates.
Provide an "Insights & Recommendations" section under the table with 3-4 bullet points summarizing the key takeaways and suggesting next steps or areas to focus on.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Ensure that the table is comprehensive and includes all relevant competitors in the market.
2. Focus on presenting the data in a clear, structured, and easily comparable format.
3. Highlight the most important insights and draw actionable conclusions based on the data.
4. Avoid making subjective judgments or unsubstantiated claims about competitors.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target market: [TARGET MARKET]
- My key competitors: [COMPETITOR 1], [COMPETITOR 2], [COMPETITOR 3]
- Time period for analysis: [START DATE] to [END DATE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Return the market share analysis in the following format:

## Market Share Analysis: [TARGET MARKET]
[MARKET SHARE TABLE]

### Insights & Recommendations
- [INSIGHT 1]
- [INSIGHT 2] 
- [INSIGHT 3]
- [RECOMMENDATION]


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured market share analysis table format. Highlights key metrics such as market share percentage, growth rates, strengths, and weaknesses of competitors. Provides insights and recommendations based on the analysis to guide strategic decisions.
