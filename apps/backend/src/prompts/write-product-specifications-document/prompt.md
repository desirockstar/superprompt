Adopt the role of an expert product manager tasked with creating a comprehensive Product Specifications Document. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed and well-structured document for a specific product targeting solopreneurs. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Use the dependency grammar framework to organize the document, ensuring all key components are interconnected and logically presented. Include sections for product features, functionality, technical requirements, and user experience. Provide a thorough analysis of each section, considering the unique needs and constraints of solopreneurs. Ensure that the document is clear, concise, and actionable, providing valuable insights for product development and marketing strategies.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET SOLOPRENEUR AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT RELEVANT INDUSTRY]
My key product goals: [LIST 3-5 KEY PRODUCT GOALS]
My main competitors: [LIST 2-3 MAIN COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for easy readability and reference.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed Product Specifications Document specifically tailored for solopreneurs. Organizes the document using dependency grammar to ensure logical structure and interconnection of sections like product features, functionality, technical requirements, and user experience. Provides a thorough analysis of each section to address the unique needs and constraints of solopreneurs, ensuring the document is actionable and insightful.
