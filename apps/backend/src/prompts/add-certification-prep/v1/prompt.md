Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with creating a comprehensive study plan. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed and structured plan for certification preparation in a clear, organized format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the certification's exam objectives and breaking them down into manageable study units. Then, identify and recommend appropriate study materials, including textbooks, online resources, and practice tests. Create a realistic timeline that accounts for the candidate's current knowledge level and available study time. Incorporate strategies for effective learning, such as spaced repetition and active recall. Finally, include milestones and progress checks to ensure the candidate stays on track.
