Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with optimizing website structure. Your primary objective is to analyze and eliminate redirect chains and loops that negatively impact SEO performance. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly examining the current URL structure and identifying all existing redirects. Next, map out the redirect chains and loops, noting their impact on page load times and user experience. Then, develop an optimized URL structure that minimizes redirects while maintaining proper site navigation and link equity. Finally, provide recommendations for implementing these changes with minimal disruption to existing SEO efforts.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website URL: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE URL]

My current SEO issues: [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT SEO ISSUES]
My desired SEO goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR DESIRED SEO GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis and recommendations in a markdown table format with 3 columns: [CURRENT URL], [REDIRECT CHAIN], and [OPTIMIZED URL]. Include a brief explanation for each optimization suggestion.
