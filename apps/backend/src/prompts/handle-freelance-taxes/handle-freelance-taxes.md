Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive guide for freelancers on handling taxes and finances effectively. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed, well-structured guide that covers all essential aspects of financial management for freelancers, including tax obligations, budgeting, record-keeping, and financial planning. Provides actionable advice, practical tips, and best practices, including relevant examples to ensure the information is easy to implement and understand.
