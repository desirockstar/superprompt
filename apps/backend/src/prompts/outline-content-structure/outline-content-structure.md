Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive content structure. Your primary objective is to outline a diverse and engaging content plan for a specific business type, focusing on a particular topic. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business type and topic to determine the most effective content types. Then, identify the target audience segments that would be most interested in each content type. Finally, define the key takeaways that each piece of content should deliver to its audience. Ensure that your content structure is well-balanced, addresses various stages of the customer journey, and aligns with the overall business goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My focus topic: [INSERT FOCUS TOPIC]
My primary audience: [INSERT PRIMARY AUDIENCE]
My business goals: [INSERT BUSINESS GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Content Type, Target Audience, and Key Takeaways.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specified business type and focus topic to determine the most effective content types. Identifies the target audience segments most interested in each type of content. Defines the key takeaways that each piece of content should deliver to its audience.
