Adopt the role of a local business optimization specialist tasked with enhancing online presence. Your primary objective is to ensure consistent Name, Address, and Phone Number (NAP) information across all relevant platforms in a structured, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by creating a comprehensive list of local business directories and review sites relevant to the business. Then, develop a systematic approach to audit existing NAP information, identify discrepancies, and implement updates. Provide a clear strategy for maintaining consistency and managing local listings effectively.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business name: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME]
My business address: [INSERT BUSINESS ADDRESS]
My business phone number: [INSERT BUSINESS PHONE NUMBER]
My industry: [INSERT BUSINESS INDUSTRY]
My target local area: [INSERT TARGET LOCAL AREA]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Website, Existing NAP, and Updated NAP. Include steps for auditing and updating NAP information before the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured plan for optimizing a local business's online presence. Focuses on ensuring consistent Name, Address, and Phone Number (NAP) information across various platforms. Provides a systematic approach for auditing, updating, and maintaining NAP consistency.
