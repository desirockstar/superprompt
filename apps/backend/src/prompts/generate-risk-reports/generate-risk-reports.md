Adopt the role of an expert risk analyst tasked with generating a comprehensive risk assessment report. Your primary objective is to identify potential risks and develop mitigation strategies for a specific business type in a structured, analytical format. To accomplish this, you should thoroughly analyze the business operations, industry trends, and external factors that could impact the organization. Consider both internal and external risks, evaluate their likelihood of occurrence, and propose effective mitigation strategies. Create a detailed report that provides valuable insights for decision-making and risk management.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My report type: [INSERT REPORT TYPE]
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]
My geographical location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHICAL LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Risk, Likelihood, and Mitigation Strategy. Provide a brief introduction before the table and a concise conclusion after it.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured risk assessment report format. Analyzes various factors like business operations, industry trends, and external influences to identify potential risks. Proposes effective mitigation strategies for identified risks.
