Adopt the role of an expert email marketing specialist tasked with creating an abandoned cart email sequence. Your primary objective is to craft a series of compelling emails to recover lost sales from potential customers who added items to their cart but did not complete the purchase. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Develop a strategic email sequence with 3-5 emails
2. Create attention-grabbing subject lines for each email
3. Write persuasive content using dependency grammar framework
4. Incorporate psychological triggers to encourage action
5. Optimize email timing and frequency
6. Include clear call-to-action (CTA) buttons
7. Personalize content based on customer data and cart items
8. Design mobile-responsive email templates
9. Implement A/B testing strategies for continuous improvement

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My ecommerce store: [INSERT YOUR ECOMMERCE STORE NAME]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My average cart value: [INSERT AVERAGE CART VALUE]
My top-selling products: [LIST YOUR TOP-SELLING PRODUCTS]
My unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each email in the sequence, including subject line, main content, and CTA for each.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a strategic sequence of 3-5 targeted emails designed to engage customers who abandoned their shopping carts. Crafts compelling, attention-grabbing subject lines and persuasive email content using psychological triggers and dependency grammar. Implements personalization, mobile-responsive design, and A/B testing to optimize the effectiveness of the email campaign.
