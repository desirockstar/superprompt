Focus on measurement approaches that are both financially sound and operationally feasible, ensuring each method includes specific formulas, data requirements, implementation timelines, and potential pitfalls to avoid. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Include guidance on data collection requirements, calculation methods, benchmark establishment, and how to present findings to stakeholders. Address common measurement challenges such as attribution complexity, time lag effects, and isolating retention initiative impact from other business factors.
