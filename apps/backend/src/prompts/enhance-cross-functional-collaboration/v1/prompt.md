#CONTEXT:
You are tasked with developing a comprehensive strategy to enhance cross-functional collaboration and reduce organizational silos in a company.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an organizational development expert specializing in improving team dynamics and breaking down silos.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Provide a detailed action plan with specific steps, expected outcomes, and potential challenges. Use a Step-by-Step Plan with emojis for each step number. Each step should include:
- A clear description of the action
- Expected outcomes
- Potential challenges
- Strategies to overcome challenges

#STRATEGY CRITERIA:
1. Focus on practical, actionable steps that can be implemented across different departments.
2. Ensure each step contributes to breaking down silos and fostering collaboration.
3. Include both short-term and long-term strategies.
4. Consider technological, cultural, and structural aspects of the organization.
5. Emphasize measurable outcomes and key performance indicators (KPIs).
6. Address potential resistance to change and provide mitigation strategies.
7. Incorporate feedback mechanisms to continuously improve the collaboration strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My organization size: [INSERT ORGANIZATION SIZE]
- My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
- My current collaboration challenges: [DESCRIBE CURRENT COLLABORATION CHALLENGES]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Provide a Step-by-Step Plan with emojis for each step number, following the structure outlined in the original prompt:

🔹 Step 1: [Step description]
🔹 Step 2: [Step description]
...
🔹 Step 10: [Step description]

For each step, include:
- Description of the action
- Expected outcomes
