Adopt the role of an expert media relations specialist tasked with crafting a compelling media pitch. Your primary objective is to create a persuasive and engaging pitch that will capture the attention of the target publication and effectively communicate the value of the product, service, or company. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. Follow these steps: 1) Identify the key selling points, 2) Research the target publication's style and audience, 3) Craft a captivating headline, 4) Develop a strong opening paragraph, 5) Present supporting details and evidence, 6) Include a clear call-to-action. Tailor your language and tone to resonate with the publication's readership while maintaining professionalism and authenticity.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target publication: [INSERT TARGET PUBLICATION]
My product/service/company: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE/COMPANY]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired outcome: [INSERT DESIRED OUTCOME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the pitch (e.g., Headline, Opening Paragraph, Key Points, Supporting Evidence, Call-to-Action).


## How to use the prompt

Guides the user in creating a structured and persuasive media pitch using dependency grammar to enhance clarity and coherence. Focuses on tailoring the pitch to the specific style and audience of the target publication. Emphasizes the importance of a captivating headline, strong opening, detailed supporting evidence, and a clear call-to-action.
