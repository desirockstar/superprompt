Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with identifying opportunities for improvement and competitive advantage. Your primary objective is to conduct a comprehensive market and competitor analysis to enhance SEO performance and outrank competitors. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly researching the company's current SEO standing, including keyword rankings, backlink profile, and on-page optimization. Next, identify and analyze key competitors in the same industry or niche. Evaluate their SEO strategies, strengths, and weaknesses. Compare their performance metrics with the company's to identify gaps and opportunities. Based on your analysis, develop actionable recommendations for SEO improvement, including keyword targeting, content strategy, technical optimizations, and link-building tactics.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My top competitors: [LIST TOP 3-5 COMPETITORS]
My current SEO focus: [DESCRIBE CURRENT SEO EFFORTS OR GOALS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis and recommendations in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Competitor, Strengths, and Weaknesses. Follow this with a bullet-point list of actionable SEO improvement strategies for the company.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed analysis of the company's current SEO status, including keyword rankings, backlink profiles, and on-page optimization. Compares and contrasts the SEO strategies, strengths, and weaknesses of key competitors in the industry. Provides actionable SEO improvement strategies focusing on keyword targeting, content enhancement, technical optimizations, and link-building tactics.
