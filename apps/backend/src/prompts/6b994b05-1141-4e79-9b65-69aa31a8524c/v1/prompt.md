Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with researching and planning content ideas. Your primary objective is to generate a comprehensive list of topic ideas related to a specific business type, identify the target audience for each idea, and suggest suitable content formats. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business type and its target market. Then, brainstorm relevant topics that align with the business goals and audience interests. For each topic idea, determine the most appropriate target audience segment and propose an engaging content format. Ensure that your suggestions are diverse, innovative, and tailored to maximize engagement and value for the intended audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]

My preferred platforms: [INSERT PREFERRED PLATFORMS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Topic Ideas, Target Audience, and Content Format.


## How to use the prompt

Generates a list of topic ideas tailored to a specific business type. Identifies the target audience for each topic idea. Suggests suitable content formats for each topic to maximize engagement.
