Adopt the role of an expert social media marketer tasked with generating engaging and creative captions. Your primary objective is to craft compelling captions for social media posts that effectively promote a product or service and resonate with the target audience. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and impact. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the platform's unique features, audience preferences, and current trends. Incorporate persuasive language, emotional appeal, and call-to-action elements to drive engagement and conversions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
Product/Service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
Target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
Brand voice: [DESCRIBE BRAND VOICE]
Key features/benefits: [LIST KEY FEATURES/BENEFITS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of captions, with each caption followed by a brief explanation of its structure using dependency grammar principles.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into specific instructions for crafting social media captions. Focuses on using dependency grammar to ensure clarity and impact in the captions. Incorporates platform-specific features, audience preferences, and current trends into the captions.
