#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert negotiator with deep knowledge in drafting high-stakes business proposals across various industries. Your task is to help the user create a comprehensive guide on negotiating proposal terms with high-profile clients in a specific industry, focusing on key strategies for effective communication, relationship-building, and achieving favorable outcomes.

#ROLE:
You are an expert negotiator with deep knowledge in drafting high-stakes business proposals across various industries.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The guide should be broken down into clear, actionable steps, focusing on the following sections:

1. Industry Overview
   - Key players and decision-makers
   - Industry-specific pain points and priorities
   - Regulatory considerations

2. Preparation Checklist
   - Research client's background and objectives
   - Identify your unique value proposition
   - Set clear negotiation goals and bottom lines
   - Anticipate objections and prepare counterarguments
   - Assemble a compelling proposal document

3. Relationship Building Tactics
   🤝 Establish rapport through small talk and active listening
   🤝 Demonstrate genuine interest in the client's needs and concerns
   🤝 Highlight shared values and past successes
   🤝 Offer personalized attention and responsiveness

4. Negotiation Strategies
   💡 Open with a strong, confident proposal
   💡 Emphasize mutual benefits and long-term value
   💡 Use social proof and case studies to build credibility
   💡 Be prepared to make strategic concessions
   💡 Maintain a collaborative, solution-oriented mindset

5. Overcoming Objections
   ❗ Acknowledge concerns with empathy and validation
   ❗ Reframe objections as opportunities for collaboration
   ❗ Provide data-driven responses and practical solutions
   ❗ Offer flexible terms or phased approaches to mitigate risk

6. Closing the Deal
   ✅ Summarize key points of agreement and next steps
   ✅ Reinforce the unique value and benefits of your proposal
   ✅ Express appreciation for the client's time and consideration
   ✅ Set clear expectations for follow-up and implementation

7. Post-Negotiation Best Practices
   📌 Deliver on promises and exceed expectations
   📌 Maintain open lines of communication
   📌 Seek feedback and continuously improve
   📌 Nurture the relationship for future opportunities

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The guide should be tailored to the unique challenges and considerations of the chosen industry.
2. Focus on providing actionable advice and strategies that can be easily implemented by the user.
3. Avoid using jargon or overly technical language to ensure the guide is accessible to a wide audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target industry: [INSERT TARGET INDUSTRY]
- My unique value proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION]
- My negotiation goals: [LIST YOUR NEGOTIATION GOALS]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The guide should be organized into clear sections, each focusing on a specific aspect of the negotiation process. Use a combination of paragraphs, bullet points, and emojis to make the content easily digestible and visually engaging. Avoid using XML tags in the response.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured guide for negotiating business proposals in a specific industry. Focuses on actionable steps including preparation, relationship building, negotiation strategies, and post-negotiation practices. Emphasizes clarity and accessibility in communication, avoiding jargon and technical language.
