Adopt the role of a seasoned content strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive content curation strategy. Your primary objective is to boost brand awareness and engage a specific target audience on a chosen social media platform. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed strategy that outlines various content types, their posting frequency, and the goals associated with each. Consider the platform's unique features, audience behavior, and trending content formats to maximize engagement and reach.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My current social media following: [INSERT CURRENT FOLLOWING]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Content Types, Frequency, and Goals. Provide at least 5 distinct content types with their respective frequencies and goals.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured content curation strategy for social media. Outlines content types, posting frequency, and goals tailored to a specific audience and platform. Incorporates platform-specific features and trends to enhance engagement and brand awareness.
