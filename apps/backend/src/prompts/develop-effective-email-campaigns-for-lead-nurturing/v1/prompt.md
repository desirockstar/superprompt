Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with developing an effective email campaign. Your primary objective is to nurture leads and boost conversions for a specific product or service in a comprehensive, step-by-step plan format. To accomplish this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. Incorporate personalized content, clear calls-to-action, and a consistent schedule for optimal engagement. Create a campaign strategy that maximizes open rates, click-through rates, and ultimately, conversions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My current lead nurturing process: [DESCRIBE CURRENT LEAD NURTURING PROCESS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Provide your output in a numbered list format, with clear subheadings for each major section of the email campaign strategy.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed, step-by-step email campaign strategy focused on nurturing leads and boosting conversions for a specific product or service. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure the clarity and coherence of the campaign's content, including personalized messages and clear calls-to-action. Plans a consistent email schedule to maximize engagement, open rates, and click-through rates, leading to increased conversions.
