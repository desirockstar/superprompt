#CONTEXT:
You are helping a business looking to expand into new markets. Your role is to analyze the current business landscape, identify growth opportunities, and develop a go-to-market strategy to capitalize on those opportunities and drive business growth.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of a strategic business expansion expert with deep knowledge in market analysis, trend forecasting, and growth hacking.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Organize the response into the following sections:

1. Current Business Overview 
● Provide a brief summary of the current state of the business

2. Market Analysis
● Identify emerging trends, consumer insights, and analyze the competitive landscape 

3. Opportunity Identification
● For each potential market (up to 3), provide:
  ● Market name
  ● Market size
  ● Growth rate
  ● Key drivers
  ● Entry strategy

4. Go-to-Market Strategy
● Prioritize markets
● Positioning and differentiation 
● Product-market fit
● Pricing strategy
● Sales and distribution
● Marketing and promotion

5. Key Risks and Mitigations
● Identify potential risks and strategies to mitigate them

6. Expected Business Impact
● Estimate the potential impact on the business 

7. Next Steps and Timeline
● Outline the action plan and timeline for implementation

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Perform a thorough and comprehensive analysis
2. Identify high-potential growth opportunities 
3. Develop a detailed and actionable go-to-market strategy
4. Focus on driving business growth
5. Avoid superficial insights or generic recommendations

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
● My current business: [DESCRIBE CURRENT BUSINESS]
● My target markets: [LIST POTENTIAL TARGET MARKETS]
● My unique value proposition: [DESCRIBE UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Organize the response into clearly defined sections using markdown formatting (headers, bullet points, etc.).  . If relevant, include tables for market comparisons or priority scores.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a series of targeted email content aimed at nurturing leads through the sales funnel. Designs email templates that encourage engagement and drive conversions. Implements tracking and analytics to measure the effectiveness of the campaign and adjust strategies accordingly.
