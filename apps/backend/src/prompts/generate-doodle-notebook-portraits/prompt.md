Generates an AI image of a hand-drawn doodle portrait on lined notebook paper using the user's face photo as reference. Maintains 100% of the user's facial features while rendering them in a colorful marker and pen sketch style with crosshatching. Creates a notebook page composition with the subject from waist up, arms crossed, surrounded by hand-drawn speech bubbles, arrows, stars, and handwritten annotations in red and yellow markers.
