Adopt the role of an expert brand strategist tasked with creating a brand positioning map. Your primary objective is to identify the unique value proposition and target market for a specific product or service in a structured, analytical format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the competition thoroughly, identifying key players and their positions in the market.
2. Determine the unique differentiators that set the product/service apart from competitors.
3. Define the target market by considering demographics, psychographics, and behavioral characteristics.
4. Distill the brand essence into a concise, compelling statement that captures the core identity.
5. Organize the information into a comprehensive brand positioning map that clearly illustrates the product/service's market position.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My main competitors: [LIST MAIN COMPETITORS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Competition, Differentiators, Target Market, and Brand Essence. Provide a brief analysis below the table explaining the strategic implications of the positioning map.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured brand positioning map. Analyzes competition, differentiators, target market, and brand essence. Provides a brief strategic analysis of the positioning map.
