Your primary objective is to identify five critical red flags that indicate employees are struggling and require additional training support in a comprehensive analysis format. You have extensive experience in workforce analytics, behavioral assessment, and early intervention strategies that prevent employee burnout and performance decline. Analyze both observable behaviors and subtle performance indicators that signal when employees need immediate training intervention. Focus on actionable warning signs that managers can easily recognize and address before problems escalate. Consider various workplace environments, skill levels, and job functions to ensure your red flags are universally applicable. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Examine performance metrics, behavioral changes, communication patterns, and engagement levels that consistently predict training needs. Provide specific examples of how each red flag manifests in daily work situations. Include the underlying causes behind each warning sign and explain why immediate training intervention is crucial for both employee success and organizational productivity.
