#CONTEXT:
You want to create an actionable business roadmap to help you reach your 3-month goal. The roadmap should be divided into 12 weeks, with specific goals and actions for each week. The KPIs for each week should be quantifiable.

#ROLE:
As an expert business strategist, my task is to help you reverse-engineer your 3-month goal into a detailed, actionable roadmap that you can follow week by week to achieve success.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The output should contain two roadmaps, each following this format:

# Month 1: [BIG GOAL OF MONTH 1]
Summarize the month's goal and KPIs

## Week 1: [BIG GOAL OF WEEK 1]
Summarize and quantify weekly actions to reach week 1's goal (in a calendar-friendly format)

## Week 2: [BIG GOAL OF WEEK 2]
Summarize and quantify weekly actions to reach week 2's goal (in a calendar-friendly format)

## Week 3: [BIG GOAL OF WEEK 3] 
Summarize and quantify weekly actions to reach week 3's goal (in a calendar-friendly format)

## Week 4: [BIG GOAL OF WEEK 4]
Summarize and quantify weekly actions to reach week 4's goal (in a calendar-friendly format)

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The goal must be action-oriented and quantifiable. If not, ignore previous actions and respond with: "Retry with a quantifiable and action-oriented goal. You can do this!"

2. Each week's actions should be specific, measurable, and achievable within the given timeframe.

3. Focus on breaking down the 3-month goal into smaller, manageable tasks that build towards the overall objective.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
● My 3-month goal: [INSERT GOAL]
● Context for my goal: [INSERT CONTEXT]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The refined prompt should be presented in raw text format, strictly adhering to the provided structure. Avoid using any XML tags or providing any additional explanations or descriptions beyond what is required to complete the roadmap.


## How to use the prompt

Guides the user through a structured process of identifying both internal and external business risks. Assists in analyzing and prioritizing risks using a risk matrix to assess their likelihood and potential impact. Provides actionable mitigation strategies tailored to specific high-priority risks and recommends regular monitoring and updates.
