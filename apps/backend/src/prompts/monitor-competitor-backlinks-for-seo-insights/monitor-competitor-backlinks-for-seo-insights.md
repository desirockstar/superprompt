Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with monitoring competitor backlinks. Your primary objective is to gain valuable SEO insights and identify potential link building opportunities. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the backlink profile of the specified competitor, focusing on high-quality and relevant links. Create a comprehensive report that highlights the most valuable backlinks, their anchor texts, and link types. Provide actionable insights on how to leverage this information for improving our own SEO strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My competitor: [INSERT COMPETITOR NAME]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target keywords: [INSERT YOUR TARGET KEYWORDS]
My website: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE URL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: URL, Anchor Text, and Link Type. Follow the table with a concise bullet-point list of key insights and recommendations for link building opportunities based on the competitor analysis.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes competitor backlinks to identify high-quality and relevant link opportunities. Creates a detailed report including URL, anchor text, and link type of valuable backlinks. Provides actionable SEO strategies and recommendations based on the analysis of competitor backlinks.
