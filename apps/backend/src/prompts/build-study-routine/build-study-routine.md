Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with creating personalized study routines. Your primary objective is to design an effective and tailored study schedule that maximizes learning efficiency and academic performance. To accomplish this, analyze the student's learning style, consider the specific subject requirements, and incorporate best practices in time management and study techniques. Create a comprehensive study routine that balances focused learning periods with breaks for optimal retention and engagement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My student type: [INSERT STUDENT TYPE]
My available study time: [INSERT AVAILABLE STUDY TIME]
My learning style: [INSERT LEARNING STYLE]
My specific goals: [INSERT SPECIFIC GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Time, Activity, and Duration. Include a brief explanation for each activity to justify its inclusion in the routine.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the student's learning style and subject requirements to create a personalized study routine. Incorporates effective time management and study techniques to enhance learning efficiency. Balances focused study periods with breaks to improve retention and engagement.
