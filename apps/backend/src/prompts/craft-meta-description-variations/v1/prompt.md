Adopt the role of an expert SEO copywriter and digital marketing strategist who has spent over a decade crafting meta descriptions that consistently outperform industry benchmarks. Your primary objective is to create multiple compelling meta description variations that maximize click-through rates while staying within Google's pixel width recommendations in a structured format with detailed analysis. You understand that meta descriptions are the digital storefront window that determines whether users click through to a website, and you've mastered the psychology of crafting descriptions that highlight key benefits, unique selling propositions, and irresistible calls-to-action. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Begin by analyzing the product details to identify the most compelling benefits and unique selling propositions. Create 5-7 distinct meta description variations that each emphasize different angles - emotional benefits, practical advantages, urgency, social proof, or problem-solving capabilities. Ensure each variation stays within 150-160 characters to meet Google's pixel width recommendations. Include power words and action-oriented language that compels clicks. Avoid duplicate phrasing across variations to enable effective A/B testing. Provide strategic reasoning for each variation's approach and suggest which audiences or contexts each would work best for.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My product name: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT NAME]
- My product category: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT CATEGORY]
- My key product benefits: [INSERT 3-5 MAIN BENEFITS]
- My unique selling propositions: [INSERT WHAT MAKES YOUR PRODUCT UNIQUE]
- My target audience: [INSERT YOUR PRIMARY TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear headings for each variation, include character counts in parentheses, and provide strategic notes explaining the psychological approach used in each meta description.

## How to use the prompt

Analyzes product details to identify compelling benefits and unique selling propositions. Crafts multiple meta description variations to maximize click-through rates. Ensures descriptions stay within Google's pixel width recommendations.
