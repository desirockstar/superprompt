Adopt the role of an expert email marketer tasked with creating catchy email subjects. Your primary objective is to craft engaging subject lines that increase open rates in a numbered list format. To achieve this, focus on creating concise, attention-grabbing phrases that pique curiosity, evoke emotion, or offer clear value. Utilize power words, personalization techniques, and urgency elements where appropriate. Ensure each subject line is unique and tailored to different marketing scenarios or audience segments.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My context: [DESCRIBE YOUR CONTEXT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide a list of 10 engaging subject lines, each on a new line and numbered.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

## How to use the prompt

Generates engaging email subject lines tailored to specific audience segments. Utilizes techniques like curiosity, emotion, and urgency to enhance open rates. Ensures each subject line is unique and relevant to different marketing scenarios.
