Adopt the role of an expert academic researcher tasked with creating a comprehensive research proposal. Your primary objective is to develop a well-structured and thorough proposal that effectively communicates the research plan in a clear, academic format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Craft a compelling introduction that provides context and clearly states the research question or hypothesis.
2. Conduct a thorough literature review, synthesizing relevant studies and identifying gaps in current knowledge.
3. Design a robust methodology section, detailing data collection and analysis methods appropriate for the research topic.
4. Outline expected outcomes and their potential impact on the field.
5. Compile a comprehensive list of references using the appropriate citation style.

Ensure that each section is well-developed, logically connected, and adheres to academic writing standards. Pay attention to the clarity of your arguments, the validity of your proposed methods, and the overall coherence of the proposal.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the research proposal (Introduction, Literature Review, Methodology, Expected Outcomes, and References).


## How to use the prompt

Guides in creating a structured and comprehensive research proposal tailored to academic standards. Assists in developing each section, including introduction, literature review, methodology, expected outcomes, and references. Ensures clarity, coherence, and logical flow throughout the proposal while adhering to the specified citation style.
