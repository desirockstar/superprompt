#CONTEXT:
As an expert copywriter with deep understanding of various social media platforms, your task is to create a compelling ad copy tailored specifically for [platform]. This ad copy needs to be engaging, tapping into the unique audience demographics and behavior characteristic of [platform]. Your content should spotlight the benefits of [product], employing a tone and language that deeply resonates with the intended audience. The copy must be concise yet powerful, crafted to catch attention and motivate the viewer to take immediate action. A clear and persuasive call-to-action (CTA) is crucial, nudging the audience towards a swift response. It's vital to consider the distinct format and constraints of [platform] to optimize the ad for the highest possible impact.

#GOAL:
You will craft ad copy that stands out on [platform], connecting effectively with the audience and compelling them to engage with [product]. The aim is to maximize attention, interest, and action from viewers, using strategic messaging that's optimized for the platform's unique characteristics.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this detailed step-by-step approach to craft your ad copy:

1. **Understand [platform]'s Audience**: Research and pinpoint the demographic details, interests, and behaviors of [platform]'s user base. This will inform the tone, style, and content of your ad copy to ensure it speaks directly to the target audience.
   
2. **Highlight [product]'s Benefits**: Clearly articulate the unique benefits and value proposition of [product]. Focus on how it solves specific problems or enhances the lives of your target audience, making it irresistible.

3. **Craft an Engaging Hook**: Start with a compelling opening line or question that immediately grabs attention. This hook should resonate with the audience's desires, challenges, or curiosities.

4. **Develop the Body of Your Ad Copy**: In a concise manner, elaborate on the benefits of [product], incorporating emotional appeals or storytelling elements if appropriate. The goal is to build interest and desire by showcasing how [product] stands out.

5. **Incorporate Social Proof**: If available, include testimonials, ratings, or user count to lend credibility to your offer and leverage the power of social proof to build trust.

6. **Clear and Direct CTA**: Conclude with a strong call-to-action that clearly states what the viewer should do next (e.g., "Shop Now", "Sign Up", "Learn More"). Make it as easy as possible for the audience to take the next step.

7. **Adhere to [platform] Guidelines**: Ensure your ad copy fits within the character limits, format requirements, and content policies of [platform]. Optimize the ad for visual impact if the platform is image or video-centric.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My product/service: [PRODUCT/SERVICE]
- Target audience on [platform]: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- Platform-specific considerations (e.g., character limit, visual content): [PLATFORM SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS]
- Unique selling points of my product/service: [UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]
- Testimonials or social proof available: [TESTIMONIALS/SOCIAL PROOF]

#OUTPUT:
Your final output will be a tailored, platform-optimized ad copy for [product] that includes an engaging hook, a clear showcase of benefits, social proof (if available), and a compelling CTA. The ad copy will be succinct, impactful, and designed to resonate with the target audience on [platform], encouraging them to take immediate action.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured guide for creating ad copy tailored to a specific social media platform. Emphasizes understanding the platform's audience, highlighting product benefits, and crafting a compelling call-to-action. Ensures the ad copy adheres to platform-specific constraints and effectively engages the target audience.
