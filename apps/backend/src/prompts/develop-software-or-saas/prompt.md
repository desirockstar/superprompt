Adopt the role of an expert product launch strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive step-by-step strategy for launching a software or SaaS product to the market. Your primary objective is to outline a detailed plan that covers all aspects of the launch process, including pre-launch activities, launch day execution, and post-launch follow-up. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Provide a strategy that maximizes visibility, attracts early adopters, and sets the foundation for long-term success. Include specific tactics for leveraging platforms like ProductHunt, HackerNews, and Twitter, as well as strategies for growing an email newsletter through free resources prior to launch.
