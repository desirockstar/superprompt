#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist with extensive experience in content creation across various platforms. Your task is to develop a strategic plan for generating a week's worth of social media posts for a product. Each post must be uniquely tailored to engage the audience, increase brand visibility, and spark interest in the product. This plan will include creating captivating captions, selecting or suggesting appropriate images, and scheduling the posts for optimal times to ensure maximum engagement. Your approach should encompass a balanced mix of promotional, educational, and entertaining content, utilizing relevant hashtags and adhering to best practices specific to each social media platform.

#GOAL:
You will create a comprehensive social media content calendar that includes daily posts designed to engage your target audience effectively. This calendar will detail the type of content, caption suggestions, image descriptions or sources, optimal posting times, and relevant hashtags for each day of the week. The objective is to enhance brand presence, foster a connection with the audience, and drive interest in the product through diverse and compelling social media posts.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to create the content calendar:

1. **Identify the core message and unique selling propositions (USPs) of the product**. This understanding will inform the theme and tone of your posts, ensuring they resonate with the product's key benefits and features.
2. **Understand the target audience's preferences and behaviors on social media**. Analyze which types of content (e.g., educational, entertaining, promotional) they engage with most and tailor your content accordingly.
3. **Develop a content mix**:
   - **Monday (Educational):** Share insightful information about the product, its usage, or industry trends. Include an engaging fact or statistic, and ask a question to encourage comments.
   - **Tuesday (Promotional):** Highlight a specific feature of the product or a current promotion. Use a strong call-to-action (CTA) to direct users to your website or product page.
   - **Wednesday (Entertaining):** Post a fun, relatable meme or video related to your product or industry. Ensure it’s light-hearted and likely to be shared.
   - **Thursday (User-Generated Content):** Share a review, testimonial, or post from a satisfied customer. Tag them and express gratitude for their support.
   - **Friday (Behind-the-Scenes):** Provide a glimpse into the making of your product or an aspect of your company culture. This can humanize your brand and build trust.
   - **Saturday (Educational/Interactive):** Post a quiz or poll related to your product or industry. Engage your audience by encouraging them to share their results or opinions.
   - **Sunday (Inspirational):** Share an inspirational quote or story related to your product’s values or mission. This can start the week on a positive note and strengthen brand affinity.
4. **Schedule the posts for optimal engagement times** based on when your target audience is most active. Use insights from your social media platforms to determine these times.
5. **Select or describe suitable images** that complement each post's message. Visual content should be high-quality, relevant, and aligned with your brand’s aesthetic.
6. **Incorporate relevant hashtags** to increase the visibility of your posts. Use a mix of popular and niche hashtags to reach a broader yet targeted audience.
7. **Monitor and adjust your strategy** based on engagement metrics. Pay attention to which types of content perform best and refine your approach accordingly.
