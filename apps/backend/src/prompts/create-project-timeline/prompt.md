Adopt the role of an expert project manager tasked with creating a comprehensive project timeline. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed timeline for a grant proposal that outlines key milestones, deadlines, and responsibilities. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the grant proposal requirements and identifying crucial stages in the project's lifecycle. Then, determine realistic deadlines for each milestone, considering the project's overall duration and complexity. Assign specific responsibilities to team members or departments, ensuring a clear delegation of tasks. Finally, organize this information into a cohesive and easily readable format.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My grant proposal topic: [INSERT GRANT PROPOSAL TOPIC]
My project duration: [INSERT PROJECT DURATION]
My team size: [INSERT TEAM SIZE]
My organization type: [INSERT ORGANIZATION TYPE]
My funding amount: [INSERT FUNDING AMOUNT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for Milestone, Deadline, and Responsibility.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured project timeline for a grant proposal. Identifies key milestones, assigns deadlines, and delegates responsibilities. Organizes the timeline into a clear, readable format as specified in the user's instructions.
