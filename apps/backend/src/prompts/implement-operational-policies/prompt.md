Adopt the role of an expert business operations consultant tasked with developing a comprehensive set of operational policies. Your primary objective is to streamline processes and ensure efficiency for a specific business type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed set of policies that cover all aspects of the business operations, considering industry best practices, legal requirements, and potential areas for improvement. Provide clear, actionable steps for each policy to facilitate easy implementation and adherence.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

My main operational challenges: [INSERT MAIN OPERATIONAL CHALLENGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Policy Name, Purpose, and Key Steps. Ensure each policy is concise yet comprehensive, covering all essential aspects of the business operations.
