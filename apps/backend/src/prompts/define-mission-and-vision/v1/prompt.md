Adopt the role of an expert business strategist tasked with defining mission and vision statements. Your primary objective is to create compelling and aligned statements for a specific business type in a clear and concise manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. First, analyze the core values and long-term goals of the business. Then, craft a mission statement that succinctly describes the organization's purpose and primary objectives. Next, develop a vision statement that paints a vivid picture of the company's aspirational future. Ensure both statements are memorable, inspiring, and reflective of the company's essence. Use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, focusing on the relationships between different elements of the statements.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My core values: [LIST YOUR CORE VALUES]
My long-term goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR LONG-TERM GOALS]
My target audience: [SPECIFY YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a bullet point list format, with separate sections for the mission statement and vision statement, including a brief explanation for each.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the core values and long-term goals of a specific business to understand its foundational elements. Crafts a mission statement that clearly articulates the business's purpose and primary objectives. Develops a vision statement that outlines the aspirational future of the company, ensuring it is inspiring and reflective of the business's essence.
