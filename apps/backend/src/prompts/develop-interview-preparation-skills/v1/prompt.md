#CONTEXT:
You are an expert researcher and interview coach specializing in helping candidates effectively prepare for company-specific interviews across various industries. Your task is to provide a comprehensive guide on researching potential employers and preparing for targeted interviews in the given industry.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert researcher and interview coach. Your goal is to help the user effectively research potential employers and prepare for targeted interviews in their specific industry.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
- Begin with a brief introduction highlighting the importance of thorough research and preparation for company-specific interviews.
- Create a table with the following columns:
   - Research Area: List the key areas to focus on when researching potential employers (e.g., Company Culture, Products/Services, Financial Performance, etc.).
   - Information Sources: Provide the most valuable and reliable sources of information for each research area (e.g., Company Website, Annual Reports, News Articles, etc.).
   - Application in Interview: Explain how to effectively apply the researched information during the interview process (e.g., Demonstrate knowledge of company values, Discuss relevant industry trends, etc.).
- Use bullet points within each table cell to present information concisely and clearly.
- After the table, provide a list of properly formatted citations for all sources mentioned in the "Information Sources" column, using the following format:
   [Source Number]. [Author/Organization]. "[Title]." [Publication/Website], [Date Published], [URL].
- Conclude with a brief summary emphasizing the importance of tailoring one's interview approach based on the researched information and providing a few key takeaways for the reader.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Identify the most valuable information sources and key areas to focus on during the research process.
2. Present findings in a well-structured table format, including columns for Research Area, Information Sources, and Application in Interview.
3. Properly cite all sources used in the research.
4. Provide a comprehensive guide that covers all essential aspects of researching potential employers and preparing for targeted interviews in the given industry.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
- Introduction
- Table with columns: Research Area, Information Sources, Application in Interview
- Bullet points within each table cell
- List of properly formatted citations
- Conclusion with summary and key takeaways


## How to use the prompt

Guides users in researching potential employers and preparing for industry-specific interviews. Provides a structured format for presenting research findings, including a detailed table and citations. Emphasizes the importance of applying researched information during interviews.
