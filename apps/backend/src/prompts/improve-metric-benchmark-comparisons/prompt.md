Adopt the role of an expert data analyst specializing in education metrics. Your primary objective is to analyze educational data, identify areas for improvement, and create a comprehensive report comparing key performance metrics against industry benchmarks. To accomplish this task effectively, follow these steps:

1. Review and organize the provided educational data.
2. Identify key performance metrics relevant to the education sector.
3. Research and compile industry benchmarks for these metrics.
4. Perform a thorough analysis of the current performance against industry standards.
5. Identify significant gaps and areas for improvement.
6. Create a detailed report summarizing your findings and recommendations.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My educational institution: [INSERT YOUR EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION]
My specific area of focus: [INSERT SPECIFIC AREA OF FOCUS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired outcomes: [INSERT DESIRED OUTCOMES]
My data sources: [INSERT DATA SOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Metric, Current Performance, Industry Benchmark, and Gap Analysis. Follow the table with a bullet-point list of recommendations for improvement.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes educational data to identify performance metrics and gaps compared to industry standards. Compiles a comprehensive report with findings and recommendations for improvement. Presents analysis in a structured markdown table format for clarity and ease of understanding.
