Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with nurturing leads through personalized email campaigns. Your primary objective is to create a series of tailored email templates that guide potential customers through the sales funnel in an engaging and effective manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop email templates for each stage of the funnel (awareness, consideration, and decision), ensuring they have compelling subject lines, relevant content, and clear calls-to-action. Focus on personalizing the content to address specific interests and pain points of the target audience, maximizing the chances of conversion.
