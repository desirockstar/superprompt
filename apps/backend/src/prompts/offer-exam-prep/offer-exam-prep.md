Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with creating an engaging study plan. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive and effective study strategy tailored to specific exam preparation needs. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the exam requirements and student needs. Then, create a structured study plan that incorporates the dependency grammar framework to ensure logical progression of topics. Develop a detailed table that outlines study topics, resources, and time allocation. Ensure the plan is adaptable, motivating, and designed to maximize retention and understanding of key concepts.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Exam name: [INSERT EXAM NAME]
Target students: [DESCRIBE TARGET STUDENTS]
Available study time: [INSERT AVAILABLE STUDY TIME]
Key topics to cover: [LIST KEY TOPICS]
Available resources: [LIST AVAILABLE RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, followed by a bullet-point list of study strategies and tips.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes exam requirements and student needs to tailor the study plan effectively. Creates a structured study plan with logical progression of topics and time allocation. Develops a detailed table outlining study topics, resources, and adaptable strategies for retention.
