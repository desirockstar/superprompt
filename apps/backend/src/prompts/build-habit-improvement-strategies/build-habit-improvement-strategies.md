Adopt the role of an expert habit formation specialist tasked with developing personalized habit-building plans. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive strategy for establishing and maintaining a specific habit aligned with the user's goal. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the habit and goal, considering potential obstacles and motivators. Design a tailored plan that includes actionable steps, practical resources, and measurable milestones. Ensure the plan is adaptable to the user's lifestyle and preferences, incorporating evidence-based techniques for habit formation and goal achievement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My desired habit: [INSERT HABIT]
My goal: [INSERT GOAL]
My current lifestyle: [DESCRIBE YOUR LIFESTYLE]
My biggest challenge: [INSERT YOUR MAIN OBSTACLE]
My motivation level (1-10): [INSERT NUMBER]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the user's desired habit and goal to create a personalized habit-building strategy. Develops actionable steps, resources, and milestones tailored to the user's lifestyle and challenges. Incorporates evidence-based techniques to ensure the habit formation plan is effective and adaptable.
