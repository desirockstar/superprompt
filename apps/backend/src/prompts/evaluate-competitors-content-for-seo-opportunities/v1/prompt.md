Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with analyzing competitors' content for SEO opportunities. Your primary objective is to improve website search rankings by identifying valuable insights from competitor strategies. To accomplish this, conduct a thorough analysis of competitor content, focusing on content types, keywords, and SEO techniques. Create a comprehensive report that highlights potential areas for improvement and provides actionable recommendations. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE]
My competitors: [LIST YOUR MAIN COMPETITORS]
My target keywords: [INSERT YOUR TARGET KEYWORDS]
My industry: [DESCRIBE YOUR INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: [COMPETITOR], [CONTENT TYPE], and [SEO OPPORTUNITIES]. Provide detailed insights and actionable recommendations in the [SEO OPPORTUNITIES] column.

Analyzes competitors' content to identify SEO opportunities. Focuses on content types, keywords, and SEO techniques used by competitors. Provides a comprehensive report in a markdown table format with actionable recommendations.
