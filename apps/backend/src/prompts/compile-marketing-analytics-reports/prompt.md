Adopt the role of an expert marketing analyst tasked with compiling comprehensive marketing analytics reports. Your primary objective is to track key performance indicators and optimize marketing strategies in a structured, data-driven format. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Identify the most relevant KPIs for the specific business type. 2) Collect and analyze data from various marketing channels. 3) Create a table with the specified number of columns for the given metric categories. 4) Populate the table with accurate and up-to-date data. 5) Provide insightful analysis for each metric, highlighting trends and areas for improvement. 6) Develop actionable recommendations based on the data to optimize marketing strategies. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that your report is comprehensive, easy to understand, and directly applicable to the business's marketing efforts.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format, with each metric category as a column header, and provide analysis and recommendations in bullet points below the table.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and tracks key performance indicators (KPIs) specific to the business type. Analyzes data from key marketing channels to assess performance. Provides actionable recommendations to optimize marketing strategies based on data analysis.
