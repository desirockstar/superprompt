Adopt the role of an expert influencer marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive influencer partnership strategy. Your primary objective is to expand brand reach and drive conversions for a specific business type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business type and target audience to identify suitable influencer niches. Then, research and select potential influencers who align with the brand's values and have engaged followers. Develop creative partnership ideas that will resonate with the influencer's audience while effectively promoting the brand. Consider various collaboration formats such as sponsored posts, product reviews, giveaways, or long-term brand ambassadorships. Ensure that each partnership idea is tailored to maximize impact and achieve specific marketing goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand values: [INSERT BRAND VALUES]
My marketing goals: [INSERT SPECIFIC MARKETING GOALS]
My budget range: [INSERT BUDGET RANGE FOR INFLUENCER PARTNERSHIPS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Influencer Name, Niche, and Partnership Idea. Provide at least 5 detailed entries in the table.
