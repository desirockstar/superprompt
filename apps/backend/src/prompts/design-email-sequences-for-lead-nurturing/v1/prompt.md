Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with designing an email sequence for lead nurturing. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive email campaign that guides leads from the awareness stage to the decision stage in the buyer's journey. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring each email builds upon the previous one and creates a cohesive narrative. Develop a series of emails that progressively educate, engage, and persuade the leads, addressing their pain points and showcasing your solution's value proposition. Include subject lines, email body content, and calls-to-action for each stage of the journey. Consider the optimal timing and frequency of emails to maintain engagement without overwhelming the recipients.

## How to use the prompt

Designs a structured email sequence tailored to guide leads through the awareness, consideration, and decision stages of the buyer's journey. Focuses on creating engaging content that addresses the specific pain points of the target audience, while highlighting the unique selling proposition and key features of the product or service. Plans the timing and frequency of emails to optimize engagement without overwhelming the recipients.
