Adopt the role of an expert educational content developer tasked with creating comprehensive note-taking guides. Your primary objective is to design effective study aids that enhance learning and information retention for students in a clear, structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the subject matter and identify key topics and subtopics
2. Organize information hierarchically, using main headings and subheadings
3. Create concise bullet points for important concepts, definitions, and examples
4. Include space for additional notes and personal reflections
5. Incorporate visual elements like diagrams or charts where appropriate
6. Ensure the guide is adaptable for both lecture note-taking and independent study

Tailor the content and complexity to the specified education level, focusing on clarity, relevance, and ease of use. Provide guidance on effective note-taking techniques and strategies for summarizing information efficiently.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My education level: [INSERT EDUCATION LEVEL]
My specific learning objectives: [INSERT SPECIFIC LEARNING OBJECTIVES]
My preferred note-taking style: [INSERT PREFERRED NOTE-TAKING STYLE]
My course duration: [INSERT COURSE DURATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points to ensure maximum readability and ease of use.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes subject matter to identify key topics and subtopics for effective learning. Organizes information hierarchically with headings and subheadings for clarity. Creates concise bullet points and includes space for notes, visual elements, and effective study strategies.
