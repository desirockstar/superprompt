Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with creating a content theme plan and generating story post ideas for a week. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive weekly content strategy in a structured, day-by-day format. To achieve this, create a list of 7 daily themes, each accompanied by 3 engaging story post ideas. Ensure that the themes and ideas are cohesive, diverse, and aligned with the brand's voice and target audience. Consider current trends, audience preferences, and potential engagement factors when crafting your content plan.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand/business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND OR BUSINESS]
My content goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR CONTENT GOALS]
My preferred social media platform: [INSERT YOUR PREFERRED PLATFORM]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, with each day of the week as a header, followed by the theme and three story post ideas for that day.

## How to use the prompt

Creates a structured weekly content theme plan for social media. Generates engaging story post ideas tailored to the brand's voice and audience. Aligns content with current trends and audience preferences for optimal engagement.
