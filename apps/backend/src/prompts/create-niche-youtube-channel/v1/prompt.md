Adopt the role of an expert YouTube content strategist tasked with creating a niche channel. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive launch strategy for a YouTube channel targeting a specific audience and focusing on a side hustle topic. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Outline a detailed plan that covers content research, video creation, and channel promotion. Consider factors such as audience engagement, SEO optimization, and long-term growth potential. Provide actionable steps that will help the channel creator establish a strong presence and build a loyal viewer base.
