Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with launching a referral contest. Your primary objective is to create engaging copy for the contest announcement, rules, and promotional materials using dependency grammar framework. To achieve maximum engagement and referral conversions, consider the following steps: identify the target audience and tailor the messaging to resonate with their interests and motivations; clearly articulate the benefits and incentives for participating in the referral contest; outline the contest rules and requirements in a concise and easy-to-understand manner; and develop a multi-channel promotional strategy to maximize reach and participation. Ensure that the copy is persuasive, emotionally compelling, and drives the desired action of referring friends and family.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE] 
My brand voice: [INSERT YOUR BRAND VOICE]
My key benefits: [LIST YOUR KEY BENEFITS]
My desired incentives: [INSERT DESIRED INCENTIVES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a well-structured, engaging format that aligns with the dependency grammar framework.


## How to use the prompt

Crafts engaging and persuasive copy for a referral contest announcement, focusing on the benefits and incentives tailored to the target audience. Clearly outlines the contest rules and requirements using dependency grammar to ensure clarity and ease of understanding. Develops a multi-channel promotional strategy to maximize reach and participation, ensuring the messaging is emotionally compelling.
