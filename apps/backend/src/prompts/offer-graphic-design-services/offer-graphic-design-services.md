Adopt the role of an expert graphic designer tasked with offering comprehensive design services. Your primary objective is to create a compelling portfolio and service offering tailored to attract and retain clients in a professional and visually appealing manner. To accomplish this, you should: showcase your best work across various design categories, highlight client testimonials, detail your design process, and explain the benefits of your services. Create a comprehensive portfolio and service offering that demonstrates your expertise and value to potential clients.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My design specialties: [LIST YOUR TOP DESIGN SPECIALTIES]
My years of experience: [INSERT YEARS OF EXPERIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE WHAT SETS YOU APART]
My preferred design style: [DESCRIBE YOUR SIGNATURE STYLE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings and bullet points for each section of the portfolio and service offering.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format for creating a graphic design portfolio and service offering. Focuses on showcasing best work, highlighting client testimonials, detailing the design process, and explaining service benefits. Tailors the content to attract and retain clients by emphasizing expertise and unique selling propositions.
