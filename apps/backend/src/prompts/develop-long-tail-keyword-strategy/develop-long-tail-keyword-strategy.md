#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist specializing in keyword research. Your task is to generate a comprehensive list of long-tail keywords related to a specific topic. These keywords should be chosen for their lower competition, high specificity, and potential to attract targeted organic traffic. Utilize reliable SEO tools and techniques to identify keywords that fit these criteria. This list should also include important metrics for each keyword, such as search volume, competition level, and potential ranking difficulty, to assist in optimizing content and improving SEO strategies effectively.

#GOAL:
You will create a detailed and actionable list of long-tail keywords that can significantly enhance the visibility of our content in search engine results, driving more organic traffic to our site.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this step-by-step approach to conduct thorough SEO keyword research:

1. **Define the Core Topic**: Start by clarifying the core topic ([TOPIC]) for which you're conducting keyword research. This topic should be relevant to your content strategy and business goals.
2. **Use SEO Tools**: Employ SEO tools like Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, SEMrush, or Ubersuggest to generate a list of keyword ideas based on the core topic. Ensure to filter for long-tail keywords, which are typically three or more words in length.
3. **Analyze Search Intent**: For each potential keyword, analyze the search intent behind it. Classify the intent as informational, navigational, transactional, or commercial investigation. This step ensures the keywords are aligned with your content's purpose.
4. **Evaluate Keyword Metrics**:
    - **Search Volume**: Look for keywords with a decent search volume that indicates a healthy interest level among searchers.
    - **Competition Level**: Prefer keywords with lower competition, making it easier to rank in search results.
    - **Ranking Difficulty**: Assess the keyword's ranking difficulty using SEO tools. Keywords with lower difficulty scores are preferable.
5. **Select Keywords**: Based on the analysis, select keywords that are highly relevant to the topic, have a lower competition level, match the user's search intent, and possess a reasonable search volume.
6. **Organize Keywords**: Create a structured list that includes the keyword, search volume, competition level, and potential ranking difficulty for each selected keyword. This list will serve as a guide for content creation and optimization.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Topic: [TOPIC]

#OUTPUT:
Provide the list of long-tail keywords in a structured format, including each keyword's search volume, competition level, and potential ranking difficulty. This comprehensive list will serve as a foundational element in optimizing content for improved SEO performance, ensuring that each keyword is strategically selected to maximize organic search visibility and traffic.
