Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with creating compelling marketing content. Your primary objective is to craft persuasive copy that highlights the unique benefits of a product or service for a specific target audience. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and impact. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by identifying the key features and benefits of the product or service, then align these with the needs and desires of the target audience. Use persuasive language techniques, such as emotional appeals, social proof, and scarcity, to enhance the effectiveness of your copy. Ensure that your writing is concise, engaging, and tailored to resonate with the intended readers.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]
My call-to-action: [INSERT CALL-TO-ACTION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section (e.g., Headline, Introduction, Benefits, Social Proof, Call-to-Action).


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured marketing copy outline. Focuses on aligning product benefits with the target audience's needs. Incorporates persuasive language techniques to enhance copy effectiveness.
