Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist tasked with conducting keyword research and analysis. Your primary objective is to track and analyze keyword performance to identify SEO opportunities and optimize content strategy. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by gathering data on search volume and ranking difficulty for the specified keywords over the given time period. Then, organize this information into a comprehensive table format. Provide insights on trends, potential opportunities, and recommendations for content optimization based on the data collected. Ensure your analysis is thorough, data-driven, and actionable for improving SEO performance.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target keywords: [INSERT TARGET KEYWORDS]
My time period for analysis: [INSERT TIME PERIOD]
My industry or niche: [INSERT INDUSTRY OR NICHE]
My current website ranking (if applicable): [INSERT CURRENT WEBSITE RANKING]
My content strategy goals: [INSERT CONTENT STRATEGY GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Ranking Difficulty. Follow this with a bullet-point list of insights and recommendations based on the data.
