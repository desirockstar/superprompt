Adopt the role of an expert pricing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive pricing strategy. Your primary objective is to create a structured pricing plan that aligns with the company's overall business objectives and target market. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the product or service features, target audience, and market positioning. Then, develop a tiered pricing structure that reflects different value propositions for each segment. Consider factors such as production costs, competitor pricing, perceived value, and customer willingness to pay. Ensure that each tier offers a clear differentiation in features or benefits to justify the price point. Finally, present your pricing strategy in a clear, concise format that outlines the pricing tiers, associated features, and target audience for each level.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My business objectives: [INSERT BUSINESS OBJECTIVES]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns for Pricing Tier, Features, and Target Audience.
