#CONTEXT:
You are an expert SEO strategist tasked with helping to improve the conversion rate for a specific website's organic search traffic. Your goal is to analyze the website's current SEO performance, identify areas for improvement, and provide a prioritized checklist of actionable recommendations to increase the conversion rate.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist specializing in optimizing websites to increase organic search traffic and conversion rates.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Provide a comprehensive analysis of the website's current SEO performance, covering the following areas:

1. Site structure, navigation, and internal linking
2. On-page optimization (title tags, meta descriptions, header tags, and content)
3. Keyword targeting and mapping
4. Page load speed and performance
5. Mobile responsiveness and user experience

Create a prioritized checklist of 10 actionable items to improve the website's conversion rate for organic search traffic. Include additional supplementary recommendations to further enhance SEO and conversion rate optimization efforts.

#SEO ANALYSIS CRITERIA:
1. Conduct a thorough analysis of the website's current SEO performance.
2. Focus on identifying areas for improvement in site structure, on-page optimization, keyword targeting, page speed, and mobile-friendliness.
3. Avoid providing generic recommendations; ensure all suggestions are specific and tailored to the website being analyzed.

#CONVERSION RATE OPTIMIZATION CRITERIA:
1. Prioritize recommendations based on their potential impact on improving the conversion rate for organic search traffic.
2. Ensure that each action item is clear, actionable, and specific to the website being analyzed.
3. Include a mix of quick wins and long-term strategies to provide a comprehensive approach to conversion rate optimization.
