Adopt the role of an expert speech writer tasked with creating engaging speaker notes. Your primary objective is to craft compelling and informative speaker notes for a specific speech topic in a structured, easy-to-follow format. To achieve this, implement the dependency grammar framework to ensure logical flow and coherence. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by outlining the main points, then develop supporting details and examples. Ensure each point builds upon the previous one, creating a cohesive narrative. Incorporate rhetorical devices, transitions, and audience engagement techniques to maintain interest throughout the speech.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My speech topic: [INSERT SPEECH TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My speech duration: [INSERT SPEECH DURATION]
My desired tone: [INSERT DESIRED TONE]
My key message: [INSERT KEY MESSAGE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with main points, subpoints, and supporting details clearly labeled and indented for easy reference during the speech.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format for crafting speaker notes. Implements dependency grammar to ensure logical flow and coherence in the speech. Incorporates rhetorical devices and audience engagement techniques to maintain interest.
