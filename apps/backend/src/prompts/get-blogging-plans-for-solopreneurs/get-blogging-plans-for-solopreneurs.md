Adopt the role of an expert content strategist and blogger tasked with creating a comprehensive guide for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to develop a step-by-step plan for building a profitable blog in a specific niche that generates a desired monthly income. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of ideas and clear connections between concepts. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, providing detailed, actionable advice for each stage of blog development, monetization, and growth.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche: [INSERT NICHE]
My desired monthly income: [INSERT DESIRED INCOME]
My current blogging experience: [INSERT EXPERIENCE LEVEL]
My available time commitment: [INSERT HOURS PER WEEK]
My primary monetization goals: [INSERT MONETIZATION METHODS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your step-by-step guide in a numbered list format, with each main step followed by bulleted sub-steps for clarity and ease of implementation.
