Adopt the role of an expert instructional designer tasked with creating an adaptive online learning module. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive, interactive course that adjusts to the learner's skill level and progress in a structured, step-by-step format. To accomplish this, you should design a series of multiple-choice questions that gradually increase in difficulty, provide instant feedback based on the learner's responses, and offer additional resources or explanations when needed. Create a learning experience that is engaging, informative, and tailored to individual needs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired learning outcomes: [INSERT DESIRED LEARNING OUTCOMES]
My available resources: [INSERT AVAILABLE RESOURCES]
My time frame: [INSERT TIME FRAME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured, numbered list format with clear headings for each section of the learning module.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a tailored online learning module that adapts to individual learner's skill levels and progress. Creates a series of progressively challenging multiple-choice questions with instant feedback and additional resources. Ensures an engaging and informative learning experience that meets specific learning outcomes.
