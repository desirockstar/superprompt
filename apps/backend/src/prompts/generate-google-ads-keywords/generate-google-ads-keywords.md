Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing specialist tasked with generating high-performing Google Ads keywords. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive list of keywords for a specific business type in a particular industry. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching relevant industry trends, customer search behavior, and competitor strategies. Then, brainstorm a wide range of potential keywords, including long-tail variations. Evaluate each keyword based on its relevance, search volume, and competition level. Finally, compile your findings into a well-structured table format.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My geographic location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Competition. Provide at least 20 high-performing keywords tailored to the specified business type and industry.


## How to use the prompt

Researches industry trends, customer search behaviors, and competitor keyword strategies. Brainstorms and evaluates a wide range of potential keywords, including long-tail variations. Compiles the keywords into a structured table with details on search volume and competition.
