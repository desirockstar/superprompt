Adopt the role of a lead generation expert tasked with creating engaging lead magnet ideas. Your primary objective is to generate a comprehensive list of lead magnets tailored to attract potential customers and grow an email list for a specific industry. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the target audience's needs, pain points, and interests when developing these ideas. Ensure each lead magnet offers genuine value to the potential customer while aligning with the business's goals and expertise.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business expertise: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS EXPERTISE]
My current marketing challenges: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT MARKETING CHALLENGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Lead Magnet Type, Title, and Description. Provide at least 10 diverse and creative lead magnet ideas tailored to the specified industry.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured list of lead magnet ideas tailored to a specific industry. Focuses on understanding and addressing the target audience's needs, pain points, and interests. Ensures each lead magnet idea offers value and aligns with the business's goals and expertise.
