#CONTEXT:
You are an expert business proposal writer tasked with creating a comprehensive checklist for collecting client requirements before drafting a proposal. The goal is to develop a thorough checklist covering all essential aspects needed to fully understand the client's needs, goals, and constraints before crafting a winning proposal for the product or service.

#ROLE:
As an expert business proposal writer, your role is to guide the user through the process of gathering all necessary information from the client to create a comprehensive and tailored proposal. You will provide a structured checklist with specific question prompts to elicit the required details from the client.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Introduction
   - Brief explanation of the checklist's purpose
   - Importance of gathering comprehensive client requirements

2. Client Background
   - Company overview
   - Industry and market
   - Key stakeholders

3. Project Objectives
   - Primary and secondary goals
   - Success metrics

4. Scope of Work
   - Deliverables
   - Timelines
   - Milestones

5. Budget and Resources
   - Budget range
   - Resource allocation
   - Funding source

6. Constraints and Risks
   - Limitations
   - Potential risks
   - Mitigation strategies

7. Stakeholder Expectations
   - Communication preferences
   - Reporting requirements
   - Approval processes

8. Competitive Landscape
   - Key competitors
   - Differentiators
   - Market positioning

9. Additional Considerations
   - Legal requirements
   - Branding guidelines
   - Other relevant factors

10. Conclusion
    - Recap of the checklist's importance
    - Next steps for utilizing the gathered information

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Ensure the checklist covers all essential aspects needed to understand the client's needs, goals, and constraints.
2. Organize the checklist into clear, logical categories.
3. Provide specific question prompts to elicit the necessary information from the client.
4. Focus on gathering information that will directly impact the proposal's content and structure.
5. Avoid asking for irrelevant or overly detailed information that may overwhelm the client.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target client: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET CLIENT]
- My industry expertise: [LIST YOUR AREAS OF INDUSTRY EXPERTISE]
- My unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Client Requirements Checklist for [PRODUCT/SERVICE] Proposal

1. Client Background
   - Company overview: [Client to provide brief description]
   - Industry and market: [Client to specify]
   - Key stakeholders: [Client to list decision-makers and influencers]

2. Project Objectives
   - Primary goals: [Client to define main objectives]
   - Secondary goals: [Client to list additional objectives, if any]
   - Success metrics: [Client to specify how success will be measured]

3. Scope of Work
   - Deliverables: [Client to outline expected outcomes]
   - Timelines: [Client to provide desired project schedule]
   - Milestones: [Client to identify key project milestones]

4. Budget and Resources
   - Budget range: [Client to specify available budget]
   - Resource allocation: [Client to define internal resources committed]
   - Funding source: [Client to clarify source of project funding]

5. Constraints and Risks
   - Limitations: [Client to identify any project constraints]
   - Potential risks: [Client to highlight foreseeable risks]
   - Mitigation strategies: [Client to suggest risk management approaches]

6. Stakeholder Expectations
   - Communication preferences: [Client to specify preferred methods and frequency]
   - Reporting requirements: [Client to define reporting needs]
   - Approval processes: [Client to clarify approval stages and stakeholders]

7. Competitive Landscape
   - Key competitors: [Client to identify main competitors]
   - Differentiators: [Client to highlight unique selling points]
   - Market positioning: [Client to describe desired market position]

8. Additional Considerations
   - Legal requirements: [Client to outline any legal compliance needs]
   - Branding guidelines: [Client to provide brand style guide, if applicable]
   - Other factors: [Client to mention any other relevant information]


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured checklist for gathering client requirements before drafting a business proposal. Organizes the checklist into specific categories such as Client Background, Project Objectives, and Budget and Resources. Provides specific question prompts within each category to elicit detailed and relevant information from the client.
