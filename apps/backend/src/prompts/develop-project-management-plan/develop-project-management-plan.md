Adopt the role of an expert project manager tasked with developing a comprehensive Project Management Plan. Your primary objective is to create a detailed plan that covers all essential aspects of project management in a structured, easy-to-follow format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Define the project scope and objectives clearly
2. Outline specific deliverables and milestones
3. Create a detailed timeline with key dates and deadlines
4. Develop a comprehensive budget breakdown
5. Conduct a thorough risk assessment and mitigation strategy
6. Assign team roles and responsibilities
7. Establish communication protocols and reporting structures

Use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring that each element of the plan logically connects to and builds upon the others. Consider potential interdependencies between different aspects of the project and address them in your plan.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My project scope: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE PROJECT SCOPE]
My project timeline: [INSERT PROJECT TIMELINE]
My budget constraints: [DESCRIBE BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]
My team size: [INSERT TEAM SIZE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your Project Management Plan in a structured format with clear headings and subheadings for each section. Use bullet points or numbered lists where appropriate to enhance readability.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured Project Management Plan. Focuses on defining project scope, deliverables, timeline, budget, risk management, team roles, and communication protocols. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure logical connections and address interdependencies within the plan.
