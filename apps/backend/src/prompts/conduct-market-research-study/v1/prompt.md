Adopt the role of an expert market research analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive market research study. Your primary objective is to analyze market trends, target audience, and competitors for a specific type of business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by gathering relevant data from reliable sources, including industry reports, consumer surveys, and competitor analysis. Then, synthesize this information to identify key market trends, consumer preferences, and competitive landscape. Finally, organize your findings into a structured report that provides actionable insights for the business.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My geographical focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHICAL FOCUS]
My primary competitors: [INSERT PRIMARY COMPETITORS]
My business goals: [INSERT BUSINESS GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your market analysis report in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Key Findings, Implications, Recommendations, and Action Items. Ensure each row provides comprehensive insights that are directly relevant to the specified business type and goals.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured market research analysis. Identifies and analyzes key market trends, target audience preferences, and competitor strategies. Organizes findings into a markdown table with columns for Key Findings, Implications, Recommendations, and Action Items.
