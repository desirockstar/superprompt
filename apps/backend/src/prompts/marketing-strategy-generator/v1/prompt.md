#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert marketing professionl. 

#GOAL:
● You will be designing a marketing strategy for launching a new product. 
Here is a description of the product:
[PRODUCT/SERVICE DESCRIPTION]

● The target market / target audience for this product is:
[TARGET MARKET/TARGET AUDIENCE]

● The product's key unique selling points are:
[UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]

#MARKETING STRATEGY CRITERIA:
To develop an effective marketing strategy, consider the following:
● The key messages and value proposition to communicate to the target market
● The most effective marketing channels and tactics to reach and engage the target audience
● How to differentiate the product from competitors
● Pricing and promotional strategies
● Ideas for creative marketing campaigns and content
● First, brainstorm some initial ideas for the marketing strategy in a section. Consider the target market, unique selling points, potential marketing channels and tactics, key messages, and campaign ideas. 
Don't worry about fully developing the ideas yet, just get your initial thoughts down.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
After brainstorming, develop your ideas into a cohesive marketing strategy in a section. Your strategy should include:

● Key marketing messages and product positioning
● Priority marketing channels and tactics to utilize (e.g. social media, content marketing, advertising, events, etc.)
● High-level ideas for marketing campaigns, content, and creative
● Pricing and promotional strategies
● How the strategy ties back to the target market and unique selling points
● Provide a clear, logical rationale for your strategic recommendations. The strategy should be well-tailored to the given product, target market, and unique selling points.

Feel free to make reasonable assumptions as needed for details not provided in the product description, target market, and selling points. Do not make up any additional product features or selling points not explicitly stated.

Please begin by brainstorming ideas before providing your final marketing strategy.

#OUTPUT:
Use a table format and bullet point lists for your output.

## How to use the prompt

Assists in brainstorming initial marketing ideas. Develops these ideas into a cohesive marketing strategy. Focuses on key messages, marketing channels, differentiation, pricing, and promotional strategies
