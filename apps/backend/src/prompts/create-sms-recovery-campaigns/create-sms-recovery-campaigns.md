##PHASE 3: Message Crafting & Optimization

Here's your two-message abandoned cart sequence:

**Message 1 (1-2 hours after abandonment):**
```
Hi [Name], you left items in your cart at [Brand]. Complete your secure checkout here: [CART_LINK]

Free returns • Secure checkout

Reply STOP to opt out
```

**Message 2 (24 hours after abandonment, only if no purchase):**
```
[Name], your cart expires soon. Finish your order: [CART_LINK]

Questions? Text us back or call [SUPPORT_NUMBER]

Reply STOP to opt out
```

Key compliance elements included:
* Clear brand identification
* Easy opt-out instructions
* No misleading urgency
* Helpful tone without pressure
