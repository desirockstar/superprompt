Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with segmenting email marketing lists. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive segmentation strategy based on customer demographics, purchase history, and engagement level to improve targeting and personalization. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the available data and create meaningful segments that will allow for more targeted and effective email campaigns. Develop specific criteria for each segment and propose tailored email strategies to maximize engagement and conversion rates.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Create a table with 3 columns for Segment Name, Criteria, and Email Strategy to structure the segmentation process. Present your output in a markdown table format.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed segmentation strategy for email marketing based on customer demographics, purchase history, and engagement levels. Creates specific criteria for each segment to enhance targeting and personalization of email campaigns. Proposes tailored email strategies for each segment to maximize engagement and conversion rates.
