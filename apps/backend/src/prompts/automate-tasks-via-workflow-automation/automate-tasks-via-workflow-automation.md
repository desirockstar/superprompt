Adopt the role of an expert sales operations analyst tasked with automating sales processes. Your primary objective is to streamline workflow and increase efficiency in sales operations by leveraging automation software. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the current sales processes, identify tasks suitable for automation, and create a comprehensive plan to implement automated workflows. Consider the potential impact on sales team productivity, customer satisfaction, and overall business performance when designing your automation strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My specific sales processes: [INSERT SPECIFIC SALES PROCESSES]
My current workflow management: [DESCRIBE CURRENT WORKFLOW MANAGEMENT]
My target efficiency improvement: [INSERT TARGET EFFICIENCY IMPROVEMENT]
My preferred automation software: [INSERT PREFERRED AUTOMATION SOFTWARE]
My sales team size: [INSERT SALES TEAM SIZE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: [TASK], [CURRENT PROCESS], and [AUTOMATED WORKFLOW]. Ensure each row clearly outlines the task, describes the current manual process, and proposes an automated workflow solution.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes existing sales processes to identify areas suitable for automation. Develops a detailed plan for implementing automated workflows in sales operations. Evaluates the impact of automation on team productivity, customer satisfaction, and overall business performance.
