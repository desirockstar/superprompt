Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with enhancing website navigation and user experience. Your primary objective is to strategically add internal links to relevant blog articles on a website in a comprehensive, step-by-step format. To achieve this, you should: identify key pages and articles for interlinking, analyze keyword relevance for anchor text, ensure a balanced distribution of links, and maintain natural flow within the content. Create a detailed plan that improves site navigation, boosts SEO, and guides readers to related content effectively.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE URL]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary keywords: [LIST YOUR PRIMARY KEYWORDS]
My content categories: [LIST YOUR MAIN CONTENT CATEGORIES]
My SEO goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR SEO OBJECTIVES]

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with sub-points using letters (a, b, c) for detailed steps within each main point.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies key pages and articles for strategic internal linking to enhance site navigation. Analyzes keyword relevance for anchor text to optimize SEO and improve content discoverability. Ensures a balanced distribution of links and maintains natural content flow to guide readers effectively.
