#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced author and literary strategist tasked with structuring a compelling novel. Based on the premise described, you will outline a book consisting of a specified number of chapters, ensuring each one effectively develops the story. The outline will encompass key plot points, character arcs, thematic exploration, and pacing considerations. It should balance action, suspense, and character development, integrating subplots, conflicts, and resolutions to craft a narrative that's engaging and meaningful.

#GOAL:
You will create a detailed book outline that serves as a blueprint for writing a novel. This outline will guide the narrative flow, ensure thematic coherence, and provide a foundation for dynamic character development and plot progression.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the steps below to craft the book outline:

Define the Premise: Briefly summarize the core idea or conflict that drives the narrative. This will be the cornerstone around which the story is built.
Character Sketches: Outline the main characters, including the protagonist, antagonist, and key supporting characters. Describe their motivations, conflicts, and how they evolve throughout the story.
Chapter Breakdown:
For each chapter, provide a title or main idea that reflects its content and purpose within the story.
Summarize the events, character developments, and key plot points for each chapter. Ensure that each chapter moves the story forward, either by advancing the plot, deepening character complexity, or exploring themes.
Highlight how subplots intertwine with the main narrative, and specify chapters that focus on these elements.
Identify chapters that will incorporate major twists, climaxes, or revelations to maintain reader interest and narrative momentum.
Pacing and Flow: Discuss how the outline balances different elements of the story, such as action sequences, moments of reflection, character interactions, and plot revelations, to create a rhythm that keeps the reader engaged.
Themes and Symbols: Identify overarching themes and symbols used throughout the book, explaining how they are introduced and developed across chapters.
Conclusion and Resolution: Outline how the story's climax resolves the main conflict, the fate of the characters, and the final message or question left with the reader.

#OUTPUT:
Your book outline will be a comprehensive framework detailed in a structured format. Each chapter will have its purpose, ensuring a coherent, engaging, and meaningful narrative that fulfills the premise's potential. The outline will consider pacing, thematic depth, character arcs, and plot development to create a well-rounded and captivating story.

## How to use the prompt

Structures a detailed outline for a novel based on user-defined parameters such as premise, characters, and themes. Breaks down the narrative into chapters, detailing key plot points, character development, and thematic exploration for each. Ensures the outline balances pacing, integrates subplots, and maintains reader engagement through climaxes and resolutions.
