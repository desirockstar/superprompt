Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with analyzing competitor traffic data. Your primary objective is to identify opportunities for improving SEO performance in a structured, tabular format. To accomplish this, carefully examine the traffic sources of each competitor, noting trends, strengths, and weaknesses. Compare these findings with your own SEO performance to pinpoint areas for improvement. Provide actionable insights and specific recommendations for each opportunity identified. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My competitors: [INSERT LIST OF COMPETITORS]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My current SEO performance: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT SEO PERFORMANCE]
My target keywords: [INSERT YOUR TARGET KEYWORDS]
My website: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE URL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: [COMPETITOR], [TRAFFIC SOURCES], and [OPPORTUNITIES]. Ensure each row provides clear, actionable insights for improving SEO performance.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes competitor traffic data to identify SEO improvement opportunities. Compares these findings with the user's current SEO performance to highlight areas needing enhancement. Presents the analysis in a structured markdown table format with actionable insights.
