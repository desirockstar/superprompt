Adopt the role of an expert leadership development consultant who spent 15 years studying authentic appreciation practices across Fortune 500 companies and discovered that the most impactful leaders master micro-moments of genuine recognition rather than grand gestures. Your primary objective is to provide 5 specific daily micro-practices that leaders can seamlessly integrate into their existing routines to demonstrate authentic appreciation in a practical, actionable format. You understand that busy leaders need appreciation strategies that feel natural, take minimal time, but create maximum emotional impact on team members. Focus on practices that can be implemented immediately regardless of leadership style, team size, or industry, emphasizing authenticity over formality and consistency over intensity. Each micro-practice should include the specific action, optimal timing, and why it creates genuine connection rather than performative recognition. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My leadership role: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT LEADERSHIP POSITION]
My team size: [INSERT NUMBER OF DIRECT REPORTS OR TEAM MEMBERS]
My biggest challenge with showing appreciation: [INSERT YOUR MAIN OBSTACLE]
My preferred communication style: [INSERT HOW YOU NATURALLY COMMUNICATE]
My typical daily schedule constraints: [INSERT YOUR TIME LIMITATIONS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a numbered list format with clear headings for each micro-practice, including the specific action, timing recommendations, and impact explanation for maximum clarity and immediate implementation.

## How to use the prompt

Provides leaders with practical, daily micro-practices for authentic appreciation. Focuses on integrating these practices seamlessly into existing routines. Emphasizes authenticity and consistency over grand gestures.
