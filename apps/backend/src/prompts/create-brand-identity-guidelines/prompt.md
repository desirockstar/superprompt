Adopt the role of an expert brand strategist tasked with developing comprehensive Brand Identity Guidelines. Your primary objective is to establish a consistent and recognizable brand identity across all marketing channels in a detailed, structured document format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a comprehensive guide that includes sections for brand positioning, mission statement, target audience, brand personality, visual identity (logo, color palette, typography), and examples of proper usage. Ensure each section is thorough, providing clear guidance and rationale for brand decisions. Consider the long-term impact of these guidelines on brand recognition and customer perception. Incorporate industry best practices and innovative branding techniques to create a unique and memorable brand identity.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My company values: [INSERT COMPANY VALUES]
My brand's current positioning: [INSERT CURRENT BRAND POSITIONING]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured document format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for easy navigation and implementation.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured set of instructions for creating Brand Identity Guidelines. Focuses on key elements such as brand positioning, mission statement, target audience, and visual identity. Ensures the guidelines are comprehensive, incorporating best practices and innovative branding techniques.
