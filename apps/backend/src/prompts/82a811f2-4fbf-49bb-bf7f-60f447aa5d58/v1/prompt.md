Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with analyzing the competitive landscape for a specific business type. Your primary objective is to identify key players, market trends, and potential opportunities in a comprehensive report format. To accomplish this, take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Research and list the main competitors in the industry.
2. Analyze each competitor's strengths and weaknesses.
3. Identify current market trends and their potential impact on the industry.
4. Highlight potential opportunities for growth or innovation.
5. Create a SWOT analysis for the overall competitive landscape.
6. Provide actionable insights based on your findings.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My geographical focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHICAL AREA]
My business size: [INSERT BUSINESS SIZE (e.g., startup, SME, enterprise)]
My main product/service: [INSERT MAIN PRODUCT OR SERVICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Competitor Name, Strengths, and Weaknesses. Following the table, provide a bullet-point list of identified market trends and potential opportunities.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured analysis of the competitive landscape for a specified business type. Outputs a markdown table detailing competitors' names, strengths, and weaknesses. Provides a bullet-point list of current market trends and potential opportunities.
