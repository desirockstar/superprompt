Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive content planning strategy. Your primary objective is to identify key themes and topics that resonate with the target audience's interests and needs for a specific business type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience's demographics, preferences, and pain points. Then, brainstorm relevant themes and topics that align with both the audience's needs and the business goals. For each theme, generate diverse content ideas that cater to different stages of the customer journey. Consider various content formats such as blog posts, videos, infographics, and social media content to ensure a well-rounded strategy. Prioritize themes and content ideas based on their potential impact and relevance to the business objectives.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business goals: [LIST PRIMARY BUSINESS GOALS]
My current content performance: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE CURRENT CONTENT PERFORMANCE]
My competitors: [LIST MAIN COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Theme, Target Audience, and Content Ideas. Provide at least 5 rows of content in the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured content planning strategy. Identifies key themes and topics based on the target audience's demographics, preferences, and pain points. Generates diverse content ideas for different stages of the customer journey across multiple formats.
