Adopt the role of an expert script analyst tasked with optimizing a script's structure and narrative flow. Your primary objective is to enhance audience engagement through a comprehensive scene-by-scene analysis in a detailed evaluation format. To achieve this, follow these steps:

1. Carefully review each scene, assessing its purpose, pacing impact, and overall story value.
2. Identify the script's strengths and areas for improvement, focusing on key elements that contribute to or detract from the narrative flow.
3. Provide specific, actionable recommendations for restructuring the script, including scene order adjustments, transition improvements, and pacing modifications.
4. Evaluate the potential impact of each recommendation on the story arc, audience engagement, and narrative cohesion.
5. Rate each recommendation's importance using a star system to prioritize changes.
6. Offer strategic rationale for each suggested change, drawing upon industry best practices and your expertise in script analysis.

Present your analysis in the following format:

📊 SCENE-BY-SCENE ANALYSIS TABLE:
| Scene # | Purpose | Pacing Impact | Story Value | Recommended Changes |
|---------|---------|---------------|--------------|-------------------|
[Fill with scene details]

📈 FLOW ASSESSMENT:
### Strengths
✅ [Key positive elements]

### Areas for Improvement
❌ [Issues to address]

🔄 RESTRUCTURING RECOMMENDATIONS:
1. Scene Order Adjustments
2. Transition Improvements
3. Pacing Modifications

💡 OPTIMIZATION NOTES:
• Impact on Story Arc
• Audience Engagement
• Narrative Cohesion

Rate each recommendation's potential impact:
⭐ Critical (Must implement)
⭐⭐ Important
⭐⭐⭐ Optional Enhancement

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My script title: [INSERT SCRIPT TITLE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My genre: [INSERT GENRE]
My script length: [INSERT SCRIPT LENGTH]
My primary goal: [INSERT PRIMARY GOAL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in the exact format specified above, ensuring that each section is complete and well-structured.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes each scene to determine its effectiveness and contribution to the overall narrative. Identifies strengths and weaknesses in the script, providing targeted recommendations for improvement. Presents a structured evaluation format that enhances clarity and prioritizes actionable changes for better audience engagement.
