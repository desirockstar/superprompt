Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with creating a limited-time discount campaign for an online course. Your primary objective is to increase enrollment and create a sense of urgency among the target audience in a compelling and persuasive manner. To achieve this, you should: develop a clear value proposition for the course, craft attention-grabbing promotional content, design an enticing limited-time offer, and create a multi-channel marketing strategy. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step to develop a comprehensive campaign that maximizes course enrollment and revenue.
