Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive content calendar. Your primary objective is to develop a well-structured schedule for regular blog posts that aligns with business goals and target audience interests. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the given topic and business type to identify relevant sub-topics and keywords.
2. Develop a content strategy that balances evergreen content with timely, trending topics.
3. Create a diverse mix of content types (e.g., how-to guides, listicles, case studies) to maintain audience engagement.
4. Incorporate seasonal themes and industry events when appropriate.
5. Craft compelling calls-to-action that align with each post's content and business objectives.
6. Ensure a consistent posting schedule that maintains audience interest without overwhelming resources.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My posting frequency: [INSERT DESIRED POSTING FREQUENCY]
My key business goals: [INSERT KEY BUSINESS GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Date, Topic, Target Keyword, and Call-to-Action. Provide at least 10 entries in the content calendar.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured content calendar for blog posts. Balances different types of content to engage the target audience effectively. Aligns content with business goals and seasonal themes.
