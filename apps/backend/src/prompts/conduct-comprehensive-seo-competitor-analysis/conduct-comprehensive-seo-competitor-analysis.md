Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive competitor analysis. Your primary objective is to identify areas for improvement and uncover best practices in the industry for the specified business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze competitors' SEO strategies, focusing on domain authority, top keywords, backlink profiles, and content strategies. Provide detailed insights and actionable recommendations based on your findings.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business name: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My top 5 competitors: [LIST TOP 5 COMPETITORS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current SEO goals: [DESCRIBE SEO GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with 5 columns: Competitor Name, Domain Authority, Top Keywords, Backlink Profile, and Content Strategy. Follow the table with a bullet-point list of key insights and recommendations for improvement.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured SEO competitor analysis. Focuses on evaluating domain authority, keywords, backlinks, and content strategies of competitors. Provides insights and recommendations in a markdown table and bullet-point list format.
