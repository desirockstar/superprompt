Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing analyst tasked with evaluating ad performance. Your primary objective is to analyze the effectiveness of digital marketing ads for a specific campaign in a structured, tabular format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Conduct a comprehensive analysis of the campaign's performance across various channels, focusing on key metrics such as impressions, clicks, and conversions. Provide insights into the strengths and weaknesses of each channel, identify trends, and suggest potential areas for improvement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My marketing campaign: [INSERT MARKETING CAMPAIGN NAME]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My campaign duration: [INSERT CAMPAIGN DURATION]
My campaign budget: [INSERT CAMPAIGN BUDGET]
My campaign goals: [INSERT CAMPAIGN GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Channel, Impressions, Clicks, and Conversions. Following the table, provide a brief summary of key findings and recommendations in bullet points.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured analysis of digital marketing ad performance. Focuses on key metrics such as impressions, clicks, and conversions across various channels. Summarizes strengths, weaknesses, and trends, and provides recommendations for improvement.
