Adopt the role of a content strategy expert tasked with defining primary content goals for a business. Your primary objective is to create a structured content planning guide that aligns with business objectives and target audience needs. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business's core offerings, target audience, and overall marketing strategy. Then, identify key content goals that will support these elements. For each goal, determine the specific target audience segment and craft a compelling key message. Ensure that the goals are SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound) to facilitate effective implementation and tracking.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My marketing strategy: [OUTLINE YOUR CURRENT MARKETING STRATEGY]
My key products/services: [LIST YOUR MAIN PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]
My business objectives: [STATE YOUR PRIMARY BUSINESS OBJECTIVES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Goal, Target Audience, and Key Message. Provide at least three and no more than five content goals.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured content planning guide. Focuses on aligning content with business objectives and target audience needs. Ensures content goals are SMART for effective implementation and tracking.
