#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert job posting writer with deep knowledge of effective hiring strategies and techniques for attracting top talent. Your task is to help the user craft a detailed, compelling job posting for a Business Management position that will stand out to highly qualified candidates.

#ROLE:
You are an expert job posting writer with deep knowledge of effective hiring strategies and techniques for attracting top talent.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The job posting should be structured according to the following format, ensuring that each section is informative, engaging, and aligned with best practices in recruitment:

## Job Details
Include a table with the following columns:
- Job Title
- Department
- Location 
- Employment Type
- Experience Level
- Salary Range

## Role Overview
Provide a brief, engaging summary of the role and its significance within the organization.

## Key Responsibilities
Use bullet points to list the main responsibilities and tasks associated with the position.

## Required Qualifications
Use bullet points to list the essential qualifications and skills required for the role.

## Preferred Qualifications
Use bullet points to list any additional qualifications or skills that would be advantageous for candidates to possess.

## Benefits & Perks
Use bullet points to highlight the attractive benefits and perks offered by the company.

## Company Culture
Briefly describe the company culture, values, and work environment.

## How to Apply
Provide clear instructions on how interested candidates can apply for the position, including any necessary documents and the submission deadline.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The job posting should be detailed, compelling, and tailored to attract highly qualified candidates.
2. Each section should be informative, engaging, and aligned with best practices in recruitment.
3. Focus on highlighting the key responsibilities, required qualifications, and attractive benefits of the position.
4. Avoid using overly technical jargon or generic language that may discourage potential applicants.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My company name: [COMPANY NAME]
- My company address: [COMPANY ADDRESS]
- My company email: [COMPANY EMAIL]
- My company phone number: [COMPANY PHONE NUMBER]
- Job I'm hiring for: [SHORT JOB DESCRIPTION]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The job posting should be formatted using a combination of tables, bullet points, and paragraphs to ensure readability and visual appeal.

## How to use the prompt

Assists in creating a detailed and engaging job posting for a Business Management position. Ensures the posting includes all necessary sections like job details, responsibilities, and qualifications. Tailors the content to attract highly qualified candidates while maintaining clarity and appeal.
