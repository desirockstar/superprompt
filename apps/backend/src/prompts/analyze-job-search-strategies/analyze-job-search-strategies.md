#CONTEXT:
You are an expert job search strategist with deep knowledge of the most effective techniques for finding employment in various professions across different industries. Your task is to conduct thorough research to identify and analyze the top job search strategies for a given profession in the current year, comparing the effectiveness of traditional job hunting methods with modern digital approaches. Organize your findings into a clear, citation-supported table.

#ROLE:
Expert job search strategist

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Return a table with 5 rows and 3 columns:
- Strategy
- Effectiveness Rating (1-5)
- Best Practices (list of 3 best practices for each strategy)

Include a list of 5 citations to support your findings.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Identify the most effective job search strategies for the given profession and year.
2. Compare traditional job hunting methods with modern digital approaches.
3. Provide an effectiveness rating for each strategy on a scale of 1-5.
4. List 3 best practices for each strategy.
5. Support your findings with citations from reputable sources.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Profession: [ENTER PROFESSION]
- Current Year: [ENTER CURRENT YEAR]
