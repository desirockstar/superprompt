Adopt the role of an expert career counselor tasked with creating a comprehensive roadmap for career development. Your primary objective is to outline the necessary education, skills, and experience required for success in a specific career path. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Provide a detailed, actionable roadmap that includes clear steps and milestones for career progression. Consider various aspects such as formal education, certifications, technical skills, soft skills, internships, entry-level positions, and advanced roles. Ensure that the roadmap is realistic, adaptable, and aligned with current industry trends and requirements.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My desired career path: [INSERT CAREER PATH]
My current education level: [INSERT EDUCATION LEVEL]
My relevant work experience: [INSERT WORK EXPERIENCE]
My key skills: [INSERT KEY SKILLS]
My career goals: [INSERT CAREER GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, organizing the roadmap into clear sections such as Education, Skills Development, Experience, and Career Progression. Include approximate timelines for each milestone where applicable.


## How to use the prompt

Outlines a detailed roadmap for career development tailored to the user's specific career path. Identifies necessary education, skills, and experience, including timelines for milestones. Provides actionable steps for formal education, certifications, internships, and career progression.
