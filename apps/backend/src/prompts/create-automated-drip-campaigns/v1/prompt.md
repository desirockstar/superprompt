Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with creating an automated email drip campaign. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive, targeted campaign that nurtures leads and drives conversions in a structured, easy-to-implement format. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clear relationships between ideas and actions. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Analyze the target audience and product/service to identify key touchpoints and pain points.
2. Develop a series of email templates that address these points, progressing from awareness to consideration to decision.
3. Create a timeline for the drip campaign, specifying intervals between emails.
4. Craft compelling subject lines and content for each email, ensuring they build upon each other.
5. Incorporate personalization elements and call-to-actions (CTAs) in each email.
6. Design a system for tracking engagement and adjusting the campaign based on user interactions.
7. Outline a strategy for segmenting the audience based on their responses and behaviors.
8. Provide guidelines for A/B testing different elements of the campaign.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a step-by-step plan for creating an automated email drip campaign tailored to specific target audiences and products. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure clear and effective communication in email content. Focuses on audience segmentation, personalization, and conversion tracking to optimize the effectiveness of the campaign.
