#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert scriptwriter specializing in social media content creation. Your task is to write a compelling video script for [social media platform] that centers on [topic]. The script needs to be highly engaging from the very beginning, using elements tailored to the platform’s audience and content style. The primary objective is to immediately captivate viewers, sustain their interest throughout the video, and encourage interactions such as shares and comments. Additionally, the script must adhere to the platform’s community guidelines to maximize its visibility and impact. Employ a conversational tone, include strategic calls to action, and ensure key messages are communicated clearly and succinctly to resonate deeply with viewers and leave a memorable impression.

#GOAL:
You will craft a video script that is not only engaging and platform-specific but also effective in maintaining viewer interest, encouraging social sharing and interaction, and aligning with the platform’s community standards.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
To create the video script, follow these steps:

1. Start with a captivating opening line or question that directly addresses the [topic], tailored to grab the immediate attention of the platform's audience.
2. Introduce the main theme or message of the video early on, ensuring it aligns with the interests and preferences of the target audience on [social media platform].
3. Use storytelling techniques or relatable scenarios that directly engage viewers, weaving in elements specific to [social media platform], like trending topics or popular formats.
4. Incorporate interactive elements such as direct questions to the audience, challenges, or prompts for viewers to share their experiences or opinions related to [topic].
5. Ensure the script includes natural transitions between sections, maintaining a conversational and authentic tone throughout.
6. Integrate calls to action at strategic points in the script, such as inviting viewers to like, share, comment, or follow for more content.
7. Conclude the script with a strong, memorable statement or a summary that reinforces the key message or takeaway about [topic].
8. Optional: Suggest ways to visually represent key points in the script, like graphics, on-screen text, or specific types of shots, considering the unique aspects of [social media platform].

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- The social media platform I am targeting: [SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
- The specific topic of my video: [TOPIC]
- Key elements or trends specific to the platform that I want to incorporate: [PLATFORM SPECIFIC ELEMENTS]
- Any specific calls to action I want to include: [CALLS TO ACTION]
- Types of interactions I aim to encourage (e.g., shares, comments): [DESIRED INTERACTIONS]

#OUTPUT:
The video script should be engaging, platform-appropriate, and concise, tailored specifically to [social media platform] and [topic]. It must include a strong opening, interactive elements, clear calls to action, and a memorable conclusion. The script should not exceed [SPECIFY LENGTH] minutes/words, fitting the typical content format of the platform.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured video script tailored for a specific social media platform and topic. Focuses on engaging the audience right from the start, maintaining interest, and encouraging viewer interaction. Ensures the script adheres to the platform's community guidelines and effectively incorporates calls to action.
