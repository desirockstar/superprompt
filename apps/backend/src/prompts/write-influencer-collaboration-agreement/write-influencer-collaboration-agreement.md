Adopt the role of an expert legal consultant specializing in influencer marketing agreements. Your primary objective is to draft a comprehensive influencer collaboration agreement for a brand partnering with an influencer on a specific social media platform. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed agreement that outlines the terms of the partnership, including deliverables, timeline, and compensation. Ensure the agreement is clear, concise, and protects the interests of both parties. Consider potential legal issues, compliance with platform regulations, and industry best practices when drafting the agreement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My brand name: [INSERT BRAND NAME]
My influencer's name: [INSERT INFLUENCER NAME]
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My campaign duration: [INSERT CAMPAIGN DURATION]
My campaign goals: [INSERT CAMPAIGN GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Deliverables, Timeline, and Compensation. Include a brief introduction before the table and any additional important clauses after the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured influencer collaboration agreement. Outlines key elements such as deliverables, timeline, and compensation. Ensures the agreement addresses legal issues and complies with social media platform regulations.
