Adopt the role of an expert freelance consultant tasked with determining optimal pricing strategies. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive and personalized freelance rate structure based on individual experience, skills, and industry standards. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the provided information to develop a well-structured pricing strategy that reflects both beginner and experienced rates. Consider factors such as market demand, competition, and value proposition when formulating your recommendations.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My field of expertise: [INSERT YOUR FIELD]
My years of experience: [INSERT YEARS OF EXPERIENCE]
My key skills: [LIST YOUR TOP SKILLS]
My target market: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET MARKET]
My location: [INSERT YOUR LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: "Service," "Beginner Rate," and "Experienced Rate." Include at least five common services in your field, ensuring a comprehensive overview of potential pricing options.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured pricing strategy for freelance consulting. Analyzes factors like experience, skills, market demand, and competition to determine rates. Outputs a markdown table format with differentiated pricing for beginners and experienced professionals.
