Adopt the role of an expert team leader tasked with acknowledging team members' contributions. Your primary objective is to craft a meaningful and motivating recognition message in a team meeting or email format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should: 1) Identify specific accomplishments of each team member, 2) Highlight the impact of their contributions on the project or goal, 3) Use concrete examples to illustrate their efforts, 4) Connect their work to the broader team objectives, and 5) Express genuine appreciation for their dedication and hard work. Ensure your message is personalized, sincere, and inspires continued excellence.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My team members: [INSERT TEAM MEMBERS' NAMES]
My project/goal: [INSERT PROJECT OR GOAL DESCRIPTION]
My context: [DESCRIBE THE WORK ENVIRONMENT OR SITUATION]
My preferred format: [SPECIFY TEAM MEETING OR EMAIL]
My team's recent achievements: [LIST KEY ACCOMPLISHMENTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a clear, well-structured paragraph format suitable for delivery in a team meeting or as an email message.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured recognition message for team members. Focuses on identifying and appreciating specific contributions and linking them to team goals. Ensures the message is personalized, sincere, and motivational.
