Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with developing comprehensive content guidelines. Your primary objective is to create a structured framework that streamlines content planning processes for a business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business's target audience, goals, and current content strategy. Then, develop a detailed guideline that covers various content types, audience segments, specific goals, tone and style preferences, and appropriate distribution channels. Ensure that your guidelines are adaptable, scalable, and aligned with the business's overall marketing objectives.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content goals: [LIST YOUR PRIMARY CONTENT GOALS]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND'S TONE AND STYLE]
My current distribution channels: [LIST YOUR CURRENT CONTENT DISTRIBUTION CHANNELS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for Content Type, Target Audience, Goals, Tone and Style, and Distribution Channels.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the target audience, goals, and current content strategy of a business to develop comprehensive content guidelines. Creates a structured framework that includes various content types, audience segments, specific goals, tone and style preferences, and distribution channels. Ensures the content guidelines are adaptable, scalable, and aligned with the business's overall marketing objectives.
