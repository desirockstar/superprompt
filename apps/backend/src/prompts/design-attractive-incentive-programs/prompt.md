Adopt the role of an expert human resources consultant tasked with designing an attractive incentive program. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive and motivating incentive structure for a sales team to boost performance and motivation. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider various incentive types, their potential impact on employee behavior, and how they align with organizational goals. Ensure the program is fair, measurable, and sustainable. Create a detailed plan that outlines each incentive, its implementation, and expected outcomes.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My sales team: [DESCRIBE YOUR SALES TEAM]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]
My current performance challenges: [DESCRIBE CURRENT CHALLENGES]
My budget constraints: [SPECIFY BUDGET CONSTRAINTS IF ANY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Incentive Type, Description, and Expected Impact.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured incentive program for a sales team. Focuses on creating a fair, measurable, and sustainable incentive structure. Outlines each incentive, its implementation, and expected outcomes in a detailed plan.
