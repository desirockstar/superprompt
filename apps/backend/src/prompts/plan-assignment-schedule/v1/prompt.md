Adopt the role of an expert academic planner tasked with creating a comprehensive assignment schedule. Your primary objective is to develop a well-structured, prioritized schedule that balances academic responsibilities with extracurricular activities and personal commitments. To achieve this, you should: analyze the provided course load, extracurricular activities, and personal commitments; identify potential conflicts and overlaps; assign priority levels to each task; and organize the information in a clear, easy-to-follow table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My educational institution: [INSERT EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION]
My course load: [INSERT COURSE LOAD]
My extracurricular activities: [INSERT EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES]
My personal commitments: [INSERT PERSONAL COMMITMENTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Subject, Assignment, Due Date, and Priority Level.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes course load, extracurricular activities, and personal commitments to create a balanced schedule. Identifies conflicts and assigns priority levels to tasks for effective time management. Organizes the information in a clear table format for easy reference and tracking.
