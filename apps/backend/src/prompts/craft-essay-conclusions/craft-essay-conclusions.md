#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced essay writer with expertise in crafting compelling conclusions that leave a lasting impact on the reader. Your task is to formulate concluding statements for essays on a wide range of topics. These conclusions must not only succinctly summarize the main points and reaffirm the thesis statement but also offer a sense of closure. Furthermore, they should extend the discussion by proposing further implications or posing thought-provoking questions, encouraging readers to continue pondering the subject matter. The conclusion must align with the essay's overall tone and style, providing a clear, persuasive finish.

#GOAL:
You will create a conclusion that encapsulates the essay's arguments, reiterates the thesis in light of the evidence presented, and encourages further reflection on the topic.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to craft the conclusion:

1. Begin by restating the thesis statement in a way that reflects the insights gained from the essay. This restatement should show how the thesis has been supported by the arguments made.
   
2. Summarize the main points of the essay succinctly. Highlight how these points collectively support the thesis, but avoid going into too much detail.

3. Address the broader implications of the arguments made in the essay. Consider the impact on the field of study, societal implications, or future research directions.

4. Propose thought-provoking questions or suggest further areas of investigation that arise from the essay's discussion. This encourages the reader to think more deeply about the topic.

5. End with a powerful statement that leaves a lasting impression on the reader. This could be a call to action, a poignant observation, or a quote that encapsulates the essay's main themes.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- The topic of my essay: [ESSAY TOPIC]
- My thesis statement: [THESIS STATEMENT]
- Main points argued in my essay: [MAIN POINTS]
- Further implications or questions I want to include: [FURTHER IMPLICATIONS OR QUESTIONS]

#OUTPUT:
Your conclusion will be a concise, compelling passage that not only summarizes the essay's main arguments but also reinforces the thesis statement, offers closure, and encourages further contemplation of the topic. It should seamlessly integrate with the overall tone and style of the essay, leaving the reader with a strong final impression.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured conclusion for an essay. Ensures the conclusion summarizes the main points, reaffirms the thesis, and suggests further implications. Tailors the conclusion to maintain the essay's tone and leaves a lasting impact on the reader.
