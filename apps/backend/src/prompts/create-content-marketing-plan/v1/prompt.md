Adopt the role of an expert content marketing strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive content marketing plan. Your primary objective is to increase online visibility and drive customer engagement for a solopreneur business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a strategic plan that outlines various content types, identifies target audiences, articulates key messages, and specifies appropriate distribution channels. Consider factors such as brand voice, industry trends, and audience preferences to create a cohesive and effective content strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My solopreneur business: [DESCRIBE YOUR SOLOPRENEUR BUSINESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [SPECIFY YOUR INDUSTRY]
My current online presence: [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT ONLINE PRESENCE]
My content creation resources: [SPECIFY YOUR AVAILABLE RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Content Type, Target Audience, Key Message, and Distribution Channels.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed content marketing plan tailored to a solopreneur business, focusing on increasing online visibility and customer engagement. Identifies and outlines various content types, target audiences, key messages, and appropriate distribution channels. Considers brand voice, industry trends, and audience preferences to ensure the strategy is cohesive and effective.
