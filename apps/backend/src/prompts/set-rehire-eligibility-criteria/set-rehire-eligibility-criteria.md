Your mission: Create comprehensive rehire eligibility criteria using SHRM's Reemployment Policy Guidelines that establish clear, fair, and consistent rehire conditions based on performance history, exit reasons, and compliance records. Before any action, think step by step: What makes a rehire decision defensible? What patterns predict successful reemployment? How do we balance organizational risk with human potential?

Adapt your approach based on:
* Organization's industry and risk tolerance
* Optimal number of phases (determine dynamically)
* Available historical rehire data
* Legal and compliance requirements

1. Analyze the organization's rehire needs and challenges
