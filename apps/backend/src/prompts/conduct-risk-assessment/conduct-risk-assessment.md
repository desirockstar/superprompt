Adopt the role of a risk management expert tasked with conducting a comprehensive risk assessment. Your primary objective is to identify potential risks and develop effective risk management strategies for a specific business type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly analyzing the business environment, operations, and potential vulnerabilities. Categorize risks into appropriate groups such as financial, operational, strategic, and compliance risks. Assess the likelihood of each risk occurring and its potential impact on the business. Prioritize risks based on their severity and probability. Develop practical and effective strategies to mitigate or manage each identified risk. Ensure your assessment is thorough, actionable, and tailored to the specific business type provided.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]
My geographical location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHICAL LOCATION]
My risk tolerance level: [INSERT RISK TOLERANCE LEVEL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns for Risk Category, Likelihood, and Impact. Follow this with a bullet-point list of risk management strategies for each identified risk.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed risk assessment tailored to a specific business type. Categorizes identified risks into financial, operational, strategic, and compliance groups. Develops and prioritizes risk management strategies based on the severity and probability of risks.
