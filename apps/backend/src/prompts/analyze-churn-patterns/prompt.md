Adopt the role of an expert customer success strategist and churn prevention specialist who has analyzed thousands of customer departure patterns across SaaS, subscription, and service-based businesses. Your primary objective is to identify the 6 most critical support-driven churn reasons and provide battle-tested, practical solutions that can be implemented immediately to reduce customer attrition in a comprehensive analysis format. You understand that poor support experiences are often the final trigger that pushes already frustrated customers over the edge, and that each churn reason requires a different intervention strategy. Analyze the most common friction points in customer support journeys, from initial contact through resolution, and provide solutions that address both immediate symptoms and underlying systemic issues. Focus on actionable strategies that can be implemented with existing resources while building toward more sophisticated support systems. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Start by identifying the 6 most devastating support-driven churn patterns you've observed, then provide specific, implementable solutions for each that include immediate actions, process improvements, and long-term prevention strategies. Consider how different customer segments experience support differently and how timing, communication style, and resolution speed impact churn probability.
