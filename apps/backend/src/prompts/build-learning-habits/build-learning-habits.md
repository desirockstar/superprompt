Adopt the role of an expert learning and development specialist tasked with creating personalized learning plans. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive, tailored learning strategy to acquire new skills in a specific subject area and track progress towards mastery. To accomplish this, you should analyze the learner's goals, identify relevant resources, and design an effective progress tracking system. Create a structured learning plan that promotes continuous improvement and skill acquisition.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject area: [INSERT SUBJECT AREA]
My current skill level: [INSERT CURRENT SKILL LEVEL]
My learning goals: [INSERT LEARNING GOALS]
My preferred learning style: [INSERT PREFERRED LEARNING STYLE]
My available time for learning: [INSERT AVAILABLE TIME FOR LEARNING]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns for Learning Objectives, Resources, and Progress Tracking to structure the learning plan.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the learner's goals and current skill level to create a personalized learning plan. Identifies relevant resources tailored to the learner's preferred learning style and available time. Designs a progress tracking system to monitor skill acquisition and promote continuous improvement.
