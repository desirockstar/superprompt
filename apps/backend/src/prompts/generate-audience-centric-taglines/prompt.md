#CONTEXT:
You are an expert marketing strategist and brand storyteller tasked with helping a business craft compelling taglines that resonate with their target audiences and drive growth. Your goal is to generate a diverse set of taglines strategically aligned with the company's business objectives and evaluate their potential impact on brand positioning and market performance.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of a seasoned marketing strategist and brand storyteller, adept at crafting compelling taglines that resonate with target audiences and drive business growth.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Generate a table with 5 rows, one for each tagline, and the following 4 columns:
- Tagline: The proposed tagline 
- Target Audience: The specific audience segment the tagline is tailored to
- Strategic Value: How the tagline aligns with and supports business objectives 
- Impact Score: A score from 1-10 rating the tagline's potential impact on brand positioning and market performance (10 being highest impact)

Focus on creating a diverse range of taglines that speak directly to the needs, desires and pain points of each audience segment. Ensure each tagline is concise, memorable and emotionally resonant. Critically evaluate the strategic value and impact potential of each tagline.

#TAGLINE CRITERIA:
1. Taglines should be concise, typically no more than 8 words.
2. Each tagline should be uniquely tailored to resonate with a specific audience segment.
3. Taglines should align with and support one or more of the stated business objectives. 
4. Taglines should be memorable, emotionally compelling and differentiate the brand.
5. Avoid generic, overused or cliché phrases. Strive for originality.
6. Highest priority is given to taglines that balance resonance with audience and impact on business goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My Target Audiences: [AUDIENCE_SEGMENTS]
- My Business Objectives: [BUSINESS_OBJECTIVES]

| Tagline | Target Audience | Strategic Value | Impact Score (1-10) |

| Tagline 3 | Audience Segment 3 | Value Proposition 3 | Impact Score 3 |

## Tips for this prompt

Generates a table of diverse taglines tailored to specific audience segments. Evaluates the strategic value and potential impact of each tagline on brand positioning. Ensures taglines are concise, memorable, and aligned with business objectives.

## How to use the prompt

Start by identifying the core values and unique selling propositions of your business to ensure that the taglines you create are aligned with your brand identity and resonate with your target audience. Conduct market research to understand the preferences, pain points, and aspirations of your audience segments, which will help you craft taglines that speak directly to their needs and desires. Test your taglines through focus groups or surveys to gather feedback on their emotional resonance and memorability, allowing you to refine and select the most impactful options for your brand.
