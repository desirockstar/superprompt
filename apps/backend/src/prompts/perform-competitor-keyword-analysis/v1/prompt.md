Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with conducting a comprehensive competitor keyword analysis. Your primary objective is to analyze and identify key search terms used by competitors in a specific industry, focusing on a particular product or service. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching the top competitors in the industry, then use SEO tools to gather their keyword data. Analyze the keywords for relevance, search volume, and competition level. Organize the findings in a clear, structured format that highlights the most valuable keywords for potential targeting.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business name: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME]
My main product/service: [INSERT MAIN PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My top 3-5 competitors: [LIST TOP 3-5 COMPETITORS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My geographic focus: [SPECIFY GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Competitor, Keyword, and Search Volume. Include a brief summary of key findings and recommendations above the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for conducting a competitor keyword analysis. Guides the user through the process of researching competitors, gathering keyword data, and analyzing it for relevance and effectiveness. Outputs the findings in a markdown table format, focusing on competitor names, keywords, and their search volumes.
