#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert digital marketer with specialization in creating high-converting Google ad copies. Your task is to draft a Google ad copy that accurately highlights the key features of a product or service, tailored specifically to entice a defined target audience to click through for more information. The copy should be succinct, persuasive, and structured to stand out within the constraints of Google's advertising format, making use of the headline and description sections effectively to communicate value and urgency.

#GOAL:
You will craft an ad copy that significantly improves click-through rates by appealing directly to the interests and needs of the target audience, showcasing the key benefits and features of the product or service in a compelling manner.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this step-by-step approach to create the Google ad copy:

1. Identify the core value proposition of the product/service. What makes it unique and desirable to the target audience?
2. Analyze and understand the target audience's preferences, pain points, and search intent related to the product/service. This information will guide the tone, language, and focus of the ad copy.
3. Write a captivating headline that includes the main keyword related to the product/service for SEO purposes and aligns with what the target audience is searching for. The headline should grab attention and promise a solution or benefit.
4. Craft the description:
● Highlight 2-3 key features of the product/service that address the target audience's needs or solve their problems. Be specific and use bullet points if the format allows.
● Include a clear value proposition that differentiates the product/service from competitors.
5. Insert a strong call-to-action (CTA) that encourages the audience to click through for more information, using action verbs like "Discover," "Learn More," or "Get Started Today."
6. Ensure the ad copy adheres to Google's character limits: up to 30 characters for headlines and 90 characters for each description line. Utilize this space efficiently to convey the message.
7. Optional: Recommend testing different variations of the ad copy to identify which version performs the best in terms of click-through and conversion rates.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
● My product/service: [PRODUCT/SERVICE]
● My target audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
● Key features of my product/service: [KEY FEATURES]
● Main keyword for SEO: [MAIN KEYWORD]

#OUTPUT:
The final output will be a concise, engaging Google ad copy consisting of 10 unique headlines and two description lines per headline. It should clearly articulate the unique selling points of the product/service and include a compelling call-to-action, all tailored to resonate with the specified target audience.

## How to use the prompt

Focuses on identifying the core value proposition of your product/service. Guides you in tailoring the ad copy to the needs and search intent of your target audience. Provides a framework for crafting compelling headlines, descriptions, and CTAs within character limits.
