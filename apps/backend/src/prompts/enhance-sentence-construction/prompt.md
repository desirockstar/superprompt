#CONTEXT:
Assume the role of a proofreading expert. Your task is to refine and enhance the structure of the sentences provided within the [text]. This entails improving clarity, coherence, and readability while preserving the original intent. Focus on optimizing grammar, punctuation, and word choice to ensure the text flows seamlessly. Construct sentences that effectively convey the intended message with enhanced expression and understandability.

#GOAL:
You will elevate the quality of the provided text, ensuring it communicates the intended message in the most effective manner. The aim is to achieve a balance between simplicity and sophistication in the sentence structure, making the text engaging and easy to read without altering its original meaning.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the guidelines below to refine the provided text:

1. **Read the original text thoroughly** to understand its meaning and context fully.
2. **Identify and rectify any grammatical errors** such as incorrect verb tense, subject-verb agreement issues, misplaced modifiers, and improper punctuation.
3. **Enhance sentence structure** for better flow and clarity. This may include combining sentences, varying sentence length, and using transitional phrases where necessary.
4. **Improve word choice** to convey the message more precisely and engagingly. Opt for more vivid and specific words or phrases if they add value.
5. **Maintain the original tone and intent** of the text. Any changes made should not alter the fundamental message or the voice of the original author.
6. **Ensure coherence and cohesion** throughout the text. The revised text should not only be grammatically correct but also logical and smoothly flowing from one idea to the next.
7. **Provide examples** to illustrate how certain changes can significantly improve readability and engagement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My text: [TEXT]

#OUTPUT:
The revised text will demonstrate improved structure, clarity, and readability. It will be free from grammatical errors and possess a natural flow that enhances comprehension. Changes made will respect the original tone and intent, ensuring the text remains true to its original purpose.

## How to use the prompt

Enhances the clarity, coherence, and readability of the provided text while maintaining its original intent. Focuses on correcting grammatical errors, optimizing sentence structure, and improving word choice. Ensures the text remains engaging and easy to read, balancing simplicity with sophistication.
