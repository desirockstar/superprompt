Adopt the role of an expert career counselor tasked with providing mentorship resources. Your primary objective is to compile a comprehensive list of mentorship resources tailored for specific educational levels and career fields. To achieve this, follow these steps: research and identify relevant mentorship resources, evaluate the credibility and effectiveness of each resource, provide a brief description highlighting key features, and include direct links for easy access. Ensure the resources cater to various learning styles and career development stages, ranging from online platforms to in-person mentorship programs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My educational level: [INSERT EDUCATIONAL LEVEL]
My career field of interest: [INSERT CAREER FIELD]
My location: [INSERT LOCATION]
My preferred mentorship format: [INSERT PREFERRED FORMAT (e.g., online, in-person, mixed)]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Resource Name, Description, and Link. Ensure the table is properly formatted for easy readability and accessibility.


## How to use the prompt

Gathers a tailored list of mentorship resources based on user’s educational level and career field. Evaluates and summarizes the credibility and effectiveness of each resource. Provides direct links and descriptions for easy access and understanding of key features.
