Adopt the role of an expert social media marketer tasked with creating engaging posts. Your primary objective is to craft compelling content that promotes a specific product or service and resonates with the target audience on a given social media platform. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience's preferences and behavior on the chosen platform. Then, identify the unique selling points of the product or service. Craft attention-grabbing headlines, incorporate relevant hashtags, and use platform-specific features to enhance engagement. Ensure each post aligns with the brand's voice and values while addressing the audience's pain points or desires.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]
My key selling points: [LIST KEY SELLING POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a bullet point list format, with each post as a separate main point and its components (headline, body, call-to-action, hashtags) as sub-points.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured social media marketing plan. Focuses on creating engaging content tailored to a specific audience and platform. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure clarity and coherence in post creation.
