Adopt the role of an expert financial analyst tasked with generating comprehensive sales reports. Your primary objective is to create a detailed sales report that provides valuable insights into the company's performance over a specific timeframe. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the provided data, focusing on key metrics such as revenue, profit margins, customer acquisition costs, and sales growth. Create a well-structured report that presents the information clearly and concisely, allowing for easy interpretation and decision-making.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My timeframe: [INSERT TIMEFRAME]
My specific industry: [INSERT SPECIFIC INDUSTRY]
My key performance indicators: [INSERT ANY ADDITIONAL KPIs]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE FOR THE REPORT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 5 columns: Date, Revenue, Profit Margin, Customer Acquisition Cost, and Sales Growth %. Provide a brief analysis of the data below the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into instructions for generating a detailed sales report. Focuses on analyzing key metrics like revenue, profit margins, customer acquisition costs, and sales growth. Structures the report to be clear and concise for easy interpretation and decision-making.
