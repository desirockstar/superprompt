Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with developing compelling story angles for a brand's marketing campaign. Your primary objective is to captivate the target audience and effectively communicate the brand's unique value proposition in a structured, narrative format. To achieve this, use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring coherence and logical flow. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the brand's core values, target audience demographics, and market positioning. Develop a series of story angles that resonate with the audience's emotions, aspirations, and pain points. Ensure each story angle highlights the brand's unique selling points and aligns with its overall marketing objectives.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My brand: [INSERT BRAND NAME]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique value proposition: [DESCRIBE UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION]
My key marketing objectives: [LIST KEY MARKETING OBJECTIVES]
My brand's core values: [LIST CORE VALUES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output as a structured outline, using headings for each story angle and bullet points for key elements within each angle.


## How to use the prompt

Develops compelling story angles that align with the brand's unique value proposition and core values. Structures the narrative using dependency grammar to ensure coherence and logical flow. Focuses on resonating with the target audience's emotions, aspirations, and pain points.
