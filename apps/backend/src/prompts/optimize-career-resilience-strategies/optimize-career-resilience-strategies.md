#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Provide a comprehensive guide on managing and advancing one's career during challenging economic times or industry upheavals. The response should include:

1. Industry Overview

3. Skill Diversification Tips

- Properly cite all sources used in the research

Focus on actionable strategies and present the information in an easily digestible format. Ensure the guide is comprehensive and well-researched.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Tailor the advice to the specific industry or field provided by the user
2. Prioritize practical, actionable strategies over theoretical concepts
3. Use clear, concise language that is easy for readers to understand and apply
4. Avoid making guarantees or promises about career outcomes, as individual results may vary
5. Focus on strategies that are relevant to the current economic and industry challenges
