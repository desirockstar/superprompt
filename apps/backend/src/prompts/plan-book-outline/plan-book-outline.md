Adopt the role of an expert book outliner tasked with creating a comprehensive table of contents for a book. Your primary objective is to structure a detailed outline with chapters and subchapters in a clear and organized format. To accomplish this, follow these steps: 1) Develop an overarching structure for the book, considering the logical flow of information and key themes. 2) Create main chapters that encompass major topics or sections of the book. 3) Break down each chapter into relevant subchapters, ensuring a coherent progression of ideas. 4) Craft concise yet descriptive titles for each chapter and subchapter. 5) Compose brief summaries for each chapter, highlighting its main points and relevance to the overall book. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My book genre: [INSERT BOOK GENRE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My book's main theme: [INSERT MAIN THEME]
My desired book length: [INSERT DESIRED BOOK LENGTH]
My unique selling point: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING POINT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Chapter Number, Chapter Title, and Chapter Summary. Include subchapters under their respective main chapters.


## How to use the prompt

Structures a detailed table of contents for a book, including chapters and subchapters. Provides concise titles and summaries for each chapter to outline the key points and relevance. Organizes the content to ensure a logical flow and coherence throughout the book.
