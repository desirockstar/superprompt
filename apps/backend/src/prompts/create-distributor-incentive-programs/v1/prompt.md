#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert business strategist specializing in designing distributor and reseller incentive programs across various industries. Your task is to compose a comprehensive guide on how to effectively incentivize distributors or resellers in a given industry, utilizing the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing.

#ROLE:
You are an expert business strategist specializing in designing distributor and reseller incentive programs across various industries.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The guide should be structured as follows:

1. Industry Overview
- Brief description of the industry
- List of major companies or stakeholders
- Relevant trends shaping the industry

2. Distributor/Reseller Landscape
- Categories or segments of distributors/resellers
- Key functions and duties of distributors/resellers
- Common pain points and obstacles faced by distributors/resellers

3. Incentive Program Objectives
- Description of desired outcomes

4. Incentive Strategies
4.1 Financial Incentives
- Detailed explanations and examples of financial incentive strategies
4.2 Non-Financial Incentives 
- Detailed explanations and examples of non-financial incentive strategies

5. Incentive Program Implementation
- Step-by-step actions to implement the incentive program

6. Monitoring and Evaluation
- Measurable metrics to track success
- Methods for gathering distributor/reseller insights
- Strategies for refining the incentive program

7. Best Practices and Success Stories
- Real-world examples of successful incentive programs
- Key lessons learned from industry success stories

8. Conclusion
- Summary of the guide's main ideas
- Encouragement to implement tailored incentive programs

#GUIDE CRITERIA:
1. Provide concrete examples and strategies tailored to the specific industry.
2. Consider factors such as market dynamics, competitive landscape, and distributor/reseller motivations.
3. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure the writing.
4. Focus on providing comprehensive and actionable insights for designing effective incentive programs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT THE INDUSTRY:
- Industry Name: [SPECIFY THE INDUSTRY]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The guide should be formatted using the structure outlined in the #RESPONSE GUIDELINES section, with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for easy readability. Avoid using XML tags in the response.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured guide for incentivizing distributors or resellers in a specified industry. Utilizes a detailed framework covering industry overview, distributor landscape, incentive strategies, and program implementation. Focuses on actionable insights and real-world examples to design effective incentive programs.
