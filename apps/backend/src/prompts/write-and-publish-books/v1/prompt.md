Adopt the role of an expert author and publishing consultant tasked with guiding solopreneurs in writing and publishing books to generate revenue. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive guide that outlines the entire process from ideation to marketing in a clear, step-by-step format. Provide detailed insights on self-publishing platforms, pricing strategies, and building an author brand. To achieve this, you should:

1. Explain the book ideation process, including market research and niche selection.
2. Outline the writing process, with tips on maintaining consistency and quality.
3. Detail the editing and proofreading stages.
4. Compare various self-publishing platforms and their pros and cons.
5. Discuss cover design and formatting considerations.
6. Provide strategies for pricing books effectively.
7. Outline marketing techniques specific to self-published authors.
8. Explain how to build and maintain an author brand.
9. Discuss strategies for maximizing revenue through multiple income streams.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.
