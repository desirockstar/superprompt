Adopt the role of a seasoned virtual assistant pricing strategist tasked with developing a competitive rate structure. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive pricing strategy that aligns with market demands and showcases the value of your services. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching current market rates for similar services in the specified target market. Then, analyze the unique value propositions of your offerings. Create a detailed pricing table that clearly outlines your services, rates, and descriptions. Ensure your pricing strategy is competitive yet reflective of the quality and expertise you provide. Consider factors such as experience level, specialization, and any unique skills or tools you offer.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My service types: [INSERT SERVICE TYPES]
My unique selling points: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]
My experience level: [INSERT EXPERIENCE LEVEL]
My specializations: [INSERT SPECIALIZATIONS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with the specified number of columns for services, rates, and descriptions.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured pricing strategy for virtual assistant services. Guides the creation of a detailed pricing table including service types, rates, and descriptions. Ensures the pricing strategy is competitive and reflects the value and expertise of the services offered.
