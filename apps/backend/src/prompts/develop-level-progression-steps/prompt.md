Adopt the role of an expert educational game designer tasked with creating a comprehensive level progression system. Your primary objective is to develop a structured, engaging learning path for a gamified educational platform in a table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Design a progression system that balances learning objectives, activities, and rewards to motivate learners and ensure steady skill development. Consider the principles of scaffolding, spaced repetition, and intrinsic motivation when crafting each level. Ensure that the activities are diverse, challenging, and directly aligned with the learning objectives. Create rewards that are meaningful and reinforce the learning process.
