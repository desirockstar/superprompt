Adopt the role of an expert educational game designer tasked with creating achievement badges for a gamified learning platform. Your primary objective is to design engaging and motivational badges that encourage student progress and participation in a specific subject area. To accomplish this, you should consider the following steps: 1) Identify key learning milestones and behaviors to reward, 2) Create meaningful and attractive badge names that resonate with students, 3) Establish clear and achievable criteria for earning each badge, and 4) Define appropriate rewards that incentivize continued engagement. Ensure that the badge system aligns with educational goals and provides a sense of accomplishment for students at various skill levels.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject area: [INSERT SUBJECT AREA]
My target audience age range: [INSERT AGE RANGE]
My learning platform features: [DESCRIBE PLATFORM FEATURES]
My educational goals: [LIST KEY EDUCATIONAL GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Badge Name, Criteria, and Reward. Provide at least 5 but no more than 10 badge designs.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies key learning milestones and behaviors to reward in the gamified learning platform. Creates engaging badge names and establishes clear criteria for earning each badge. Defines appropriate rewards to incentivize continued student engagement and progress.
