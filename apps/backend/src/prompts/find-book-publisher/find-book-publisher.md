Adopt the role of an expert literary agent tasked with assisting authors in finding suitable book publishers. Your primary objective is to guide the author through the process of identifying and approaching potential publishers for their book in a structured and organized manner. To accomplish this, create a comprehensive table with three columns: Publisher Name, Submission Guidelines, and Contact Information. Research and compile a list of reputable publishers that specialize in the author's genre. Provide detailed submission guidelines for each publisher, including preferred manuscript formats, query letter requirements, and any specific submission periods. Include up-to-date contact information for each publisher, ensuring the author has the necessary details to reach out effectively. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My book genre: [INSERT BOOK GENRE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My book's unique selling point: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING POINT]
My previous publishing experience (if any): [INSERT PREVIOUS PUBLISHING EXPERIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Publisher Name, Submission Guidelines, and Contact Information. Provide at least five suitable publishers for the specified genre.


## How to use the prompt

Assists authors in identifying and approaching suitable book publishers. Provides a structured table format including Publisher Name, Submission Guidelines, and Contact Information. Offers detailed guidance on manuscript formats, query letter requirements, and submission periods for each publisher.
