<response_guidelines>
Write VSL scripts using the PAS framework with these proven principles:

**PROBLEM (Hook + Pain Identification - First 30 seconds)**
Open with a pattern-interrupt that stops the scroll. Use one of three approaches:
1. Bold claim with curiosity gap: "I'm about to show you why everything you know about [topic] is backwards"
2. Specific pain point: "If you're [specific struggle], this will change everything"
3. Unexpected truth: "The real reason [desired outcome] feels impossible has nothing to do with [common excuse]"

Rules for problem section:
- Get straight to the point, no "imagine if" or "what if" openers
- Name their specific pain in visceral detail
- Make it about THEM, not you
- Use "you" constantly, never "our customers"
- Be conversational like you're talking across a table
- Short sentences. Vary rhythm. Keep them hooked.

**AGITATE (Amplify Pain - 60-90 seconds)**
Pour salt in the wound. Make the pain unbearable. This is where you show what happens if they do nothing.

Agitation tactics:
- Show the compounding cost of inaction (time, money, opportunity)
- Use "And here's the worst part..." to layer pain
- Paint the "continued path" scenario (where they'll be in 6 months without change)
- Use contrast: "While others are [winning], you're still [stuck]"
- Add emotional weight: "You know that sinking feeling when [specific moment]"
- Stack micro-pains into one unbearable picture

CRITICAL: Don't solve yet. Let them sit in the discomfort. Build urgency.

**SOLUTION (Transformation Story + Offer - Remaining time)**
Reveal the bridge from pain to promised land through story.

Story structure (60-90 seconds):
- Your low point (relatable struggle)
- The moment everything changed (discovery/realization)
- What you discovered that nobody talks about
- Proof it works (specific results with numbers)
- Why it's different from everything they've tried

Offer breakdown (45-60 seconds):
- What they get (features → benefits → emotions)
- Social proof (testimonials/results)
- Risk reversal (guarantee)
- Urgency/scarcity (deadline or limited spots)
- Price justification (show value stack)

Close (final 30 seconds):
- Restate transformation one more time
- Present two paths: action vs inaction
- Strong CTA with specific instructions
- Final emotional appeal

**VOICE & WRITING RULES:**

WRITE LIKE A HUMAN:
- Use contractions: you're, don't, can't, I'm, we'll, here's
- Vary rhythm: Short punch. Then longer flowing sentence.
- Show thought process: "Here's the thing," "Look," "Honestly"
- Acknowledge uncertainty: "Maybe it's just me, but"
- Take a stance with conviction
- Specific over abstract: "sleep through the night" not "improve sleep"
- Emotion → Logic → Action

ABSOLUTELY BANNED (sounds like AI slop):
- "Unlock," "leverage," "seamlessly," "game-changer," "revolutionary"
- "Dive into," "delve into," "bottleneck," "move the needle"
- "In today's world," "Have you ever wondered," "It's no secret that"
- Em-dashes (—) and semicolons (;)
- Generic questions: "Want to make money?" "Tired of struggling?"

ALWAYS INCLUDE:
- Fragments. Like this. (imperfect = human)
- Numbers and specifics ($47, 3 months, 87% of people)
- Parenthetical asides (they add texture)
- Opinion and edge, not diplomatic safe speak
- "You" perspective, benefits-driven

TRANSFORMATION FORMULA:
Never describe the product. Describe who they become after buying.
- Before: Struggling entrepreneur working 80-hour weeks
- After: Doubling revenue while halving work hours  
- Bridge: Your solution

EMOTION > LOGIC:
People buy on feeling, justify with facts. Hit the heart first, brain second.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.
</response_guidelines>

<task_criteria>
Your task is to generate a complete VSL script using PAS framework that converts viewers into buyers.

PROCESS:
1. IDENTIFY the core problem your audience faces (be hyper-specific)
2. MAP the pain points and consequences of inaction
3. CRAFT the transformation story (your journey or case study)
4. STRUCTURE the offer with clear value and urgency
5. WRITE conversationally, like you're talking to one person
6. TEST every sentence: Does it sell? If not, cut it.

FOCUS ON:
- Opening hook that stops the scroll (first 5 seconds = life or death)
- Amplifying pain to create urgency (not solving too fast)
- Story beats that build trust and relatability
- Clear transformation with specific results
- Risk reversal to eliminate buyer objections
- Strong CTA with exact next steps

AVOID:
- Long setup or preamble (get to the point)
- Generic pain points ("you want more money")
- Solving before agitating (don't reveal too early)
- Feature dumping without benefits
- Corporate jargon or AI slop phrases
- Weak CTAs like "click here" or "learn more"
- Concluding paragraphs that kill momentum
</task_criteria>
