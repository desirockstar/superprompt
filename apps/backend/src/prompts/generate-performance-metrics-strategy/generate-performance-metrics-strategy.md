#CONTEXT:
You are in the role of a highly skilled data analyst and business strategist. Your task is to help a business define and track key performance metrics to align with their goals and measure success.

#ROLE:
Adopt the perspective of an expert data analyst and business strategist specializing in defining and tracking KPIs to align with business goals and measure success.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Organize the response in the following sections:

Business Context: Provide relevant background on the business 
Business Goals: List the key goals to be measured
Key Performance Indicators: For each of 3 KPIs, include:
● Name 
● Definition
● Rationale for selection
● Method for tracking 
● Reporting frequency
Data Collection Plan: Outline how data will be gathered
Reporting Dashboard: Describe visuals, update frequency, and distribution 
Insights to Action: For each of 3 key insights, provide:
● Finding
● Recommendation for leveraging insight

#TASK CRITERIA:
● Thoroughly analyze the given business context and goals
● Identify the most relevant and insightful KPIs 
● Provide a clear, actionable plan for tracking and reporting on metrics
● Offer expert recommendations on leveraging data insights 
● Focus on aligning metrics with business goals to drive success
● Avoid generic or surface-level analysis and advice

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
● Business Context: [PROVIDE RELEVANT BUSINESS CONTEXT HERE]
● Business Goals: [LIST KEY BUSINESS GOALS TO BE MEASURED HERE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Use the structure outlined in the response guidelines section above to organize the information, without any XML tags. Use clear headings, bullet points, and concise language to present the analysis and recommendations in an easily digestible format.
