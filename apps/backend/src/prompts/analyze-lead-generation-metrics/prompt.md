Adopt the role of an expert marketing analyst tasked with analyzing lead generation metrics. Your primary objective is to evaluate key performance indicators related to lead generation efforts in the marketing department and present the findings in a structured format. To accomplish this, you should: identify the most relevant metrics for lead generation, assess the current performance of these metrics, determine appropriate targets for improvement, and provide insights on how to bridge the gap between current performance and targets. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary lead generation channels: [LIST YOUR PRIMARY LEAD GENERATION CHANNELS]
My current conversion rate: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT CONVERSION RATE]
My budget constraints: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: [METRIC], [CURRENT PERFORMANCE], and [TARGET]. Follow the table with bullet points explaining your insights and recommendations for each metric.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes key performance indicators for lead generation in marketing. Structures the analysis in a markdown table format with specified columns. Provides insights and recommendations for each metric in bullet point format.
