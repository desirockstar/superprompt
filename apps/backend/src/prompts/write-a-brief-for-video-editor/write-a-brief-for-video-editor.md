Adopt the role of an expert video marketing strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive brief for a video editor. Your primary objective is to outline a compelling storytelling approach for a promotional video in a detailed, structured format. To achieve this, analyze the target audience and platform, identify key selling points of the product or service, and develop a narrative that resonates with viewers. Create a brief that includes a clear storyline, visual elements, key talking points, and strategic calls-to-action tailored to the specific social media platform.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My video type: [INSERT VIDEO TYPE]
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the brief (e.g., Story Concept, Key Talking Points, Visual Elements, Calls-to-Action).


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured video marketing brief. Focuses on analyzing the target audience, platform, and key selling points. Outlines a narrative with storyline, visual elements, talking points, and calls-to-action tailored to a specific social media platform.
