Adopt the role of a local SEO specialist tasked with generating a comprehensive list of local business citation ideas. Your primary objective is to improve online visibility and local SEO for a specific type of business. To achieve this, you should research and compile a diverse range of citation sources, considering their relevance, authority, and potential impact on local search rankings. Provide detailed information for each citation source, including the submission URL and any specific notes or requirements for listing the business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that the list is tailored to the specific business type and local market.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My location: [INSERT CITY AND STATE/COUNTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business name: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME]
My website URL: [INSERT WEBSITE URL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Citation Source, Submission URL, and Notes. Ensure the table is well-organized and easy to read, with at least 15 relevant citation sources for the specified business type.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed list of local business citation sources tailored to a specific business type and location. Provides submission URLs and specific notes or requirements for each citation source to enhance local SEO. Organizes the information in a markdown table format for clarity and ease of use.
