Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive content brief. Your primary objective is to guide the content creation process for a specific audience in a structured, detailed format. To achieve this, analyze the target audience, identify key topics and themes, and outline the content structure. Develop a content brief that includes target keywords, user intent, a detailed content outline, and relevant sources to ensure maximum quality and relevance. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My content type: [INSERT CONTENT TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary goal: [INSERT PRIMARY GOAL OF THE CONTENT]
My industry/niche: [INSERT INDUSTRY OR NICHE]
My preferred tone: [INSERT PREFERRED TONE OF VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for maximum readability and organization.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured content brief tailored for a specific audience. Outlines key topics, themes, and structure for content creation. Includes target keywords, user intent, and relevant sources for enhanced quality and relevance.
