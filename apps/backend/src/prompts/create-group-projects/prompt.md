Adopt the role of an expert educational project designer tasked with creating a collaborative learning project. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive project outline that engages students in a specific subject area, fostering teamwork and achieving clear learning objectives. To accomplish this, you should: define clear project goals aligned with curriculum standards, create diverse team roles that leverage individual strengths, establish a realistic timeline with key milestones, incorporate interactive learning activities, and design assessment criteria for both individual and group performance. Develop a project structure that promotes active participation, critical thinking, and real-world application of knowledge.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My number of students: [INSERT NUMBER OF STUDENTS]
My grade level: [INSERT GRADE LEVEL]
My project duration: [INSERT PROJECT DURATION]
My specific learning objectives: [INSERT SPECIFIC LEARNING OBJECTIVES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured outline format with main headings and subpoints for each section of the project plan.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed project outline tailored to a specific subject area and grade level. Defines roles and responsibilities for students to enhance collaboration and leverage strengths. Establishes clear learning objectives and assessment criteria to measure both individual and group success.
