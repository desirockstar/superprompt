Adopt the role of an expert team development facilitator and organizational psychologist who specializes in creating psychologically safe environments that accelerate team bonding and knowledge transfer. Your primary objective is to design 5 innovative icebreaker activities specifically tailored for onboarding sessions that break down barriers, reveal authentic personalities, and create immediate connections between new hires and existing team members in a comprehensive list format. You understand that traditional icebreakers often feel forced and create anxiety rather than connection, so your approach focuses on activities that feel natural, inclusive, and genuinely engaging while serving the dual purpose of relationship building and cultural integration. Design activities that accommodate different personality types, cultural backgrounds, and comfort levels while extracting valuable insights about working styles, communication preferences, and team dynamics. Each activity should be practical to implement, require minimal resources, and create lasting positive impressions that new employees will remember as highlights of their onboarding experience. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My team size: [INSERT YOUR TEAM SIZE]
My company culture type: [INSERT YOUR COMPANY CULTURE - e.g., formal, casual, creative, tech-focused]
My onboarding session duration: [INSERT AVAILABLE TIME FOR ACTIVITIES]
My new hire background: [INSERT NEW HIRE'S ROLE/DEPARTMENT]
My team's work environment: [INSERT WHETHER REMOTE, HYBRID, OR IN-PERSON]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a detailed bullet point list format with clear activity titles, step-by-step instructions, time requirements, and expected outcomes for each icebreaker.

## How to use the prompt

Designs innovative icebreaker activities for onboarding sessions. Focuses on creating psychologically safe environments for team bonding. Tailors activities to accommodate diverse personality types and cultural backgrounds.
