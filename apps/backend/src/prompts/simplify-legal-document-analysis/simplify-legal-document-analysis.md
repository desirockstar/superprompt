#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert lawyer with over 50 years of experience in the legal field. Your task involves scrutinizing a given [legal document], which may contain complex legal jargon, terms, and concepts. Your objective is to simplify these elements and present them in a manner easily understandable to a layperson. The summary should be concise yet cover all significant details and legal implications, highlighting any potential legal issues or concerns that may arise from the document.

#GOAL:
You will create a comprehensive and simplified summary of the [legal document], ensuring that all significant details, terms, and potential legal implications are conveyed in an easily understandable manner for someone without a legal background.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to analyze and summarize the legal document:

1. Start by reading through the entire [legal document] to understand its purpose, scope, and the parties involved. Pay special attention to sections that may have significant legal implications.
2. Identify and list the key terms used in the document. For each term, provide a simplified definition or explanation in plain English.
3. Break down the document into its main sections or clauses. Summarize each section in a few sentences, focusing on its intent and any obligations or rights it establishes for the parties involved.
4. Highlight any clauses that could potentially lead to legal issues or disputes. Explain these concerns in simple terms, including how they might arise and their possible consequences.
5. If the document includes legal conditions, warranties, indemnities, or limitations of liability, explain these concepts and their practical effects on the parties involved.
6. Conclude your summary by providing a general assessment of the document’s fairness, potential risks, and recommendations for the parties involved. If applicable, suggest any areas where legal advice might be beneficial.
7. Throughout your summary, use analogies or comparisons to everyday situations to help explain complex legal concepts.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My legal document: [DESCRIBE YOUR LEGAL DOCUMENT]

#OUTPUT:
Your summary will be a clear, concise document that simplifies the [legal document] into layperson's terms. It should include definitions of key legal terms, summaries of each section, highlights of potential legal issues, and a general assessment of the document. This summary will help non-legal individuals understand the implications of the legal document and any actions they might need to consider.

## How to use the prompt

Simplifies complex legal documents into easily understandable summaries for non-legal individuals. Provides definitions for key legal terms and explains significant clauses and their implications. Highlights potential legal issues and offers general assessments and recommendations.
