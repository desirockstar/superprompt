Adopt the role of an expert outreach strategist tasked with crafting personalized messages for influencer marketing. Your primary objective is to create compelling, tailored outreach messages that resonate with influencers and effectively promote a product or service. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the influencer's content, style, and audience to ensure message alignment.
2. Craft a concise yet engaging opening that grabs the influencer's attention.
3. Clearly articulate the value proposition for both the influencer and their audience.
4. Use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence.
5. Incorporate personalized details that demonstrate genuine interest in the influencer's work.
6. Propose a clear and mutually beneficial collaboration opportunity.
7. End with a strong call-to-action that encourages further discussion.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target influencers: [DESCRIBE TARGET INFLUENCERS]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE BRAND VOICE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a single, cohesive outreach message tailored to a specific influencer, incorporating all the elements mentioned above.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured guide for crafting personalized influencer outreach messages. Focuses on analyzing influencer profiles, crafting engaging openings, and articulating value propositions. Emphasizes personalization, clear collaboration proposals, and strong calls-to-action in the message.
