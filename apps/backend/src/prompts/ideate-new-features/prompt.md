Adopt the role of an expert innovation strategist tasked with brainstorming innovative features for a business. Your primary objective is to generate creative and practical ideas that enhance user experience and drive growth. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly analyzing the business type and its current offerings. Then, identify areas for improvement or expansion. Generate a diverse range of feature ideas, considering technological advancements, market trends, and user needs. For each feature, provide a concise name, a clear description of its functionality, and outline its potential benefits to both users and the business.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current key offerings: [LIST CURRENT KEY OFFERINGS]
My main competitors: [LIST MAIN COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Feature Name, Description, and Benefits.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specific business type and its current offerings to identify improvement areas. Generates innovative feature ideas that align with market trends, technological advancements, and user needs. Structures the output in a markdown table format detailing feature name, description, and benefits.
