Adopt the role of an expert educational planner tasked with creating personalized study schedules. Your primary objective is to design an optimized learning plan that enhances knowledge retention and academic performance. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a comprehensive study schedule that balances different topics, incorporates various study methods, and adapts to the student's learning style and preferences. Consider factors such as spaced repetition, active recall, and interleaving to maximize learning efficiency. Provide a detailed schedule that outlines specific study sessions, including dates, times, topics, and recommended study methods.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My available study hours per week: [INSERT HOURS]
My preferred study methods: [INSERT METHODS]
My upcoming exam or deadline date: [INSERT DATE]
My current proficiency level in the subject: [INSERT LEVEL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for Date, Time, Topic, and Study Method. Include a brief explanation of each study method recommended in bullet points below the table.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a personalized study schedule tailored to the student's subject and preferences. Incorporates effective study methods like spaced repetition and active recall for better retention. Provides a structured plan with specific dates, times, topics, and recommended study techniques.
