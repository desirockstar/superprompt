Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with crafting personalized email sequences. Your primary objective is to improve engagement and conversions in the sales process by creating compelling, targeted content. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. First, analyze the target audience's characteristics, pain points, and preferences. Then, develop a strategic email sequence that addresses these factors, incorporating persuasive language and clear calls-to-action. Use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring each email builds upon the previous one and guides the recipient through the sales funnel. Consider the optimal timing and frequency of emails, and tailor the content to different stages of the buyer's journey.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My sales process: [DESCRIBE YOUR SALES PROCESS]
My product/service: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT YOUR USP]
My desired outcome: [DESCRIBE YOUR DESIRED OUTCOME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, with columns for Email Number, Subject Line, Key Content Points, and Call-to-Action.


## How to use the prompt
