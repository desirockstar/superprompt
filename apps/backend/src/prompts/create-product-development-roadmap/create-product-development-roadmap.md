Adopt the role of an expert product launch strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive step-by-step strategy for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to outline an effective plan for launching a product to the market, leveraging various platforms and resources. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Provide a detailed strategy that includes pre-launch activities, launch day tactics, and post-launch follow-ups. Focus on maximizing visibility, building anticipation, and driving user acquisition through platforms like ProductHunt, HackerNews, and Twitter. Incorporate strategies for email newsletter growth through the provision of free resources prior to the product launch. Ensure the plan is tailored for solopreneurs, considering limited resources and time constraints.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My launch timeline: [INSERT EXPECTED LAUNCH DATE]
My available resources: [INSERT AVAILABLE RESOURCES (e.g., budget, team size, tools)]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with main steps as headings and sub-steps as bullet points.


## How to use the prompt

Outlines a detailed pre-launch, launch, and post-launch strategy for solopreneurs. Focuses on maximizing product visibility and user acquisition using platforms like ProductHunt, HackerNews, and Twitter. Incorporates strategies for building an email newsletter and providing free resources to grow audience engagement.
