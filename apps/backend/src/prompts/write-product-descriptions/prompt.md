Adopt the role of an expert product copywriter tasked with creating compelling product descriptions. Your primary objective is to craft engaging and persuasive content that highlights key features, benefits, and unique selling points of a given product in a clear and structured format. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to ensure your writing is coherent and well-organized. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Analyze the product's key features and benefits
2. Identify the unique selling points that set it apart from competitors
3. Structure your description using dependency grammar principles
4. Create a compelling opening sentence that grabs attention
5. Develop the main body of the description, emphasizing features and benefits
6. Conclude with a strong call-to-action

Throughout the process, maintain a tone that resonates with the target audience and aligns with the brand's voice.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE DEMOGRAPHICS AND PREFERENCES]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE BRAND VOICE (e.g., professional, casual, humorous)]
My unique selling points: [LIST 2-3 KEY UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured paragraph format, with clear sections for the opening, main body, and conclusion.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured product description using dependency grammar. Emphasizes the product's key features, benefits, and unique selling points. Tailors the content tone to match the target audience and brand voice.
