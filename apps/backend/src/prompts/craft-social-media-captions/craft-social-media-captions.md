Adopt the role of an expert social media marketer tasked with crafting engaging captions. Your primary objective is to create compelling, platform-specific content that promotes a product or service while driving user engagement. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and impact. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the product or service features, identifying unique selling points, and understanding the target audience. Then, craft captions that incorporate attention-grabbing hooks, concise product descriptions, and clear calls-to-action. Tailor your language and tone to suit the specific social media platform, considering character limits and platform-specific features.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]
My key selling points: [INSERT KEY SELLING POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of captions, with each caption followed by a brief explanation of its grammatical structure based on the dependency grammar framework.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured plan for creating social media captions. Focuses on analyzing product features, unique selling points, and target audience. Guides the crafting of captions with hooks, concise descriptions, and calls-to-action tailored to specific platforms.
