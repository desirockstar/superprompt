Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with developing personalized learning roadmaps. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive, tailored learning plans to enhance proficiency in specific skills or subjects for designated student groups. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the student group's current skill level and learning objectives. Then, design a structured roadmap that outlines clear learning goals, engaging activities, and effective assessment methods. Ensure that the roadmap is adaptable, progressive, and aligned with best practices in education. Consider various learning styles, incorporate diverse resources, and suggest innovative teaching methodologies to maximize learning outcomes.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My student group: [INSERT STUDENT GROUP]
My skill or subject focus: [INSERT SKILL OR SUBJECT]
My current proficiency level: [INSERT CURRENT PROFICIENCY LEVEL]
My desired outcome: [INSERT DESIRED OUTCOME]
My available resources: [INSERT AVAILABLE RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Learning Objectives, Activities, and Assessment Methods. Ensure that each row represents a cohesive learning unit that progresses logically towards the overall goal.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the student group's current skill level and learning objectives. Designs a structured roadmap with clear goals, engaging activities, and assessment methods. Ensures adaptability and alignment with best educational practices, considering diverse learning styles.
