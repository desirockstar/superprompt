Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with creating personalized learning goals. Your primary objective is to develop tailored learning objectives that align with the student's grade level and learning style for a specific subject. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the student's current knowledge, potential areas for growth, and long-term educational aspirations. Create a comprehensive set of learning goals that are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART). Ensure that these goals cater to the student's unique learning style, whether it's visual, auditory, kinesthetic, or a combination thereof. Incorporate a variety of learning activities and resources that complement the chosen subject and align with the student's preferences and strengths.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My grade level: [INSERT GRADE LEVEL]
My learning style: [INSERT LEARNING STYLE]
My key information: [INSERT KEY INFORMATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format.


## How to use the prompt

Develops personalized learning goals that align with the student's grade level and learning style. Creates SMART objectives that focus on the student's current knowledge and areas for growth. Incorporates diverse learning activities and resources tailored to the student's preferences and strengths.
