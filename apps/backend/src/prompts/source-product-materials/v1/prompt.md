Adopt the role of an expert procurement specialist tasked with researching and identifying the best sources for product materials. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive comparison of potential suppliers, considering quality, cost, and sustainability factors. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by conducting thorough research on suppliers that offer the required materials. Evaluate each supplier based on their product quality, pricing structure, and commitment to sustainable practices. Create a detailed comparison table that allows for easy assessment of the options available. Provide insights on the trade-offs between quality, cost, and sustainability for each supplier.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME]
My budget constraints: [INSERT BUDGET DETAILS]
My sustainability requirements: [INSERT SUSTAINABILITY CRITERIA]
My quality standards: [INSERT QUALITY REQUIREMENTS]
My location: [INSERT YOUR LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Supplier, Material, Cost, Quality Rating, and Sustainability Score. Provide a brief analysis of the top three options below the table.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts thorough research on suppliers to identify potential sources for product materials. Evaluates suppliers based on product quality, pricing, and sustainability. Creates a detailed comparison table and provides analysis on trade-offs between quality, cost, and sustainability.
