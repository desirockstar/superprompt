What this prompt does Generates an AI image of a bright outdoor selfie featuring a person with specific facial features, long wavy hair with braids, and floral accessories during golden hour. Creates a warm summery scene set in a grassy field with picnic elements like a red blanket, strawberry cake, and wildflowers in soft natural lighting. Produces a playful and cozy AI image with a cute aesthetic using warm golden tones, pinks, and natural greens for a sweet picnic atmosphere.

## Tips for this prompt

## How to use the prompt

Choose an expression like joyful smile, peaceful gaze, or playful wink that matches the sweet picnic mood you want your AI image to convey. Fill the outfit description variable with seasonal layers such as cream knit cardigan, denim jacket with patches, or floral button-up vest to complement the summery aesthetic. Select a prop interaction like holding a strawberry, reaching for the cake, or writing in the notebook to add storytelling depth to your AI image prompt.
