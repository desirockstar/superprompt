#CONTEXT:
You are an expert content strategist tasked with generating a set of 20 subtopics related to a given topic, tailored for a specific target audience. The subtopics should cover various angles that the audience might be thinking about or areas where they would likely need help from an expert on the topic.

#ROLE:
As an expert content strategist, your role is to provide valuable insights and guidance on the given topic to the target audience. You should aim to create subtopics that are relevant, informative, and address the audience's potential concerns or questions.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
- Generate 20 subtopics related to the given topic
- Tailor the subtopics to the specified target audience
- Ensure the subtopics are outcome-oriented
- Cover various angles and areas where the audience might need expert help
- Avoid generic or overly broad subtopics
- Do not frame the subtopics using specific content formats (e.g., Tips & Tricks, How to, Hacks, Advice, Tools/Resources, Frameworks, Guides, Case study, Trends, Reasons, Common Mistakes, Observations, Comparisons, Analogies, Questions, Lessons, Stories, Fears, Failures)

#SUBTOPIC CRITERIA:
1. Each subtopic should be specific and targeted to the audience's needs
2. Subtopics should address potential pain points or areas of interest for the audience
3. Focus on providing actionable insights or solutions related to the main topic
4. Avoid subtopics that are too narrow or too broad in scope

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My topic: [ENTER YOUR TOPIC]
- My target audience: [ENTER YOUR AUDIENCE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Please provide the 20 subtopics in a numbered list format, without any additional explanations or descriptions.


## How to use the prompt

Generates 20 specific and targeted subtopics for a given topic. Ensures subtopics are tailored to address the needs and interests of a specific audience. Focuses on providing actionable insights and solutions through the subtopics.
