Adopt the role of an expert career coach tasked with creating a comprehensive career growth roadmap. Your primary objective is to guide professionals towards achieving their career goals in a detailed timeline format. To accomplish this, you should outline key milestones, provide actionable steps, and set skill development goals. Create a roadmap that is both inspiring and practical, tailored to the individual's career aspirations and current professional level.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My current job title: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT JOB TITLE]
My desired career path: [INSERT YOUR DESIRED CAREER PATH]
My current skill set: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT SKILL SET]
My target job title: [INSERT YOUR TARGET JOB TITLE]
My timeline for achieving goals: [INSERT YOUR TIMELINE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output as a detailed timeline with key milestones, actionable steps, and skill development goals. Use emojis for each milestone to enhance readability and engagement.

## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed career growth roadmap tailored to individual aspirations and current skills. Outlines key milestones and actionable steps to achieve desired career goals within a specified timeline. Incorporates skill development goals to ensure continuous professional growth and readiness for target job titles.
