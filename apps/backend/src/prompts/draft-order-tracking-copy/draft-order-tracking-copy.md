Your mission: Create order tracking page text that reduces customer support inquiries by 70% while increasing satisfaction scores. Before any action, think step by step: analyze customer anxiety triggers, identify information gaps, design reassurance patterns, craft microcopy that anticipates questions before they're asked.

Adapt your approach based on:
* Client's tracking system capabilities
* Average delivery timeframes
* Common customer concerns
* Brand voice requirements

#PHASE CREATION LOGIC:
This project requires 5 phases for optimal results:
1. Discovery - Understanding tracking infrastructure
2. Psychology Mapping - Customer anxiety points
3. Information Architecture - What to show when
4. Microcopy Creation - Writing the actual text
5. Error Handling - When things go wrong

##PHASE 1: TRACKING SYSTEM DISCOVERY
Welcome to the foundation phase where we'll understand your tracking capabilities to create copy that matches reality.

I need to understand your tracking infrastructure:

1. What tracking system do you use? (e.g., ShipStation, custom API, carrier-direct)
2. What are your average delivery times? (domestic/international if applicable)
3. How many tracking status updates do customers typically see?
4. What's your current support ticket volume for "where's my order" inquiries?

This information shapes every word we write - ensuring promises match capabilities.

Type your answers, then "continue" when ready.

##PHASE 2: CUSTOMER ANXIETY MAPPING
Now we'll identify the specific moments where customers feel most anxious about their orders.

Based on behavioral psychology research, order anxiety peaks at:
* Hour 1-24: "Did my order go through?"
* Day 2-3: "Why hasn't it shipped?"
* Mid-transit: "Is it lost?"
* Day of delivery: "Will I miss it?"

Quick input needed:
1. What's your most common customer complaint about tracking?
2. Do you offer expedited shipping options?

I'll map these anxiety points to specific microcopy interventions.

Type your answers, then "continue" to proceed.

##PHASE 3: INFORMATION ARCHITECTURE
Let's structure what information appears at each tracking stage for maximum reassurance.

Your tracking page will follow this psychological framework:
* Immediate confirmation (reduces post-purchase anxiety)
* Proactive timeline (sets realistic expectations)
* Visual progress indicators (provides control perception)
* Contextual help (prevents support tickets)

Key sections to include:
1. Current Status (big, bold, impossible to miss)
2. Expected Timeline (with buffer built in)
3. What Happens Next (reduces uncertainty)
4. Common Questions (preemptive support)
5. Help Options (clear escalation path)

Ready to write the actual copy? Type "continue".

##PHASE 4: MICROCOPY CREATION
Here's your complete tracking page copy, optimized for clarity and reassurance:

**Current Status Section:**
"Your order is [STATUS]"
- Order Confirmed: "We've got it! Your order is being prepared with care"
- Processing: "Almost ready! We're picking and packing your items"
- Shipped: "On the way! Your package is traveling to you"
- Out for Delivery: "Today's the day! Your order will arrive soon"
- Delivered: "Success! Your order has arrived"

**Timeline Section:**
"What to expect:"
- Order placed: ✓ Complete
- Processing: 1-2 business days
- Shipping: [X-Y] business days
- Estimated arrival: [Date]

*Note: Most orders arrive 1-2 days earlier than estimated*

**What's Happening Now:**
- Order Confirmed: "We're reviewing your order and will begin preparing it within 24 hours"
- Processing: "Our team is carefully selecting and packing your items"
- Shipped: "Your package is with [Carrier] and moving through their network"
- In Transit: "Your order is [X] stops away from you"

**Something Look Wrong?**
"Tracking hasn't updated in 48 hours? Package showing delivered but not there? 
Don't worry - we're here to help. [Contact Support] and we'll investigate immediately."

**Quick Help:**
- Change delivery address: [Link]
- Add delivery instructions: [Link]
- Track on carrier site: [Link]
- Contact us: [Link] (we typically respond in 2 hours)

Type "continue" for error handling scenarios.

##PHASE 5: ERROR HANDLING EXCELLENCE
Let's create copy for when things don't go as planned - turning potential frustration into trust:

**Delayed Shipment:**
"Your order is taking a bit longer than usual. This sometimes happens during busy periods or weather events. Expected arrival has been updated to [Date]. We're monitoring closely and will email you if anything changes."

**Lost Package Investigation:**
"We're looking into this right away. While we investigate (usually takes 24-48 hours), here's what's happening:
- We've contacted the carrier
- We're checking all delivery points
- We'll email you within 24 hours with an update
- If we can't locate it, we'll send a replacement immediately"

**Delivered But Not Received:**
"Let's find your package. Often it's:
- With a neighbor or building manager
- In a secure location (check garage/porch/mailroom)
- Delivered to a similar address nearby

Still can't find it? [Start Missing Package Report] - we'll resolve this within 48 hours."

**Implementation Checklist:**

- [ ] Create visual progress bar for tracking page
- [ ] Test all help links
- [ ] Monitor support ticket reduction
