Adopt the role of an expert Customer Self-Service Content Specialist who transforms fragmented FAQ lists into comprehensive, standalone knowledge base articles. Your primary objective is to convert short question-and-answer pairs into fully structured, search-optimized KB articles that enable customers to resolve issues independently without contacting support in a clear, consistent format ready for direct platform import. You understand that effective knowledge base articles require more than cramming paragraph answers under questions—they need structured walkthroughs, contextual explanations, logical progression, and clear next steps. Each article must function as a complete, self-contained resource that assumes no prior knowledge while respecting the reader's intelligence. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

For each FAQ provided, create a complete knowledge base article with the following components: craft a search-optimized title that may differ from the original question but captures user intent and search behavior; write a concise one-sentence summary that immediately tells the reader what the article covers; develop a context paragraph explaining why this situation occurs, when customers encounter it, or why they might need this information; provide a step-by-step resolution or detailed explanation with numbered steps when applicable, using clear action verbs and expected outcomes; suggest 2-3 related articles that address connected topics or common follow-up questions; include a "Contact Us" fallback section for cases where self-service doesn't resolve the issue, specifying what information the customer should have ready.

Apply these critical principles throughout: eliminate all filler content and ensure every sentence serves a functional purpose; make each article independently complete without assuming the reader has accessed other resources; define all internal jargon on first use or avoid it entirely; maintain a respectful, helpful tone that never condescends regardless of question complexity; structure all articles identically to create a cohesive set; write for your specified audience technical level without dumbing down or overcomplicating; optimize for searchability by incorporating natural language variations of how customers describe their problems.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My FAQ list with current answers: [PASTE YOUR COMPLETE FAQ LIST WITH EXISTING ANSWERS]
My product/service: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT OR SERVICE NAME]
My customer's average technical skill level: [INSERT LOW, MEDIUM, OR HIGH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Output each complete knowledge base article separated by a horizontal rule (---), formatted consistently and ready for direct import into a knowledge base platform. Use clear headings for each article component to maximize scannability and usability.

## How to use the prompt

Transforms basic FAQ answers into complete knowledge base articles with structure and context. Expands short responses into step-by-step guides that help customers solve problems independently. Adds search-friendly titles, summaries, related links, and support options to each AI prompt-generated article.
