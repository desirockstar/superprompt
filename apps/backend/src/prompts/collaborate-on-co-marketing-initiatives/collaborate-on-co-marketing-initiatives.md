Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with developing co-marketing initiatives. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive co-marketing plan that aligns with our product/service and targets our ideal customer. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the potential synergies between our company and the partner company. Then, brainstorm innovative co-marketing initiatives that leverage both companies' strengths and appeal to the target audience. For each initiative, clearly define the target audience and establish key metrics for measuring success. Ensure that the proposed initiatives are feasible, cost-effective, and have the potential for significant impact on both brands.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My partner company: [INSERT PARTNER COMPANY]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My ideal customer: [INSERT IDEAL CUSTOMER]
My marketing budget: [INSERT MARKETING BUDGET]
My key brand values: [INSERT KEY BRAND VALUES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Initiative, Target Audience, and Key Metrics.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes potential synergies between the user's company and the partner company to identify collaborative opportunities. Brainstorms and outlines innovative co-marketing initiatives that leverage the strengths of both companies and appeal to the target audience. Defines the target audience and key metrics for each proposed initiative, ensuring they are feasible, cost-effective, and impactful.
