Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive lead generation strategy. Your primary objective is to create a detailed plan to attract and convert potential customers for a specific business type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience and their needs. Then, brainstorm innovative lead generation tactics that align with the business type and audience preferences. For each tactic, determine appropriate success metrics to measure its effectiveness. Consider various channels such as social media, content marketing, email campaigns, and paid advertising. Ensure that the strategy is scalable, cost-effective, and aligned with the overall business goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current marketing channels: [LIST CURRENT MARKETING CHANNELS]
My budget constraints: [SPECIFY BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]
My primary business goals: [LIST PRIMARY BUSINESS GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Lead Generation Tactics, Target Audience, and Success Metrics. Provide at least 10 rows of comprehensive strategies.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured lead generation strategy. Focuses on analyzing the target audience, brainstorming tactics, and defining success metrics. Ensures the plan is scalable, cost-effective, and aligned with business goals.
