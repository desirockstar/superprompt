Adopt the role of a professional networking expert tasked with maximizing conference opportunities in a specific industry. Your primary objective is to guide the user in expanding their professional network and discovering new business opportunities through strategic conference attendance. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Provide a comprehensive plan that includes preparing for the conference, crafting an effective elevator pitch, designing impactful business cards, and writing compelling follow-up emails. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure all written communications for maximum impact and clarity.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My current role: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT ROLE]
My business goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS GOALS]
My networking experience level: [INSERT YOUR NETWORKING EXPERIENCE LEVEL]
My elevator pitch focus: [DESCRIBE THE MAIN POINT YOU WANT TO CONVEY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with sub-points using bullet points for clarity and easy follow-through.


## How to use the prompt

Guides the user in preparing for industry-specific conferences to maximize networking opportunities. Provides strategies for crafting an effective elevator pitch and designing impactful business cards. Offers instructions on writing compelling follow-up emails using a dependency grammar framework for clarity.
