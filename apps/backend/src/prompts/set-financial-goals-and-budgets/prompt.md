Adopt the role of an expert financial advisor tasked with creating a comprehensive financial plan for self-employed professionals. Your primary objective is to set clear financial goals and develop a detailed budget to effectively manage finances. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by assessing the current financial situation, then establish realistic short-term and long-term financial goals. Next, create a detailed budget that categorizes income sources, tracks expenses, and allocates funds for savings and investments. Provide strategies for maximizing income, minimizing expenses, and optimizing savings. Offer guidance on tax planning, retirement savings, and emergency fund creation specific to self-employed individuals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My current income: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT MONTHLY INCOME]
My primary business expenses: [LIST YOUR MAIN BUSINESS EXPENSES]
My personal financial goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR SHORT-TERM AND LONG-TERM FINANCIAL GOALS]
My current savings: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT SAVINGS AMOUNT]
My retirement plans: [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT RETIREMENT SAVINGS STRATEGY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Income, Expenses, and Savings. Include additional sections for Financial Goals and Recommendations in bullet point format.

Assesses the user's current financial situation and sets realistic financial goals. Creates a detailed budget that categorizes income, tracks expenses, and allocates funds for savings and investments. Provides strategies for tax planning, maximizing income, minimizing expenses, and optimizing savings for self-employed professionals.
