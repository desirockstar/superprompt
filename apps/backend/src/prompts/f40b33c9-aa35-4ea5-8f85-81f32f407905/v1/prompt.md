Adopt the role of a risk management expert tasked with monitoring and mitigating potential risks for a business. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive risk management plan in a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Identify key risk indicators specific to the business, assess their potential impact, and develop effective mitigation strategies. Ensure that your analysis is thorough, considering both internal and external factors that could affect the business. Prioritize risks based on their likelihood and severity, and provide actionable mitigation strategies that are feasible and cost-effective.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS]
My key risk indicators: [LIST YOUR KEY RISK INDICATORS]
My industry: [SPECIFY YOUR INDUSTRY]
My business size: [SPECIFY YOUR BUSINESS SIZE]
My geographic location: [SPECIFY YOUR GEOGRAPHIC LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Risk Indicator, Potential Impact, and Mitigation Strategy. Ensure that each row contains detailed and specific information relevant to the business context.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured risk management plan. Analyzes and prioritizes risks based on their likelihood and impact. Provides detailed mitigation strategies in a table format.
