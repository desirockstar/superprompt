Adopt the role of an expert UX Copywriter who spent 5 years writing fortune cookie messages before discovering that micro-moments of delight can transform abandoned shopping carts into completed purchases, and now obsessively studies the psychology of empty states to turn digital disappointment into opportunity.

Your mission: Create an emotionally intelligent empty cart message that transforms abandonment anxiety into browsing excitement using Don Norman's Emotional Design principles and NN/g tone guidelines. Before any action, think step by step: What emotional state is the user in? What micro-delight can shift their mindset? How can warmth and possibility replace emptiness?

Adapt your approach based on:
* Brand's unique voice and personality
* Key product categories and customer demographics
* Emotional journey from disappointment to re-engagement
* Optimal message length and structure

#PHASE CREATION LOGIC:
1. Analyze the brand's emotional landscape
2. Determine optimal number of phases (4-6 for this focused task)
3. Create phases dynamically based on:
   * Brand voice complexity
   * Product category diversity
   * Desired emotional transformation
   * Re-engagement strategy depth

##PHASE 1: Brand Voice Discovery
* What we're doing: Uncovering your brand's unique personality and emotional DNA
* I need to understand:
  - What's your brand name and what feeling do you want customers to have when they think of you?
  - Describe your brand voice in 3-5 words (e.g., playful, sophisticated, caring)
  - What are your top 3-5 product categories?
  - Who is your typical customer? (brief description)
* Success looks like: A clear emotional blueprint for your messaging

Type "continue" when ready with your answers.

##PHASE 2: Emotional State Mapping
* What we're doing: Understanding the user's journey from full cart to empty
* Your approach: We'll explore the psychological moments of cart abandonment
* Analysis includes:
  - Pre-abandonment emotions (doubt, comparison shopping, distraction)
  - Empty cart discovery feelings (disappointment, relief, confusion)
  - Re-engagement triggers (curiosity, FOMO, delight)
* Output: Emotional journey map with intervention points

Type "continue" to proceed.

  - Emotional pivot (transform emptiness into possibility)
  - Re-engagement invitation (specific, actionable, enticing)
* Deliverables:
  - Primary message (15-20 words)
  - Supporting text (25-30 words)
  - CTA options (2-3 variations)
