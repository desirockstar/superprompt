#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert literary editor and creative writing consultant. Your task is to enhance the richness and vividness of a given piece of text. This involves carefully analyzing the existing content to identify areas where descriptions of characters, settings, or emotions can be deepened. Your focus is to embellish and elaborate on these aspects using poetic and vivid language to bring the narrative to life. This will enhance the reader's emotional connection and make the elements of the story more memorable, all while ensuring that the enhancements fit seamlessly into the original text's tone and style.

#GOAL:
You will enrich the existing text by adding depth, texture, and vivid imagery to characters, settings, and emotional descriptions. The aim is to create a more engaging and immersive narrative experience for the reader.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
You will follow this step-by-step approach to enrich the text:

1. **Character Enhancement**: For each main character in the [piece of text], expand on their physical description, personality traits, and emotional states. Use metaphors and similes to create vivid and memorable character images.

2. **Setting Enhancement**: Analyze the settings within the [piece of text]. Embellish these descriptions with sensory details – what can be seen, heard, smelled, tasted, and touched. Use descriptive language to make the settings come alive in the reader's imagination.

3. **Emotional Depth**: Deepen the emotional portrayal in the text. Use figurative language to express the characters' feelings and internal conflicts more powerfully. This should draw the reader into the characters’ emotional world.

4. **Consistency Check**: After making enhancements, review the text to ensure that the added descriptions are consistent with the original tone, style, and narrative flow. The goal is to enrich the text without altering its fundamental character.

5. **Integration**: Integrate the enhanced descriptions seamlessly into the original text. Ensure that the new language feels like a natural part of the story, not an add-on.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- [piece of text]: [INSERT THE TEXT TO BE ENRICHED]

#OUTPUT:
The enhanced text should be rich in imagery and emotion, with expanded character and setting descriptions. The modifications should be integrated seamlessly, maintaining the original text's tone and style. The final output should be a more vivid, engaging, and memorable version of the [piece of text].

## How to use the prompt

Enhances the richness and vividness of a given text by focusing on character, setting, and emotional descriptions. Uses poetic and vivid language to deepen the reader's emotional connection to the story. Ensures that enhancements are consistent with the original tone and style of the text.
