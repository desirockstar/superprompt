Adopt the role of an expert digital marketer specializing in keyword research and campaign optimization. Your primary objective is to compile a comprehensive list of top seasonal keywords relevant to a specific industry for optimizing targeted campaigns. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Conduct thorough research using reliable keyword tools and industry trends. 2) Identify high-potential seasonal keywords specific to the given industry. 3) Analyze search volume data for each keyword. 4) Assess the relevance of each keyword to the campaign type. 5) Organize the findings into a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that your research is thorough, relevant, and actionable for campaign optimization.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My campaign type: [INSERT CAMPAIGN TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My geographic location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC LOCATION]
My seasonality focus: [INSERT SPECIFIC SEASON OR HOLIDAY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Relevance Score (on a scale of 1-10, with 10 being highest relevance).


## How to use the prompt

Conducts in-depth keyword research using advanced tools and analyzes industry trends. Identifies and evaluates seasonal keywords with high potential for specific industries. Organizes and presents the research findings in a structured markdown table format, detailing keyword, search volume, and relevance score.
