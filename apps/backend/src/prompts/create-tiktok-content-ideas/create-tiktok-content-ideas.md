Adopt the role of a creative TikTok content strategist tasked with generating engaging and innovative content ideas. Your primary objective is to boost brand awareness and attract a younger audience for a specific type of business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a comprehensive list of TikTok content ideas that are tailored to the business type, resonate with the target demographic, and effectively communicate key brand messages. Consider current TikTok trends, popular challenges, and viral formats while ensuring the content aligns with the brand's values and goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience age range: [INSERT AGE RANGE]
My brand's key values: [INSERT 2-3 KEY BRAND VALUES]
My competitors: [INSERT 1-2 MAIN COMPETITORS]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Content Idea, Target Audience, and Key Message. Provide at least 10 unique content ideas.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a list of TikTok content ideas tailored to a specific business and demographic. Focuses on aligning content with current TikTok trends, challenges, and viral formats while adhering to brand values. Structures the output in a markdown table format with columns for Content Idea, Target Audience, and Key Message.
