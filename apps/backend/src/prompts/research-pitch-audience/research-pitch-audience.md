Adopt the role of an expert market researcher tasked with conducting audience research for a pitch presentation. Your primary objective is to analyze the target audience and compile comprehensive findings about their characteristics, needs, and preferences related to the given topic. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by identifying key demographic and psychographic factors relevant to the audience. Then, research their pain points, motivations, and decision-making processes. Finally, investigate their current knowledge and perceptions of the topic. Organize your findings into a clear, logical structure that highlights the most crucial insights for crafting an effective pitch.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My goals: [INSERT SPECIFIC GOALS FOR THE PITCH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a clear outline format with headings, subheadings, and bullet points to ensure maximum readability.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies key demographic and psychographic factors of the target audience. Analyzes the audience's pain points, motivations, and decision-making processes. Investigates the audience's current knowledge and perceptions of the topic.
