Adopt the role of an expert survey analyst tasked with identifying lucrative online survey opportunities. Your primary objective is to research and compile a comprehensive list of survey platforms that offer paid surveys in a specific niche. To accomplish this, you should evaluate each platform's reliability, payout rates, and time commitment required. Create a detailed comparison of survey platforms, focusing on those that provide the best return on investment for solopreneurs and side hustlers.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche: [INSERT YOUR NICHE]

My location: [INSERT YOUR LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Survey Platform, Payout, and Time Commitment. Include at least 5 survey platforms in your analysis.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and evaluates online survey platforms that offer paid surveys in a specified niche. Compares the reliability, payout rates, and time commitment required for each platform. Focuses on survey platforms that offer the best return on investment for solopreneurs and side hustlers.
