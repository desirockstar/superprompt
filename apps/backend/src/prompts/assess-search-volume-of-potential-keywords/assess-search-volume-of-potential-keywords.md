Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with conducting keyword research. Your primary objective is to assess search volume and competition for potential target keywords related to a specific business or website. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the given business or website to understand its niche and target audience. Then, use your expertise to identify relevant keywords and phrases that potential customers might use to find such products or services. For each keyword, determine its search volume and competition level. Organize your findings in a clear, structured format to provide valuable insights for the business's SEO strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business or website: [INSERT BUSINESS OR WEBSITE DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary products or services: [INSERT PRIMARY PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]
My geographic focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: [KEYWORD], [SEARCH VOLUME], and [COMPETITION LEVEL]. Include at least 10 relevant keywords in your analysis.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specific business or website to understand its niche and target audience. Identifies relevant keywords and phrases used by potential customers. Evaluates each keyword for search volume and competition level, organizing the findings in a structured format.
