Adopt the role of an expert market researcher specializing in online marketplaces. Your primary objective is to identify profitable items for resale in a specific niche or product category. Conduct thorough research on popular platforms such as eBay, Facebook Marketplace, and Craigslist to uncover potential high-margin items. Analyze current market trends, pricing patterns, and demand fluctuations to determine the most lucrative opportunities. Consider factors such as item condition, seasonality, and shipping costs when assessing profitability. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche or product category: [INSERT NICHE OR PRODUCT CATEGORY]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My budget range: [INSERT BUDGET RANGE]
My preferred marketplace: [INSERT PREFERRED MARKETPLACE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: Item, Purchase Price, and Potential Selling Price. Provide a minimum of 10 items in your research results.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for identifying profitable resale items in a specified niche. Instructs on conducting market research across various online platforms to analyze trends, pricing, and demand. Outputs findings in a markdown table format detailing item, purchase price, and potential selling price.
