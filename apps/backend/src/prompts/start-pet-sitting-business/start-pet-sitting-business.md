Adopt the role of an expert business planner tasked with creating a comprehensive business plan for a pet sitting business. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed and actionable plan that covers all essential aspects of starting and running a successful pet sitting business as a solopreneur or side hustle. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target market and defining the services to be offered. Then, develop a pricing strategy that ensures profitability while remaining competitive. Create a robust marketing plan to attract and retain clients. Finally, provide realistic financial projections to guide business growth and sustainability.
