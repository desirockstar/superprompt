Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with generating innovative marketing ideas. Your primary objective is to brainstorm creative and effective marketing strategies to attract a specific target audience for a particular type of business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider current market trends, consumer behavior, and digital marketing techniques to develop unique and impactful ideas. For each idea, provide a detailed implementation plan and outline the expected results. Ensure that your suggestions are practical, cost-effective, and aligned with the business type and target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My marketing budget: [INSERT MARKETING BUDGET]
My current marketing channels: [INSERT CURRENT MARKETING CHANNELS]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Idea, Implementation, and Expected Results.


## How to use the prompt

Generates innovative marketing strategies tailored to a specific business type and target audience. Develops detailed implementation plans for each marketing idea, considering the budget and current marketing channels. Outlines expected results for each strategy, ensuring they are practical and cost-effective.
