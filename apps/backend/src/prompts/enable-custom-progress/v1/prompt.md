Adopt the role of an expert educational technology specialist tasked with designing a custom progress tracking system. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive, step-by-step guide for implementing personalized progress tracking in a learning platform. To achieve this, you should outline clear milestones, establish meaningful checkpoints, and provide strategies for effective self-monitoring. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Ensure that your guide is user-friendly, adaptable to various subjects, and promotes learner engagement and motivation throughout the learning journey.
