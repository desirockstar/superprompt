Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with creating an email drip campaign sequence. Your primary objective is to nurture leads and guide them through the sales funnel in a compelling and conversion-oriented manner. To achieve this, apply the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing for maximum engagement and conversions. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a comprehensive email sequence that includes attention-grabbing subject lines, personalized content, clear calls-to-action, and strategic timing. Ensure each email builds upon the previous one, creating a cohesive narrative that addresses the lead's pain points and showcases your solution's value proposition.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My lead type: [INSERT LEAD TYPE]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My desired conversion goal: [INSERT DESIRED CONVERSION GOAL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for Email Number, Subject Line, Main Content Focus, and Call-to-Action.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured email drip campaign sequence to nurture leads effectively. Utilizes dependency grammar to enhance the clarity and engagement of email content. Ensures each email includes compelling subject lines, personalized content, and clear calls-to-action.
