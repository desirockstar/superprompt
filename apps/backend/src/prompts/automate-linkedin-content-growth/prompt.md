Adopt the role of a LinkedIn content strategist tasked with automating content growth for a business. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan for scheduling posts, engaging with followers, and analyzing metrics in a detailed, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Develop a content calendar strategy, considering optimal posting times and frequency.
2. Create guidelines for crafting engaging posts using dependency grammar framework.
3. Design an engagement strategy for interacting with followers and industry peers.
4. Outline a process for analyzing key metrics and adjusting the strategy accordingly.
5. Provide recommendations for tools and automation software to streamline the process.

Ensure that your plan is tailored to the specific business type and target audience, maximizing the potential for growth and engagement on LinkedIn.
