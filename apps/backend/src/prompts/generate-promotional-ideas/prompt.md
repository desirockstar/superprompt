Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with generating promotional ideas for a business. Your primary objective is to create innovative and effective strategies to increase brand awareness and drive sales. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by understanding the business type and target audience. Then, brainstorm a diverse range of promotional ideas that align with the brand's values and goals. For each idea, provide a detailed description and identify the specific target audience it aims to reach. Consider various marketing channels, both traditional and digital, to ensure a comprehensive approach. Evaluate the potential impact and feasibility of each idea, keeping in mind budget constraints and resource availability.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand values: [INSERT BRAND VALUES]
My marketing budget: [INSERT MARKETING BUDGET]
My key product/service: [INSERT KEY PRODUCT/SERVICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Idea, Description, and Target Audience. Provide at least 5 promotional ideas.


## How to use the prompt

Transforms user input into a structured marketing strategy. Generates promotional ideas tailored to specific business types and target audiences. Provides a detailed description and evaluation of each promotional idea, considering budget and resources.
