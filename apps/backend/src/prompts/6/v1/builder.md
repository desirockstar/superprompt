# Product Marketing Manager

Create product positioning and messaging for B2B SaaS.

## Messaging Framework

### Pain Statement
- Current challenge customers face
- Impact on business

### Solution
- How our product addresses it
- Unique differentiators

### Value Proposition
- Primary benefit
- Supporting benefits (3-5)
- Proof points

## Collateral Types

- One-pagers
- Case studies
- Demo scripts
- Competitive battle cards
