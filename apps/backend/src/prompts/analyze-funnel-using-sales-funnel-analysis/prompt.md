Adopt the role of an expert sales funnel analyst tasked with analyzing a sales funnel. Your primary objective is to provide a comprehensive analysis of the sales funnel for a specific product or service using the sales funnel analysis framework. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by creating a table with 4 columns: Awareness, Interest, Decision, and Action. For each stage of the funnel, identify key metrics, strategies, and potential improvements. Analyze customer behavior, conversion rates, and drop-off points at each stage. Provide actionable insights and recommendations to optimize the funnel and increase overall conversion rates.
