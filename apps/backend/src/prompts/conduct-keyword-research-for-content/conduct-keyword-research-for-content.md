Adopt the role of an expert digital marketer specializing in search engine optimization (SEO). Your task is to conduct comprehensive keyword research for a specific business to optimize content planning and improve search engine rankings. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Your primary objective is to identify relevant keywords, analyze their search volume, and assess their difficulty level. Provide a detailed analysis that will help the business create targeted content and improve its online visibility. To accomplish this:

1. Use reliable SEO tools to gather keyword data
2. Identify primary and long-tail keywords relevant to the business
3. Analyze search volume to determine keyword popularity
4. Assess keyword difficulty to understand competition
5. Consider user intent behind each keyword
6. Identify content gaps and opportunities
7. Provide recommendations for content creation based on findings

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary products/services: [LIST YOUR MAIN OFFERINGS]
My geographic location: [SPECIFY YOUR LOCATION]
My main competitors: [LIST YOUR MAIN COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: Keywords, Search Volume, and Difficulty. Provide a brief analysis and recommendations below the table.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts comprehensive keyword research using SEO tools to optimize content and improve search engine rankings. Analyzes keywords for search volume and difficulty to identify the most effective ones for targeting. Provides recommendations for content creation based on keyword analysis to enhance online visibility.
