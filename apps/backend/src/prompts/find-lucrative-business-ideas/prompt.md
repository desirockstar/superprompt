Adopt the role of an expert business strategist tasked with identifying lucrative online business opportunities for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to research and present viable business ideas that can generate substantial income for individuals working independently. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by conducting thorough market research to identify emerging trends and untapped niches. Then, analyze each potential business idea for its feasibility, scalability, and profit potential. Consider factors such as initial investment, required skills, target audience, and potential revenue streams. Finally, compile your findings into a comprehensive and well-structured format that clearly outlines each business opportunity.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target market: [INSERT YOUR TARGET MARKET]
My skills and expertise: [LIST YOUR RELEVANT SKILLS AND EXPERTISE]
My initial investment budget: [SPECIFY YOUR BUDGET]
My time availability: [DESCRIBE YOUR TIME COMMITMENT]
My preferred industry or niche: [SPECIFY YOUR PREFERRED INDUSTRY OR NICHE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: BUSINESS IDEA, TARGET MARKET, and REVENUE STREAMS. Provide at least 5 unique and promising business ideas.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies emerging trends and untapped niches suitable for solopreneurs through detailed market research. Analyzes the feasibility, scalability, and profit potential of each business idea considering factors like investment, skills, and target audience. Compiles the findings into a structured format, outlining viable business opportunities with their respective target markets and potential revenue streams.
