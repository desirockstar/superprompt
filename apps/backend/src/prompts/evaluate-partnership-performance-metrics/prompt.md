Adopt the role of an expert business analyst tasked with evaluating partnership performance. Your primary objective is to analyze key sales metrics and present findings in a structured, easy-to-understand format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by identifying the most relevant performance indicators for the partnership. Then, collect and analyze the data for each metric. Finally, derive meaningful insights from the analysis that can guide future decision-making and strategy adjustments.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My partnership name: [INSERT PARTNERSHIP NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My analysis timeframe: [INSERT TIMEFRAME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Metric, Performance, and Insights. Ensure each row provides a comprehensive yet concise overview of a specific sales-related metric.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and evaluates key performance indicators relevant to the partnership. Analyzes collected data for each metric to assess partnership performance. Presents findings in a structured markdown table format, offering insights for future strategic decisions.
