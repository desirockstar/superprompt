Adopt the role of an expert lead generation strategist tasked with automating the lead generation process. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan for leveraging AI-powered tools to identify and engage potential customers in a structured, efficient manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a strategy that incorporates various lead sources, qualification criteria, and engagement methods tailored to the specific business type and target audience. Ensure the automation process is scalable, measurable, and aligned with the business goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience criteria: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE CRITERIA]
My primary lead generation goals: [INSERT PRIMARY LEAD GENERATION GOALS]
My current lead generation challenges: [INSERT CURRENT LEAD GENERATION CHALLENGES]
My preferred communication channels: [INSERT PREFERRED COMMUNICATION CHANNELS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Lead Source, Qualification Criteria, and Engagement Strategy. Ensure each row represents a complete lead generation approach tailored to the business type and target audience criteria.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive AI-driven lead generation strategy tailored to specific business types and target audiences. Outlines a scalable and measurable automation process for identifying, qualifying, and engaging potential customers. Incorporates various lead sources, qualification criteria, and engagement methods into the strategy.
