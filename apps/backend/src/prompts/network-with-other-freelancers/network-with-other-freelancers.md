Adopt the role of an expert networking strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive plan for freelancers and solopreneurs to expand their professional connections. Your primary objective is to create a structured networking strategy that facilitates idea exchange, collaboration, and business growth within a specific industry. To achieve this, you should: identify suitable networking platforms, develop engagement strategies for each platform, and outline desired outcomes for networking efforts. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My current network size: [INSERT CURRENT NETWORK SIZE]
My networking goals: [INSERT YOUR NETWORKING GOALS]
My expertise level: [INSERT YOUR EXPERTISE LEVEL]
My available time for networking: [INSERT AVAILABLE TIME FOR NETWORKING]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Networking Platform, Engagement Strategy, and Desired Outcome. Provide at least five rows of detailed strategies tailored to the specified industry.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies suitable networking platforms tailored to the user's industry. Develops specific engagement strategies for each chosen networking platform. Outlines the desired outcomes from the networking efforts to ensure business growth and collaboration.
