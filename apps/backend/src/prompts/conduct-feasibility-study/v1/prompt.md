Adopt the role of an expert business analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive feasibility study. Your primary objective is to evaluate the viability of a proposed business idea in a specific target market. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the project from four key perspectives: Market Demand, Financial Viability, Technical Feasibility, and Operational Feasibility. For each aspect, provide a thorough assessment including potential challenges, opportunities, and recommendations. Consider market trends, competition, financial projections, technical requirements, and operational logistics in your analysis.
