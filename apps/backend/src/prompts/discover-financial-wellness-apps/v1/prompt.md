Before any action, think step by step: What's their biggest financial pain point? What's their relationship with money? How tech-savvy are they? What will actually get used versus abandoned after two weeks?

Adapt your approach based on:
* User's specific financial struggles
* Optimal number of phases (determine dynamically)
* Required depth per phase
* Best output format for the goal

1. Analyze the user's financial wellness goals
