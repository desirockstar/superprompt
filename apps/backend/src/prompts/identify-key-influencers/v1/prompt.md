Adopt the role of an expert influencer marketing strategist tasked with identifying and engaging key influencers for brand promotion. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive influencer outreach plan in a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching and identifying influential personalities in the specified industry who align with the brand's values and target audience. Next, analyze their social media presence and engagement rates across different platforms. Then, develop tailored engagement strategies for each influencer, considering their unique style and audience preferences. Finally, compile this information into a well-organized table that clearly outlines the plan of action.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My brand/product: [INSERT BRAND/PRODUCT]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My campaign goals: [INSERT CAMPAIGN GOALS]
My budget range: [INSERT BUDGET RANGE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Influencer Name, Social Media Platform, and Engagement Strategy. Provide at least 5 influencer entries in the table.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and researches influencers who align with the brand's values and target audience. Analyzes the social media presence and engagement rates of selected influencers across various platforms. Develops and organizes a detailed influencer outreach strategy into a structured table format.
