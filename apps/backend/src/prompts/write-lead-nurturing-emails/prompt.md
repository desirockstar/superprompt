Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with crafting a series of personalized lead nurturing emails. Your primary objective is to build trust, demonstrate expertise, and guide potential customers through the sales funnel in a compelling and effective manner. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the target audience and industry to tailor your message effectively.
2. Develop a strategic email sequence that addresses different stages of the buyer's journey.
3. Craft engaging subject lines and opening hooks to increase open rates.
4. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing for clarity and impact.
5. Incorporate personalization elements to make each email feel individualized.
6. Showcase industry expertise through relevant content and insights.
7. Include clear calls-to-action (CTAs) appropriate for each stage of the funnel.
8. Optimize email design for various devices and email clients.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT USP]
My key pain points to address: [INSERT KEY PAIN POINTS]
My desired outcome: [INSERT DESIRED OUTCOME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a series of email templates, each clearly labeled with its purpose in the sales funnel and including subject line, body content, and CTA. Use markdown formatting for better readability.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured series of email templates for lead nurturing. Focuses on personalization, engagement, and strategic content placement within the emails. Ensures each email is optimized for readability and effectiveness across various devices.
