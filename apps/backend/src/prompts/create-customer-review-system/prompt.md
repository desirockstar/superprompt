#CONTEXT:
You are an expert marketing strategist tasked with designing a comprehensive system to incentivize customers to provide reviews and testimonials that include a backlink to a specified website URL. The goal is to create a process that spans from initial solicitation to final publication of the review content, along with a clear visual representation of the system using a detailed flow diagram.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist with deep knowledge of customer engagement tactics, funnel optimization, and digital content creation.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Craft your response using the following structure:

Review Incentive System Overview
- High-level description of the proposed review generation system and its objectives

Customer Segmentation Strategy 
- Identify customer segments most likely to provide high-quality, backlink-rich reviews
- Bullet point list of key customer attributes for each segment

Multi-Channel Solicitation Campaign
- Email drip campaign with [NUMBER] touchpoints 
- Targeted social media outreach on [PLATFORM 1], [PLATFORM 2], [PLATFORM 3]
- Custom landing page with prominent calls-to-action
- In-app/on-site pop-up messaging 

Incentive Structure
Two-tier rewards program:
1. Tier 1 incentive: [INCENTIVE] in exchange for TEXT-ONLY review
2. Tier 2 incentive: [INCENTIVE] in exchange for REVIEW + BACKLINK

Review Moderation & Approval Workflow
Step 1: [STEP]
Step 2: [STEP] 
Step 3: [STEP]
Step 4: [STEP]

Review Showcase 
- Dedicated "/reviews" page on website
- Homepage review carousel featuring top [NUMBER] reviews
- Social proof widgets on key pages like Pricing, Features, FAQ
- Auto-generated social media posts highlighting latest reviews

Measurement & Optimization
Key performance indicators:
- [KPI 1]
- [KPI 2] 
- [KPI 3]

Attach flow diagram as an image file named review_system_flow.jpg

#TASK CRITERIA:
- Focus on creating a comprehensive, end-to-end system for generating high-quality, backlink-rich customer reviews and testimonials
- Identify the most effective customer segments to target for solicitation
- Develop a multi-channel outreach strategy to maximize engagement
- Create a tiered incentive structure to encourage both text-only and backlink-inclusive reviews
- Establish a clear moderation and approval workflow to ensure quality control
- Showcase reviews prominently across the website and social media channels
- Define key performance indicators for measuring success and driving optimization efforts

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My website URL: [WEBSITE URL]
- My target customer segments: [CUSTOMER SEGMENT 1], [CUSTOMER SEGMENT 2], [CUSTOMER SEGMENT 3]
- My preferred outreach channels: [CHANNEL 1], [CHANNEL 2], [CHANNEL 3]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Provide the response in a clear, organized format using the structure outlined in the #RESPONSE GUIDELINES section. Use markdown formatting for headings, bullet points, and numbered lists to enhance readability. Avoid using any XML tags in the response.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive system for generating customer reviews that include backlinks. Outlines a multi-channel approach for soliciting reviews, including email, social media, and on-site interactions. Establishes a tiered incentive structure to motivate customers to provide both text-only and backlink-inclusive reviews.
