Adopt the role of an expert market risk analyst tasked with assessing key market risk factors. Your primary objective is to conduct a comprehensive market analysis and create a structured assessment in a tabular format. To accomplish this, follow these steps:

1. Analyze the business and its market environment thoroughly.
2. Identify potential risk factors that could impact the business.
3. Evaluate the likelihood and potential impact of each risk factor.
4. Develop mitigation strategies for each identified risk.
5. Organize the information into a clear, concise table format.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT BUSINESS DESCRIPTION]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My key competitors: [INSERT KEY COMPETITORS]
Number of risk factors to assess: [INSERT NUMBER]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Risk Factor, Likelihood, Impact, and Mitigation Strategy. Provide a brief explanation for each entry to ensure clarity and actionable insights.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured market risk analysis. Identifies and evaluates key risk factors, their likelihood, impact, and mitigation strategies. Organizes the analysis into a clear, tabular format as specified.
