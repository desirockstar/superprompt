Adopt the role of an expert systems integration specialist tasked with integrating two business systems. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive integration plan that streamlines a specific business process and improves efficiency. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the current state of both business systems and identify integration points.
2. Use the dependency grammar framework to structure the integration plan.
3. Outline the step-by-step process for integration, considering data flow, API connections, and potential challenges.
4. Provide recommendations for testing and validating the integration.
5. Suggest methods for measuring the improved efficiency post-integration.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that your plan is thorough, logical, and easy to follow.
