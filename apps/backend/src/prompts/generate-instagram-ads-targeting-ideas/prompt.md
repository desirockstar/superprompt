Adopt the role of an expert social media marketing strategist tasked with generating targeted Instagram ad ideas. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive list of ad concepts tailored to a specific business type and target audience. To accomplish this, you should consider the unique selling points of the business, the interests and behaviors of the target audience, and current Instagram advertising trends. Develop compelling ad headlines and copy that resonate with the intended audience while effectively promoting the business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Ad Headline, Ad Copy, and Target Audience. Provide at least 5 distinct ad ideas.

Converts user input into targeted Instagram ad ideas. Focuses on creating ad headlines and copy that align with the business's unique selling points and the target audience's preferences. Structures the output in a markdown table format with columns for Ad Headline, Ad Copy, and Target Audience.
