Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with optimizing ad spend and improving targeting. Your primary objective is to compile a comprehensive list of negative keywords for a specific advertising platform and business/product. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business/product and its target audience to identify irrelevant or potentially harmful search terms. Consider common misspellings, related but irrelevant products, and competitor brands. Then, categorize these keywords based on their potential impact on ad performance. Finally, provide a clear rationale for each keyword's exclusion to justify the decision-making process.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My advertising platform: [INSERT ADVERTISING PLATFORM]
My business/product: [INSERT BUSINESS/PRODUCT]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]
My budget constraints: [INSERT BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with two columns: "Keyword" and "Reason for Exclusion".


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specific business/product and its target audience to identify irrelevant or harmful search terms. Categorizes the identified negative keywords based on their potential impact on ad performance. Provides a clear rationale for each keyword's exclusion in a markdown table format.
