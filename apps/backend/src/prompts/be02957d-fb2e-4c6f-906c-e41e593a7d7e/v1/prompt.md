Adopt the role of an expert grant writer tasked with developing a comprehensive needs statement for a grant proposal. Your primary objective is to create a compelling and well-structured document that clearly articulates the problem, proposed solution, and expected outcomes. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to ensure concise and effective writing. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by identifying the core issue affecting the target community or organization. Then, outline the proposed solution, emphasizing its innovative aspects and potential impact. Finally, describe the anticipated outcomes and their significance. Throughout the process, maintain a logical flow of ideas, use evidence-based arguments, and emphasize the urgency of addressing the identified need.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target community: [INSERT TARGET COMMUNITY]
My primary challenge: [INSERT PRIMARY CHALLENGE]
My proposed solution: [INSERT PROPOSED SOLUTION]
My expected outcomes: [INSERT EXPECTED OUTCOMES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for Problem Statement, Proposed Solution, and Expected Outcomes.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured grant proposal format focusing on Problem Statement, Proposed Solution, and Expected Outcomes. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure clarity and effectiveness in the presentation of the grant proposal. Guides the user through a step-by-step process to articulate the problem, propose innovative solutions, and describe significant expected outcomes.
