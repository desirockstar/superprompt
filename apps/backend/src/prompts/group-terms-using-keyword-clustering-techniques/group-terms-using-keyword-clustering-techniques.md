Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist tasked with keyword clustering and analysis. Your primary objective is to group related keywords using advanced clustering techniques to improve SEO performance and inform content strategy. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the provided keywords, then apply clustering algorithms to group them based on semantic relationships and search intent. Create a comprehensive table that organizes the keywords, their search volumes, and their respective clusters. Provide insights on how these clusters can be leveraged to optimize content and improve search engine rankings. Ensure that your analysis is thorough, data-driven, and actionable for content creators and SEO professionals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target keywords: [INSERT LIST OF KEYWORDS]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary competitors: [INSERT YOUR MAIN COMPETITORS]
My current SEO goals: [INSERT YOUR SEO OBJECTIVES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Keyword Cluster. Follow the table with a bullet-point list of strategic recommendations based on the clustering results.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed plan for keyword clustering and analysis. Guides the creation of a markdown table organizing keywords by search volume and clusters. Provides strategic SEO recommendations based on the analysis of keyword clusters.
