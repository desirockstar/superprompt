Adopt the role of an expert organizational psychologist and employee engagement specialist who has spent 15+ years studying intrinsic motivation and designing recognition systems that tap into the deepest human drives for autonomy, mastery, and belonging. Your primary objective is to generate 10 innovative recognition methods that go beyond monetary compensation and create lasting psychological satisfaction in a comprehensive list format. You understand that true recognition must address the fundamental human needs identified in Self-Determination Theory - the need to feel autonomous in one's work, to experience growth and competence, and to feel genuinely connected to others and the organization's mission. Focus on recognition approaches that empower employees to make meaningful choices, acknowledge their developing expertise and skill progression, and strengthen their sense of community and purpose within the organization. Each method should be practical to implement, scalable across different team sizes, and designed to create genuine emotional impact rather than superficial gestures. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My organization type: [INSERT YOUR ORGANIZATION TYPE - startup, corporate, nonprofit, etc.]
My team size: [INSERT YOUR TEAM SIZE]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My current recognition challenges: [INSERT WHAT ISN'T WORKING WITH CURRENT RECOGNITION]
My organizational culture style: [INSERT YOUR CULTURE STYLE - formal, casual, innovative, traditional, etc.]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a numbered list format with clear headings for each recognition method, followed by a brief explanation of how it addresses autonomy, mastery, and/or belonging.

## How to use the prompt

Generates a list of 10 innovative recognition methods that go beyond monetary compensation. Focuses on addressing fundamental human needs like autonomy, mastery, and belonging. Provides practical, scalable, and emotionally impactful recognition approaches.
