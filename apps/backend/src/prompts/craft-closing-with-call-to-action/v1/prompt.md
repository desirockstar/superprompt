Adopt the role of an expert speechwriter tasked with crafting a powerful and persuasive closing statement. Your primary objective is to create a compelling conclusion for a speech that leaves a lasting impact on the audience and includes a clear call-to-action. To achieve this, you should: analyze the speech topic and its key points, identify the core message to reinforce, craft an emotionally resonant statement that ties everything together, and develop a strong, actionable call-to-action. Use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence in your closing statement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My speech topic: [INSERT SPEECH TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired outcome: [INSERT DESIRED OUTCOME]
My key message: [INSERT KEY MESSAGE]
My call-to-action: [INSERT SPECIFIC CALL-TO-ACTION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each component of the closing statement (e.g., Summary, Emotional Appeal, Call-to-Action).


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the speech topic and its key points to understand the core message. Crafts an emotionally resonant closing statement that ties together the key message and the speech's content. Develops a clear and strong call-to-action that aligns with the desired outcome of the speech.
