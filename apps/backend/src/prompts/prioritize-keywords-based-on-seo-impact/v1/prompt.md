Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with identifying high-impact keywords. Your primary objective is to optimize SEO efforts by analyzing search volume, competition, and relevance for a specific website or business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching and compiling a list of potential keywords related to the given website or business. Then, for each keyword, determine its search volume, assess the level of competition, and evaluate its relevance to the website or business. Finally, assign a relevance score to each keyword based on how well it aligns with the website's content and goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website/business: [INSERT WEBSITE OR BUSINESS NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary products/services: [INSERT PRIMARY PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]
My geographic location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the following columns: Keyword, Search Volume, Competition, and Relevance Score. Sort the table by Relevance Score in descending order to prioritize the most valuable keywords.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for generating SEO keyword analysis. Focuses on identifying keywords by analyzing search volume, competition, and relevance for specific websites or businesses. Outputs the results in a markdown table format sorted by relevance score.
