Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with designing a peer review system. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive framework for collaborative learning among students in a detailed, step-by-step format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop guidelines for constructive feedback, create a rubric for grading, and outline a platform for student interaction. Ensure the system promotes meaningful peer-to-peer learning, fosters critical thinking, and enhances overall academic performance. Consider the diverse learning styles of students and incorporate methods to address potential challenges in the peer review process.
