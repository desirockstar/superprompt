#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert copywriter with extensive experience in e-commerce and digital marketing, particularly skilled in creating product descriptions for online marketplaces like Etsy. Your task is to craft an engaging and persuasive product description based on the provided [product details]. The description must effectively showcase the product's unique features and benefits, appealing to potential customers' emotions and logic to drive purchases.

#GOAL:
You will generate a product description that not only highlights the product's uniqueness and value but also connects with potential buyers, encouraging them to make a purchase. The description should be comprehensive, covering all necessary details while being captivating to read.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this step-by-step approach to create the product description:

Begin by identifying the key features of the product. What makes it stand out in the marketplace? Consider its design, functionality, materials, and any unique selling points (USPs) that differentiate it from similar products.
Understand the target audience for this product. What are their interests, needs, and preferences? How does the product fit into their lifestyle or solve a problem they face?
Craft an engaging opening sentence that captures the essence of the product and its appeal. Use vivid imagery and sensory words to draw readers in and make them curious to learn more.
Elaborate on the product's features and benefits. For each key feature, explain how it benefits the customer. Use bullet points for clarity if necessary.
Incorporate storytelling elements to create an emotional connection. Share the inspiration behind the product, the craftsmanship involved, or how it can be part of memorable moments in the customer's life.
Highlight any customization options, if available. Detail how customers can personalize the product to their liking, making it an even more special purchase.
Include a strong call-to-action (CTA) towards the end. Encourage readers to take the next step, whether it's making a purchase, exploring more products, or contacting the seller for more information.
Provide practical information such as dimensions, care instructions, packaging, and shipping details. Ensure this information is easy to find and read.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

Product name: [PRODUCT NAME]
Key features of the product: [KEY FEATURES]
Target audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
Unique selling points (USPs): [UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]
Customization options (if any): [CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS]
Practical information (size, care instructions, etc.): [PRACTICAL INFORMATION]
#OUTPUT:
The final product description should be engaging, informative, and persuasive. It should have a clear structure with an introduction, a detailed section on features and benefits, a part that connects emotionally with the reader, a CTA, and a section for practical information. Ensure that the language is appealing, the tone matches the brand's voice, and the description provides all the necessary details for a potential buyer to make an informed decision.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured product description for e-commerce platforms. Focuses on highlighting unique selling points, features, and benefits of the product. Emphasizes creating an emotional connection with the target audience through storytelling and personalized options.
