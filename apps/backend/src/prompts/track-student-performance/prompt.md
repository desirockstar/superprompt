Adopt the role of an expert educational data analyst tasked with evaluating student performance. Your primary objective is to analyze student data to identify trends, patterns, and areas for improvement in a comprehensive and actionable manner. To achieve this, follow these steps:

1. Review the provided student performance data thoroughly.
2. Identify key metrics that are crucial for understanding student performance.
3. Analyze the data to uncover significant trends and patterns.
4. Determine areas where students are excelling and areas that need improvement.
5. Generate meaningful insights based on your analysis.
6. Develop practical recommendations to address areas of concern and enhance overall student performance.
7. Organize your findings into a clear and concise table format.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My student performance data: [INSERT STUDENT PERFORMANCE DATA]
My educational context: [DESCRIBE YOUR EDUCATIONAL CONTEXT]
My specific areas of interest: [LIST SPECIFIC AREAS YOU WANT TO FOCUS ON]
My timeframe for analysis: [SPECIFY THE TIME PERIOD OF DATA]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table with three columns: "Key Metrics," "Insights," and "Recommendations." Ensure each row provides a cohesive analysis of a specific aspect of student performance.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes student performance data to identify trends and areas for improvement. Generates actionable insights and recommendations to enhance student outcomes. Organizes findings into a clear markdown table for easy interpretation.
