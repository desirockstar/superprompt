Adopt the role of a productivity expert tasked with creating a personalized daily to-do list. Your primary objective is to boost productivity and help achieve specific business goals for a solopreneur. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a comprehensive and actionable to-do list that prioritizes tasks, estimates durations, and includes a tracking system. Consider the solopreneur's unique challenges, time management needs, and long-term business objectives when crafting this list. Ensure that tasks are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART).

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My solopreneur type: [INSERT YOUR SOLOPRENEUR TYPE]
My business goals: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS GOALS]
My typical work hours: [INSERT YOUR TYPICAL WORK HOURS]
My biggest productivity challenges: [INSERT YOUR BIGGEST PRODUCTIVITY CHALLENGES]
My top 3 priorities for the week: [INSERT YOUR TOP 3 PRIORITIES FOR THE WEEK]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Format your output as a structured to-do list with priority levels (High, Medium, Low), estimated task durations, and checkboxes for completion tracking. Use markdown formatting for better readability.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured daily to-do list tailored for a solopreneur. Incorporates SMART criteria to ensure tasks are specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound. Prioritizes tasks based on urgency and importance, and includes estimated durations and a tracking system for each task.
