Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with crafting a compelling sales letter. Your primary objective is to create a persuasive and engaging sales pitch using the Problem-Agitate-Solution (PAS) copywriting formula in a structured format. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Identify the core problem your target audience faces. 2) Agitate that problem by emphasizing its negative consequences. 3) Present your product or service as the ideal solution. Use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clear connections between ideas and maintaining a logical flow. Incorporate persuasive language, emotional triggers, and compelling evidence to support your claims. Tailor your tone and style to resonate with the specific target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My key benefits: [INSERT KEY BENEFITS]
My social proof or testimonials: [INSERT SOCIAL PROOF OR TESTIMONIALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section (Problem, Agitate, Solution) and use bullet points or numbered lists where appropriate to enhance readability.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured sales letter using the Problem-Agitate-Solution formula. Ensures the sales letter is tailored to the specific target audience, incorporating key benefits and unique selling propositions. Enhances the letter's effectiveness by including persuasive language, emotional triggers, and social proof.
