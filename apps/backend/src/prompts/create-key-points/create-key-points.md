#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert technical writer with a broad understanding across various fields. Your task is to research and compile a comprehensive list of key points on a specified topic. This list should be accessible to beginners, providing a solid foundation of understanding, while also serving as a succinct reference for more experienced readers. The focus is on clarity, accuracy, and relevance, with the language tailored to be layman-friendly. Logical organization is paramount to emphasize the significance of each point in relation to the topic. When necessary, cross-reference your findings with credible sources, including references to ensure verifiability.

#GOAL:
You will generate a detailed list of key points on the [SPECIFIED TOPIC], ensuring each point is validated, concise, and presented in clear, easy-to-understand language. This list aims to educate readers unfamiliar with the topic and serve as a quick recap for those more knowledgeable.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
To achieve a thorough and informative list, follow these steps:

1. Conduct preliminary research on the [SPECIFIED TOPIC] to gather a broad spectrum of information. This includes historical context, current trends, and future projections where applicable.
2. Narrow down your findings to the most relevant and significant points. Each point should add distinct value to the reader's understanding of the topic.
3. Simplify complex concepts or terms into layman-friendly language without sacrificing accuracy. Avoid jargon unless it's crucial to the topic, and in such cases, provide clear definitions.
4. Organize the points logically, starting with foundational information before moving to more specific details or advanced concepts. This helps in building the reader's knowledge step by step.
5. When referring to data, studies, or other specific information, include references in a simplified citation format that's easy for readers to follow. This increases the credibility of the information provided.
6. Ensure the final list is comprehensive yet concise, covering the topic's breadth without overwhelming the reader with unnecessary detail. Aim for a balance that caters to both beginners and those seeking a quick reference.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My topic of interest: [SPECIFIED TOPIC]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a well-structured, comprehensive list of key points about [SPECIFIED TOPIC], written in clear, layman-friendly language. Each point will be concise yet informative, offering valuable insights into the topic. The list will be organized logically to build understanding progressively, making it suitable for readers with varying levels of knowledge on the subject. References will be provided where necessary to validate the information, ensuring the list serves as both an introductory guide and a quick reference.

## How to use the prompt

Conducts in-depth research on a specified topic to create an accessible and comprehensive list of key points. Simplifies complex information into clear, layman-friendly language, ensuring accuracy and relevance. Organizes the information logically to cater to both beginners and experienced readers, with references for credibility.
