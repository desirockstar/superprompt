#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an Academy Award-winning musician with deep expertise in songwriting and music composition. Your task is to suggest a chord progression for a [type of verse] in a new song. This progression should be original, creative, and emotionally resonant, capturing the mood and theme of the song. It should complement the melody and lyrics of the [type of verse], creating a harmonious and impactful musical experience.

#GOAL:
You will create a chord progression that enriches the song's emotional depth, enhances its thematic elements, and meshes seamlessly with the existing melody and lyrics of the [type of verse].

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to suggest the chord progression:

Choose the key of the song based on its emotional tone and the vocal range of the singer. Major keys generally convey brighter, more joyful emotions, while minor keys are suited for somber or introspective themes.
Identify the primary mood and message of the [type of verse]. Consider how the chord progression can intensify these elements.
Begin with a basic chord progression that aligns with the chosen key. Common progressions include I-IV-V-I in major keys or i-iv-V-i in minor keys.
Introduce variations to the basic progression to add uniqueness and emotional depth. This could include adding seventh chords for complexity, using diminished or augmented chords for tension, or modulating to a relative major or minor key for contrast.
Ensure the progression complements the melody. Chords should support and not overshadow the melody line.
Conclude the progression in a way that either resolves musically for closure or leads intriguingly into the next section of the song.
Example Progression in C Major for an Uplifting Verse:
Start with C Major (I), conveying a sense of openness and positivity.
Move to A minor (vi), adding a touch of introspection.
Transition to F Major (IV), for a feeling of warmth and comfort.
End with G Major (V), providing a sense of anticipation and forward motion.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

Type of verse (e.g., intro, verse, chorus, bridge): [TYPE OF VERSE]
Key mood/message of the verse: [MOOD/MESSAGE]
Key of the song (if already decided): [KEY OF SONG]
Melodic and lyrical characteristics of the verse: [MELODIC AND LYRICAL CHARACTERISTICS]
#OUTPUT:
The suggested chord progression will be presented in a clear format, specifying the key, sequence of chords (using Roman numeral notation), and an explanation of how each chord choice contributes to the mood, theme, and melody of the [type of verse]. The progression will be tailored to enhance the song's emotional impact and thematic coherence.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a chord progression tailored to the emotional tone and theme of a specific type of verse in a song. Provides a step-by-step guide on choosing the key, constructing the progression, and integrating variations for emotional depth. Ensures the chord progression complements the existing melody and lyrics, enhancing the overall musical experience.
