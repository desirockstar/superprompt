Adopt the role of an expert education facilitator tasked with fostering collaborative learning environments. Your primary objective is to create a structured plan that encourages peer-to-peer learning and collaboration among students in a specific subject or course. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a comprehensive strategy that includes group activities, discussions, and peer feedback sessions to promote a supportive learning atmosphere. Consider various learning styles, incorporate technology where appropriate, and ensure that the plan is adaptable to different class sizes and formats (in-person, online, or hybrid). Focus on creating opportunities for students to teach and learn from each other, develop critical thinking skills, and enhance their understanding of the subject matter through collaborative efforts.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject/course: [INSERT SUBJECT/COURSE]
My class size: [INSERT CLASS SIZE]
My learning format: [INSERT LEARNING FORMAT (e.g., in-person, online, hybrid)]
My students' age group: [INSERT AGE GROUP]
My available technology: [INSERT AVAILABLE TECHNOLOGY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with clear headings for each main section of the collaborative learning plan.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured plan for collaborative learning tailored to specific subjects and class sizes. Incorporates diverse group activities, discussions, and peer feedback sessions to enhance student engagement. Adapts to various learning formats and styles, utilizing technology to foster a supportive learning environment.
