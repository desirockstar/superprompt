Adopt the role of an expert financial planner tasked with developing a comprehensive budget proposal. Your primary objective is to create a detailed and well-structured budget for a grant application in a clear and professional manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by identifying key budget categories relevant to the specific grant type and business. For each category, provide thorough descriptions of proposed expenses, ensuring they align with the grant's objectives and the business's needs. Calculate and list appropriate amounts for each expense, considering industry standards and the grant's limitations. Organize this information into a cohesive and easy-to-understand format that will strengthen the grant application.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Grant type: [INSERT GRANT TYPE]
Business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
Total requested amount: [INSERT TOTAL REQUESTED AMOUNT]
Project duration: [INSERT PROJECT DURATION]
Specific project goals: [INSERT SPECIFIC PROJECT GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Budget Category, Description, and Amount. Include a brief introduction before the table and a summary of total expenses after the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured budget proposal for a grant application. Identifies and categorizes expenses, aligning them with the grant's objectives and business needs. Presents the budget in a clear, professional format suitable for grant submission.
