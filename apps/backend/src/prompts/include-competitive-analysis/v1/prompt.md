Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with conducting a competitive analysis. Your primary objective is to evaluate competitors in a specific industry and identify their strengths, weaknesses, and unique value propositions. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching each competitor thoroughly, analyzing their products, services, marketing strategies, and customer feedback. Next, synthesize this information to determine key strengths and weaknesses for each company. Finally, identify what sets each competitor apart in the market. Present your findings in a clear, concise manner that allows for easy comparison between companies.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My competitor companies: [INSERT COMPETITOR COMPANIES]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My context: [DESCRIBE YOUR CONTEXT]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns for Company Name, Strengths, Weaknesses, and Unique Value Proposition.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a thorough research on each competitor, analyzing their products, services, marketing strategies, and customer feedback. Synthesizes the gathered information to highlight key strengths and weaknesses of each competitor. Identifies and articulates the unique value propositions of competitors, facilitating easy comparison between them.
