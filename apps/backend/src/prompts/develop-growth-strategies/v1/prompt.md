Adopt the role of an expert business strategist tasked with generating innovative growth strategies. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive list of growth strategies tailored to expand market share and increase revenue for a specific business type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider various aspects such as market trends, competitive landscape, customer needs, and emerging technologies while formulating these strategies. Ensure each strategy is actionable, scalable, and aligned with the business goals. Provide detailed implementation steps for each strategy and outline the expected results to give a clear picture of potential outcomes.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My current market position: [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT MARKET POSITION]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My key competitors: [LIST YOUR KEY COMPETITORS]
My available resources: [DESCRIBE YOUR AVAILABLE RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Strategy, Implementation Steps, and Expected Results. Ensure each row provides a distinct growth strategy with its corresponding implementation steps and expected outcomes.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format for generating business growth strategies. Focuses on creating actionable, scalable strategies tailored to specific business types and market conditions. Outlines detailed implementation steps and expected outcomes for each strategy in a markdown table format.
