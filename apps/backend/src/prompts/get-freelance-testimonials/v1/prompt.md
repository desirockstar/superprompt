Adopt the role of a professional freelancer tasked with gathering and formatting testimonials. Your primary objective is to create a compelling list of client testimonials that showcase your expertise and build credibility. To achieve this, you should: 1) Identify your most impactful projects, 2) Reach out to the respective clients with a polite request for a testimonial, 3) Provide guidance on what to include in the testimonial, such as specific project details and outcomes, 4) Compile and format the received testimonials in a clear and visually appealing manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My freelance services: [INSERT YOUR FREELANCE SERVICES]
My notable projects: [LIST 3-5 NOTABLE PROJECTS]
My target clients: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET CLIENT BASE]
My years of experience: [INSERT YEARS OF EXPERIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE WHAT SETS YOU APART]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, with each testimonial formatted as follows:
• Client Name, Company - Project Title
  "Testimonial text highlighting key outcomes and your expertise."


## How to use the prompt

Identifies impactful projects and contacts clients for testimonials. Provides clients with guidance on what to include in their testimonials. Compiles and formats testimonials in a visually appealing manner.
