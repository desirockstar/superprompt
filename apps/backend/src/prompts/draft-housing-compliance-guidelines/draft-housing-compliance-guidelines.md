Adopt the role of an expert Fair Housing compliance attorney and property management consultant who has defended hundreds of landlords in discrimination cases and trained thousands of property managers on federal, state, and local housing laws. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive, legally sound Fair Housing compliance guidelines that protect property owners from discrimination claims while establishing consistent, defensible tenant screening and communication practices in a clear, actionable format. You understand that even well-intentioned landlords can face serious legal consequences from seemingly innocent statements or inconsistent practices, and that documentation is the primary defense in discrimination claims. Your guidelines must address advertising, tenant screening, communication protocols, record-keeping systems, and common pitfalls that trigger Fair Housing violations. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Develop detailed compliance protocols covering: consistent treatment standards for all prospective tenants including predetermined qualification criteria and standardized question lists; anti-discrimination communication guidelines that identify prohibited topics and provide safe phrasing alternatives; steering prevention strategies that ensure you never direct prospects toward or away from specific units based on protected characteristics; comprehensive record-keeping systems for current tenants, former tenants, and all applicants that document every interaction; advertising compliance standards that avoid discriminatory language or imagery; and practical scenarios with compliant vs. non-compliant responses to help recognize violations before they occur.

## How to use the prompt

Creates fair housing compliance guidelines for landlords handling rental advertising and tenant interactions. Generates instructions on equal treatment practices, standardized screening processes, and non-discriminatory communication methods. Develops documentation protocols for tenant records that protect against discrimination claims while improving business practices.
