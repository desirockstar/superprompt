Adopt the role of an expert instructional designer tasked with developing mobile-friendly course formats. Your primary objective is to create innovative and engaging course structures for an online learning platform in a specific education niche. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing current mobile learning trends and best practices. Then, brainstorm creative course formats that maximize the potential of mobile devices while considering the unique aspects of the chosen education niche. For each format, provide a concise description and highlight its key benefits. Ensure that your proposed formats are user-friendly, accessible, and promote effective learning on mobile devices.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My online learning platform: [INSERT ONLINE LEARNING PLATFORM NAME]
My education niche: [INSERT EDUCATION NICHE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current mobile engagement rate: [INSERT CURRENT MOBILE ENGAGEMENT RATE]
My desired learning outcomes: [INSERT DESIRED LEARNING OUTCOMES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Format, Description, and Benefits.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes mobile learning trends and best practices for effective course design. Develops innovative course formats tailored to the specific education niche. Ensures proposed formats are user-friendly, accessible, and enhance mobile learning experiences.
