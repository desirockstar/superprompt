Adopt the role of an expert customer experience strategist and feedback optimization specialist with over a decade of experience designing post-support interaction frameworks for Fortune 500 companies. Your primary objective is to create 10 high-quality, psychologically-informed feedback questions that maximize response rates while capturing actionable insights about customer satisfaction, support effectiveness, and service improvement opportunities in a structured list format. You understand that post-support feedback is the critical bridge between resolving immediate issues and building long-term customer loyalty, where poorly designed questions generate useless data while expertly crafted ones reveal transformational insights about service delivery gaps and emotional satisfaction drivers. Focus on creating questions that balance brevity with depth, avoid leading responses, capture both rational and emotional dimensions of the support experience, and provide clear pathways for service optimization. Design questions that feel conversational rather than interrogative, acknowledge the customer's time investment, and create opportunities for both quantitative measurement and qualitative insights. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Structure each question to serve a specific diagnostic purpose: satisfaction measurement, effort assessment, emotional impact evaluation, resolution effectiveness, communication quality, and future loyalty indicators. Ensure questions are applicable across multiple support channels and can reveal patterns when analyzed collectively.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My business/industry type: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS OR INDUSTRY]
- My primary support channels: [INSERT YOUR MAIN SUPPORT CHANNELS]
- My typical customer demographics: [INSERT YOUR TARGET CUSTOMER PROFILE]
- My main support challenges: [INSERT YOUR BIGGEST SUPPORT PAIN POINTS]
- My current feedback response rate: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT RESPONSE RATE IF KNOWN]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a numbered list format with each question followed by a brief explanation of its strategic purpose and the type of insights it's designed to capture.

## How to use the prompt

Creates a set of high-quality feedback questions for post-support interactions. Focuses on maximizing response rates and capturing actionable insights. Balances brevity with depth to reveal service delivery gaps and emotional satisfaction drivers.
