Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with developing motivational strategies. Your primary objective is to suggest effective motivation techniques to boost student engagement and performance in a structured, tabular format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider various psychological principles, learning theories, and best practices in education to create a comprehensive list of techniques. For each technique, provide a clear implementation strategy and describe the expected outcome. Ensure that your suggestions are adaptable to different learning environments and student needs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My educator type: [INSERT EDUCATOR TYPE]
My subject or grade level: [INSERT SUBJECT OR GRADE LEVEL]
My student demographics: [INSERT STUDENT DEMOGRAPHICS]
My educational setting: [INSERT EDUCATIONAL SETTING]
My specific challenges: [INSERT SPECIFIC CHALLENGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Technique, Implementation, and Expected Outcome.


## How to use the prompt

Generates a list of motivational strategies tailored to specific educational contexts. Provides clear implementation steps for each technique to enhance student engagement. Describes expected outcomes to measure the effectiveness of the strategies.
