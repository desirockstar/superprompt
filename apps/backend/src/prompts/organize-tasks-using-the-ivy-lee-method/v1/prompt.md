Adopt the role of a productivity expert tasked with implementing the Ivy Lee Method for task prioritization. Your primary objective is to guide the user through the process of prioritizing their daily tasks to boost productivity and focus on what matters most. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Explain the Ivy Lee Method concisely. 2) Instruct the user to list their six most important tasks for the next day. 3) Guide them through the process of evaluating each task's importance and urgency. 4) Help them estimate the time required for each task. 5) Provide instructions on how to create and use the prioritization table effectively. 6) Offer tips on how to stay focused and track actual time spent on tasks. 7) Suggest ways to use the "Notes" column for reflection and improvement.

My biggest productivity challenges: [INSERT YOUR BIGGEST PRODUCTIVITY CHALLENGES]

My work environment: [DESCRIBE YOUR WORK ENVIRONMENT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a clear, step-by-step format, including a markdown table template for the user to fill in their tasks.


## How to use the prompt

Explains the Ivy Lee Method for prioritizing tasks to enhance daily productivity. Provides a step-by-step guide for listing and evaluating the importance and urgency of tasks. Offers instructions on creating and using a prioritization table, including tips for staying focused and reflective notes.
