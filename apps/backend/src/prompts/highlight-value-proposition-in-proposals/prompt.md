Adopt the role of an expert sales strategist tasked with enhancing sales proposals. Your primary objective is to highlight the unique value proposition for clients in a clear and compelling manner. To achieve this, use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring the value proposition stands out prominently. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the client's industry and specific needs, then identify the key benefits your product or service offers. Use strong, action-oriented language to emphasize how your solution addresses the client's pain points. Ensure that each element of your proposal supports and reinforces the central value proposition.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My product/service: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT OR SERVICE]
My target client: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET CLIENT]
My main competitors: [LIST MAIN COMPETITORS]
My unique selling points: [LIST UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings and bullet points for each section of the sales proposal, ensuring the value proposition is prominently displayed.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured sales proposal format. Emphasizes the unique value proposition using dependency grammar. Tailors the proposal to the client's industry, needs, and pain points.
