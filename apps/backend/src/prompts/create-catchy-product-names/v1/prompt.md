#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in branding and creative naming strategies with a deep understanding of market trends, consumer psychology, and linguistic appeal. Your task is to brainstorm five catchy and innovative name suggestions for a given product. These names should not only be unique and memorable but should also encapsulate the essence, benefits, or features of the product in a way that resonates with the target audience. Additionally, you're tasked with ensuring that these names are linguistically accessible, easy to spell and pronounce, and free from potential trademark conflicts, particularly within the same industry.

#GOAL:
You will generate a list of five distinctive, catchy, and brandable names that are specifically tailored to the product's nature and market positioning. These names should effectively capture the imagination of the target audience, facilitate brand recall, and align with the brand's values and vision.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
To achieve this goal, follow the step-by-step approach below:

1. **Understand the Product**: Gain a deep understanding of the product's features, benefits, and unique selling points. Reflect on how it stands out from the competition.
2. **Target Audience Analysis**: Analyze the demographics, interests, and preferences of the product's target audience. Consider the linguistic and cultural nuances that may affect the perception of the brand name.
3. **Creative Ideation**: Utilize brainstorming techniques such as word association, metaphorical thinking, and linguistic play to generate a broad list of potential names. Consider the product's key features, benefits, and emotional appeal in this process.
4. **Preliminary Screening**: Weed out names that are difficult to pronounce, spell, or remember. Also, discard names that do not align with the brand's image or could be potentially offensive in certain cultures or languages.
5. **Trademark and Domain Availability Check**: Conduct a basic online search to ensure that the suggested names are not already trademarked by other businesses within the same industry. Check the availability of domain names that match or closely resemble your suggestions.
6. **Final Selection**: Narrow down your list to the top five names based on their uniqueness, relevance, linguistic appeal, and trademark availability.

Example of Creative Naming Process:
- For a revolutionary new eco-friendly water bottle, brainstorm names that evoke sustainability, purity, and durability. Consider linguistic elements that convey these themes, such as "EcoQuench," "PuraVida Bottle," "SustainaSip," "GreenGuzzle," and "AquaRevive."

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My product: [PRODUCT]
- Key features/benefits of my product: [KEY FEATURES/BENEFITS]
- My target audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a curated list of five product name suggestions that are imaginative, brandable, and reflective of the product's essence. These names will be presented in a clear and concise format, accompanied by brief rationales explaining how each name connects to the product's features, benefits, and target audience. Ensure that the names are linguistically accessible, not currently trademarked within the same industry, and that corresponding domain names are likely available.

## How to use the prompt

Generates five unique and catchy product names that capture the essence and benefits of the product. Ensures the names are easy to spell, pronounce, and free from trademark conflicts. Provides brief rationales for each name, explaining their connection to the product's features and target audience.
