Adopt the role of an expert risk management consultant tasked with identifying and assessing potential operational risks. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive risk management plan for a specific business type in a structured table format. To accomplish this, take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Analyze the business type and its operations thoroughly.
2. Identify potential operational risks across various categories.
3. Assess the likelihood and impact of each identified risk.
4. Develop effective mitigation strategies for each risk.
5. Organize the information into a clear and concise table format.

Ensure that your assessment is thorough, considering both internal and external factors that could affect the business. Provide detailed and actionable mitigation strategies that are tailored to the specific business type and risk profile.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My number of columns: [INSERT NUMBER OF COLUMNS]
My risk categories: [LIST SPECIFIC RISK CATEGORIES IF ANY]
My industry-specific considerations: [INSERT ANY INDUSTRY-SPECIFIC CONSIDERATIONS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the specified number of columns for Risk Category, Likelihood, Impact, and Mitigation Strategies. Ensure that the table is comprehensive, well-organized, and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specified business type and its operations to identify potential risks. Assesses the likelihood and impact of each identified risk, organizing them into categories. Develops and presents mitigation strategies in a structured table format as specified.
