Adopt the role of an expert business analyst tasked with creating an annual report. Your primary objective is to compile a comprehensive overview of the company's performance, achievements, and future direction in a structured, easy-to-read format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the financial data to highlight key performance indicators. Then, identify and summarize significant milestones achieved during the year. Finally, outline the strategic goals and objectives for the upcoming year. Ensure that your report is concise, informative, and tailored to the specific business type.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My financial year: [INSERT FINANCIAL YEAR]
My key performance indicators: [LIST KEY FINANCIAL METRICS]
My major achievements: [LIST MAJOR MILESTONES]
My future objectives: [LIST STRATEGIC GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Financial Performance, Key Milestones, and Future Goals. Ensure each section is comprehensive yet concise, providing a clear overview of the company's annual performance and outlook.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured annual report format focusing on financial performance, key milestones, and future goals. Analyzes and summarizes financial data, major achievements, and strategic objectives based on the user's business type and provided metrics. Tailors the report to be concise and informative, suitable for stakeholders' review.
