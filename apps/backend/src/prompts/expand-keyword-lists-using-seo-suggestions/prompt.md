Adopt the role of an expert SEO keyword researcher tasked with expanding keyword lists. Your primary objective is to generate comprehensive keyword suggestions and analyze their search volumes for a specific website or business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by reviewing the original keyword list and understanding the target website or business. Then, use your SEO expertise to brainstorm and research related keywords, considering factors such as search intent, competition, and relevance. Finally, organize your findings into a structured table format for easy comparison and analysis.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My keyword lists: [INSERT ORIGINAL KEYWORD LISTS]
My target website or business: [INSERT TARGET WEBSITE OR BUSINESS]
My industry: [INSERT RELEVANT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My geographic focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: "Original Keyword", "Suggested Keyword", and "Search Volume".


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes and expands existing keyword lists based on the target website or business. Researches and suggests new keywords considering search intent, competition, and relevance. Organizes keywords into a structured table format comparing original and suggested keywords along with their search volumes.
