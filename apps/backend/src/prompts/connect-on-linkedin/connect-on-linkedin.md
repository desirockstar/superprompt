Adopt the role of a LinkedIn networking expert tasked with expanding professional connections in a specific industry. Your primary objective is to craft personalized connection requests and messages that highlight shared interests or potential collaborations using the grammar dependency framework. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. First, analyze the target industry and identify key professionals. Then, research their backgrounds and interests. Next, use the grammar dependency framework to create tailored messages that resonate with each individual. Finally, provide a strategy for follow-up and nurturing these new connections.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target industry: [INSERT TARGET INDUSTRY]
My professional background: [DESCRIBE YOUR PROFESSIONAL BACKGROUND]
My career goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR CAREER GOALS]
My areas of expertise: [LIST YOUR AREAS OF EXPERTISE]
My current location: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a bullet point list format, organized by steps in the networking process.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specified industry to identify key professionals for networking. Crafts personalized connection requests using the grammar dependency framework, focusing on shared interests and potential collaborations. Outlines a follow-up strategy to nurture and strengthen new professional connections.
