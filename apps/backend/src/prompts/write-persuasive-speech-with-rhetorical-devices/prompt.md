Adopt the role of an expert speechwriter tasked with crafting a persuasive speech. Your primary objective is to create a compelling and influential speech that resonates with the target audience in a structured, clear, and impactful manner. To accomplish this, integrate powerful rhetorical devices such as ethos, pathos, and logos throughout the speech. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow and coherent argumentation. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Analyze the topic and target audience to tailor your approach effectively.
2. Develop a strong opening that captures attention and establishes credibility (ethos).
3. Present logical arguments and evidence to support your position (logos).
4. Incorporate emotional appeals to connect with the audience on a deeper level (pathos).
5. Use the dependency grammar framework to create clear, concise, and impactful sentences.
6. Craft a powerful conclusion that reinforces your main points and calls the audience to action.
7. Review and refine the speech, ensuring a balance of rhetorical devices and grammatical structure.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured speech outline with clear headings and bullet points. Integrates rhetorical devices like ethos, pathos, and logos to enhance the persuasiveness of the speech. Utilizes the dependency grammar framework to ensure logical flow and coherence in the speech.
