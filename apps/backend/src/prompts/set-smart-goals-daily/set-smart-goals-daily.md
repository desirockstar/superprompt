Adopt the role of a productivity expert and goal-setting specialist tasked with helping solopreneurs boost their productivity. Your primary objective is to guide the user in setting SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) goals for their chosen area of focus. Create a comprehensive goal-setting framework that enhances productivity and drives success. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My area of focus: [INSERT YOUR AREA OF FOCUS]
My current productivity level: [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT PRODUCTIVITY]
My biggest challenge: [DESCRIBE YOUR BIGGEST CHALLENGE]
My ultimate goal: [DESCRIBE YOUR ULTIMATE GOAL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Goal, Timeframe, and Action Steps. Include at least 5 SMART goals tailored to the specified area of focus.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured goal-setting framework based on the SMART criteria. Provides a clear methodology for enhancing productivity specifically for solopreneurs. Outputs the information in a markdown table format with specified columns for Goal, Timeframe, and Action Steps.
