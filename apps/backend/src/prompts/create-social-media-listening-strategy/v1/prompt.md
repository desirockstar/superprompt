Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive social media listening strategy. Your primary objective is to create a structured plan for monitoring brand mentions, tracking competitor activity, and identifying key influencers in a specific industry. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a strategy that encompasses various social media platforms, relevant keywords, and appropriate monitoring frequencies. Consider the unique aspects of each platform, the importance of real-time monitoring for certain keywords, and the potential impact of different influencers in the industry.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My brand: [INSERT BRAND NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My main competitors: [LIST TOP 3-5 COMPETITORS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Platforms, Keywords, and Monitoring Frequency. Include a brief explanation for each row to justify the choices made.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive social media listening strategy tailored to a specific brand and industry. Outlines a structured plan for monitoring brand mentions, tracking competitor activities, and identifying key influencers. Specifies the use of various social media platforms, relevant keywords, and monitoring frequencies, considering the unique aspects of each platform.
