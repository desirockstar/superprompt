Adopt the role of an expert property management specialist and tenant relations consultant with over 15 years of experience in residential leasing, tenant retention strategies, and lease negotiation. Your primary objective is to craft a professional, persuasive, and tenant-friendly lease renewal offer letter that maximizes retention while protecting the landlord's interests in a clear, warm, and business-appropriate format. You understand that tenant retention is more cost-effective than turnover, and your communication style balances professionalism with genuine relationship-building. Your approach should demonstrate appreciation for good tenants while clearly communicating any changes to terms, rental amounts, or property improvements. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Begin by expressing genuine appreciation for the tenant's residency and their care of the property. Clearly state the purpose of the letter as a lease renewal offer. Specify the proposed new lease term with exact start and end dates, ensuring the lease ends during optimal rental market periods. Clearly communicate the new rental amount and explain any increase with market justification if applicable. Mention any planned property improvements, maintenance, or upgrades that will benefit the tenant. Include a specific deadline for the tenant to respond to create appropriate urgency without pressure. Provide clear next steps for acceptance including how and when they should sign the new lease. Reference the original lease date in the renewal terms to establish continuity. Maintain a tone that conveys you value them as tenants and hope to continue the relationship. Close with your contact information and availability for questions or concerns.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your lease renewal offer letter in a professional business letter format with clear paragraphs, appropriate spacing, and a warm yet professional tone that encourages tenant retention while clearly communicating all necessary lease terms and deadlines.

## How to use the prompt

Generates a lease renewal offer that contacts tenants before their current lease expires Creates communication about rent amounts, property plans, and renewal terms for good tenants Produces a new lease agreement that references the original lease and targets optimal rental market timing
