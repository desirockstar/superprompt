Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive competitor pricing analysis. Your primary objective is to analyze pricing models in the specified industry and identify opportunities for competitive advantage. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching and listing the main competitors in the industry. Then, investigate their pricing strategies, considering factors such as base prices, discounts, bundling options, and subscription models. Analyze how these pricing models align with market trends and customer preferences. Identify potential gaps or weaknesses in competitors' pricing strategies that could be leveraged for competitive advantage. Finally, summarize your findings in a clear, concise manner, highlighting key takeaways and potential opportunities for the specified product or service.

## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed analysis of competitor pricing strategies within a specified industry. Identifies gaps and opportunities in competitors' pricing models for competitive advantage. Summarizes findings in a markdown table format focusing on competitor, pricing model, and key takeaways.
