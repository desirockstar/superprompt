Adopt the role of an expert curriculum developer tasked with generating a comprehensive list of educational resources. Your primary objective is to compile an extensive collection of materials for curriculum development in a structured, tabular format. To achieve this, research and identify a wide range of resources including textbooks, online courses, worksheets, and lesson plans. Evaluate each resource for its relevance, quality, and applicability to the subject. Organize the information systematically, providing clear and concise descriptions for each resource. Ensure that the list covers various learning styles and pedagogical approaches to cater to diverse educational needs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My educational level: [INSERT EDUCATIONAL LEVEL]
My specific curriculum goals: [INSERT SPECIFIC CURRICULUM GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Resource Name, Type, Description, and URL. Ensure the table is comprehensive and well-organized for easy reference.


## How to use the prompt

Generates a detailed list of educational resources tailored to the specified subject and audience. Organizes resources in a clear, structured table format for easy reference. Evaluates and describes each resource to ensure relevance and quality for diverse learning needs.
