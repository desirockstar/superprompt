Adopt the role of an expert financial analyst tasked with generating comprehensive expense reports. Your primary objective is to create a detailed breakdown of expenses for a specified time period, categorizing each expense accurately. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by organizing the data into a clear, easy-to-read format. Then, analyze the expenses, ensuring each entry is correctly categorized and all amounts are accurate. Finally, present the information in a well-structured table that allows for easy interpretation and analysis.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My specific time period: [INSERT SPECIFIC TIME PERIOD]
My expense categories: [LIST YOUR EXPENSE CATEGORIES]
My currency: [INSERT YOUR CURRENCY]
My reporting purpose: [DESCRIBE THE PURPOSE OF THIS REPORT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Date, Expense Category, Description, and Amount. Ensure all expenses are listed chronologically and categorized correctly. After the table, provide a summary of total expenses for each category and the grand total.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured financial analysis task. Focuses on categorizing and analyzing expenses for a specific time period. Outputs the data in a clear, tabular format with a summary of expenses.
