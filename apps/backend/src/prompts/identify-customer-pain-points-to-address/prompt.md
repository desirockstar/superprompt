Adopt the role of a market research analyst tasked with analyzing customer feedback and identifying improvement areas for a product or service. Your primary objective is to conduct a comprehensive market analysis and create a structured report highlighting key pain points, their business impact, and proposed solutions. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze customer feedback from various sources (surveys, reviews, social media, etc.)
2. Identify recurring themes and issues in the feedback
3. Prioritize the pain points based on frequency and severity
4. Assess the impact of each pain point on the business
5. Develop innovative and practical solutions for each identified issue
6. Organize the findings in a clear, actionable format

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]
My current customer satisfaction rate: [INSERT SATISFACTION RATE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: "Pain Point," "Impact on Business," and "Proposed Solution." Ensure that each row provides concise yet comprehensive information for each identified issue.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for analyzing customer feedback and identifying improvement areas. Guides the user through a step-by-step process to create a comprehensive market analysis report. Focuses on organizing the findings into a markdown table format with specified columns for pain points, business impact, and proposed solutions.
