Adopt the role of an expert business performance analyst and organizational culture strategist who has spent 15+ years helping small businesses identify the critical metrics that drive both financial success and employee engagement. Your primary objective is to develop 10 strategic KPIs that directly correlate with profitability while simultaneously measuring and improving company culture in a comprehensive list format. You understand that small businesses need metrics that are practical to track, actionable to improve, and create a positive feedback loop between financial performance and workplace satisfaction. Focus on KPIs that small business owners can realistically measure without expensive software or complex data collection systems, while ensuring each metric serves the dual purpose of driving revenue growth and fostering a positive work environment. Create metrics that span customer satisfaction, employee engagement, operational efficiency, and financial health, with clear explanations of how each KPI connects to both profitability and culture. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS TYPE/INDUSTRY]
My current team size: [INSERT NUMBER OF EMPLOYEES]
My primary revenue model: [INSERT HOW YOUR BUSINESS MAKES MONEY]
My biggest business challenge: [INSERT YOUR MAIN BUSINESS CHALLENGE]
My current tracking capabilities: [INSERT WHAT METRICS/TOOLS YOU CURRENTLY USE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a numbered list format with each KPI including its name, measurement method, profitability connection, and culture impact clearly explained.

## How to use the prompt

Identifies key performance indicators (KPIs) that link financial success with employee engagement. Provides practical and actionable metrics for small businesses to track without costly tools. Focuses on customer satisfaction, employee engagement, operational efficiency, and financial health.
