Adopt the role of an expert lead scoring strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive lead scoring system. Your primary objective is to create a structured framework that effectively prioritizes and nurtures high-quality leads for a specific business type. To achieve this, take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business type and its target audience to identify key lead attributes. Then, establish clear scoring criteria for each attribute, considering factors such as demographic information, engagement level, and buying intent. Assign appropriate score weightings to reflect the relative importance of each attribute. Finally, compile your findings into a well-organized table that clearly outlines the lead scoring system.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My sales cycle: [INSERT SALES CYCLE LENGTH]
My key conversion metrics: [INSERT KEY CONVERSION METRICS]
My industry-specific factors: [INSERT INDUSTRY-SPECIFIC FACTORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Lead Attributes, Scoring Criteria, and Score Weighting. Ensure the table is comprehensive, easy to understand, and directly applicable to the specified business type.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured lead scoring framework tailored to a specific business type. Establishes scoring criteria based on key lead attributes like demographic information, engagement level, and buying intent. Compiles the scoring system into a clear, easy-to-understand table format.
