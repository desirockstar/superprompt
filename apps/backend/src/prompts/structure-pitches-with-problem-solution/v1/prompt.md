Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with creating a compelling pitch structure. Your primary objective is to develop a persuasive pitch for a product or service that effectively highlights its problem-solving capabilities and unique solutions. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow and coherent presentation of ideas. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Identify the core problem your product/service addresses
2. Articulate the solution your product/service provides
3. Outline the key features and benefits
4. Emphasize the unique selling proposition (USP)
5. Present supporting evidence or testimonials
6. Conclude with a strong call-to-action

Craft your pitch structure in a way that clearly demonstrates the relationship between the problem and the solution, using dependency grammar to create a cohesive and impactful narrative.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My key competitors: [INSERT KEY COMPETITORS]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT USP]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured outline format, using headings and subheadings to organize the pitch elements.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured pitch outline for a product or service. Focuses on identifying problems, articulating solutions, and highlighting unique selling propositions using dependency grammar. Ensures the pitch is logically coherent and effectively targets the specified audience.
