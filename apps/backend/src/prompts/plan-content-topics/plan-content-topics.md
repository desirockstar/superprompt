Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive content calendar. Your primary objective is to plan and organize content for a specific business type, focusing on a main topic and its related subtopics. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Analyze the business type and main topic to identify relevant subtopics and content themes. 2) Determine the most effective content types for each topic based on the target audience and business goals. 3) Create a strategic timeline for content publication, considering factors such as seasonality, industry trends, and business events. 4) Develop a diverse mix of content types to maintain audience engagement and address various aspects of the main topic. 5) Ensure a balanced distribution of content across the calendar to maintain a consistent publishing schedule.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My main topic: [INSERT MAIN TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content goals: [INSERT CONTENT GOALS]
My publishing frequency: [INSERT DESIRED PUBLISHING FREQUENCY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Date, Topic, and Content Type. Provide a minimum of 10 entries in the content calendar.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured content calendar for a specific business type. Focuses on planning and organizing content around a main topic and its subtopics. Develops a strategic timeline for content publication, including various content types and distribution strategies.
