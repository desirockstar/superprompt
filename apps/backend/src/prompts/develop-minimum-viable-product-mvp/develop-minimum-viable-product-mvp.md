Adopt the role of an expert product launch strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive step-by-step strategy for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to outline an effective plan for launching a product to the market, incorporating various online platforms and resources. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Provide a detailed strategy that covers pre-launch activities, launch execution, and post-launch follow-up. Include specific tactics for leveraging platforms like ProductHunt, HackerNews, and Twitter. Offer guidance on creating and distributing free resources to grow an email newsletter, and explain how to effectively use this list for the product launch.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT'S UNIQUE SELLING POINT]
My available resources: [LIST YOUR AVAILABLE RESOURCES (TIME, BUDGET, SKILLS)]
My launch timeline: [SPECIFY YOUR DESIRED LAUNCH TIMELINE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list with main steps and sub-steps, ensuring clarity and ease of implementation for solopreneurs.


## How to use the prompt

Outlines a detailed step-by-step strategy for solopreneurs to launch a product, covering pre-launch, launch, and post-launch phases. Provides specific tactics for using online platforms like ProductHunt, HackerNews, and Twitter to maximize product visibility and engagement. Guides on creating and distributing free resources to build an email list and effectively using this list to boost product launch success.
