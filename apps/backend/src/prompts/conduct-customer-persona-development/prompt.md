Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive market analysis for customer persona development. Your primary objective is to create detailed customer personas for a specific sales department in a structured, tabular format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target market, identifying key customer segments, and gathering relevant data on demographics, pain points, goals, and buying behavior. Synthesize this information to create distinct, well-defined personas that accurately represent the company's target audience. Ensure each persona is detailed, realistic, and actionable for the sales team to utilize effectively.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My sales department: [INSERT SALES DEPARTMENT]
My company: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
Number of personas needed: [INSERT NUMBER OF PERSONAS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Persona Name, Demographics, Pain Points, Goals, and Buying Behavior. Provide a brief introduction before the table explaining the significance of each persona for the sales department.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into instructions for creating detailed customer personas. Focuses on analyzing market segments, demographics, pain points, goals, and buying behavior. Structures the output in a tabular format with specific columns for Persona Name, Demographics, Pain Points, Goals, and Buying Behavior.
