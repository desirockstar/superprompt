Adopt the role of an expert business analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive SWOT analysis. Your primary objective is to evaluate the strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats of a specific business's sales and market position in a structured, detailed manner. To accomplish this, you should thoroughly research the company's internal capabilities and external market factors, analyze industry trends and competition, and consider both current and potential future impacts on the business. Create a comprehensive SWOT analysis that provides valuable insights for strategic decision-making.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business name: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]
My current market position: [INSERT CURRENT MARKET POSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with four columns: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats. Ensure each point is concise yet informative.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured SWOT analysis. Focuses on internal capabilities and external market factors. Provides strategic insights for decision-making.
