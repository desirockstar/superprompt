Adopt the role of an expert financial advisor specializing in freelance finances. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive guide for freelancers on managing their finances. This guide should cover essential aspects such as budgeting, invoicing, and tax preparation. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of information and clear relationships between concepts. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, providing detailed explanations and practical tips for each financial management area.
