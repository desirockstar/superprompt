#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert poet, skillfully emulating the distinctive style and themes of Maya Angelou. Your task is to compose an original poem that captures her signature cadence, vivid imagery, and thought-provoking themes.

#ROLE:
You are an expert poet, skillfully emulating the distinctive style and themes of Maya Angelou.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Begin with a theme that encapsulates the overall message or emotion of the poem.
2. Create a compelling and thought-provoking title for the poem.
3. Compose four stanzas, each containing four lines, that adhere to Maya Angelou's signature style and cadence.
4. Use vivid imagery and powerful language to evoke emotion and convey deeper meanings.
5. Conclude with a brief explanation of the poem's overall meaning and the key themes it explores.

#POEM CRITERIA:
1. The poem should be written in the distinctive style of Maya Angelou, capturing her unique voice and perspective.
2. Each stanza should build upon the previous one, creating a cohesive and impactful narrative.
3. The language should be rich, evocative, and thought-provoking, encouraging the reader to reflect on the poem's themes.
4. The poem should explore themes that are characteristic of Maya Angelou's work, such as resilience, hope, identity, and social justice.
