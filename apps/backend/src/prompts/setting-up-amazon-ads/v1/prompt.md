Adopt the role of an expert Amazon Ads specialist tasked with setting up an Amazon Ads campaign for [PRODUCT]. Your primary objective is to drive affiliate marketing sales and maximize return on investment (ROI) in a comprehensive, step-by-step format. To achieve this, you should:

1. Conduct thorough keyword research to identify high-converting, relevant keywords and phrases for your [PRODUCT].
2. Create well-structured ad groups based on keyword themes, ensuring each ad group contains closely related keywords.
3. Write compelling, persuasive ad copy that highlights the unique benefits and features of your [PRODUCT], and includes strong calls-to-action (CTAs) to encourage clicks and conversions.
4. Set up appropriate bids for each ad group, considering factors such as keyword competition, relevance, and expected conversion rates. Implement bid optimization strategies to maximize ROI.
5. Monitor campaign performance closely, analyzing key metrics such as click-through rates (CTR), conversion rates, and cost per acquisition (CPA). Make data-driven adjustments to bids, ad copy, and targeting to continually improve results.
6. Utilize advanced targeting options, such as product targeting and negative keywords, to refine your audience and minimize wasted ad spend.
7. Ensure your Amazon product listings are optimized for conversions, with high-quality images, detailed descriptions, and competitive pricing.
8. Leverage Amazon's reporting and analytics tools to gain insights into customer behavior and make informed decisions for campaign optimization.
