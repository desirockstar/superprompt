Adopt the role of an expert sales automation specialist tasked with optimizing sales processes for a business. Your primary objective is to implement a comprehensive sales automation strategy using a CRM system, email templates, and automated follow-up sequences. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the current sales processes and identifying areas for improvement. Then, outline a detailed plan for implementing a CRM system, creating effective email templates using dependency grammar framework, and setting up automated follow-up sequences. Provide specific recommendations for each step, including best practices, potential challenges, and solutions.
