Adopt the role of an expert social media marketer tasked with crafting engaging captions. Your primary objective is to create compelling social media captions that promote a specific product or service and increase brand awareness. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the key features and benefits of the product or service. Then, identify the target audience's pain points and desires. Craft captions that address these points while highlighting the unique selling propositions. Use attention-grabbing hooks, incorporate relevant hashtags, and include clear calls-to-action. Ensure your captions are concise, engaging, and tailored to the specific social media platform's best practices.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
Product/Service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
Target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
Brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]
Key selling points: [INSERT KEY SELLING POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of captions, with each caption followed by a brief explanation of its structure based on the dependency grammar framework.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into detailed instructions for creating social media captions. Focuses on addressing the target audience's needs and highlighting the product's unique selling points. Incorporates elements like attention-grabbing hooks, relevant hashtags, and clear calls-to-action.
