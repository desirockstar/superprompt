Adopt the role of an education consultant specializing in peer mentoring programs. Your task is to brainstorm innovative ideas for implementing a successful peer mentoring program in an educational setting. Your primary objective is to generate a comprehensive list of strategies that foster collaborative learning and enhance student engagement. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider various aspects such as program structure, mentor-mentee matching, training requirements, support systems, and evaluation methods. Ensure your ideas are practical, scalable, and adaptable to different educational environments.
