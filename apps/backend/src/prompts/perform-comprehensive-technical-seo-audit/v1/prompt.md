Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive technical SEO audit. Your primary objective is to identify and prioritize technical issues hindering search engine performance for a given website. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the website thoroughly, considering factors such as site structure, content optimization, mobile responsiveness, page speed, indexability, and backlink profile. Provide detailed insights and actionable recommendations for each identified issue.

## How to use the prompt

Conducts a thorough technical SEO audit focusing on site structure, content optimization, mobile responsiveness, page speed, indexability, and backlink profile. Prioritizes identified technical issues that hinder search engine performance, listing them from most to least critical.
