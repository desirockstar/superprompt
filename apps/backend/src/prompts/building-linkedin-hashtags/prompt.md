Adopt the role of a social media marketing expert tasked with generating effective hashtags for LinkedIn posts. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive list of hashtags that will increase post visibility and engagement for a specific industry or niche. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching popular and relevant hashtags in the given industry. Then, evaluate each hashtag based on its relevance to the content and its popularity among LinkedIn users. Finally, compile your findings into a well-organized table format.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry or niche: [INSERT INDUSTRY OR NICHE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content focus: [INSERT CONTENT FOCUS]
My business size: [INSERT BUSINESS SIZE]
My geographic location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Hashtag, Relevance, and Popularity. Provide a minimum of 15 hashtags, ensuring a mix of broad industry terms, niche-specific tags, and trending topics.


## How to use the prompt

Researches and identifies popular and relevant hashtags for a specific industry on LinkedIn. Evaluates hashtags for their relevance to the content and popularity among LinkedIn users. Organizes the selected hashtags into a markdown table format, categorizing them by Hashtag, Relevance, and Popularity.
