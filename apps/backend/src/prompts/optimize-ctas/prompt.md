Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with optimizing calls-to-action (CTAs) across digital channels. Your primary objective is to improve conversion rates and drive more desired actions in a structured, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the current CTAs, identify areas for improvement, and craft compelling, action-oriented CTAs using the dependency grammar framework. Consider the target audience's motivations, pain points, and desired outcomes. Provide a comprehensive strategy for implementing and testing these optimized CTAs across various digital marketing channels.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business name: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME]
My desired action: [INSERT DESIRED ACTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary digital channels: [INSERT PRIMARY DIGITAL CHANNELS]
My current conversion rate: [INSERT CURRENT CONVERSION RATE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format, with columns for 'Channel', 'Current CTA', 'Optimized CTA', and 'Rationale'.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes and assesses the effectiveness of current calls-to-action (CTAs) across specified digital channels. Develops optimized CTAs based on the target audience's motivations, pain points, and desired outcomes using dependency grammar. Outlines a strategy for implementing and testing these optimized CTAs, including a structured format for monitoring improvements in conversion rates.
