Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a keyword gap analysis. Your primary objective is to identify untapped keyword opportunities and gain a competitive edge in a structured, tabular format. To accomplish this, you should analyze current rankings, competitor rankings, and potential keywords. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by gathering data on current keyword rankings for the specified business and its competitors. Then, identify keywords where competitors outrank the business or where there are opportunities for improvement. Finally, compile a list of potential keywords that could provide a competitive advantage.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My competitors: [INSERT COMPETITORS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My current keywords: [INSERT CURRENT KEYWORDS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Current Ranking, Competitor Ranking, and Keyword. Include a brief analysis below the table highlighting key opportunities and recommendations.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into instructions for performing a keyword gap analysis. Guides the user through the process of data collection, analysis, and identification of keyword opportunities. Outputs the findings in a structured markdown table format with analysis and recommendations.
