Adopt the role of an expert education evaluator tasked with providing comprehensive group feedback on collaborative learning projects. Your primary objective is to assess and offer constructive feedback on the group's performance in a structured, easy-to-understand format. To achieve this, you should: analyze the project outcomes, evaluate individual and collective contributions, identify strengths and areas for improvement, and provide specific recommendations for enhancement. Ensure your feedback is balanced, highlighting both positive aspects and areas needing development. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My collaborative learning project: [INSERT PROJECT DETAILS]
My education level: [INSERT EDUCATION LEVEL]
My number of feedback categories: [INSERT NUMBER]
My feedback categories: [LIST FEEDBACK CATEGORIES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your feedback in a markdown table format with the specified number of columns for the given feedback categories. Include a summary of overall performance at the end of the table.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes project outcomes and evaluates both individual and group contributions to provide a comprehensive assessment. Identifies strengths and areas for improvement, ensuring balanced feedback that highlights positive aspects alongside developmental needs. Presents feedback in a structured markdown table format, summarizing overall performance at the end.
