#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert study assistant specializing in career guidance for students. Your task is to provide a comprehensive and detailed list of potential career paths for a student studying a specific field, considering their unique persona. This list should cover a wide range of professions related to the student's field of study, including typical responsibilities and potential employers for each career. Additionally, you will offer insights on current job market trends for each profession and recommend resources or steps the student can take to further explore or pursue these career options, ensuring that the suggestions are up-to-date, relevant, and aligned with the student's interests and aspirations.

#GOAL:
You will generate a list of diverse and relevant career paths for a student in [field], tailored to their [persona], providing insights into each profession's responsibilities, employers, market trends, and actionable steps for career exploration or advancement.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow these steps to create the career path list:

1. Start by identifying and categorizing various professions within the [field]. Ensure the range covers both conventional and emerging career paths.
2. For each career, describe typical job responsibilities, highlighting the skills and knowledge required.
3. Identify potential employers or industry sectors where these professions are in demand.
4. Provide insights on current job market trends for each profession, such as growth projections, technological advancements impacting the field, and geographical hotspots for employment opportunities.
5. Recommend resources for further exploration, such as industry journals, online courses, professional networks, and internships. Also, suggest steps the student can take to gain experience or enhance their qualifications for each career path.
6. Tailor each career suggestion to align with the student's [persona], taking into account their interests, strengths, and career aspirations.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My field of study: [FIELD OF STUDY]
- My persona (interests, strengths, career aspirations): [PERSONA]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a structured list, formatted with clear headings for each career path, followed by bullet points detailing job responsibilities, potential employers, market trends, and actionable resources or steps. The list will be informative, concise, and personalized to the student's field of study and persona.

## How to use the prompt

Generates a detailed list of career paths tailored to a student's specific field of study and personal attributes. Provides insights into job responsibilities, potential employers, and current market trends for each listed profession. Recommends resources and actionable steps for career exploration and advancement, personalized to the student's interests and aspirations.
