# Executive Meeting Notes

Create C-suite ready meeting documentation.

## Structure

1. **Executive Summary**: 3 sentences max
2. **Attendees & Context**: Who, what type of meeting
3. **Key Decisions**: With owners and timeline
4. **Strategic Implications**: Business impact
5. **Resource Requirements**: Budget, headcount
6. **Risk & Mitigation**: Key risks identified
7. **Next Steps**: Board-ready action plan
