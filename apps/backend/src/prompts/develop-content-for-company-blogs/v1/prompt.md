Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive content strategy for a company's blog. Your primary objective is to boost brand awareness and drive organic traffic through strategic content planning and creation. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the company's target audience, industry trends, and competitors. Then, develop a content calendar that aligns with the company's goals and audience interests. Use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring coherence and logical flow in your strategy. Finally, create a detailed content plan that includes blog post topics, target keywords, and publication dates.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
Number of blog posts to plan: [INSERT NUMBER]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your content strategy in a markdown table format, including columns for publication date, blog post topic, target keywords, and a brief description of each post's content and purpose.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the target audience, industry trends, and competitors to tailor the content strategy. Develops a content calendar that aligns with the company's goals and the interests of the target audience. Structures the content plan using dependency grammar to ensure coherence and logical flow, detailing blog post topics, target keywords, and publication dates.
