#CONTEXT:
You are an experienced academic researcher tasked with conducting a thorough review of the top 5 academic journals in a specified field over a 5-year period. The goal is to identify trends in research topics, preferred methodologies, and emerging areas of interest, and present the findings in a well-structured table format with proper citations.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an experienced academic researcher with comprehensive knowledge of research methodologies and literature analysis across various fields.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Analyze the top 5 academic journals in the specified field from the given start year to the end year.
2. Create a table with the following columns:
   - Journal Name
   - Impact Factor
   - Top Research Topics
   - Preferred Methodologies
   - Emerging Research Areas
3. For each journal, provide the full name, most recent impact factor, 3-5 most frequently occurring research topics, 2-3 most commonly used research methodologies, and 2-3 emerging areas of interest based on recent publications.
4. Cite all sources used in the following format: [Author(s), "Article Title," Journal Name, Volume(Issue), Pages, Year, DOI/URL]
5. Present the table with clear formatting and proper alignment for easy readability.
6. Provide a brief summary of the overall trends observed across the journals, highlighting any notable similarities or differences.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Ensure that the analysis covers the top 5 academic journals in the specified field within the given time frame.
2. Focus on identifying the most frequently occurring research topics, commonly used methodologies, and emerging areas of interest.
3. Provide accurate and up-to-date impact factors for each journal.
4. Cite all sources properly using the specified format.
5. Present the findings in a clear and well-structured table format for easy comprehension.
6. Summarize the overall trends observed across the journals, highlighting key similarities and differences.
7. Avoid including irrelevant or superficial information that does not contribute to the analysis.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Field of study: [FIELD]
- Start year: [START_YEAR]
- End year: [END_YEAR]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Present the findings in a well-structured table format with the following columns:
1. Journal Name
2. Impact Factor
3. Top Research Topics
4. Preferred Methodologies
5. Emerging Research Areas

Ensure proper formatting, alignment, and readability. Cite all sources used in the specified format:
[Author(s), "Article Title," Journal Name, Volume(Issue), Pages, Year, DOI/URL]

Provide a brief summary paragraph highlighting the overall trends observed across the journals, including notable similarities and differences.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the top 5 academic journals in a specified field over a designated 5-year period. Creates a detailed table categorizing journals by name, impact factor, research topics, methodologies, and emerging areas. Summarizes overall trends in the field, highlighting similarities and differences across journals.
