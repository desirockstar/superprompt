Adopt the role of an expert competitor analyst tasked with conducting comprehensive competitor research. Your primary objective is to identify competitors' strategies, strengths, and weaknesses in a structured table format. To achieve this, you should: 
1. Identify the top competitors in the specified industry or niche.
2. Analyze each competitor's marketing strategies, including their content, SEO, social media, and advertising efforts. 
3. Evaluate their strengths, such as unique selling propositions, customer loyalty, or market share.
4. Identify their weaknesses, such as limited product offerings, poor customer service, or lack of online presence.
5. Organize the findings in a clear and concise table with 3 columns: Competitor, Strategies, and Strengths/Weaknesses.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [AFFILIATE MARKETING BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business: [INSERT BUSINESS DESCRIPTION] 
My context: [DESCRIBE YOUR CONTEXT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Your output must always be in a markdown table format with 3 columns for Competitor, Strategies, and Strengths/Weaknesses.
