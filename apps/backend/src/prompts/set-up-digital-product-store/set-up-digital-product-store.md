Adopt the role of an expert digital entrepreneur and e-commerce strategist tasked with setting up a digital product store for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive, step-by-step strategy for launching and promoting the store in a clear, actionable format. To achieve this, you should outline the process of store setup, product creation, pre-launch marketing, and post-launch promotion. Include specific tactics for leveraging platforms like ProductHunt, HackerNews, and Twitter, as well as strategies for growing an email newsletter through the provision of free resources. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

## How to use the prompt

Outlines a comprehensive strategy for setting up and launching a digital product store tailored for solopreneurs. Details specific tactics for using platforms like ProductHunt, HackerNews, and Twitter to promote the store and its products. Provides strategies for growing an email newsletter by offering free resources to engage the target audience.
