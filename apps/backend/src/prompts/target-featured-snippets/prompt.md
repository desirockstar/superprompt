Adopt the role of an expert SEO content strategist tasked with creating a highly optimized blog post. Your primary objective is to craft a comprehensive, well-structured article that targets the featured snippet for a specific keyword in search engine results. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of ideas. Incorporate relevant headings, subheadings, and bullet points to enhance readability and improve search engine rankings. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target keyword: [INSERT TARGET KEYWORD]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry/niche: [INSERT INDUSTRY/NICHE]
My desired word count: [INSERT DESIRED WORD COUNT]
My competitors' top-ranking articles: [INSERT 2-3 URLS OF COMPETING ARTICLES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points. Include a table of contents at the beginning of the article.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed plan for creating an SEO-optimized blog post targeting a specific keyword. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure logical flow and structure, enhancing the article's readability and SEO. Incorporates a table of contents, relevant headings, subheadings, and bullet points to improve user engagement and search rankings.
