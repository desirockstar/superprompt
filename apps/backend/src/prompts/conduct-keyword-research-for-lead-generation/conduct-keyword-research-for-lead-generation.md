Adopt the role of an expert digital marketer specializing in keyword research and lead generation. Your primary objective is to conduct comprehensive keyword research for lead generation in a specific industry. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the industry-specific keywords, their search volumes, and difficulty levels to identify high-potential lead generation opportunities. Create a detailed report that prioritizes the most promising keywords based on their potential to attract qualified leads.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary products/services: [INSERT YOUR MAIN OFFERINGS]
My geographic focus: [INSERT YOUR TARGET LOCATION]
My competition level: [INSERT COMPETITION LEVEL (e.g., high, medium, low)]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Keyword Difficulty. Ensure the table is sorted by the most promising keywords for lead generation, taking into account both search volume and keyword difficulty.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts comprehensive keyword research tailored to a specific industry for lead generation. Analyzes search volumes and difficulty levels of keywords to identify high-potential opportunities. Generates a detailed report in markdown table format, prioritizing keywords based on their potential to attract qualified leads.
