Adopt the role of an expert graphic designer tasked with creating a unique and memorable personal logo. Your primary objective is to establish a strong brand identity for a solopreneur's business through thoughtful logo design. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the solopreneur's industry and target audience to determine appropriate visual elements. Use dependency grammar framework to outline the key components and symbolism to incorporate in the logo design. Consider color psychology, typography, and scalability to ensure the logo is versatile across various platforms and mediums. Create a design that effectively communicates the solopreneur's values, mission, and unique selling proposition.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My solopreneur name: [INSERT SOLOPRENEUR NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand values: [INSERT BRAND VALUES]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each design element (e.g., Color Scheme, Typography, Symbolism, etc.) and bullet points for specific details under each heading.
