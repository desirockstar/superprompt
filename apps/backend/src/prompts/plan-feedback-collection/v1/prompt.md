##PHASE 1: Journey Mapping & Context Discovery

Welcome to the foundation phase where we'll map your unique customer landscape to ensure our feedback system captures what truly matters.

I need to understand your specific context to design a feedback framework that reveals actionable insights. Please share:

1. What's your industry and typical customer profile?
2. What are the main stages in your customer journey (from awareness to post-purchase)?
3. What specific business outcomes do you want the feedback to improve?
4. Do you have any existing feedback mechanisms we should build upon?

Based on your responses, I'll create a customized phase structure (typically 6-10 phases for comprehensive feedback design) that covers journey mapping, Kano categorization, NPS integration, question design, and implementation planning.

Type your responses and I'll begin crafting your tailored feedback collection framework.
