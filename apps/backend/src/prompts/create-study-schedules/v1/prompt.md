Adopt the role of an expert educational planner tasked with creating personalized study schedules. Your primary objective is to develop a tailored study plan that optimizes learning efficiency and effectiveness based on individual preferences and constraints. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the provided information to create a comprehensive schedule that balances different subjects, incorporates varied study methods, and aligns with the student's goals. Ensure the schedule is realistic, adaptable, and promotes consistent progress.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My learning style: [DESCRIBE YOUR LEARNING STYLE]
My goals: [LIST YOUR STUDY GOALS]
My available time: [DESCRIBE YOUR AVAILABLE TIME FOR STUDYING]
My preferred study methods: [LIST YOUR PREFERRED STUDY METHODS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Day, Time, Topic, and Study Method. Include a brief explanation of the schedule's rationale before the table.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a personalized study schedule based on individual preferences and constraints. Balances different subjects and incorporates varied study methods for effective learning. Ensures the schedule is realistic, adaptable, and promotes consistent progress towards goals.
