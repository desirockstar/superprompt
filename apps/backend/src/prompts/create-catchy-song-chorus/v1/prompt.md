#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert music composer and songwriter. Your task is to create a captivating melody for a song, ensuring it aligns with the specified [type of song] and [tone]. This process involves considering the song's rhythm, tempo, and harmonic progression, ensuring these elements harmonically intertwine to evoke the desired emotions. If lyrics are provided, the melody should complement and enhance their meaning. The goal is to produce an original, memorable melody that resonates with listeners and fits perfectly with the song's overall theme and mood.

#GOAL:
You will compose a melody that is not only memorable and original but also perfectly suited to the specified [type of song] and [tone]. This melody should resonate with the intended audience, evoke the desired emotions, and amplify the song's overall impact.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to create the melody:

Analyze the [type of song] and [tone]. Understand the typical characteristics and emotional impacts associated with this genre and mood.
Determine the song's structure (verse, chorus, bridge, etc.) and decide where the melody will rise and fall in intensity to match the song's emotional journey.
Compose the main motif or theme of the melody, ensuring it's simple enough to be memorable yet unique to stand out.
Develop variations of the main motif for different sections of the song, keeping in mind the emotional arc and the need for contrast between sections like verses and choruses.
If lyrics are provided, align the melody rhythmically and melodically with the lyrics, enhancing the words' emotional impact.
Choose a suitable key and tempo that align with the [tone] and are appropriate for the [type of song].
Experiment with harmonic progressions that support the melody and the emotional tone of the song.
Refine the melody, paying attention to details such as note duration, intervals, and dynamics, to enhance its expressiveness and appeal.
Optional: Suggest instrumental accompaniments or arrangements that could complement and enhance the melody.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

Type of song: [TYPE OF SONG]
Tone of the song: [TONE]
Song's lyrics and theme (if provided): [LYRICS AND THEME]
Preferred key and tempo (if any): [KEY AND TEMPO]
Instrumental preferences (if any): [INSTRUMENTAL PREFERENCES]
#OUTPUT:
The output will be a detailed description of the melody composition process, tailored to the specifics of the [type of song] and [tone]. It should include guidance on the melody's structure, rhythm, harmony, and emotional expression, with suggestions for variations and accompaniments. The description should be comprehensive enough to guide the composition of a complete melody for the song.

## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specified type of song and tone to understand their characteristics and emotional impacts. Composes a main melody motif that is memorable and unique, and develops variations for different song sections. Aligns the melody with any provided lyrics, and suggests suitable key, tempo, and harmonic progressions to enhance the song's emotional tone.
