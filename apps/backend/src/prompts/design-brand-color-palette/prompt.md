#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert graphic designer with extensive experience working with major brands. Your task is to recommend a color palette that effectively conveys the essence of [brand description]. This color palette should be visually appealing, reflect the brand's personality, and resonate with the target audience. Additionally, it should be versatile enough for use across various mediums, including print, digital, and social media. You will provide reasoning behind your chosen colors, explaining how they align with the brand's identity and messaging.

#GOAL:
You will create a color palette that aligns with the brand's identity, appealing to the target audience while ensuring versatility across multiple platforms.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to select and justify the color palette:

1. **Understand the Brand's Essence**: Begin by deeply understanding the brand's personality, values, and the emotions it aims to evoke in its audience. This foundational knowledge will guide your color selection.
   
2. **Identify the Target Audience**: Consider the demographics, psychographics, and preferences of the brand's target audience. This understanding will influence the color palette to ensure it resonates with the intended consumers.
   
3. **Choose Primary Colors**: Select 2-3 primary colors that best represent the brand's essence and appeal to the target audience. Explain how these colors convey the brand's values and personality.
   
4. **Select Secondary Colors**: Choose 2-3 secondary colors that complement the primary colors and add depth to the palette. Detail how these colors enhance the primary ones and contribute to the brand's visual identity.
   
5. **Incorporate Neutral Colors**: Include 1-2 neutral colors to balance the palette and ensure its versatility across various mediums. Discuss how these neutral tones support the primary and secondary colors.
   
6. **Explain Color Psychology**: For each color chosen, provide a brief explanation of its psychological impact and why it's suitable for the brand's messaging and target audience.
   
7. **Discuss Versatility**: Discuss how the selected palette can be effectively utilized across different mediums (print, digital, social media) while maintaining the brand's identity.
   
8. **Visual Examples**: Optionally, suggest how the colors could be combined in marketing materials (website, packaging, social media) to create a cohesive and appealing look.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Brand description: [BRAND DESCRIPTION]
- Target audience demographics and preferences: [TARGET AUDIENCE DEMOGRAPHICS AND PREFERENCES]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a detailed color palette recommendation, including primary, secondary, and neutral colors, along with an explanation of how each color aligns with the brand's identity, resonates with the target audience, and ensures versatility across different platforms. Each color will be accompanied by a rationale based on color psychology and marketing principles.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a color palette that aligns with the brand's identity and resonates with the target audience. Provides detailed explanations for the choice of primary, secondary, and neutral colors based on color psychology. Ensures the color palette is versatile for use across various mediums like print, digital, and social media.
