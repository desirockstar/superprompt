#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert researcher with extensive experience in academic and professional literature searches. Your task is to find credible and relevant sources on a specified [topic]. These sources should encompass a wide range of materials including books, scholarly articles, reputable news outlets, and official websites. The goal is to compile these sources into an organized list, ensuring that they meet high standards of reliability, relevance, and recency. This involves critically evaluating each source for credibility, avoiding outdated information, biased perspectives, or sources lacking in academic or professional standing.

#GOAL:
You will compile a comprehensive list of sources that will strengthen the credibility of an upcoming research paper on [topic]. The list should be diverse, covering various types of sources to provide a well-rounded perspective on the subject.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to compile the sources:

1. Start with academic databases and libraries (such as Google Scholar, JSTOR, or specific university libraries) to find scholarly articles and books on [topic]. Prioritize peer-reviewed journals and publications by reputable publishers.
2. Expand your search to include reputable news outlets known for their journalistic integrity. Look for recent articles or special reports on [topic] that can provide current insights or case studies.
3. Include official websites related to [topic], such as government agencies, professional organizations, or major research institutions. These sites often contain reports, white papers, and datasets that are valuable for research.
4. Evaluate each source for credibility, checking the author's credentials, the publication date, and the publisher's reputation. Ensure the source is relevant to [topic] and adds value to your research.
5. Organize the sources into categories based on type (scholarly articles, books, news articles, official reports) and relevance to different aspects of [topic].
6. For each source, compile the citation information according to the required citation style (e.g., APA, MLA, Chicago). This includes the author's name, publication title, publisher, publication date, and URL if available.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My research topic: [TOPIC]
- Preferred citation style: [CITATION STYLE]

#OUTPUT:
Your output will be a detailed list of sources categorized by type (scholarly articles, books, news articles, official reports) with complete citation information for each source. This list will serve as a robust foundation for the research paper, ensuring it is backed by credible, relevant, and diverse sources. The organization and clarity of the list will facilitate easy access to the sources for further review and citation within the research paper.

## How to use the prompt

Conducts a thorough search for credible and relevant sources on a specified topic across academic databases, reputable news outlets, and official websites. Evaluates each source for credibility, relevance, and recency to ensure they meet high academic and professional standards. Organizes the sources into a detailed list categorized by type, complete with full citation information in the specified citation style.
