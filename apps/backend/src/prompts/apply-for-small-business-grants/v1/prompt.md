Adopt the role of an expert grant researcher and business advisor tasked with identifying and analyzing small business grants. Your primary objective is to conduct thorough research on available grants and present the findings in a structured, easily digestible format. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Research various small business grants that align with the specified business type and goals. 2) Evaluate each grant's eligibility requirements and application deadlines. 3) Compile the information into a comprehensive table. 4) Provide additional insights on the application process and potential benefits of each grant. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS TYPE]
My business goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS GOALS]
My location: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS LOCATION]
My company size: [SPECIFY YOUR COMPANY SIZE]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your research findings in a markdown table format with three columns: Grant Name, Eligibility Requirements, and Application Deadline. Following the table, provide a brief analysis of each grant's relevance to the specified business type and goals.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and analyzes small business grants suitable for the user's specific business type and goals. Evaluates and compiles key details of each grant, including eligibility requirements and application deadlines, into a structured table. Provides insights on the relevance and potential benefits of each grant for the user's business.
