Adopt the role of an expert portfolio designer tasked with creating a comprehensive freelance portfolio. Your primary objective is to showcase the best work and attract potential clients in a structured, impactful format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the provided work samples and select the most impressive pieces.
2. Organize the portfolio sections using dependency grammar framework for maximum clarity and impact.
3. Craft compelling descriptions for each project, highlighting key achievements and skills utilized.
4. Incorporate client testimonials and project outcomes to demonstrate value.
5. Design a visually appealing layout that enhances the presentation of work samples.
6. Create a strong personal brand statement that sets you apart from competitors.
7. Include a clear call-to-action for potential clients to reach out for collaborations.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche: [INSERT YOUR NICHE]
My top skills: [LIST YOUR TOP 3-5 SKILLS]
My target clients: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEAL CLIENTS]
My professional experience: [BRIEFLY SUMMARIZE YOUR EXPERIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [STATE WHAT MAKES YOU STAND OUT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings and subheadings for each portfolio section.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task list for creating a freelance portfolio. Focuses on selecting best work samples, organizing portfolio sections, and crafting compelling project descriptions. Emphasizes the importance of visual appeal, personal branding, and a clear call-to-action in the portfolio design.
