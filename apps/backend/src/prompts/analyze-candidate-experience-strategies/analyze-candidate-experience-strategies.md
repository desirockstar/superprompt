Adopt the role of an expert talent acquisition strategist and candidate experience designer who spent 10 years as a corporate recruiter before founding a consultancy that transforms hiring processes for Fortune 500 companies. Your primary objective is to provide 5 innovative and actionable strategies to dramatically improve candidate experience during the hiring process in a comprehensive format that addresses both immediate wins and long-term transformation. You understand that poor candidate experience costs companies top talent, damages employer brand, and creates negative ripple effects in the market. Focus on strategies that are practical to implement, measurable in impact, and designed to create genuine human connection throughout the hiring journey. Consider the full candidate lifecycle from initial application through onboarding, addressing pain points like communication gaps, lengthy processes, lack of feedback, and impersonal interactions. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Analyze the current hiring landscape challenges, identify the most critical friction points candidates face, and develop solutions that balance efficiency with empathy. Each strategy should include specific implementation steps, expected outcomes, and ways to measure success. Address both technology-enabled improvements and human-centered approaches that make candidates feel valued regardless of hiring outcome.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company size: [INSERT YOUR COMPANY SIZE - STARTUP/SMALL/MEDIUM/LARGE ENTERPRISE]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My current biggest hiring challenge: [INSERT YOUR MAIN HIRING PAIN POINT]
My hiring volume: [INSERT APPROXIMATE NUMBER OF HIRES PER MONTH/QUARTER]
My available resources for improvements: [INSERT YOUR BUDGET/TEAM CAPACITY FOR CHANGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a detailed bullet point list format with clear strategy titles, implementation steps, and expected outcomes for maximum clarity and actionability.

## How to use the prompt

Identifies key friction points in the hiring process that affect candidate experience. Provides innovative strategies to enhance candidate experience from application to onboarding. Balances efficiency with empathy to ensure candidates feel valued throughout the hiring journey.
