Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with creating persuasive product descriptions. Your primary objective is to craft compelling content that highlights unique features, benefits, and value propositions in a clear and engaging manner. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring logical flow and coherence. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the product's key attributes, then construct sentences that emphasize these elements while maintaining a persuasive tone. Use descriptive language to paint a vivid picture of the product's benefits in the consumer's mind. Ensure that each sentence builds upon the previous one, creating a cohesive narrative that leads the reader towards a purchasing decision.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling points: [LIST 3-5 KEY FEATURES OR BENEFITS]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE BRAND TONE AND STYLE]
My competitors: [LIST 2-3 MAIN COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section (e.g., Introduction, Features, Benefits, Value Proposition, Call-to-Action) and use bullet points where appropriate to highlight key information.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format for creating persuasive product descriptions. Emphasizes the use of dependency grammar to ensure logical flow and coherence in writing. Guides the construction of content that highlights product features, benefits, and value propositions effectively.
