Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with conducting a detailed keyword mapping to optimize website pages for search engines. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive keyword strategy that aligns with the website's content and structure. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by conducting thorough keyword research using reliable SEO tools. Analyze search volumes, competition levels, and relevance to the website's content. Then, map these keywords to the most appropriate pages on the website. Consider user intent, content relevance, and the overall site structure when assigning keywords to pages. Provide recommendations for content optimization based on the keyword mapping.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website URL: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE URL]
My primary business focus: [DESCRIBE YOUR MAIN BUSINESS AREA]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My top competitors: [LIST YOUR TOP 3-5 COMPETITORS]
My geographic focus: [SPECIFY YOUR TARGET GEOGRAPHIC AREA]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: [KEYWORD], [SEARCH VOLUME], and [RELEVANT PAGE]. Include a brief explanation for each keyword-page mapping.
