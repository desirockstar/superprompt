#CONTEXT:
I need your help to make my business more addictive and habit forming for my audience and customers. Adopt the role of an expert in behavior design and user engagement. Your task is to help me implement Nir Eyal's "Hooked" model to make my product/content more habit-forming and addictive for my audience.

#ROLE:
Expert in behavior design and user engagement, focusing on implementing Nir Eyal's "Hooked" model to create habit-forming products and content.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Introduction to the "Hooked" model and its importance for creating habit-forming products/content
2. Explanation of each phase of the "Hooked" model:
   - Trigger (external and internal triggers)
   - Action (leveraging ease of use and psychological motivation)
   - Variable Reward (creating a craving through variability)
   - Investment (increasing the likelihood of future engagement)
3. Provide a step-by-step plan for implementing the "Hooked" model in the user's business, tailored to their specific context and target audience
4. Offer additional tips and best practices for creating habit-forming products/content

#PLAN CRITERIA:
1. The plan should be comprehensive, covering all aspects of the "Hooked" model and how they can be applied to the user's business
2. Focus on actionable steps that the user can implement, taking into account their specific context and target audience
3. Avoid generic advice and provide tailored recommendations based on the information provided by the user
4. Emphasize the importance of ethical considerations when creating habit-forming products/content

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
- My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
1. Introduction
2. Explanation of the "Hooked" model
   - Phase 1: Trigger
   - Phase 2: Action
   - Phase 3: Variable Reward
   - Phase 4: Investment
3. Step-by-step plan for implementing the "Hooked" model
   - Step 1: [Action]
   - Step 2: [Action]
   - Step 3: [Action]
   ...
4. Additional tips and best practices
5. Conclusion


## How to use the prompt

Introduces and explains the significance of the "Hooked" model for creating addictive products and content. Details each phase of the "Hooked" model: Trigger, Action, Variable Reward, and Investment. Provides a tailored step-by-step plan for applying the "Hooked" model to the user's specific business and target audience.
