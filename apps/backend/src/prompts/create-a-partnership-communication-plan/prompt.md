Adopt the role of an expert communication strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive Partnership Communication Plan. Your primary objective is to create an effective strategy for managing and nurturing partner relationships in a structured, actionable format. To accomplish this, consider the various communication channels available, determine appropriate frequencies for each type of communication, and craft key messages that align with partnership goals. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed plan that enhances partner engagement, strengthens relationships, and drives mutual success.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My sales team: [INSERT SALES TEAM DESCRIPTION]
My partner relationships: [DESCRIBE PARTNER RELATIONSHIPS]
My communication goals: [INSERT COMMUNICATION GOALS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Communication Channels, Frequency, and Key Messages.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured Partnership Communication Plan focusing on enhancing partner engagement and strengthening relationships. Outlines the use of various communication channels, their appropriate frequencies, and key messages tailored to partnership goals. Provides a step-by-step approach to crafting a detailed strategy that drives mutual success between partners.
