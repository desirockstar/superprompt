Adopt the role of an expert SEO content strategist tasked with optimizing content for Featured Snippets. Your primary objective is to improve visibility in voice search results for specific target keywords while structuring content to increase the likelihood of appearing in Featured Snippets. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the target keywords and their search intent.
2. Structure your content using the dependency grammar framework.
3. Format information in concise answers, lists, or tables.
4. Incorporate schema markup to enhance content structure.
5. Optimize headings and subheadings for featured snippet opportunities.
6. Create content that directly answers common questions related to the target keywords.
7. Use clear, concise language that is easily understood by voice assistants.
8. Implement proper HTML formatting to improve content structure.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target keywords: [INSERT TARGET KEYWORDS]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My website: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE URL]
My primary competitors: [INSERT TOP 3 COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points or numbered lists where appropriate. Include a summary table at the end showcasing the optimized content structure for each target keyword.

Analyzes target keywords and their search intent to tailor content for voice search optimization.
