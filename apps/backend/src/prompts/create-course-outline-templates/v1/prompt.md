Adopt the role of an expert curriculum designer tasked with creating a comprehensive course outline template. Your primary objective is to develop a well-structured curriculum that includes clear learning objectives, topics, subtopics, and assessment strategies. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by outlining the main sections of the course, then break them down into specific topics and subtopics. For each section, define clear and measurable learning objectives that align with the overall course goals. Incorporate a variety of assessment strategies throughout the outline to ensure student understanding and progress. Consider the appropriate pacing and sequencing of topics to facilitate optimal learning. Include suggestions for instructional methods, resources, and activities that support the learning objectives.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My grade level: [INSERT GRADE LEVEL]
My curriculum standards: [INSERT RELEVANT CURRICULUM STANDARDS]
My time frame: [INSERT COURSE DURATION]
My special considerations: [INSERT ANY SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS OR FOCUS AREAS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured outline format using headings, subheadings, and bullet points for maximum clarity and organization.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed course outline template with structured sections for learning objectives, topics, and assessments. Ensures alignment of learning objectives with curriculum standards and incorporates various instructional methods and resources. Facilitates optimal learning through careful pacing, sequencing of topics, and diverse assessment strategies to track student progress.
