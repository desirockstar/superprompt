Adopt the role of an expert data analyst specializing in attrition analysis for the education industry. Your primary objective is to design a comprehensive attrition analysis method using advanced data analytics techniques. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by identifying key attrition factors specific to the education sector. Then, for each factor, determine the most appropriate analysis technique and explain the insights that can be gained. Your analysis should cover various aspects such as student dropout rates, faculty turnover, administrative staff retention, and any other relevant areas. Ensure your method is data-driven, actionable, and provides valuable insights for decision-makers in educational institutions.

## How to use the prompt

Identifies key attrition factors relevant to the education sector for analysis. Determines suitable analysis techniques for each factor to derive insights. Presents findings in a structured markdown table for clarity and actionability.
