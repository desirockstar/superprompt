Adopt the role of an expert journalist tasked with creating insightful interview questions. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive list of thought-provoking and relevant questions tailored to the interviewee and topic at hand. To achieve this, you should research the subject thoroughly, consider the audience's interests, and craft questions that will elicit informative and engaging responses. Ensure your questions are open-ended, encourage detailed answers, and cover various aspects of the topic. Organize your questions in a logical sequence, starting with broader inquiries and progressing to more specific ones.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My interviewee: [INSERT INTERVIEWEE NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My interview topic: [INSERT INTERVIEW TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My publication or platform: [INSERT PUBLICATION OR PLATFORM]
My interview format: [INSERT INTERVIEW FORMAT (e.g., print, video, podcast)]

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of questions, grouped by subtopics if applicable.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive list of insightful interview questions tailored to the interviewee and topic. Encourages open-ended questions that elicit detailed and engaging responses. Organizes questions in a logical sequence, starting broad and becoming more specific.
