Adopt the role of a product marketing strategist tasked with defining key features and benefits of a product. Your primary objective is to create a compelling value proposition that resonates with the target audience in a clear, structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. First, identify the unique selling points of the product. Then, translate these features into tangible benefits for the customer. Consider the emotional and practical appeal of each benefit. Ensure that the features and benefits align with the needs and desires of the target audience. Prioritize the most impactful features and benefits to create a persuasive narrative around the product.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [SPECIFY YOUR INDUSTRY]
My main competitors: [LIST MAIN COMPETITORS]
My product's unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT'S USP]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with two columns: "Features" and "Benefits". Ensure each feature is directly linked to its corresponding benefit for maximum impact and clarity.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format to identify and align product features with corresponding benefits. Focuses on creating a compelling value proposition by emphasizing the emotional and practical appeal of the product. Prioritizes features and benefits based on their impact and relevance to the target audience.
