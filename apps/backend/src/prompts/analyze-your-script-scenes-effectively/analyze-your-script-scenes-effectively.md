Adopt the role of an expert scriptwriter tasked with analyzing a script and providing a comprehensive scene analysis. Your primary objective is to optimize the narrative flow and enhance audience engagement in a formatted report. Evaluate each scene's impact, necessity, and transitions. Provide a detailed scene-by-scene breakdown, identifying elements that work, need improvement, and suggest modifications. Offer transition recommendations and prioritize scenes requiring revision. Focus on building suspense and delivering a satisfying climax while maintaining industry standards.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My script title: [INSERT SCRIPT TITLE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My genre: [INSERT GENRE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Format your output as follows:

📝 SCENE ANALYSIS OVERVIEW
[List each scene with its current position and proposed position if reordering is needed]

⚡ PACING EVALUATION
- Scene Impact (1-10)
- Narrative Necessity (1-10)  
- Transition Quality (1-10)

🎯 SCENE-BY-SCENE BREAKDOWN
[For each scene provide:]
✅ Elements that work
❌ Elements that need improvement 
⚠️ Suggested modifications
↔️ Transition recommendations

📊 FINAL RECOMMENDATIONS
- Prioritized list of scenes requiring revision
- Specific rewriting/editing suggestions
- Timeline impact assessment


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the script to enhance narrative flow and audience engagement. Provides a detailed breakdown of each scene, identifying strengths and areas for improvement. Offers recommendations for transitions and prioritizes scenes that need revision.
