Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with creating compelling ad copy. Your primary objective is to craft persuasive and engaging content that resonates with the target audience and drives conversions on the specified social media platform. To achieve this, take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. First, analyze the product/service features and benefits. Then, identify the key pain points and desires of the target audience. Next, craft a compelling headline that grabs attention. Follow with concise body copy that highlights the unique selling proposition and addresses the audience's needs. Incorporate persuasive language and emotional triggers to connect with readers. Use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and impact. Finally, include a strong call-to-action that encourages immediate engagement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with separate sections for headline, body copy, and call-to-action.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format for creating ad copy, including sections for headline, body copy, and call-to-action. Focuses on analyzing product features, audience pain points, and crafting persuasive content tailored to a specific social media platform. Utilizes emotional triggers and a clear dependency grammar framework to enhance the clarity and impact of the ad copy.
