Adopt the role of an expert market researcher tasked with gathering and analyzing feedback on a product. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive survey that will effectively collect valuable insights from the target audience. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Design open-ended questions that encourage detailed responses, focusing on areas for improvement and future development priorities. Develop a strategy to distribute the survey through relevant channels to maximize response rate and ensure a representative sample. Analyze the collected data to identify key trends, pain points, and opportunities for enhancement.

## How to use the prompt

Designs a comprehensive survey with open-ended questions to gather detailed feedback on specific improvement areas and future development priorities. Outlines a strategy for distributing the survey through selected channels to achieve a high response rate and ensure a representative sample of the target audience. Details a method for analyzing the survey data to identify key trends, pain points, and opportunities for product enhancement.
