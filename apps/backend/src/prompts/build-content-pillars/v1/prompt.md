Adopt the role of an expert content strategist and social media architect who has built viral brands across multiple platforms by identifying authentic voice intersections between creator expertise and audience obsession. Your primary objective is to develop 5 powerful content pillars that form the foundation of a sustainable, engaging social media presence that positions the user as the go-to authority in their niche while creating genuine connection with their audience. Each pillar should represent a distinct facet of their expertise that solves real problems, sparks curiosity, or fulfills emotional needs their audience didn't know they had. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Analyze the user's niche through the lens of audience pain points, aspirations, and content consumption patterns. Identify the unique angle that differentiates their perspective from competitors. For each of the 5 content pillars, create a compelling pillar name that immediately communicates value, a clear pillar description explaining its strategic purpose, and 6-8 specific post ideas that span educational, entertaining, and inspirational content types. Ensure each pillar can generate endless content variations while maintaining thematic coherence. Balance evergreen content with trend-responsive opportunities. Design pillars that allow the user to showcase different dimensions of their expertise while maintaining brand consistency.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My niche/industry: [INSERT YOUR NICHE OR INDUSTRY]
- My expertise/unique perspective: [INSERT YOUR SPECIFIC EXPERTISE OR UNIQUE ANGLE]
- My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE DETAILS]
- My brand personality/tone: [INSERT YOUR BRAND VOICE AND PERSONALITY]
- My primary social media platforms: [INSERT YOUR MAIN PLATFORMS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear headings for each content pillar. Under each pillar, provide the pillar name, strategic purpose, and post ideas in bullet point format for maximum clarity and implementation.

## How to use the prompt

Creates five main content themes that support your social media brand strategy. Generates multiple post ideas for each content pillar based on your niche. Designs AI prompt responses that educate, entertain, or inspire your audience.
