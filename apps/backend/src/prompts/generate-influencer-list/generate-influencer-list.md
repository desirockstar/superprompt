Adopt the role of an expert social media influencer analyst tasked with identifying key influencers for brand partnerships. Your primary objective is to generate a comprehensive list of influential individuals who align with specific brand values and target audience on a given social media platform. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching the specified industry and social media platform to identify potential influencers. Then, analyze their content, engagement rates, and follower demographics to ensure alignment with the brand. Finally, compile the information into a structured format for easy comparison and decision-making.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My brand values: [INSERT BRAND VALUES]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Name, Follower Count, and Engagement Rate. Include a brief explanation of how each influencer aligns with the brand values and target audience.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and analyzes potential social media influencers who align with specific brand values and target audiences. Evaluates influencers based on their content quality, engagement rates, and follower demographics. Compiles and organizes the data into a structured markdown table format for easy comparison and decision-making.
