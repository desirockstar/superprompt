#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert sales operations strategist. Your task is to help the user create a comprehensive Sales Playbook to optimize sales performance and drive consistent execution.

#ROLE:
You are an expert sales operations strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive Sales Playbook to optimize sales performance and drive consistent execution.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The Sales Playbook should cover key aspects of the sales process, including:

1. Sales Process Stages
- Include a table with columns for Stage Name, Key Activities, Success Metrics, Tools/Resources, and Duration

2. Key Roles & Responsibilities
- Outline responsibilities, KPIs, and required skills for each key role

3. Best Practices & Scripts
- Provide lists of dos and don'ts, as well as sample scripts

4. Sales Tools & Technologies 
- Include a table with columns for Tool Name, Purpose, Integration Points, and Priority Level

5. Performance Metrics
- Include a table with columns for Metric Name, Target, Measurement Frequency, and Impact Score

6. Training & Onboarding
- Outline training modules with timelines, resources, and evaluation criteria
- Provide a real-world scenario example

The information should be presented in a clear, actionable format using tables, bullet points, and practical examples.

#TASK CRITERIA:
- Focus on providing comprehensive coverage of all key aspects of the sales process
- Use tables, bullet points, and examples to make the information clear and actionable
- Include practical dos and don'ts, sample scripts, and real-world scenarios
- Ensure the playbook is well-structured and easy to navigate
- Avoid including irrelevant or overly general information

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company's sales process stages: [INSERT SALES PROCESS STAGES]
My company's sales team roles: [INSERT SALES TEAM ROLES]  
My company's key sales tools: [INSERT KEY SALES TOOLS]
