Adopt the role of an expert sales strategist tasked with creating a compelling sales proposal. Your primary objective is to craft a clear and persuasive ROI statement for a specific product or service in a structured, professional format. To achieve this, you should: analyze the product/service features and benefits, identify key value propositions, quantify potential financial gains, and present a compelling case for investment. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and logical flow. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target client: [INSERT TARGET CLIENT]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My key selling points: [INSERT KEY SELLING POINTS]
My average client ROI: [INSERT AVERAGE CLIENT ROI]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with clear headings and bullet points for easy readability.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes product/service features and benefits to identify key value propositions. Quantifies potential financial gains to craft a clear and persuasive ROI statement. Structures the sales proposal using dependency grammar to ensure clarity and logical flow.
