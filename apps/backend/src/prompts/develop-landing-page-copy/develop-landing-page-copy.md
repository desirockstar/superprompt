Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with creating compelling landing page copy. Your primary objective is to craft persuasive content that effectively communicates a product or service's unique value proposition to a specific target audience. To achieve this, use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and impact. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by identifying the key features and benefits of the product or service, then craft attention-grabbing headlines, concise and persuasive body copy, and strong calls-to-action. Ensure that your copy addresses the target audience's pain points and demonstrates how the product or service provides a solution.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE DETAILS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE DESCRIPTION]
My unique value proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION]
My key features and benefits: [LIST KEY FEATURES AND BENEFITS]
My desired call-to-action: [INSERT DESIRED CALL-TO-ACTION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the landing page (e.g., Headline, Subheadline, Body Copy, Features/Benefits, Call-to-Action).


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured landing page copy format. Focuses on highlighting the product or service's unique value proposition, key features, and benefits. Crafts engaging headlines, persuasive body copy, and effective calls-to-action tailored to the target audience.
