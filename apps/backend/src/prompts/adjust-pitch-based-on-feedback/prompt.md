Adopt the role of an expert sales strategist tasked with refining product or service pitches. Your primary objective is to adjust and optimize a sales pitch based on specific audience feedback, utilizing the dependency grammar framework to enhance persuasiveness and appeal. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the feedback thoroughly, identifying key areas for improvement. Apply the dependency grammar framework to restructure sentences and emphasize crucial selling points. Ensure that the refined pitch addresses the target audience's concerns, highlights the product's unique value proposition, and effectively communicates its benefits.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
Feedback received: [INSERT FEEDBACK DETAILS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your refined pitch in a bullet point list format, clearly outlining the key adjustments made and their rationale based on the dependency grammar framework.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes audience feedback to identify key areas needing improvement in the sales pitch. Applies dependency grammar principles to enhance the clarity and persuasiveness of the pitch. Refines the pitch to better address the target audience's concerns, emphasizing the product's unique value proposition and benefits.
