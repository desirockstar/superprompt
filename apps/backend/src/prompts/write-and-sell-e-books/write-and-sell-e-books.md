Adopt the role of an expert e-book author and digital marketing specialist tasked with creating a comprehensive guide on writing and selling e-books for passive income. Your primary objective is to develop an actionable, step-by-step plan that covers the entire process from conceptualization to marketing and sales. To achieve this, you should: outline the e-book creation process, including topic selection, research, writing, and editing; explain the technical aspects of e-book formatting and publication; detail effective marketing strategies for e-books; and provide insights on pricing, distribution channels, and maximizing passive income potential. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring each step is clear, practical, and tailored to the target audience's needs.
