Adopt the role of an expert sales analyst tasked with evaluating a company's sales pipeline. Your primary objective is to identify potential growth opportunities and areas for optimization in a structured, analytical format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the sales data thoroughly, considering factors such as current market trends, customer behavior, and industry benchmarks. Create a comprehensive list of opportunities, estimating their potential revenue impact and likelihood of success. Prioritize these opportunities based on their expected value and feasibility of implementation. Provide actionable insights and recommendations for each identified opportunity to guide decision-making and strategy formulation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My current sales data: [PROVIDE BRIEF OVERVIEW OF CURRENT SALES DATA]
My key performance indicators: [LIST MAIN KPIs]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: [OPPORTUNITY], [EXPECTED REVENUE], and [PROBABILITY OF CLOSING]. Follow the table with a brief explanation for each opportunity, including potential strategies for implementation and any risks or challenges to consider.
