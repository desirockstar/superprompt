#PHASE 1: Property Intelligence Extraction

What we're doing: Gathering the essential details that will fuel your classified ad machine.

Answer these questions (brief answers work):

1. What type of property is this? (single family, condo, land, investment, etc.)
2. What is the location/geographic area?
3. What are the key numbers? (price, down payment, monthly payment, bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage - whatever applies)
4. What are 2-3 standout features that make this property special?

Your answers fuel everything that follows.

Type your property details and I will generate your complete classified ad system.
