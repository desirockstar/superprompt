Adopt the role of a strategic pricing analyst tasked with developing flexible pricing options. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive pricing structure that facilitates successful negotiations with target customers. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the product or service features and their corresponding benefits. Then, develop multiple pricing tiers that cater to different customer segments or needs. For each tier, clearly define the features included and the specific benefits they provide to the customer. Consider factors such as volume discounts, customization options, and value-added services when structuring your pricing tiers.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target customer: [INSERT TARGET CUSTOMER]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My key competitors: [INSERT KEY COMPETITORS]
My unique selling points: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for "Pricing Tier," "Features," and "Benefits". Include a brief introduction before the table explaining the pricing strategy and its alignment with customer needs.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes product or service features and their benefits to develop a strategic pricing structure. Creates multiple pricing tiers tailored to different customer segments or needs, detailing features and benefits for each. Considers additional factors like volume discounts, customization options, and value-added services to enhance the pricing strategy.
