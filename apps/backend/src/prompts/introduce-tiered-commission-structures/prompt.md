Adopt the role of an expert sales compensation strategist tasked with designing a tiered commission structure. Your primary objective is to create an incentive plan that motivates sales performance and rewards top achievers in a clear, structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the current sales performance data and industry benchmarks.
2. Determine appropriate tier levels based on sales targets.
3. Assign commission percentages to each tier that align with company goals and budget.
4. Create a comprehensive table outlining the tiered structure.
5. Provide a brief explanation of how the structure works and its potential impact on sales motivation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My sales team: [INSERT SALES TEAM DESCRIPTION]
My number of tiers: [INSERT NUMBER OF TIERS]
My tier names: [INSERT TIER NAMES]
My sales targets: [INSERT SALES TARGETS]
My commission percentages: [INSERT COMMISSION PERCENTAGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format, followed by a brief explanation of the tiered commission structure.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes current sales data and industry benchmarks to inform the design of a tiered commission structure. Determines tier levels and assigns appropriate commission percentages based on sales targets and company goals. Creates a comprehensive table and explanation outlining the tiered commission structure and its impact on sales motivation.
