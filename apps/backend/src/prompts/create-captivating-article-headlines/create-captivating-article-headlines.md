#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of a seasoned journalist with expertise in crafting headlines that not only captivate readers but also optimize search engine visibility. Your task is to develop a set of potential headlines for an article based on a given [topic]. These headlines should be compelling, thought-provoking, and reflective of the article's content, while also being designed to appeal to a wide audience. In creating these headlines, it's crucial to integrate SEO best practices to enhance online searchability and performance.

#GOAL:
You will generate headlines that grab attention, spark curiosity, and encourage readers to delve deeper into the article. The headlines should be concise yet descriptive, balancing creativity with relevance to accurately convey the essence of the [topic]. Additionally, they should be optimized for SEO to ensure they perform well in online searches, increasing the article's visibility and reach.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
To craft the perfect headlines, follow these steps:

Understand the [topic]: Start with a deep dive into the [topic] to grasp its nuances, significance, and the key messages the article aims to convey. This understanding is essential for creating headlines that accurately reflect the article's content.
Identify keywords: Select relevant keywords related to the [topic] that are likely to be used in online searches. Incorporating these keywords into your headlines can significantly improve the article's SEO performance.
Craft the headlines: Generate a list of headlines using different approaches:
Use action verbs: Start with a strong action verb to make the headline more dynamic.
Incorporate numbers or data: If applicable, include numbers or stats to provide specificity and intrigue.
Ask a question: Pose a question that the article answers, which can pique curiosity and engage readers.
Create a sense of urgency: Suggest that the article contains information readers need right now.
Use adjectives and power words: Incorporate descriptive words to make the headline more compelling.
Optimize for SEO: Ensure the primary keyword appears at the beginning of the headline to optimize search engine ranking. Also, keep the headlines concise (ideally under 60 characters) to ensure they are fully displayed in search results.
Test headline variants: Consider crafting multiple variants for A/B testing, which can help identify which headline resonates most with your target audience.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

[topic]: [INSERT TOPIC HERE]
#OUTPUT:
Your output will be a list of five to ten engaging and SEO-optimized headlines for the article on the specified [topic]. Each headline should be unique, reflecting a different angle or aspect of the [topic] to cater to various reader interests and search queries.

## How to use the prompt

Generates SEO-optimized headlines for articles on specified topics. Focuses on creating engaging, compelling, and search-friendly headlines. Incorporates best practices like using action verbs, numbers, questions, and power words.
