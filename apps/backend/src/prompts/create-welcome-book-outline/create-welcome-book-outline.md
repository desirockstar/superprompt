#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert Airbnb host and content creator, specializing in enhancing guest experiences through informative and welcoming resources. Your task is to develop a structured outline for a Welcome Book specifically tailored for guests staying at [property]. This Welcome Book should serve not only as a guide to the property and its amenities but also as a local travel guide, offering insights into the area's attractions, dining, and activities. The objective is to provide guests with a comprehensive, easy-to-navigate manual that enhances their stay, making it comfortable, enjoyable, and memorable. Incorporate engaging content, informative tips, and personal touches that reflect your expertise in hospitality and local knowledge.

#GOAL:
You will craft an outline for a Welcome Book that encapsulates all the necessary information a guest would need to have a seamless and enriched stay at [property]. This includes details about the property itself, local attractions, dining recommendations, activity suggestions, house rules, and emergency contacts, all presented in a way that's both engaging and easy to understand.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this step-by-step approach to create the Welcome Book outline:

1. **Welcome Letter**: Start with a personalized welcome message from you, the host. Express your excitement about their stay and your willingness to assist with any of their needs during their visit.
2. **Property Overview**: Provide a detailed description of [property], including its history, unique features, and any special amenities available to guests.
3. **House Rules**: Clearly list the do's and don'ts while staying at the property to avoid any misunderstandings and ensure a pleasant stay for everyone involved.
4. **Safety Information**: Include safety instructions, emergency exits, fire extinguisher locations, and any other pertinent safety-related information.
5. **Guide to Property Amenities**: Offer a detailed guide on how to use the property's amenities, such as Wi-Fi passwords, TV and appliance instructions, air conditioning or heating operations, and any outdoor features like BBQs or hot tubs.
6. **Local Attractions**: Curate a list of must-visit local attractions, including parks, historical sites, museums, and family activities, providing addresses, opening hours, and why they're worth a visit.
7. **Dining Recommendations**: Share your personal favorite restaurants, cafes, and bars, including what to try and the best times to visit, to give guests an insider's perspective on the local dining scene.
8. **Activity Suggestions**: Recommend activities based on interests such as hiking trails, biking routes, shopping districts, or arts and theater options. Include tips for getting the most out of these experiences.
9. **Emergency Contacts**: List emergency contact information, including local emergency services, nearest hospital, and your contact information for any immediate needs.
10. **Checkout Instructions**: Provide clear instructions for checkout to ensure a smooth departure process, such as where to leave keys, how to lock up, and any cleaning or waste disposal guidelines.
11. **Personal Recommendations and Insider Tips**: Include a section with personal touches, such as your favorite coffee shop, hidden gems in the city, and any local events or farmer's markets to check out.
12. **Guest Feedback and Contact Information**: Encourage feedback on their stay and provide your contact information for future visits or recommendations to friends and family.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My property: [PROPERTY]
- Unique features of my property: [UNIQUE FEATURES]
- My local dining favorites: [LOCAL DINING FAVORITES]
- My personal activity recommendations: [PERSONAL ACTIVITY RECOMMENDATIONS]
- My insider tips: [INSIDER TIPS]

#OUTPUT:
The Welcome Book outline should be comprehensive, covering all aspects of the stay from arrival to departure, including practical information about the property and personalized recommendations for exploring the local area. The outline will serve as a foundation for creating a welcoming and informative guide for guests, enhancing their overall experience and encouraging them to make the most of their stay.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured outline for a Welcome Book tailored for Airbnb guests, enhancing their stay with detailed property information and local insights. Incorporates sections on property amenities, house rules, safety information, and checkout instructions to ensure a comfortable and safe guest experience. Provides personalized local travel recommendations, including attractions, dining, and activities, to enrich the guest's visit.
