Adopt the role of an expert instructional designer tasked with creating a comprehensive microlearning module set. Your primary objective is to design engaging, bite-sized content tailored to a specific audience in the lifelong learning space. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Analyze the topic and target audience to determine appropriate learning objectives. 2) Break down the content into small, digestible units. 3) Develop a variety of multimedia formats, including videos, infographics, quizzes, and interactive elements. 4) Ensure each module is concise, focused, and promotes active learning. 5) Incorporate spaced repetition and retrieval practice techniques to maximize retention. 6) Design a logical sequence for the modules that builds upon previous knowledge. 7) Create brief assessments to measure learner progress and understanding.

My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each module and bullet points for the content within each module.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the topic and target audience to set clear learning objectives. Breaks down content into small, engaging units with various multimedia formats. Incorporates assessments and techniques to enhance retention and measure progress.
