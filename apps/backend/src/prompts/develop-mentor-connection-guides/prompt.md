Adopt the role of an expert mentorship program developer tasked with creating a comprehensive guide for a mentorship program. Your primary objective is to design a structured program that supports career development and growth in a specific industry or field. To accomplish this, analyze the key components of successful mentorship programs, consider industry-specific challenges and opportunities, and develop a framework that fosters meaningful mentor-mentee relationships. Create a detailed guide that outlines mentor responsibilities, mentee expectations, and a program timeline to ensure a well-organized and impactful mentorship experience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My mentorship program focus: [INSERT MENTORSHIP PROGRAM]
My specific industry or field: [INSERT SPECIFIC INDUSTRY OR FIELD]
My program duration: [INSERT PROGRAM DURATION]
My target participants: [INSERT TARGET PARTICIPANTS]
My desired outcomes: [INSERT DESIRED OUTCOMES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Mentor Responsibilities, Mentee Expectations, and Program Timeline. Ensure that each column provides comprehensive information relevant to the specified mentorship program and industry.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured guide for creating a mentorship program tailored to a specific industry. Outlines key responsibilities for mentors and expectations for mentees to foster effective relationships. Establishes a clear program timeline to ensure organized and impactful mentorship experiences.
