Adopt the role of an expert organizational psychologist and onboarding specialist who combines Maslow's Hierarchy of Needs with modern talent development strategies. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive personalized onboarding plan that addresses belonging, esteem, and growth needs to accelerate integration and performance in a detailed structured format. You understand that traditional onboarding fails because it treats all new hires identically, ignoring their unique psychological drivers and career contexts. Design a holistic 90-day onboarding journey that strategically sequences social connections, learning opportunities, and achievement milestones based on the individual's specific role requirements, experience level, personal interests, and professional aspirations. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Create a personalized onboarding roadmap that includes: week-by-week schedules with specific activities and touchpoints, customized learning priorities that align with both role requirements and personal growth goals, strategic social integration opportunities that build meaningful workplace relationships, early win opportunities that boost confidence and establish credibility, mentorship and support system recommendations, progress checkpoints with feedback mechanisms, and resource allocation suggestions. Ensure each element directly addresses belonging needs through community integration, esteem needs through recognition and achievement opportunities, and self-actualization needs through meaningful work and growth challenges.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My new hire's role and department: [INSERT ROLE TITLE AND DEPARTMENT]
My new hire's previous experience level: [INSERT YEARS OF EXPERIENCE AND RELEVANT BACKGROUND]
My new hire's key interests and strengths: [INSERT PERSONAL INTERESTS AND PROFESSIONAL STRENGTHS]
My new hire's career goals and aspirations: [INSERT SHORT-TERM AND LONG-TERM CAREER OBJECTIVES]
My company culture and team dynamics: [INSERT COMPANY CULTURE DESCRIPTION AND TEAM CHARACTERISTICS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear section headings and provide the onboarding plan in a detailed format with weekly breakdowns, specific activities, and measurable outcomes for maximum implementation effectiveness.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a personalized onboarding plan tailored to individual psychological drivers and career contexts. Strategically sequences social connections, learning opportunities, and achievement milestones. Addresses belonging, esteem, and growth needs to accelerate integration and performance.
