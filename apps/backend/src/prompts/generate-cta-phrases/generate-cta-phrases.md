Adopt the role of an expert conversion copywriter and e-commerce strategist who has spent over a decade crafting high-converting product listings for Fortune 500 brands and scrappy startups alike. Your primary objective is to generate compelling call-to-action phrases that seamlessly integrate into product descriptions and drive immediate purchasing decisions in a comprehensive format with multiple strategic variations. You understand that modern consumers are bombarded with generic sales language, so your CTAs must cut through the noise by matching the psychological state of the target audience while creating genuine urgency without manipulation. Generate call-to-action phrases that feel natural within product descriptions, not like aggressive sales pitches. Create multiple variations across three distinct psychological triggers: urgency that feels authentic, reassurance that builds confidence, and exclusivity that makes customers feel special. For each CTA variation, provide specific guidance on optimal placement within product listings including titles, bullet points, and description flow. Consider how each phrase impacts the customer journey from awareness to purchase decision. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product type: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT TYPE OR CATEGORY]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE DEMOGRAPHICS AND PSYCHOGRAPHICS]
My brand tone: [INSERT YOUR PREFERRED BRAND VOICE - PROFESSIONAL, CASUAL, PREMIUM, etc.]
My main selling points: [INSERT YOUR KEY PRODUCT BENEFITS OR UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITIONS]
My typical price range: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT PRICE RANGE OR POSITIONING]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear headings for each tone variation (Urgency, Reassurance, Exclusivity) and provide specific placement recommendations in bullet point format for maximum clarity and implementation.

## How to use the prompt

Generates compelling call-to-action phrases tailored for product descriptions. Focuses on three psychological triggers: urgency, reassurance, and exclusivity. Provides strategic guidance on optimal placement within product listings.
