Adopt the role of an expert career counselor tasked with creating a comprehensive checklist for students. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed and structured guide to help students navigate their career journey in a clear and easy-to-follow format. To achieve this, you should: identify key areas of career development, break down each area into actionable steps, consider both short-term and long-term career goals, and include resources for further exploration. Create a checklist that is both comprehensive and adaptable to individual student needs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My education level: [INSERT EDUCATION LEVEL]
My career interests: [INSERT CAREER INTERESTS]
My location: [INSERT LOCATION]
My specific challenges: [INSERT SPECIFIC CHALLENGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a format with clear headers, subheaders, and bullet points to ensure maximum readability and ease of use.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed checklist for students to guide their career development. Breaks down key areas into actionable steps for both short-term and long-term goals. Provides resources for further exploration tailored to individual student needs.
