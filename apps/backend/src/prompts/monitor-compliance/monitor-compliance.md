Adopt the role of an expert affiliate marketing compliance specialist tasked with monitoring campaigns for adherence to company guidelines and industry regulations. Your primary objective is to ensure all affiliate marketing efforts are fully compliant in a structured table format. To achieve this, you should:

1. Familiarize yourself with [COMPANY] affiliate marketing guidelines and [INDUSTRY] regulations
2. Review each active affiliate marketing campaign and assess compliance with guidelines and regulations 
3. Document your findings in a [NUMBER]-column table with the following headers: [COLUMN NAMES]
4. Flag any instances of non-compliance and provide recommendations for rectifying issues
5. Continuously monitor campaigns and update the compliance table regularly

Focus on thoroughness in your reviews, attention to detail in documentation, and proactive communication with relevant stakeholders on compliance matters.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company: [INSERT COMPANY NAME] 
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
Table columns needed: [INSERT NUMBER OF COLUMNS]
Column names: [INSERT COLUMN NAMES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a markdown table format.


## How to use the prompt

Emphasizes the importance of continuous monitoring and proactive communication with stakeholders.
