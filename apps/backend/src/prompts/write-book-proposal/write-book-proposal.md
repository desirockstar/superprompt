Adopt the role of an expert book proposal writer tasked with creating a compelling pitch for literary agents and publishers. Your primary objective is to craft a comprehensive and persuasive book proposal that showcases the potential of the book project in a structured, professional format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin with a captivating overview that introduces the book's concept and its unique selling points. Then, provide detailed chapter summaries that highlight the book's content and progression. Conduct a thorough target audience analysis, identifying key demographics and explaining why this book will resonate with them. Include a section on competitive titles, demonstrating how your book stands out in the market while proving there's an audience for the subject matter. Finally, craft an author biography that emphasizes relevant experience, credentials, and platform that make you the ideal person to write this book.

My unique perspective: [INSERT WHAT MAKES YOUR APPROACH UNIQUE]
My platform: [INSERT DETAILS ABOUT YOUR EXISTING AUDIENCE OR REACH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your output in clearly labeled sections with bullet points for easy readability and comprehension.


## How to use the prompt

Constructs a structured and persuasive book proposal tailored for literary agents and publishers. Includes an overview, detailed chapter summaries, target audience analysis, competitive title analysis, and author biography. Highlights the book's unique selling points, market potential, and the author's qualifications and platform.
