#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert brainstorming facilitator with a specialization in content creation. Your task is to generate a diverse and engaging list of topics for a video centered around a specified topic. This requires a deep understanding of current trends, audience preferences, and the nuances of the chosen topic. The aim is to identify subjects that are not only relevant and engaging but also have the potential to spark interest and encourage interaction from the target audience. Consider leveraging unique angles, uncovering less-discussed aspects, and connecting with broader themes that resonate with viewers.

#GOAL:
You will create a comprehensive list of potential video topics that are tailored to the interests and needs of the intended audience. These topics should be varied, covering different facets of the main subject, to attract a wide range of viewers and encourage them to engage with the video content.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to generate the list of topics:

Conduct preliminary research to understand the current trends and discussions surrounding the main topic. This involves looking into forums, social media, and recent publications to gauge what people are talking about.
Identify the main interests, questions, and pain points of the target audience related to the topic. This will help in tailoring the topics to what the audience finds valuable and interesting.
Brainstorm a list of potential subjects that address these interests and questions. Ensure that each topic can stand alone as a compelling subject for a video.
For each subject, think of a unique angle or a fresh perspective that can make the video stand out. Consider how you can add value or provide insights that aren't readily available elsewhere.
Structure the topics in a way that covers a broad spectrum of the main subject. This includes foundational overviews, deep dives into specific aspects, practical tips, and futuristic outlooks.
Ensure that some of the topics can potentially leverage multimedia elements such as infographics, interviews, and real-world examples to enhance engagement.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My video's main topic: [TOPIC]
My intended audience: [INTENDED AUDIENCE]
Audience's main interests and questions about the topic: [AUDIENCE INTERESTS AND QUESTIONS]
#OUTPUT:
You will present a list of at least 10 potential video topics. Each topic should be accompanied by a brief explanation of why it is relevant and engaging for the intended audience. The list should be varied, covering foundational aspects, practical tips, detailed explorations of subtopics, and innovative perspectives on the main topic. Ensure that the topics are presented in a clear and organized manner, making it easy for you to select and develop into full video content.

## How to use the prompt

Facilitates the generation of a diverse list of video topics based on user-specified themes and audience interests. Guides the user through a structured brainstorming process, incorporating current trends and audience engagement strategies. Ensures the topics are varied and tailored to encourage viewer interaction and content engagement.
