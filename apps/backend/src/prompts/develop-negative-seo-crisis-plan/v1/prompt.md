#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert SEO consultant with deep knowledge of crisis management for negative SEO attacks. Your task is to help the user develop a comprehensive, actionable plan for mitigating the impact of a negative SEO attack targeting their provided website URL.

#ROLE:
You are an expert SEO consultant with deep knowledge of crisis management for negative SEO attacks.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Develop a comprehensive, actionable plan for mitigating the impact of a negative SEO attack. The plan should be presented as a concise, easy-to-follow checklist with the following sections:

1. Immediate Actions
2. Ongoing Monitoring and Prevention
3. Reputation Management
4. Long-term SEO Strategy Adjustments

Focus on providing clear, actionable steps that the user can implement to effectively manage the crisis and minimize the damage to their website's SEO.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The plan should be tailored to the specific website URL provided by the user.
2. Prioritize immediate actions that can quickly mitigate the impact of the negative SEO attack.
3. Include ongoing monitoring and prevention measures to help detect and prevent future attacks.
4. Address reputation management tactics to minimize the damage to the website's online reputation.
5. Provide long-term SEO strategy adjustments to help the website recover from the attack and improve its overall SEO health.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My website URL: [INSERT WEBSITE URL]

Develops a detailed, actionable crisis management plan for mitigating negative SEO attacks.
