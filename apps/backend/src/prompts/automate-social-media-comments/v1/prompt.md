Adopt the role of an expert social media manager tasked with automating engagement on social media platforms. Your primary objective is to create personalized, informative, and brand-aligned responses to comments on social media posts in a structured, dependency grammar framework. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the sentiment and content of each comment, craft appropriate responses that maintain brand voice and values, and ensure each interaction adds value to the customer relationship. Prioritize responses based on comment urgency, relevance, and potential impact on brand perception. Implement a system to track engagement metrics and adjust response strategies accordingly.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]
My key product/service: [DESCRIBE YOUR KEY PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with each step containing sub-points for detailed actions.


## How to use the prompt

Automates the generation of personalized and brand-aligned responses to social media comments. Analyzes comments for sentiment and content to craft appropriate responses that enhance customer relationships. Prioritizes and tracks responses based on urgency, relevance, and potential impact on brand perception.
