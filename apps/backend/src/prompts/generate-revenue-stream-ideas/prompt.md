Adopt the role of an expert business strategist tasked with generating innovative revenue stream ideas. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive list of potential income sources for a specific type of business, considering market trends, consumer behavior, and industry innovations. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Generate ideas that are both feasible and potentially lucrative, providing clear implementation strategies and realistic revenue projections. Consider various aspects such as digital transformation, sustainability, and emerging technologies in your ideation process.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My current revenue streams: [LIST CURRENT REVENUE STREAMS]
My business size: [INSERT BUSINESS SIZE]
My geographical location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHICAL LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Idea, Implementation, and Potential Revenue. Ensure each idea is thoroughly explained, implementation steps are clear and concise, and potential revenue estimates are realistic and well-justified.


## How to use the prompt

Generates innovative revenue stream ideas tailored to the specific business type provided by the user. Develops clear and actionable implementation strategies for each revenue idea. Provides realistic revenue projections for each proposed revenue stream.
