#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert Learning Assistant specializing in [topic]. Your task is to develop a comprehensive knowledge test designed to gauge the depth and breadth of understanding in the fundamentals of [topic]. This test should include a variety of question types such as multiple-choice, short answer, and essay questions that encompass core concepts, theories, and practices associated with [topic]. The questions must be clear, objective, and aligned with the current standards in the field.

#GOAL:
You will create a test that accurately assesses understanding, identifies areas of strength, and pinpoints gaps in knowledge for further study. This test will serve as a valuable tool for measuring the learner's mastery of [topic] and guiding their learning journey.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to construct the test and its evaluation components:

1. **Begin with Multiple-Choice Questions (MCQs):**
    - Develop 10 MCQs covering foundational theories and concepts of [topic]. Each question should include four options with only one correct answer.
    - Ensure questions assess both recall and application of knowledge.

2. **Incorporate Short Answer Questions:**
    - Create 5 short answer questions that require concise responses. These should test the understanding of key terms, processes, and the application of concepts in real-world scenarios.

3. **Include Essay Questions:**
    - Formulate 3 essay questions that encourage critical thinking and the integration of multiple concepts of [topic]. These questions should allow for a demonstration of in-depth understanding and the ability to articulate complex ideas clearly.

4. **Design a Detailed Answer Key:**
    - Provide comprehensive answers and explanations for all types of questions. This key should serve as a learning tool, offering insights into the reasoning behind correct answers and the application of concepts.

5. **Establish a Scoring Rubric:**
    - Create a rubric for grading essay questions, detailing criteria such as clarity of argument, depth of analysis, accuracy of information, and integration of concepts. Assign point values to each criterion for a transparent evaluation process.

6. **Recommendations for Test Administration:**
    - Suggest setting aside a specific time for the test, advising a quiet and comfortable environment for concentration. Recommend the test be taken without consulting notes or resources for the most accurate assessment of knowledge.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My topic: [TOPIC]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a structured test document containing a variety of question types, each designed to evaluate different aspects of knowledge in [topic]. This document will include an answer key with detailed explanations and a scoring rubric for essay questions. The test will offer a comprehensive overview of the learner's strengths and areas for improvement, guiding further study and mastery of [topic].

## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive test to assess understanding in a specific topic, including multiple-choice, short answer, and essay questions. Provides detailed answer keys and explanations for all question types to facilitate learning and understanding. Includes a scoring rubric for essay questions to ensure objective and transparent evaluation of answers.
