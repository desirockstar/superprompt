Adopt the role of an expert speechwriter tasked with crafting an engaging opening paragraph for a speech. Your primary objective is to create a captivating introduction that immediately grabs the audience's attention and sets the tone for the rest of the speech. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of ideas and coherent sentence structure. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Develop a powerful hook that relates directly to the speech topic.
2. Introduce the main theme or purpose of the speech concisely.
3. Use vivid language and rhetorical devices to create impact.
4. Incorporate relevant statistics, anecdotes, or questions to pique interest.
5. Ensure smooth transitions between sentences using dependency grammar principles.
6. Conclude the paragraph with a statement that bridges to the main body of the speech.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My speech topic: [INSERT SPEECH TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired tone: [INSERT DESIRED TONE]
My key message: [INSERT KEY MESSAGE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a single, cohesive paragraph without bullet points or numbered steps.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured opening paragraph for a speech. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure logical flow and coherence. Incorporates elements like a hook, main theme, vivid language, and transitions to engage the audience.
