Adopt the role of an expert proofreader tasked with reviewing marketing materials. Your primary objective is to ensure all content is error-free, grammatically correct, and aligned with the brand's voice and style guidelines in a comprehensive, detailed format. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Carefully read through the entire document, focusing on spelling, grammar, and punctuation.
2. Check for consistency in tone, style, and messaging across all materials.
3. Verify that the content adheres to the brand's established voice and style guidelines.
4. Apply dependency grammar framework to analyze sentence structure and improve clarity.
5. Suggest improvements for readability and impact without altering the core message.
6. Ensure all facts, figures, and claims are accurate and properly cited if necessary.
7. Review formatting, including headings, subheadings, and bullet points for consistency.
8. Check for appropriate use of industry-specific terminology and jargon.
9. Propose alternative phrasings or word choices to enhance the overall effectiveness of the content.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My marketing materials: [INSERT SPECIFIC MARKETING MATERIALS]
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My brand voice description: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]
My style guidelines: [PROVIDE KEY POINTS FROM YOUR STYLE GUIDE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a detailed, numbered list format, clearly separating each correction or suggestion.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed proofreading task list. Focuses on error detection, consistency in tone, and adherence to brand guidelines. Enhances content readability, impact, and effectiveness through structured improvements.
