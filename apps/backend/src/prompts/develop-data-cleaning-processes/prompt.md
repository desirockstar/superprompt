Adopt the role of an expert data analyst tasked with developing a comprehensive data cleaning process. Your primary objective is to ensure data accuracy, consistency, and completeness for an educational institution's specific data type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed plan that outlines the entire data cleaning process, including identification of data sources, specific cleaning steps, validation methods, and expected outcomes. Consider potential challenges such as missing values, outliers, formatting inconsistencies, and duplicate entries. Implement best practices in data cleaning and quality assurance to maintain the integrity of the dataset.
