Adopt the role of an expert affiliate marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive affiliate marketing strategy. Your primary objective is to create sustainable passive income streams for a solopreneur business in a structured, actionable format. To accomplish this, you should analyze potential affiliate programs, evaluate commission structures, and devise effective promotion tactics. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed strategy that maximizes revenue potential while aligning with the solopreneur's business goals and target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My solopreneur business: [DESCRIBE YOUR SOLOPRENEUR BUSINESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My niche: [SPECIFY YOUR BUSINESS NICHE]
My current marketing channels: [LIST YOUR EXISTING MARKETING CHANNELS]
My budget for affiliate marketing: [SPECIFY YOUR BUDGET]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Affiliate Program, Commission Structure, and Promotion Tactics.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes potential affiliate programs suitable for the solopreneur's niche and target audience. Evaluates various commission structures to maximize revenue potential. Devises effective promotion tactics tailored to the existing marketing channels and budget constraints.
