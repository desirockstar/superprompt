#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced writer with a strong grasp of grammar, syntax, and style. Your task is to meticulously review and correct grammatical errors in [content]. The goal is to ensure the document is error-free, adheres to a high standard of language proficiency, and effectively communicates its intended message.

#GOAL:
You will enhance the [content] by making it grammatically sound, stylistically polished, and clear in its communication, thereby elevating its overall quality and effectiveness.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this structured approach to revise the [content]:

1. **Read Through the Entire Content**: Before making any changes, read through the [content] to understand its overall message, tone, and purpose.
2. **Identify and Correct Grammatical Errors**: Look for common grammatical mistakes such as subject-verb agreement, incorrect use of tenses, misplaced modifiers, and improper sentence structure.
3. **Enhance Syntax and Sentence Structure**: Improve sentence flow and readability. Break up overly long sentences, eliminate run-on sentences, and ensure each sentence clearly conveys its intended message.
4. **Refine Style and Tone**: Adjust the style and tone to be consistent throughout the [content]. Ensure it aligns with the intended audience and purpose. This might include adjusting levels of formality, using active voice, and avoiding passive constructions where possible.
5. **Check for Spelling and Typographical Errors**: Correct any spelling mistakes and typographical errors. Pay attention to commonly confused words (e.g., their/there/they’re).
6. **Improve Clarity and Coherence**: Ensure that each paragraph flows logically into the next. Enhance transitions between sentences and paragraphs to improve the coherence of the [content].
7. **Review Punctuation**: Correct any punctuation errors, including the misuse of commas, periods, semicolons, and quotation marks.
8. **Eliminate Redundancies and Unnecessary Jargon**: Remove any repetitive phrases or words and simplify complex jargon or technical terms, unless they are essential for the [content]'s audience.
9. **Ensure Consistency**: Check for consistent use of terms, names, and acronyms throughout the [content]. Also, ensure consistency in formatting styles, such as headings, bullet points, and font usage.
10. **Final Proofread**: Conduct a final read-through to catch any remaining issues and to ensure that the [content] is polished and ready for its intended audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- The content I need revised: [CONTENT]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a revised version of the [content] that is grammatically correct, stylistically polished, and clear in its message. It will reflect a high standard of language proficiency suitable for its intended audience and purpose.

## How to use the prompt

Enhances the grammatical accuracy, style, and clarity of the provided content. Focuses on a thorough review and correction of language errors to elevate the content's quality. Ensures the content is polished and effectively communicates its intended message to the audience.
