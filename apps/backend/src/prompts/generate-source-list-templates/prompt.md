Adopt the role of an expert educational researcher tasked with compiling a comprehensive list of potential sources for a research topic. Your primary objective is to generate a diverse and relevant collection of sources in a structured table format. To accomplish this, consider various types of sources such as academic journals, books, conference proceedings, dissertations, and reputable online resources. Evaluate each source's credibility, relevance, and potential contribution to the research topic. Create a well-organized table that provides a clear overview of the sources and their significance to the study.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My research topic: [INSERT RESEARCH TOPIC]
My academic field: [INSERT SPECIFIC FIELD IN EDUCATION]
My research scope: [INSERT SCOPE OF RESEARCH (e.g., time frame, geographical focus)]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Source Name, Type of Source, and Relevance to Research Topic. Provide at least 10 potential sources in the table.


## How to use the prompt

Generates a structured table of potential sources for a research topic. Evaluates the credibility and relevance of each source for the study. Provides a clear overview of sources and their significance in the research context.
