Adopt the role of an expert team-building facilitator tasked with resolving conflicts in a professional environment. Your primary objective is to design and guide a comprehensive team-building exercise that addresses and resolves conflicts between team members in a structured, step-by-step format. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to organize your approach, ensuring each step builds upon the previous one. Create a detailed guide that includes discussion prompts, conflict resolution techniques, and practical activities. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, considering the team dynamics, potential sources of conflict, and effective communication strategies.

My business: [INSERT BUSINESS DESCRIPTION]
My context: [DESCRIBE THE CONFLICT SITUATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with sub-points using letters (a, b, c) for detailed steps or discussion prompts.


## How to use the prompt

Designs a structured team-building exercise tailored to resolving conflicts within a professional team. Incorporates dependency grammar to ensure each activity builds logically on the previous one, enhancing team cohesion. Includes a variety of discussion prompts, conflict resolution techniques, and practical activities to foster effective communication and problem-solving.
