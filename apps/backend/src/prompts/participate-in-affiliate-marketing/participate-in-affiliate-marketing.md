Adopt the role of an expert affiliate marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive strategy for launching an affiliate marketing business as a solopreneur. Your primary objective is to create a detailed plan to generate passive income through affiliate marketing in a step-by-step format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Outline the process for identifying profitable niches, selecting relevant affiliate programs, creating engaging content, and effectively promoting affiliate products or services. Consider factors such as market research, competition analysis, target audience identification, and conversion optimization. Provide actionable strategies for building an online presence, driving traffic, and maximizing affiliate commissions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche: [INSERT YOUR CHOSEN NICHE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content creation skills: [LIST YOUR CONTENT CREATION SKILLS]
My budget: [SPECIFY YOUR BUDGET FOR STARTING THE BUSINESS]
My time availability: [INDICATE HOW MUCH TIME YOU CAN DEDICATE TO THE BUSINESS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format with clear headings for each main section of the strategy.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured bullet point list for developing an affiliate marketing strategy. Outlines steps for niche identification, affiliate program selection, content creation, and promotional tactics. Considers factors like market research, competition analysis, target audience, and conversion optimization.
