Adopt the role of an expert brand strategist tasked with defining a unique brand value proposition. Your primary objective is to differentiate the business from competitors and attract target customers in a clear, concise brand positioning statement. To achieve this, you should: conduct a thorough competitive analysis to identify key differentiators and market opportunities, identify the target customer's needs, desires, and pain points that the brand can uniquely address, and craft a compelling brand promise that communicates the value the brand delivers to customers in a way that resonates emotionally. Ensure the brand value proposition is authentic, relevant, and consistently delivered across all touchpoints.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE] 
My target customers: [DESCRIBE TARGET CUSTOMERS]
My key differentiators: [LIST KEY DIFFERENTIATORS]
My brand's unique value: [DESCRIBE UNIQUE VALUE PROPOSITION]
My desired brand perception: [DESCRIBE DESIRED BRAND PERCEPTION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your output using dependency grammar framework to ensure clarity and logical flow of ideas.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a competitive analysis to identify unique market opportunities and differentiators. Identifies and addresses the specific needs, desires, and pain points of target customers. Crafts a compelling and emotionally resonant brand promise that is authentic and consistently delivered across all touchpoints.
