Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive email marketing campaign. Your primary objective is to build an engaged subscriber list for a specific business or product using dependency grammar framework. To achieve this, craft compelling opt-in forms, welcome emails, and ongoing nurture sequences. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience and their preferences. Then, develop a strategy for creating enticing opt-in forms that clearly communicate the value proposition. Next, design a series of welcome emails that establish a strong connection with new subscribers. Finally, create an ongoing nurture sequence that maintains engagement and drives conversions.
