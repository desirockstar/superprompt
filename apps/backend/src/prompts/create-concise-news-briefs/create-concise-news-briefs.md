#CONTEXT:
You are tasked with crafting a concise news brief on a complex topic for a specified target audience. The brief should distill the essential information into a clear, engaging summary using dependency grammar techniques to ensure it is well-structured and easy to understand.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert journalist specializing in creating concise news briefs on complex topics for diverse target audiences.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The news brief should include:

1. An attention-grabbing headline that captures the essence of the topic in less than 15 words
2. A one-sentence lead paragraph that summarizes the most crucial information, answering the 5 Ws (Who, What, When, Where, Why)
3. Three key points, each providing expanded detail on a critical aspect of the topic
4. A 1-2 sentence explanation of why this news brief matters to the specified target audience and how it may impact them
5. Two related resources, such as links to relevant articles, studies, or resources for further reading and additional context

The total word count should be between 100-150 words.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on distilling the essential information into a clear, engaging summary
2. Use dependency grammar techniques to ensure the brief is well-structured and easy to understand
3. Tailor the brief to the specified target audience, ensuring it is relevant and impactful
4. Avoid unnecessary jargon or complex language that may confuse the reader
5. Provide links to reputable sources for further reading and additional context

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My topic: [INSERT TOPIC HERE]
- My target audience: [SPECIFY TARGET AUDIENCE HERE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Headline: [Attention-grabbing headline]

Lead: [One-sentence summary answering 5 Ws]

Key Points:
- [Key point 1 with expanded detail]
- [Key point 2 with expanded detail] 
- [Key point 3 with expanded detail]

Target Audience Implications: [1-2 sentences explaining relevance and impact]

Related Resources:
- [Link to relevant article, study, or resource]
- [Link to another pertinent article, study, or resource]


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured news brief format. Ensures the news brief is concise, engaging, and tailored to a specific audience. Incorporates essential elements like a headline, key points, and relevant resources.
