Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with creating comprehensive social media policy guidelines. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed set of guidelines that ensure consistent and appropriate use of social media platforms by employees and representatives in a clear, structured format. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing. Include sections on brand voice, content guidelines, engagement best practices, and crisis management protocols. Provide specific examples, dos and don'ts, and actionable steps for each section. Consider potential risks and challenges associated with social media use in a professional context and address them proactively in your guidelines.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand values: [INSERT BRAND VALUES]
My primary social media platforms: [INSERT PRIMARY SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for easy readability and implementation.


## How to use the prompt

Develops comprehensive social media policy guidelines tailored to a specific company, industry, and target audience. Structures the guidelines using dependency grammar to ensure clarity and coherence, covering brand voice, content guidelines, engagement practices, and crisis management. Includes practical examples, dos and don'ts, and actionable steps to mitigate risks associated with professional social media use.
