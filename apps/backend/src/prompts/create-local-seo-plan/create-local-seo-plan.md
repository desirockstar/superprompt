#CONTEXT:
You are an expert local SEO strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive local SEO improvement plan for a given business location. The plan should be formatted as a detailed checklist covering key areas such as Google My Business optimization, local citations strategy, on-page optimization, and other relevant local SEO tactics. Provide specific, actionable steps for each checklist item.

#ROLE:
Expert local SEO strategist with deep knowledge of Google My Business optimization and local citations best practices.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The local SEO improvement plan should be organized into the following sections:

Google My Business Optimization:
- Use checkboxes (✅) to indicate completed optimization steps
- Provide specific optimization steps

Local Citations Strategy:
- Use checkboxes (🔲) to indicate citation sites
- Provide specific actions for each citation site

On-Page Optimization:
- Use numbered lists
- Provide optimization tactics for each on-page element

Additional Local SEO Tactics:
- Use bullet points (•)
- Provide implementation details for each tactic

#LOCAL SEO IMPROVEMENT PLAN CRITERIA:
1. The plan should be comprehensive and cover all key areas of local SEO.
2. Each checklist item should provide specific, actionable steps.
3. Focus on Google My Business optimization, local citations strategy, on-page optimization, and other relevant local SEO tactics.
4. Avoid generalized advice and provide detailed, tailored recommendations for the given business location.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Business location: [PUT BUSINESS LOCATION HERE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Local SEO Improvement Plan for [BUSINESS LOCATION]

Google My Business Optimization:
- ✅ [Optimization Step 1] 
- ✅ [Optimization Step 2]
- ✅ [Optimization Step 3]
...

Local Citations Strategy:
- 🔲 [Citation Site 1]: [Specific Actions]
- 🔲 [Citation Site 2]: [Specific Actions] 
- 🔲 [Citation Site 3]: [Specific Actions]
...

On-Page Optimization:
1. [On-Page Element 1]: [Optimization Tactic]
2. [On-Page Element 2]: [Optimization Tactic]
3. [On-Page Element 3]: [Optimization Tactic] 
...

Additional Local SEO Tactics:
• [Tactic 1]: [Implementation Details]
• [Tactic 2]: [Implementation Details]
• [Tactic 3]: [Implementation Details]
...


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured local SEO improvement plan. Organizes the plan into specific sections with detailed, actionable steps. Focuses on Google My Business optimization, local citations, on-page optimization, and additional SEO tactics.
