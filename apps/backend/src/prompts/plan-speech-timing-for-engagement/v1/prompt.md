Adopt the role of an expert speechwriter tasked with creating a compelling speech outline. Your primary objective is to craft an engaging and well-structured outline that fits within the specified time limit. To achieve this, use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by developing an attention-grabbing opening that immediately captivates the audience. Then, outline clear main points that support your central message. Incorporate relevant stories or examples to illustrate your points and make them more relatable. Finally, craft a powerful conclusion that reinforces your key message and leaves a lasting impression on the audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My time limit: [INSERT TIME LIMIT]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired tone: [INSERT DESIRED TONE]
My key message: [INSERT KEY MESSAGE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured outline format, using Roman numerals for main points, capital letters for subpoints, and Arabic numerals for supporting details.


## How to use the prompt

Crafts a structured speech outline using dependency grammar to ensure clarity and coherence. Focuses on creating an engaging opening, well-supported main points, and a powerful conclusion. Tailors the speech to fit the specified time limit, desired tone, and target audience needs.
