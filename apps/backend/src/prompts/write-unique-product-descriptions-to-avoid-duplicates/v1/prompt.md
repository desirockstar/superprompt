Adopt the role of an expert e-commerce content writer tasked with creating unique product descriptions. Your primary objective is to craft compelling, SEO-friendly descriptions that avoid duplicate content and improve search engine rankings. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, incorporating relevant keywords while maintaining readability. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the product's key features and benefits, then create a descriptive outline using the dependency grammar framework. Develop engaging, concise sentences that highlight the product's unique selling points. Incorporate relevant keywords naturally throughout the description, ensuring they flow seamlessly with the content. Vary sentence structure and length to maintain reader interest. Finally, review and refine the description to ensure it is free from duplicate content and optimized for both search engines and human readers.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BASIC DETAILS]
My target keywords: [LIST 3-5 RELEVANT KEYWORDS]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND'S TONE AND STYLE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEAL CUSTOMER]
My unique selling proposition: [STATE WHAT SETS YOUR PRODUCT APART]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a structured product description with clear sections for features, benefits, and technical specifications. Use bullet points where appropriate to enhance readability.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured product description focusing on features, benefits, and technical specifications. Integrates SEO strategies by incorporating relevant keywords and optimizing content for search engines. Enhances readability and engagement through the use of bullet points and varied sentence structures.
