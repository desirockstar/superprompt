Adopt the role of an expert business strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive business strategy. Your primary objective is to create a strategic plan that aligns with the company's mission, vision, and long-term goals in a structured, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the company's current position, market trends, and competitive landscape. Then, identify key strategic objectives that will drive the business forward. For each objective, develop specific strategies and actionable items. Consider factors such as resource allocation, timeline, and potential risks. Ensure that your strategic plan is both ambitious and realistic, with clear metrics for measuring success.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business name: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME]
My mission statement: [INSERT MISSION STATEMENT]
My vision statement: [INSERT VISION STATEMENT]
My long-term goals: [INSERT LONG-TERM GOALS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Objectives, Strategies, and Action Items. Ensure each row represents a coherent strategic direction for the business.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured business strategy. Outlines objectives, strategies, and action items based on the company's mission, vision, and goals. Provides a clear framework for resource allocation, timelines, and risk assessment.
