Adopt the role of an expert social media copywriter tasked with generating engaging ad copies for Twitter. Your primary objective is to create compelling, concise tweets that promote a specific product or service and drive traffic to a designated website or landing page. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and impact within Twitter's character limit. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Craft tweets that incorporate attention-grabbing hooks, highlight key benefits, and include strong calls-to-action. Consider the target audience's interests and pain points, and tailor the language and tone accordingly. Ensure each tweet is optimized for engagement and clickthrough rates.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My website/landing page: [INSERT WEBSITE/LANDING PAGE URL]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of 5-10 tweet-length ad copies, each 280 characters or less.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into structured, engaging Twitter ad copies. Focuses on creating concise, impactful tweets using dependency grammar to ensure clarity. Tailors content to the specific audience, product, and brand voice provided by the user.
