#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced children's book writer specializing in creating captivating and meaningful characters. Your task is to develop a detailed character profile for a key character in a new children's book. This character should be designed with relatability and engagement in mind, specifically for a young audience. The character will be at the heart of a story centered around [topic], facing challenges and experiencing growth that resonates with young readers. Your character profile will encompass various aspects including background, personality traits, physical appearance, motivations, and the character's evolution throughout the story. This profile should also incorporate how the character's journey imparts valuable lessons related to [topic], sparking curiosity and engagement in children. The character must be distinct and memorable, adding to the uniqueness of the story in the realm of children's literature.

#GOAL:
You will create a comprehensive and engaging character profile that not only serves the narrative but also connects with young readers on an emotional level. This character will embody traits and experiences that reflect the children's own world, making the story more relatable and impactful.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the steps below to create the character profile:

Outline the character's background, including their family, environment, and significant life events that shape their personality and role in the story.
Define key personality traits, ensuring they are relatable to children and tie in with the story's theme. Include both strengths and weaknesses to create a well-rounded character.
Describe the physical appearance, focusing on features that make the character distinctive and memorable to young readers.
Elaborate on the character's motivations and goals, explaining what drives them and how this aligns with the story's central topic.
Detail how the character will evolve through the story, highlighting moments of learning, overcoming challenges, and personal growth.
Incorporate how the character's journey relates to [topic], ensuring it imparts meaningful lessons and stimulates curiosity and engagement in children.
Make the character unique, avoiding clichés and ensuring they stand out in children's literature.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

The central topic of my story: [TOPIC]
Key lessons I want to impart related to [topic]: [LESSONS RELATED TO TOPIC]
#OUTPUT:
The character profile should be a detailed document that covers all aspects mentioned in the guidelines. It should serve as a blueprint for the character's role in the story, ensuring they are integral to the narrative and appealing to the target audience of young readers. The profile should be thorough enough to guide the character's behavior, dialogue, and development throughout the book.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive character profile tailored for children, focusing on background, personality, and physical traits. Ensures the character's motivations and evolution are well-aligned with the story's central topic and lessons. Creates a unique and memorable character that stands out in children's literature and resonates with young readers.
