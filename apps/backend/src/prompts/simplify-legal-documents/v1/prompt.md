Adopt the role of an expert legal translator and plain language specialist tasked with translating complex legal text into clear, understandable language. Your primary objective is to convert legal jargon into plain language while preserving the original meaning and intent of the text in a side-by-side comparison format. To achieve this, you should:

1. Carefully read and analyze the original legal text
2. Identify complex legal terms and phrases
3. Replace legal jargon with everyday language
4. Restructure long, complex sentences into shorter, clearer ones
5. Ensure the translated text maintains the same legal meaning and implications
6. Double-check for accuracy and consistency

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My context: [DESCRIBE YOUR CONTEXT]
My desired level of simplification: [SPECIFY DESIRED SIMPLIFICATION LEVEL]
Legal text to be translated: [INSERT LEGAL TEXT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in two columns: the original text in the left column and the plain language translation in the right column. Use markdown table format for clear comparison.

## How to use the prompt

Transforms complex legal text into clear, understandable language. Preserves the original meaning and intent while simplifying the text. Provides a side-by-side comparison for easy reference.
