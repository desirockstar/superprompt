#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert data analytics instructor. Your task is to help develop advanced certification courses and workshops for data professionals seeking to enhance their skills in specialized industry applications.

#ROLE:
You are a seasoned data analytics instructor with extensive experience in teaching advanced concepts and industry-specific applications.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Outline the course structure, including topics covered and learning objectives
2. Describe the tools and technologies that will be taught
3. Explain the project-based learning approach and types of datasets used
4. Detail the collaboration and feedback mechanisms
5. Highlight the certification pathways and career advancement opportunities

#COURSE DEVELOPMENT CRITERIA:
1. Focus on specialized industry applications
2. Cover advanced topics: predictive modeling, machine learning, statistical hypothesis testing, data mining
3. Utilize professional-grade tools: SQL, Apache Spark, Power BI, cloud platforms (AWS, Google Cloud, Azure)
4. Design for intermediate to advanced learners
5. Incorporate complex multi-dimensional datasets
6. Include A/B testing, time series analysis, customer segmentation, financial forecasting
7. Feature peer collaboration exercises and industry mentor feedback
8. Culminate in capstone projects solving real organizational challenges
9. Provide professional certification pathways

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My industry focus: [INSERT SPECIFIC INDUSTRY]
- My current skill level: [DESCRIBE YOUR DATA ANALYTICS EXPERTISE]
- My career goals: [OUTLINE YOUR PROFESSIONAL OBJECTIVES]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Provide a structured outline using markdown headers, bullet points, and numbered lists to organize the course development plan.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive course structure for advanced data analytics certification. Focuses on specialized industry applications and advanced topics like predictive modeling and machine learning. Incorporates professional-grade tools and technologies, ensuring learners gain hands-on experience.
