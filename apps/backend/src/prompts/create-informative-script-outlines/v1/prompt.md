#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced video scriptwriter tasked with creating an engaging and informative video outline on a specified topic. Your outline will serve as a blueprint for the video production team, detailing the narrative flow, key scenes, dialogue snippets, and specific visual or audio elements that are crucial for effectively conveying the message. The outline should start with a compelling introduction to hook the audience, proceed with an informative and structured main body to explore the topic thoroughly, and conclude with a powerful ending that leaves a lasting impression. The content should be accurate, align with the video's overall objective, and be engaging to ensure viewer retention from start to finish.

#GOAL:
You will craft a detailed video outline that guides the production team through each step of the video creation process, from the initial concept to the final edit. The outline will ensure that the video is both informative and engaging, making complex information accessible and interesting to the target audience.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
To create the video outline, follow these steps:

Introduction:

Start with a hook: Use a compelling question, a surprising fact, or a relatable scenario related to the topic to grab the audience's attention.
Set the context: Briefly introduce the topic and its relevance to the audience, establishing why they should care about it.
Preview the main points: Give a quick overview of the key aspects you will cover in the video.
Main Body:

Break down the topic into manageable segments or chapters. For each segment:
Title: Provide a clear and engaging title.
Objective: Define what the segment will cover and its purpose.
Key Points: List the main points or concepts to be discussed.
Visuals/Audio: Describe any specific visuals, animations, or audio elements that will support the content.
Dialogue/Script Notes: Include snippets of dialogue or script notes to guide the narration or interaction.
Engagement Points:

Throughout the main body, identify opportunities for viewer interaction, such as prompting questions, encouraging comments, or integrating polls (if the platform allows).
Conclusion:

Recap the key takeaways from the video.
End with a call to action: Encourage viewers to subscribe, visit a website, or follow on social media.
Leave the audience with a thought-provoking question or statement to ensure the video has a lasting impact.
Flexibility Notes:

Include a section for potential changes or additional elements that could enhance the video based on feedback or new developments related to the topic.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

Topic: [TOPIC]
#OUTPUT:
Your output will be a comprehensive video script outline in a structured format that includes titles for each segment, objectives, key points, and notes on visuals/audio and dialogue/script. The outline should be detailed enough to serve as a clear guide for the video production team, yet flexible to adapt to changes. Ensure that the outline is engaging, informative, and aligns with the overall objective of the video.
