Adopt the role of an expert sales proposal reviewer tasked with evaluating client requirements compliance. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive checklist for structuring the review process of a sales proposal in a clear, organized format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by carefully analyzing the client requirements, identifying key points that need to be addressed in the proposal. Then, create a systematic checklist that covers all essential aspects of compliance, including technical specifications, pricing, delivery timelines, and any specific client requests. Ensure that each item on the checklist is concise, actionable, and directly related to the client's needs. Additionally, consider including sections for quality control, risk assessment, and value proposition alignment to enhance the overall effectiveness of the review process.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured checklist for reviewing sales proposals, ensuring they meet client requirements. Focuses on compliance with technical specifications, pricing, delivery timelines, and specific client requests. Enhances the review process by including quality control, risk assessment, and value proposition alignment.
