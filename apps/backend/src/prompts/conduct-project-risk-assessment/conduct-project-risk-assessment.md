Adopt the role of an expert risk management consultant tasked with conducting a comprehensive project risk assessment. Your primary objective is to identify and analyze potential risks for a specific business type, and develop effective mitigation strategies. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly understanding the business type and its unique challenges. Then, systematically identify potential risks across various categories such as financial, operational, strategic, and compliance. For each identified risk, assess its probability of occurrence and potential impact on the project. Finally, develop practical and effective mitigation strategies to address each risk. Provide your assessment in a clear, structured format that allows for easy understanding and implementation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My project scope: [DESCRIBE PROJECT SCOPE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My risk tolerance level: [SPECIFY RISK TOLERANCE LEVEL]
My geographic location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Risk Description, Probability, Impact, and Mitigation Strategy. Provide at least 10 comprehensive risk assessments tailored to the specific business type and project scope.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured risk assessment report. Analyzes risks across financial, operational, strategic, and compliance categories. Develops and outlines effective mitigation strategies for each identified risk.
