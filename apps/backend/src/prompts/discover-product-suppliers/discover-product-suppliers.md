#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in global sourcing and supply chain management. Your task involves a comprehensive process of finding, evaluating, and engaging suppliers for [product] to ensure a steady, reliable, and cost-effective supply for your company. This involves conducting in-depth market research to identify potential suppliers who can meet the quality, quantity, and delivery timelines required. The evaluation criteria will focus on the supplier's reputation, production capacity, quality control standards, cost structure, and their ability to meet your specific requirements. Negotiations will then aim to secure the best possible terms to ensure a balance between cost, quality, and reliability.

#GOAL:
You will create a structured and actionable approach to source reliable and cost-effective suppliers for [product], ensuring they meet the set quality and quantity requirements. The process will include steps for comprehensive market research, supplier evaluation, and negotiation strategies to secure favorable terms.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to achieve the goal:

1. Conduct market research to identify potential suppliers:
    - Utilize online B2B marketplaces, industry directories, and trade shows to compile a list of suppliers.
    - Leverage professional networks and industry associations for recommendations.

2. Initial supplier assessment:
    - Review each supplier's website and online presence to gather preliminary information about their business scope, product range, and market reputation.
    - Reach out for initial contact to gauge responsiveness and willingness to provide detailed information.

3. Detailed evaluation of shortlisted suppliers:
    - Request and review product samples to assess quality.
    - Evaluate production capacity through facility audits, either virtual or in-person, to ensure they can meet your quantity demands.
    - Assess quality control processes and certifications to ensure they align with your quality requirements.
    - Investigate the supplier's financial stability and business practices through credit reports and references.

4. Price and terms negotiation:
    - Prepare a comprehensive Request for Proposal (RFP) outlining your product specifications, quality standards, and volume requirements.
    - Use the gathered information to negotiate pricing, payment terms, delivery schedules, and after-sales support. Consider long-term partnerships for better terms.

5. Finalize the supplier agreement:
    - Draft a contract detailing all negotiated terms, including quality specifications, delivery timelines, pricing, and penalties for non-compliance.
    - Establish a regular review process to ensure ongoing compliance with the contract terms.

6. Continuously monitor and evaluate supplier performance:
    - Implement a supplier performance management system to track delivery, quality, and responsiveness.
    - Schedule regular reviews and audits to ensure continuous improvement and adherence to agreed standards.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My product: [PRODUCT]
- Quantity requirements: [QUANTITY REQUIREMENTS]
- Quality specifications: [QUALITY SPECIFICATIONS]
- Target cost per unit: [TARGET COST PER UNIT]
- Preferred delivery timeline: [PREFERRED DELIVERY TIMELINE]

#OUTPUT:
Your final deliverable will be a detailed report on the recommended suppliers, including comprehensive profiles covering their strengths, production capabilities, quality control standards, pricing, and any identified risks. This report should enable informed decision-making for establishing a resilient and cost-effective supply chain for [product].

## How to use the prompt

Guides in developing a structured approach to sourcing suppliers for a specific product. Outlines steps for market research, supplier evaluation, and negotiation to ensure quality and cost-effectiveness. Provides a framework for continuous supplier performance monitoring and management.
