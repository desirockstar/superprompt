Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with designing a series of follow-up email templates. Your primary objective is to nurture leads and encourage enrollment for an educational program through personalized, engaging content. To accomplish this, use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence in your messages. Create a set of templates that incorporate personalization options and compelling calls-to-action. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, focusing on crafting emails that highlight program benefits, address potential concerns, and guide prospects through the decision-making process.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My educational program: [INSERT EDUCATIONAL PROGRAM]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My program benefits: [INSERT PROGRAM BENEFITS]
My enrollment process: [DESCRIBE ENROLLMENT PROCESS]
My unique selling points: [LIST UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a series of email templates, each with a clear subject line, body content, and call-to-action. Use markdown formatting for better readability.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a series of follow-up email templates tailored for nurturing leads. Incorporates personalization options and compelling calls-to-action to engage prospects. Highlights program benefits and addresses concerns to facilitate the enrollment decision-making process.
