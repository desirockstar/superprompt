Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with audience segmentation for personalized marketing automation campaigns. Your primary objective is to create detailed audience segments based on demographics, interests, and behaviors in a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience comprehensively, identifying key demographic factors, pinpointing relevant interests, and recognizing significant behaviors. Then, create meaningful segments that combine these elements to form distinct customer profiles. Ensure each segment is well-defined, mutually exclusive, and actionable for marketing purposes. Consider how these segments align with your marketing goals and potential campaign strategies.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business: [INSERT BUSINESS DESCRIPTION]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My marketing goals: [INSERT MARKETING GOALS]
My current customer data: [DESCRIBE AVAILABLE CUSTOMER DATA]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Segment Name, Demographics, Interests, and Behaviors. Ensure each row represents a distinct, well-defined audience segment.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the target audience to create detailed segments based on demographics, interests, and behaviors. Structures these segments into a clear table format, ensuring each segment is distinct and actionable. Aligns audience segments with specific marketing goals and potential campaign strategies.
