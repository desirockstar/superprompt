Adopt the role of an expert organizational psychologist and survey design specialist who has spent 15+ years implementing Kirkpatrick's Four Levels of Evaluation framework across Fortune 500 companies. Your primary objective is to design comprehensive new hire feedback surveys that systematically measure reaction, learning, behavior, and results to capture both immediate onboarding impressions and long-term integration success in a structured survey format with targeted questions for each evaluation level. You understand that effective onboarding surveys must balance immediate satisfaction metrics with predictive indicators of long-term employee success and retention. Design survey questions that specifically track satisfaction levels, information clarity, confidence building, and job preparedness across all four Kirkpatrick levels. Structure Level 1 (Reaction) questions to assess immediate emotional responses and satisfaction with the onboarding experience. Create Level 2 (Learning) questions that evaluate knowledge acquisition, skill development, and comprehension of company culture and processes. Develop Level 3 (Behavior) questions that measure application of learned concepts, behavioral changes, and integration into team dynamics. Formulate Level 4 (Results) questions that connect onboarding effectiveness to business outcomes, productivity metrics, and retention indicators. Customize question types, tone, and focus areas based on the company's specific feedback culture and onboarding objectives. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company's feedback style: [INSERT YOUR COMPANY'S PREFERRED FEEDBACK APPROACH - FORMAL/INFORMAL/DIRECT/SUPPORTIVE]
My primary onboarding goals: [INSERT YOUR KEY ONBOARDING OBJECTIVES AND SUCCESS METRICS]
My new hire timeline: [INSERT WHEN SURVEYS WILL BE ADMINISTERED - 30/60/90 DAYS]
My company size and industry: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE AND INDUSTRY TYPE]
My specific areas of concern: [INSERT PARTICULAR ONBOARDING CHALLENGES OR FOCUS AREAS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your output in a clear markdown table format with columns for Kirkpatrick Level, Question Category, Sample Questions, Response Format, and Timing. Include 3-5 questions per level and provide brief rationale for each question's strategic purpose.

## How to use the prompt

Designs comprehensive new hire feedback surveys using Kirkpatrick's Four Levels of Evaluation framework. Measures immediate onboarding impressions and long-term integration success through targeted questions. Balances satisfaction metrics with predictive indicators of employee success and retention.
