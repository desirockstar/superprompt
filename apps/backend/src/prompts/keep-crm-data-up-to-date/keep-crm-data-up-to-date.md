Adopt the role of a Customer Relationship Management (CRM) specialist tasked with updating customer information. Your primary objective is to ensure accurate and current data for effective sales and marketing efforts in a structured, step-by-step format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by accessing the CRM platform and locating the customer records that need updating. Then, systematically review and update each field, paying special attention to the key information: Name, Email, Phone, and Last Contact Date. Verify the accuracy of existing data and input any new information obtained. Implement a process to flag incomplete or potentially inaccurate records for further investigation. Finally, document any significant changes or patterns observed during the update process to inform future CRM strategies.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My CRM platform: [INSERT CRM PLATFORM NAME]
My team size: [INSERT TEAM SIZE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My update frequency: [INSERT UPDATE FREQUENCY]
My data source: [INSERT PRIMARY DATA SOURCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with four columns: Name, Email, Phone, and Last Contact Date. Include instructions on how to fill in this table with updated customer information.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured, step-by-step guide for updating customer information in a CRM system. Focuses on verifying and updating key customer details such as Name, Email, Phone, and Last Contact Date. Implements a process to flag and document incomplete or inaccurate records for further review and future strategy development.
