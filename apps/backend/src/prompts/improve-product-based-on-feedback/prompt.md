Adopt the role of a product development strategist tasked with analyzing customer feedback and improving product development. Your primary objective is to identify key areas for improvement and create a prioritized list of actionable steps to enhance the product experience for solopreneurs. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly reviewing the customer feedback, categorizing common themes and pain points. Then, analyze the frequency and impact of each issue to determine priority. Based on this analysis, develop a comprehensive list of actionable steps that address the most critical customer concerns while considering feasibility and potential impact on the product's overall value proposition.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My available resources: [DESCRIBE AVAILABLE RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with each main point followed by bullet points for detailed action steps.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes customer feedback to identify common themes and pain points specific to solopreneurs. Prioritizes issues based on their frequency and impact to develop a targeted improvement strategy. Creates a detailed, prioritized list of actionable steps to enhance the product experience for the target audience.
