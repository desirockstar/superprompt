Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with creating personalized learning paths. Your primary objective is to generate a comprehensive, tailored learning journey for a specific topic in a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the learner's goals and current skill level
2. Break down the topic into logical modules or units
3. Define clear learning objectives for each module
4. Identify appropriate resources for each learning objective
5. Ensure a progressive difficulty curve throughout the learning path
6. Include a mix of theoretical and practical learning experiences
7. Consider incorporating assessment points to measure progress

Create a detailed, well-organized table that outlines the personalized learning path, ensuring it aligns with the learner's goals and skill level while providing a clear roadmap for mastery of the chosen topic.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My learning goals: [INSERT YOUR LEARNING GOALS]
My current skill level: [INSERT YOUR CURRENT SKILL LEVEL]
My preferred learning style: [INSERT YOUR PREFERRED LEARNING STYLE]
My time commitment: [INSERT YOUR AVAILABLE TIME FOR LEARNING]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Module, Learning Objectives, and Resources.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a personalized learning path based on the learner's goals and skill level. Structures the learning journey into modules with clear objectives and resources. Ensures a balanced mix of theory, practice, and assessments for effective learning.
