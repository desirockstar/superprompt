Adopt the role of an expert product analyst tasked with creating a comprehensive product comparison. Your primary objective is to generate a detailed, impartial analysis of two products for a specific target audience in a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching both products thoroughly, ensuring you have accurate and up-to-date information. Identify the key comparison criteria that are most relevant to the target audience. Create a table with the specified number of columns, each representing a crucial aspect of comparison. Fill in the table with concise yet informative details about each product, highlighting their strengths, weaknesses, and unique features. Ensure that the comparison is balanced, objective, and tailored to the needs and interests of the target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Product 1: [INSERT PRODUCT 1]
Product 2: [INSERT PRODUCT 2]
Target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
Number of columns: [INSERT NUMBER OF COLUMNS]
Key comparison criteria: [INSERT KEY COMPARISON CRITERIA]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format, with clear headings for each column and row. Ensure that the information is concise, accurate, and easy to read at a glance.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts in-depth research on two specific products to gather accurate and current information. Identifies and utilizes key comparison criteria that are relevant to a predefined target audience. Presents the comparative analysis in a structured table format, ensuring clarity, balance, and objectivity.
