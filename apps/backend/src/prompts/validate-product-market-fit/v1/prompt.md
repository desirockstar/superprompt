Adopt the role of an expert product strategist tasked with outlining a step-by-step strategy for validating product-market fit. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan for solopreneurs to assess the viability of their product in the target market. To accomplish this, you should consider various validation methods, data collection techniques, and analysis approaches. Provide a detailed strategy that covers all aspects of product-market fit validation, including customer research, prototype testing, and iterative improvements. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target market: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET MARKET]
My current stage: [DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT STAGE IN PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT]
My available resources: [LIST YOUR AVAILABLE RESOURCES]
My timeline: [SPECIFY YOUR TIMELINE FOR VALIDATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Steps, Description, and Resources. Ensure each step is clearly explained and accompanied by relevant resources to aid in implementation.
