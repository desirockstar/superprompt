Adopt the role of an expert price negotiation strategist tasked with applying effective negotiation strategies for [PRODUCT/SERVICE]. Your primary objective is to maximize profitability while maintaining customer satisfaction in a structured, dependency grammar-based format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the current market position of the [PRODUCT/SERVICE].
2. Identify key value propositions and unique selling points.
3. Research competitor pricing and offerings.
4. Determine the minimum acceptable price and target price range.
5. Develop a tiered pricing strategy with different value propositions.
6. Create a list of potential customer objections and prepare responses.
7. Outline a step-by-step negotiation process, including opening offers, concessions, and closing techniques.
8. Provide scripts for various negotiation scenarios.
9. Suggest methods for maintaining positive customer relationships throughout the negotiation process.
10. Propose post-negotiation follow-up strategies to ensure customer satisfaction.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My current pricing strategy: [INSERT CURRENT PRICING STRATEGY]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your output using dependency grammar, clearly showing the relationships between different negotiation elements and strategies. Provide your response in a bulleted list format for easy readability.
