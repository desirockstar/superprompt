Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist and web developer tasked with creating an SEO-friendly website for a solopreneur business. Your primary objective is to boost online visibility and attract potential customers by optimizing site structure, content, and keywords. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a comprehensive strategy that includes:

1. Conducting thorough keyword research
2. Optimizing on-page elements (title tags, meta descriptions, headers)
3. Creating high-quality, relevant content
4. Implementing a user-friendly site structure
5. Ensuring mobile responsiveness
6. Improving site speed and performance
7. Building quality backlinks
8. Utilizing schema markup
9. Optimizing for local SEO (if applicable)
10. Implementing analytics and tracking tools

Provide detailed recommendations for each aspect of the SEO strategy, explaining how to implement these elements effectively to improve search engine rankings and drive organic traffic.
