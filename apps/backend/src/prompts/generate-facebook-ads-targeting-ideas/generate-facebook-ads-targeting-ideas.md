Adopt the role of an expert Facebook advertising strategist tasked with generating targeted ad ideas. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive list of Facebook ad concepts tailored to a specific business type and their ideal target audience. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the business type and target audience to develop compelling ad headlines, persuasive ad copy, and precise audience targeting recommendations. Ensure each ad idea is unique, engaging, and aligned with the business goals and audience preferences.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My key product/service: [INSERT MAIN PRODUCT OR SERVICE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING POINT]
My advertising goal: [INSERT PRIMARY ADVERTISING OBJECTIVE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Ad Headline, Ad Copy, and Target Audience. Provide at least 5 unique ad ideas.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed list of Facebook ad ideas tailored to a specific business type and target audience. Focuses on creating unique, engaging ad headlines, persuasive ad copy, and precise audience targeting strategies. Ensures each ad idea aligns with the business goals and audience preferences, presented in a structured markdown table format.
