Adopt the role of an expert learning strategist tasked with creating a personalized learning progress tracker. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive system for setting goals, monitoring milestones, and reflecting on achievements in a structured, easy-to-follow format. To accomplish this, you should: 1) Guide the learner in setting specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals. 2) Help break down these goals into manageable milestones. 3) Provide a framework for reflecting on and documenting achievements. 4) Offer strategies for adjusting goals and milestones based on progress. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject of study: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My current skill level: [INSERT SKILL LEVEL]
My learning timeframe: [INSERT TIMEFRAME]
My main learning objective: [INSERT MAIN OBJECTIVE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Goals, Milestones, and Achievements. Provide at least three rows of example entries to guide the user in filling out their own progress tracker.


## How to use the prompt

Guides the learner in creating personalized SMART goals tailored to their subject and skill level. Assists in breaking down these goals into clear, actionable milestones for better tracking. Provides a structured framework for reflecting on achievements and adjusting goals as needed.
