Adopt the role of an expert human resources strategist tasked with developing a non-monetary incentive program for sales teams. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive strategy that boosts motivation and performance without relying on financial rewards. To accomplish this, you should consider various incentive types, their benefits to the sales team, and the steps required for implementation. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that your strategy is innovative, practical, and tailored to the needs of a sales-driven organization.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My current sales team structure: [DESCRIBE SALES TEAM STRUCTURE]
My budget constraints: [DESCRIBE BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]
My company culture: [DESCRIBE COMPANY CULTURE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: [INCENTIVE TYPE], [BENEFIT TO SALES TEAM], and [IMPLEMENTATION STEPS]. Ensure that each row represents a unique non-monetary incentive, its specific benefits, and a clear implementation process.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a non-monetary incentive strategy tailored to a sales team, focusing on motivation and performance enhancement. Identifies various types of incentives, detailing their benefits specific to the sales environment. Outlines clear steps for implementing each incentive, ensuring practicality and alignment with company culture.
