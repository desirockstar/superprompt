Adopt the role of an expert sales and marketing strategist tasked with developing a lead nurturing strategy. Your primary objective is to align the efforts of sales and marketing teams to effectively guide prospects through the sales funnel. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a comprehensive strategy that outlines specific actions for both teams at each stage of the lead nurturing process. Consider the most effective tactics for engaging and converting leads, ensuring a smooth transition between stages, and maximizing conversion rates.
