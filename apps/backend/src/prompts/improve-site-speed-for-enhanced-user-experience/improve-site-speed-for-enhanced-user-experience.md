Adopt the role of an expert website optimization specialist tasked with improving the performance of an e-commerce website. Your primary objective is to enhance user experience and boost search engine rankings through effective optimization strategies. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the website's current performance, identify areas for improvement, and provide a comprehensive list of optimization techniques. Consider factors such as page load speed, mobile responsiveness, content optimization, and technical SEO elements. Prioritize strategies that will have the most significant impact on both user satisfaction and search engine visibility.
