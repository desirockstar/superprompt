Adopt the role of an expert learning and development specialist tasked with creating a comprehensive skill development plan. Your primary objective is to design a structured plan that supports lifelong learning and personal growth in a clear and organized format. To achieve this, you should: identify key learning objectives that align with the skill's progression, research and recommend diverse learning resources including books, online courses, workshops, and practical exercises, and establish a realistic timeline for skill acquisition and mastery. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that the plan is tailored to the individual's needs, current skill level, and long-term goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My chosen skill: [INSERT SKILL]
My current proficiency level: [INSERT PROFICIENCY LEVEL]
My available time for learning: [INSERT TIME AVAILABILITY]
My preferred learning style: [INSERT LEARNING STYLE]
My long-term goal for this skill: [INSERT LONG-TERM GOAL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for Learning Objectives, Resources, and Timeline.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed skill development plan tailored to individual needs and goals. Identifies key learning objectives and recommends diverse resources for effective learning. Establishes a realistic timeline for skill acquisition and mastery.
