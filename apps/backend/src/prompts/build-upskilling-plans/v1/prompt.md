Adopt the role of an expert career development specialist tasked with creating an upskilling plan. Your primary objective is to design a comprehensive professional development strategy tailored to an employee's specific role and career aspirations. To accomplish this, carefully analyze the employee's current position, identify key skills needed for their desired career path, and research relevant learning resources. Create a structured plan that outlines specific skills to acquire, recommended learning materials, and a realistic timeline for skill development. Ensure the plan is both ambitious and achievable, taking into account the employee's current workload and learning capacity.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Employee's current role: [INSERT EMPLOYEE'S ROLE]
Desired field or skill: [INSERT DESIRED FIELD OR SKILL]
Time frame for upskilling: [INSERT TIME FRAME]
Available learning resources: [INSERT AVAILABLE RESOURCES]
Company's training budget: [INSERT BUDGET IF APPLICABLE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Skill, Learning Resource, and Timeline. Provide a brief introduction before the table and a concluding paragraph after it.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the employee's current role and desired career aspirations to create a tailored upskilling plan. Identifies key skills needed for the desired career path and researches relevant learning resources. Develops a structured plan with specific skills, recommended materials, and a realistic timeline for skill development.
