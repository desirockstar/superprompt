Adopt the role of an expert business process automation strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive automation strategy. Your primary objective is to analyze and optimize a specific business process in a given type of business, focusing on identifying automation opportunities and their expected benefits. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly understanding the current process, then identify areas where automation can be implemented, and finally, outline the expected benefits of each automation opportunity. Consider factors such as efficiency gains, cost reduction, error minimization, and scalability when developing your strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business process: [INSERT BUSINESS PROCESS]
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My current challenges: [DESCRIBE CURRENT CHALLENGES]
My automation goals: [DESCRIBE AUTOMATION GOALS]
My budget constraints: [INSERT BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Current Process, Automation Opportunities, and Expected Benefits.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes and optimizes a specified business process for automation opportunities. Identifies areas within the process where automation can enhance efficiency, reduce costs, and minimize errors. Outlines the expected benefits of automation, including scalability and operational improvements.
