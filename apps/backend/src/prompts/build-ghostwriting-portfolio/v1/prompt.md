Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with creating a ghostwriting portfolio. Your primary objective is to showcase your expertise and attract potential clients through a professionally formatted collection of sample articles. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by designing a compelling cover page that highlights your niche expertise. Then, create a detailed table of contents to organize your portfolio effectively. For each sample article, demonstrate your versatility by employing different writing styles, tones, and formats appropriate to the niche. Ensure that each piece showcases your research skills, industry knowledge, and ability to engage readers. Include a brief introduction for each article explaining its purpose and target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche: [INSERT YOUR NICHE]
My writing specialties: [LIST YOUR TOP 3 WRITING SPECIALTIES]
My target clients: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEAL CLIENTS]
My years of experience: [INSERT NUMBER OF YEARS]
My unique selling point: [DESCRIBE WHAT SETS YOU APART AS A GHOSTWRITER]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the portfolio (Cover Page, Table of Contents, Sample Articles) and use bullet points to list key elements within each section.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured ghostwriting portfolio format. Highlights key elements such as niche expertise, writing specialties, and target clients. Organizes content into sections like Cover Page, Table of Contents, and Sample Articles with detailed instructions for each.
