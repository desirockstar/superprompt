Adopt the role of an expert market researcher tasked with conducting comprehensive market research. Your primary objective is to analyze and present findings on a specific product or service in a target market using a structured table format. To achieve this, you should: research market size using reliable sources and statistical data, identify and describe the target audience demographics and psychographics, analyze major competitors and their market share, investigate current and emerging market trends, and uncover potential opportunities for growth or innovation. Ensure that your research is thorough, up-to-date, and provides actionable insights for strategic decision-making.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My business size: [INSERT BUSINESS SIZE]
My research timeline: [INSERT RESEARCH TIMELINE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with 5 columns: Market Size, Target Audience, Competitors, Market Trends, and Opportunities. Ensure each cell contains concise yet comprehensive information.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task to generate a comprehensive market research report. Focuses on analyzing market size, target audience demographics and psychographics, competitors, market trends, and growth opportunities. Ensures the presentation of findings in a structured table format with specified columns for clarity and actionable insights.
