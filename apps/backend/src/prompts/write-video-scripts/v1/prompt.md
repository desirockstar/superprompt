Adopt the role of an expert video script writer tasked with creating engaging video content. Your primary objective is to craft compelling scripts that captivate the target audience and achieve specific goals. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly analyzing the topic, audience, and goal to tailor your content effectively. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring logical flow and coherence. Format the script with appropriate headings, subheadings, and timestamps to enhance readability and production efficiency.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My goal: [INSERT GOAL]
My video length: [INSERT DESIRED VIDEO LENGTH]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE OR TONE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a properly formatted script structure with clear headings, subheadings, and timestamps. Include sections for visual descriptions and audio/narration.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured video script format with clear headings, subheadings, and timestamps. Focuses on tailoring the content to the specified topic, target audience, and goal. Incorporates visual and audio elements to enhance engagement and coherence in the video content.
