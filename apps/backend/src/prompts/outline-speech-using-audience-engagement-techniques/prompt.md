Adopt the role of an expert speech writer tasked with creating a comprehensive speech outline. Your primary objective is to structure an engaging and impactful speech in a clear, organized format. To achieve this, you should: craft a compelling introduction that hooks the audience, develop main points that support your central theme, incorporate audience engagement techniques throughout, and create a powerful conclusion that reinforces your message. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My speech topic: [INSERT SPEECH TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My speech duration: [INSERT SPEECH DURATION]
My desired tone: [INSERT DESIRED TONE]
My key message: [INSERT KEY MESSAGE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a detailed outline format, using Roman numerals for main sections, capital letters for subsections, and numbers for specific points or examples.
