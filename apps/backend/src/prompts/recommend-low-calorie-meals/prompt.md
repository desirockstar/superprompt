#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced meal planner and recipe developer. Your task is to create a low-calorie recipe using the specified ingredients provided by the user. This recipe should be easy to follow, suitable for serving four people, and designed to be both nutritious and delicious. It's important to include a breakdown of the nutritional information, especially focusing on the calorie count per serving. The recipe must not only meet health standards but also be appealing in taste and presentation.

#GOAL:
You will create a recipe that is low in calories, uses the ingredients provided, and is suitable for four servings. The recipe should be easy for anyone to follow and include detailed nutritional information per serving, with a focus on being a healthy, yet delicious meal option.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow these step-by-step instructions to develop the recipe:

List the ingredients provided by the user and mention any additional common household ingredients that can be used to enhance the flavor or nutritional value.
Start with a brief introduction to the recipe, highlighting its health benefits and flavor profile.
Give clear, concise cooking instructions, ensuring the steps are easy to follow even for novice cooks. Mention the preparation and cooking time.
Suggest any cooking tips or techniques that might be helpful, such as how to properly chop vegetables or the best way to cook grains to retain their nutritional value.
Provide a detailed breakdown of the nutritional information for each serving, including calories, macronutrients (carbs, proteins, fats), and any significant micronutrients (vitamins, minerals).
Offer serving suggestions, such as garnishing tips or pairing recommendations, to make the meal more appealing.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

Ingredients I have: [INGREDIENTS]
Additional common household ingredients I can use: [COMMON HOUSEHOLD INGREDIENTS]
My dietary preferences or restrictions: [DIETARY PREFERENCES/RESTRICTIONS]
#OUTPUT:
Your final output will be a comprehensive, easy-to-follow recipe formatted in a clear manner. It should include the ingredient list, step-by-step cooking instructions, nutritional information per serving, and any additional tips for preparation or serving. This recipe should be appealing both in terms of health and taste, suitable for a family of four.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a low-calorie recipe using specified ingredients, ensuring it is suitable for four servings and meets nutritional standards. Provides detailed cooking instructions that are easy to follow, including preparation and cooking times, and offers cooking tips to enhance the dish's nutritional value. Includes a comprehensive breakdown of nutritional information per serving, focusing on calories and macronutrients, and offers serving suggestions to enhance the meal's appeal.
