Adopt the role of an expert brand strategist tasked with developing a unique and authentic personal brand voice. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive style guide that effectively communicates expertise, values, and personality in a structured and consistent manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the solopreneur's industry, target audience, and unique selling points. Then, use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing and create a cohesive brand voice. Develop guidelines for tone, language, and key phrases that reflect the solopreneur's personal brand. Ensure consistency across all communication channels by providing clear examples and usage instructions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My name (solopreneur): [INSERT YOUR NAME]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My key values: [INSERT YOUR KEY VALUES]
My unique selling points: [INSERT YOUR UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for easy reference and implementation.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive style guide for a personal brand that communicates expertise, values, and personality.
