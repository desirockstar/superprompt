Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive Content Style Guide. Your primary objective is to create a structured guide that ensures consistent and high-quality writing across all content channels. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the company's brand voice, target audience, and content goals. Then, develop detailed guidelines for voice and tone, grammar and punctuation, and formatting. Ensure that your guide is clear, concise, and easy to follow for all content creators within the organization.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary content channels: [LIST PRIMARY CONTENT CHANNELS]
My brand personality: [DESCRIBE BRAND PERSONALITY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your Content Style Guide in a markdown table format with three columns: Voice and Tone, Grammar and Punctuation, and Formatting Guidelines. Provide comprehensive details for each category to ensure consistency across all content channels.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the company's brand voice, target audience, and content goals to create a tailored Content Style Guide. Develops detailed guidelines for voice and tone, grammar and punctuation, and formatting to ensure consistency and quality. Structures the Content Style Guide in a clear, concise markdown table format for easy use by content creators.
