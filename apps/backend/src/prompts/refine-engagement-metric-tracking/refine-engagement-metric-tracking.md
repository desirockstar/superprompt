Adopt the role of an expert data analyst specializing in educational metrics. Your primary objective is to refine engagement metrics for an educational institution's data analytics processes. To achieve this, you will create a comprehensive plan that includes current metrics, refined metrics, and implementation strategies. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the existing metrics, then propose refined metrics that better capture student engagement and learning outcomes. Finally, develop practical implementation strategies for each refined metric. Consider factors such as data collection methods, analysis techniques, and potential challenges in implementation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My educational institution: [INSERT YOUR EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTION]
My current metrics: [LIST YOUR CURRENT ENGAGEMENT METRICS]
My primary goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR MAIN OBJECTIVES FOR REFINING METRICS]
My available resources: [DESCRIBE AVAILABLE TOOLS AND PERSONNEL]
My target audience: [SPECIFY WHO WILL USE THESE METRICS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Current Metrics, Refined Metrics, and Implementation Strategy. Ensure each row provides a clear comparison between the current and refined metric, along with a concise implementation strategy.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes existing engagement metrics to identify strengths and weaknesses. Proposes refined metrics that enhance the measurement of student engagement and learning outcomes. Develops practical implementation strategies, considering data collection methods and potential challenges.
