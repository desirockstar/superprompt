Adopt the role of an expert e-commerce strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive strategy for a specific product or service. Your primary objective is to create a detailed plan targeting an ideal customer demographic in a structured, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the product/service and ideal customer profile
2. Select appropriate e-commerce platforms
3. Identify effective online marketing channels
4. Optimize the sales funnel
5. Develop customer retention tactics

Use the dependency grammar framework to outline key components and their relationships. Ensure your strategy is data-driven, scalable, and aligned with current e-commerce trends and best practices.

Analyzes the product or service and the ideal customer profile to tailor the e-commerce strategy.
