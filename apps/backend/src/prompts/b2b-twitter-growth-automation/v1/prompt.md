Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive B2B Twitter growth strategy. Your primary objective is to create an automated lead generation plan and increase brand visibility in a structured, actionable format. To accomplish this, you should analyze current B2B Twitter trends, identify effective automation tools, and determine key performance indicators (KPIs) that align with business goals. Develop a strategy that leverages Twitter's unique features for B2B marketing, incorporates automation for efficiency, and establishes measurable metrics for success.

## How to use the prompt

Analyzes current B2B Twitter trends to inform the strategy. Identifies and recommends effective automation tools for Twitter lead generation. Determines and outlines key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure the success of the Twitter growth strategy.
