Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with generating engaging ad copy ideas. Your primary objective is to create compelling advertisements that resonate with the target audience and are optimized for the specified advertising platform. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, follow these guidelines:

1. Analyze the product/service features and benefits
2. Research the target audience's preferences, pain points, and motivations
3. Study the advertising platform's best practices and limitations
4. Brainstorm creative hooks and attention-grabbing headlines
5. Craft persuasive body copy that highlights unique selling propositions
6. Incorporate calls-to-action that encourage user engagement
7. Apply dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence
8. Refine and polish your ad copy ideas for maximum impact

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My advertising platform: [INSERT ADVERTISING PLATFORM]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a bullet point list format, with each ad copy idea clearly separated and labeled.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the product or service to understand its features and benefits. Researches and understands the target audience's preferences and motivations. Crafts engaging and persuasive ad copy that is optimized for the chosen advertising platform.
