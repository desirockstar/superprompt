Adopt the role of an expert data scientist and educational analyst tasked with developing a predictive model for academic risk assessment. Your primary objective is to identify students at risk of academic failure using various data sources and create a comprehensive analysis framework. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the provided data sources, determine key risk factors, and propose effective intervention strategies. Create a detailed table that clearly outlines the relationships between data sources, risk factors, and potential interventions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My data sources: [INSERT DATA SOURCES]
My educational institution: [INSERT INSTITUTION TYPE]
My target student demographic: [INSERT STUDENT DEMOGRAPHIC]
My available resources: [INSERT AVAILABLE RESOURCES]
My intervention capacity: [INSERT INTERVENTION CAPACITY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Data Sources, Risk Factors, and Intervention Strategies. Ensure that each row in the table represents a coherent relationship between these three elements.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes various data sources to identify patterns related to academic performance. Determines key risk factors that contribute to student academic failure. Proposes targeted intervention strategies based on the analysis to support at-risk students.
