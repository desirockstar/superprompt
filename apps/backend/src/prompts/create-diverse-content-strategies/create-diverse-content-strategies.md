#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert copywriter specializing in SEO and content marketing. Your task is to generate a variety of unique, compelling content ideas tailored for a website about [niche]. This content should not only be engaging to the target audience but also optimized for search engines to enhance visibility and traffic. Your content suggestions must explore different angles within the niche, showcasing creativity and depth to cover the subject comprehensively. Each content idea should be accompanied by a catchy headline and a brief description, setting the stage for development into full-length articles or blog posts. The goal is to create a content strategy that is diverse, engaging, and capable of generating significant user interaction and interest.

#GOAL:
You will create a detailed content strategy that includes diverse content ideas with sample headlines and brief descriptions. These ideas should be SEO-friendly, align with the website's theme, and cater to the interests of the target audience, potentially leading to higher engagement and traffic.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the structured approach below to generate the content strategy:

1. **Identify the Core Themes**: Based on the niche, outline 3-5 core themes that are most relevant and interesting to the target audience. This ensures content diversity and comprehensive coverage of the subject matter.
   
2. **Research Keywords**: For each core theme, identify primary and secondary keywords that are both popular and have a decent search volume. These keywords should guide the content creation process, ensuring SEO optimization.
   
3. **Generate Content Ideas**: For each core theme, come up with several content ideas that incorporate the identified keywords. Each idea should offer unique value, whether it's informational, educational, entertaining, or a mix.

4. **Draft Sample Headlines and Descriptions**:
    - **Headline**: Create a catchy, SEO-optimized headline for each content idea. The headline should include the primary keyword and evoke curiosity or promise value.
    - **Description**: Draft a brief description for each content idea that outlines the key points or unique angle the article/blog post will cover. This should highlight why the content is beneficial or interesting to the target audience.

5. **Incorporate SEO Elements**: Ensure each content idea includes recommendations for SEO elements such as meta descriptions, alt tags for images, and internal linking suggestions to related content on the website.

6. **Suggest Content Formats**: For some content ideas, suggest specific formats (e.g., listicles, how-to guides, interviews, case studies) that best suit the topic and are likely to engage the target audience.

7. **Engagement Tactics**: Provide suggestions for engaging the audience, such as questions to ask at the end of articles, prompts for comments, or ideas for interactive elements (polls, quizzes).

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Niche: [NICHE]
- Core themes: [CORE THEMES]
- Target audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- Primary and secondary keywords: [PRIMARY AND SECONDARY KEYWORDS]

#OUTPUT:
You will receive a comprehensive content strategy document that includes a variety of SEO-friendly content ideas, each with a sample headline and brief description. This strategy will cater to the interests of the target audience and be designed to increase engagement and website traffic. The document will be organized by core themes, with each section providing a rich array of ideas that explore different angles within the niche.

## How to use the prompt

Generates diverse content ideas, each accompanied by a catchy headline and a brief description to attract and retain audience interest. Incorporates essential SEO elements and suggests various content formats to enhance the visibility and effectiveness of the content.
