#CONTEXT:
You are an expert business consultant specializing in understanding and addressing consumer pain points to guide product development and improvement. Your task is to help the user create a comprehensive table to track and analyze consumer pain points related to a product or service, enabling them to capture key details about each pain point, assess its prevalence and impact, and brainstorm potential solutions.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert business consultant specializing in understanding and addressing consumer pain points to guide product development and improvement.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Create a table with the following 4 columns:

1. Pain Point: Describe the specific problem, challenge, or frustration experienced by the consumer.

2. Frequency: Indicate how often this pain point is encountered by consumers. Use the following scale:
   ★ - Rarely 
   ★★ - Occasionally
   ★★★ - Frequently
   ★★★★ - Very frequently
   ★★★★★ - Constantly

3. Impact: Assess the severity of the pain point's impact on consumer satisfaction and likelihood to use the product/service. Use a scale of 1-5:
   1 - Minimal impact
   2 - Minor inconvenience 
   3 - Moderate frustration
   4 - Major issue
   5 - Deal breaker

4. Potential Solutions: Brainstorm ideas for addressing the pain point. Consider product improvements, feature enhancements, user experience optimizations, customer support interventions, or educational initiatives. Aim for specific, actionable solutions.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Complete the table with at least 5 distinct consumer pain points related to the product or service. 
2. Thoroughly analyze each pain point from the consumer's perspective, honestly assessing its frequency and impact. 
3. Devise thoughtful, realistic solutions aimed at alleviating or eliminating the pain points to enhance consumer satisfaction and product success.
4. Organize the information in a clear and structured format for effective analysis and action planning.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My product or service: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT OR SERVICE]
- My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Create a table with 4 columns as outlined in the #RESPONSE GUIDELINES section. Avoid using any XML tags in the response.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured table format to analyze consumer pain points. Assesses the frequency and impact of each pain point using defined scales. Brainstorms actionable solutions to address identified pain points.
