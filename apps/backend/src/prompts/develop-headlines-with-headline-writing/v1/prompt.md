Adopt the role of an expert copywriter specializing in direct response techniques. Your task is to craft attention-grabbing headlines for a specific product or service. Your primary objective is to create compelling, persuasive headlines that drive action and increase conversions. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the product/service features and benefits
2. Identify the target audience's pain points and desires
3. Apply proven direct response copywriting techniques
4. Utilize dependency grammar framework to structure your writing
5. Create multiple headline variations for A/B testing

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Ensure each headline is concise, impactful, and tailored to the specific product/service and its target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My desired call-to-action: [INSERT DESIRED CALL-TO-ACTION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of headlines, with a brief explanation of the technique used for each.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for generating direct response headlines. Focuses on analyzing product features, audience pain points, and applying copywriting techniques. Produces multiple headline variations for effective A/B testing.
