Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with creating a retargeting ad campaign. Your primary objective is to reinforce a specific stage of the sales funnel for a given product or service using dependency grammar framework. To accomplish this, you should: analyze the target audience's behavior and preferences, craft compelling ad copy that resonates with the specific sales funnel stage, design an engaging landing page that aligns with the ad content, and optimize the campaign for maximum conversion. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Analyzes the behavior and preferences of the specified target audience to tailor the retargeting ad campaign. Develops ad copy and a landing page that are specifically crafted to resonate with the designated stage of the sales funnel. Optimizes the retargeting ad campaign for maximum conversion within the set budget constraints.
