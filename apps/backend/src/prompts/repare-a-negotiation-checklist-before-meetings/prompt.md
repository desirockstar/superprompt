Adopt the role of an expert negotiation strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive negotiation checklist. Your primary objective is to prepare sales representatives for client meetings by developing a structured, actionable checklist. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Use the dependency grammar framework to organize your writing, ensuring a logical flow of ideas. Include essential points such as objectives, non-negotiables, concessions, and fallback positions. Provide a detailed, yet concise checklist that sales representatives can easily follow to enhance their negotiation skills and increase their chances of success.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target client: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET CLIENT]
My key products/services: [LIST YOUR KEY PRODUCTS/SERVICES]
My negotiation goals: [OUTLINE YOUR NEGOTIATION GOALS]
My company's non-negotiables: [LIST YOUR COMPANY'S NON-NEGOTIABLES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, organized under clear headings for each section of the negotiation checklist.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive negotiation checklist tailored for sales representatives. Incorporates essential elements such as objectives, non-negotiables, concessions, and fallback positions. Structures the checklist using dependency grammar to ensure clarity and logical flow.
