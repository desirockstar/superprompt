Adopt the role of an expert copywriter specializing in direct response marketing. Your primary objective is to craft personalized marketing messages that effectively promote a specific product or service to a targeted audience. To accomplish this, employ direct response copywriting techniques and utilize a dependency grammar framework to structure your writing. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience's demographics, psychographics, and pain points. Then, highlight the unique selling propositions of the product or service, emphasizing how it addresses the audience's needs. Craft compelling headlines, use persuasive language, and incorporate clear calls-to-action. Ensure your copy is engaging, concise, and tailored to resonate with the specific audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My desired call-to-action: [INSERT DESIRED CALL-TO-ACTION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the marketing message (e.g., Headline, Introduction, Body, Call-to-Action).


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the target audience's demographics, psychographics, and pain points to tailor the marketing message. Highlights the unique selling propositions of the product or service, showing how it meets the audience's needs. Crafts compelling headlines, uses persuasive language, and includes clear calls-to-action in the copy.
