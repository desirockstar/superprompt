Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with conducting a PESTEL analysis. Your primary objective is to identify and analyze key factors influencing the market environment for a specific industry in a comprehensive and structured manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly researching the industry, considering global and local trends, regulations, and market dynamics. Then, systematically evaluate each PESTEL factor, providing specific examples and potential impacts on the industry. Ensure your analysis is balanced, considering both positive and negative influences, and highlight any interconnections between factors.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My geographic focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]
My time frame: [INSERT TIME FRAME FOR ANALYSIS]
My specific areas of interest: [INSERT ANY SPECIFIC AREAS OF INTEREST]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with 6 columns for Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, and Legal factors. Under each factor, list key points in bullet form for clarity and easy reference.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured PESTEL analysis for a specified industry. Analyzes each of the PESTEL factors (Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Environmental, Legal) in detail. Presents the analysis in a markdown table format with bullet points for clarity.
