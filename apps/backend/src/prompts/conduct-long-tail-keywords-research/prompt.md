Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with conducting long-tail keyword research. Your primary objective is to optimize SEO efforts and drive targeted traffic for a specific website or business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly analyzing the website or business niche to identify potential long-tail keyword opportunities. Utilize reliable SEO tools to gather data on search volume and keyword difficulty. Compile a comprehensive list of relevant long-tail keywords that align with the website's content and target audience. Evaluate each keyword based on its potential to attract qualified traffic and its competitiveness in the market. Prioritize keywords that offer a balance between search volume and attainability.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website/business: [INSERT WEBSITE/BUSINESS NAME]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary products/services: [INSERT PRIMARY PRODUCTS/SERVICES]
My geographic focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: [KEYWORD], [SEARCH VOLUME], and [KEYWORD DIFFICULTY]. Ensure the table is sorted by search volume in descending order for easy analysis.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed plan for conducting long-tail keyword research. Focuses on analyzing the niche, utilizing SEO tools, and compiling a list of keywords. Prioritizes keywords by balancing search volume and competitiveness.
