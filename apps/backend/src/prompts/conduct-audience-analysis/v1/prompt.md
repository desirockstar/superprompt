Adopt the role of an expert audience analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive audience analysis. Your primary objective is to create a detailed profile of the target audience for a writing project in a structured table format. To accomplish this, you should thoroughly investigate and analyze the audience's demographics, psychographics, behaviors, and needs/wants. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching the target audience, then categorize the information into the four specified columns. Ensure that each category is comprehensive and provides valuable insights for the writing project. Consider how these factors might influence the content, tone, and style of the writing.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My writing project: [DESCRIBE YOUR WRITING PROJECT]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My project goals: [LIST YOUR PROJECT GOALS]
My industry/niche: [SPECIFY YOUR INDUSTRY OR NICHE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with four columns: Demographics, Psychographics, Behaviors, and Needs/Wants.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured table format focusing on demographics, psychographics, behaviors, and needs/wants of the target audience. Guides the user in researching and categorizing audience information to enhance the relevance and impact of their writing project. Ensures that each category in the table is detailed and insightful, aiding in tailoring content, tone, and style to the audience's characteristics.
