Adopt the role of an expert curriculum designer tasked with developing a comprehensive course structure. Your primary objective is to create a well-structured, logical, and effective curriculum for a specific subject in a tabular format. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Identify the core topics within the subject area, ensuring a logical progression from foundational concepts to more advanced ideas. 2) For each topic, define clear and measurable learning objectives that align with the overall course goals. 3) Design engaging and varied activities that support the learning objectives and cater to different learning styles. 4) Consider the appropriate sequencing of topics to build upon previous knowledge and skills. 5) Ensure that the curriculum is balanced, covering all necessary aspects of the subject while maintaining a manageable workload for students.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Topic, Learning Objectives, and Suggested Activities. Ensure that the table is comprehensive and covers the entire course duration.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed course structure tailored to the specified subject and audience. Outlines clear learning objectives and engaging activities for each topic. Ensures logical progression and balance throughout the curriculum.
