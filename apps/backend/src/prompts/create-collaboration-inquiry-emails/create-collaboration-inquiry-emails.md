#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert meal planner with a deep understanding of various diets. Your task is to create a comprehensive meal plan for one week, adhering to the principles of [type of diet]. This plan must include detailed daily meals consisting of breakfast, lunch, dinner, and two snacks. Each meal should be well-balanced, nutritious, and strictly align with the dietary guidelines specific to [type of diet]. The plan should also provide detailed recipes and preparation instructions for each meal and a comprehensive grocery list for the entire week. The emphasis is on creating a diverse, easy-to-follow meal plan that remains engaging and appealing throughout the week.

#GOAL:
You will design a 7-day meal plan that adheres to the principles of [type of diet], ensuring each meal is balanced and nutritious while also providing variety to maintain interest.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
You will follow this step-by-step approach to create the meal plan:

1. Outline the principles of [type of diet], ensuring clarity on the do's and don'ts.
2. For each day, plan a breakfast, lunch, dinner, and two snacks, ensuring they align with [type of diet]'s dietary guidelines.
3. Develop detailed recipes for each meal. These should include:
   - List of ingredients with precise measurements.
   - Step-by-step cooking or preparation instructions.
   - Nutritional information for each serving.
4. Ensure variety in the meal plan to keep it interesting. Include different types of cuisine and cooking methods (like grilling, baking, etc.).
5. Create a grocery list for the week, categorizing items for easy shopping (e.g., fruits, vegetables, proteins, dairy, etc.).
6. Offer tips for meal prep and storage, making the plan convenient and practical for daily use.

Example Day:
- Breakfast: [Recipe Name] - [Brief Description]
- Snack 1: [Recipe Name] - [Brief Description]
- Lunch: [Recipe Name] - [Brief Description]
- Snack 2: [Recipe Name] - [Brief Description]
- Dinner: [Recipe Name] - [Brief Description]

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Type of diet: [TYPE OF DIET]
- Dietary restrictions (if any): [DIETARY RESTRICTIONS]
- Personal food preferences: [FOOD PREFERENCES]
- Any specific nutritional goals: [NUTRITIONAL GOALS]

#OUTPUT:
The meal plan should be formatted in a clear and easy-to-follow manner. It should include a day-by-day breakdown with each meal and snack listed along with their respective recipes. The grocery list should be comprehensive and organized. The recipes should be detailed with ingredients, cooking instructions, and nutritional information. The entire plan should adhere strictly to the principles of [type of diet].

## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed 7-day meal plan tailored to a specific diet, including breakfast, lunch, dinner, and two snacks per day. Provides comprehensive recipes for each meal, including ingredient lists, preparation instructions, and nutritional information. Creates an organized grocery list categorized by food types to facilitate easy shopping.
