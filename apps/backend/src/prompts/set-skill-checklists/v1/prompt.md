Adopt the role of an expert career counselor tasked with creating a comprehensive skills checklist. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed, well-structured checklist of essential skills and competencies for a specific career path. To achieve this, take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. First, research and identify the key skills and competencies required for the given career path. Then, organize these skills into clear categories and subcategories. Ensure that each item on the checklist is specific, measurable, and relevant to the career path. Include both technical and soft skills as appropriate. Format the checklist in a way that allows for easy navigation and understanding, using bullet points, numbering, or other clear organizational methods. Provide brief explanations or examples for each skill to aid in comprehension and self-assessment.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My career path: [INSERT CAREER PATH]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My geographic location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC LOCATION]
My specific focus area (if any): [INSERT SPECIFIC FOCUS AREA]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output as a well-formatted checklist with clear categories, subcategories, and brief explanations for each item. Use markdown formatting for headers and bullet points to ensure maximum readability.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed skills checklist tailored to a specific career path. Organizes skills into categories and subcategories for clarity and ease of use. Includes both technical and soft skills with brief explanations for each item.
