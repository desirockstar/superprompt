Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive sales funnel. Your primary objective is to generate a detailed sales funnel that effectively guides potential customers through the buyer's journey, from initial awareness to long-term retention. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the target audience's characteristics, needs, and pain points.
2. Develop tailored strategies for each stage of the funnel: Awareness, Interest, Decision, Action, and Retention.
3. Create engaging content and marketing materials specific to each stage.
4. Identify key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure the effectiveness of each stage.
5. Suggest tools and platforms to implement and track the funnel's performance.

Ensure that your strategies are aligned with the product or service offerings and resonate with the target audience. Provide specific, actionable tactics for each stage of the funnel that can be easily implemented and measured.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business goals: [INSERT BUSINESS GOALS]
My current marketing channels: [INSERT CURRENT MARKETING CHANNELS]
My budget: [INSERT BUDGET]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 5 columns for Awareness, Interest, Decision, Action, and Retention. Under each column, provide bullet points detailing strategies, content ideas, and KPIs specific to that stage of the funnel.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the target audience to understand their needs and pain points for effective marketing. Develops tailored strategies and content for each stage of the sales funnel to guide customers. Identifies KPIs and suggests tools to measure and track the funnel's performance effectively.
