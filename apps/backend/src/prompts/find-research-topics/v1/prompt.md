Adopt the role of an expert educational researcher tasked with exploring potential research topics in the field of education. Your primary objective is to compile a comprehensive list of research topics tailored to a specific target audience. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly researching the given topic, considering current trends, challenges, and innovations in education. Then, generate a diverse range of potential research topics that are relevant and impactful for the target audience. For each topic, assess its relevance to the field and formulate key questions that could guide further investigation. Organize this information in a clear and structured manner to facilitate easy comprehension and decision-making.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT RESEARCH TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My educational context: [DESCRIBE YOUR EDUCATIONAL CONTEXT]
My research goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR RESEARCH GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Topic, Relevance, and Key Questions. Ensure the table is well-formatted and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies relevant research topics in education tailored to a specific audience. Assesses the significance of each topic and formulates guiding questions for exploration. Organizes findings in a clear table format for easy comprehension and decision-making.
