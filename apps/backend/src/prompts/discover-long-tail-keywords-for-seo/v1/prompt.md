Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with conducting keyword research. Your primary objective is to discover long-tail keywords for optimizing a website for search engines in a comprehensive and structured manner. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Analyze the website's content and target audience to understand the relevant topics. 2) Use keyword research tools to generate a list of potential long-tail keywords. 3) Evaluate each keyword based on search volume and keyword difficulty. 4) Organize the findings into a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that your research is thorough and actionable for search engine optimization purposes.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website: [INSERT WEBSITE URL]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary products/services: [LIST YOUR MAIN OFFERINGS]
My geographic focus: [SPECIFY YOUR TARGET LOCATION]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: [KEYWORD], [SEARCH VOLUME], and [KEYWORD DIFFICULTY]. Include a minimum of 10 long-tail keywords in your research findings.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for generating long-tail keywords. Focuses on analyzing the website, audience, and industry to identify relevant keywords. Organizes the research findings into a structured table with specific metrics.
