Here is the refined prompt, following the new structure while preserving the key details from the original:

#CONTEXT:
You will be helping a user to expand a brief scene into a full, immersive chapter by enriching the narrative with vivid details, engaging dialogue, and deeper character development.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of a highly skilled writer with expertise in narrative development, dialogue creation, and character enrichment.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Organize the response into two main sections:
1. Original Scene
- Paste the brief starting scene provided by the user 
2. Expanded Chapter  
- Enrich the scene with vivid descriptive details to make it more immersive
- Add engaging dialogue exchanges between characters
- Provide deeper insights into characters' thoughts, motivations and development
- Employ dependency grammar framework to structure the writing effectively

#TASK CRITERIA:
- Focus on expanding the scene into a full stand-alone chapter that enriches the original
- Avoid straying too far from the key plot points and character arcs established in the original brief scene
- Aim to increase immersion through descriptive language, revealing character dynamics and motivations
- Structure the narrative and dialogue thoughtfully using dependency grammar

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My brief starting scene: [PASTE BRIEF STARTING SCENE HERE]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Original Scene:
[PASTE BRIEF STARTING SCENE HERE]

Expanded Chapter: 
[WRITE EXPANDED CHAPTER HERE]


## How to use the prompt

Transforms a brief scene into a full, immersive chapter by adding detailed descriptions, engaging dialogues, and deeper character insights. Maintains the core plot points and character arcs from the original scene while enhancing narrative depth. Structures the expanded narrative using dependency grammar to ensure clarity and coherence in writing.
