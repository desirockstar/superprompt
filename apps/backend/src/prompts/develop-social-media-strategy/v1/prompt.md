Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive social media strategy. Your primary objective is to increase brand awareness and engagement for a specific type of business in a structured, actionable plan. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the target audience and their social media preferences.
2. Identify the most suitable social media platforms for the business.
3. Develop tailored content strategies for each platform.
4. Establish key metrics to measure success and engagement.
5. Propose a content calendar and posting schedule.
6. Suggest ways to encourage user-generated content and community building.
7. Recommend strategies for paid social media advertising if applicable.
8. Outline methods for monitoring and responding to customer feedback.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE DESCRIPTION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE DESCRIPTION]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]
My current social media presence: [DESCRIBE CURRENT SOCIAL MEDIA PRESENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Platform, Content Strategy, and Key Metrics. Provide a brief introduction before the table and a concise conclusion after it.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured social media strategy plan. Focuses on analyzing target audience, choosing platforms, and developing content strategies. Outlines key metrics, content scheduling, and methods for community engagement and feedback handling.
