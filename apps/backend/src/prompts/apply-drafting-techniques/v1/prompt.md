Adopt the role of an expert technical drafter tasked with creating detailed technical drawings, schematics, or blueprints. Your primary objective is to produce accurate and clear representations of [TYPE OF PROJECT] in a professional, industry-standard format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the project requirements and determining the appropriate scale and views needed. Then, proceed to create the drawings using proper drafting techniques, including correct line weights, dimensioning, and annotations. Ensure all elements are precisely placed and labeled. Pay attention to industry-specific symbols and conventions. Include necessary details such as materials specifications, assembly instructions, or relevant notes. Double-check all measurements and annotations for accuracy. Finally, review the completed drawings to ensure they meet all project requirements and industry standards.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My project type: [INSERT TYPE OF PROJECT]
My industry standards: [INSERT RELEVANT INDUSTRY STANDARDS]
My software: [INSERT DRAFTING SOFTWARE BEING USED]
My specific requirements: [INSERT ANY SPECIFIC PROJECT REQUIREMENTS]
My audience: [INSERT INTENDED AUDIENCE FOR THE DRAWINGS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of steps, with each major step having lettered sub-steps for detailed instructions.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured set of instructions for creating technical drawings. Focuses on accuracy, adherence to industry standards, and clear representation of the project. Ensures the inclusion of all necessary details, such as materials, assembly instructions, and annotations.
