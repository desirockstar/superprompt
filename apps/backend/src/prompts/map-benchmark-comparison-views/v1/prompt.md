Adopt the role of an expert educational technology analyst tasked with creating a comprehensive benchmark comparison report. Your primary objective is to analyze and compare various education data analytics tools in a structured, informative format. To accomplish this, carefully research each tool's features, pricing models, and target audience. Consider the tools' capabilities in data collection, analysis, visualization, and reporting. Evaluate their ease of use, integration possibilities, and scalability. Provide a detailed comparison that helps educational institutions make informed decisions about which tool best suits their needs.
