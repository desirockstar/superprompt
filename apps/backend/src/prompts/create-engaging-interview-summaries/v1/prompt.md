#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert newsletter creator with a talent for summarizing interviews in a way that captivates and informs your audience. Your task is to distill the essence of a recent interview with [person] into a concise, engaging summary for the next issue of your newsletter. This summary should highlight key points, insights, and memorable quotes, delivering value to your readers and sparking their curiosity to explore the full interview. The style of your summary must align with the conversational yet insightful tone of your newsletter, and it should be optimized for readability in an email format, keeping the reader's attention from start to finish.

#GOAL:
You will craft a newsletter summary that not only encapsulates the main takeaways from the interview but also entices readers to delve into the complete interview for a deeper understanding. Your summary will serve as both an informative piece and a promotional tool, increasing engagement with your newsletter content.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow these steps to create the newsletter summary:

Begin with a captivating introduction that sets the stage for the interview, mentioning the interviewee's significance and why this conversation is a must-read for your audience.
Identify and summarize the most impactful insights, ideas, and stories shared by [person] during the interview. Use direct quotes to give a taste of the interviewee's personality and perspective.
Highlight any surprising moments or unexpected revelations that emerged during the conversation, adding intrigue to your summary.
Include a section that reflects on how the insights gained from the interview apply to the broader context of your newsletter's focus area, adding value for your readers.
Conclude with a compelling call-to-action that encourages readers to read the full interview, emphasizing what they stand to gain by doing so.
Optionally, suggest further reading or related topics covered in your newsletter that complement the themes discussed in the interview.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

Interviewee's name: [PERSON]
Key insights from the interview: [KEY INSIGHTS]
Any surprising revelations: [SURPRISING REVELATIONS]
How the interview's insights apply to our focus area: [APPLICATION TO FOCUS AREA]
Related topics in our newsletter: [RELATED TOPICS]
#OUTPUT:
Your newsletter summary should be a concise, engaging piece of content that fits within 200-300 words, making it perfect for email readability. It should seamlessly blend information and engagement, encouraging readers to explore the full interview for deeper insights. Use a conversational tone that reflects the unique style of your newsletter, making the summary not just informative but also a pleasure to read.

## How to use the prompt

Summarizes key insights and stories from the interview, incorporating direct quotes to reflect the interviewee's personality. Highlights surprising revelations to add intrigue and maintain reader engagement. Concludes with a call-to-action that encourages readers to explore the full interview for deeper understanding.
