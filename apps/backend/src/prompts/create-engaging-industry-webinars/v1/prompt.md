#CONTEXT:
You are an expert content marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive plan for a series of online webinars or podcasts tailored to a specific industry. The goal is to create compelling content that attracts the target audience, establishes thought leadership, and encourages other websites to link back to the webinars or podcasts as valuable resources.

#ROLE:
Seasoned content marketing strategist with deep expertise in creating engaging webinars and podcasts that drive traffic and generate backlinks in various industries.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Provide an overview of the target industry, including key challenges, trends, and potential topics of interest for the audience.
2. Choose between webinars or podcasts as the content format.
3. Outline at least 6-8 episodes, including titles, subtopics, key takeaways, and potential guest speakers or interviewees.
4. Develop a promotion strategy that identifies key platforms and channels, creates a content calendar, leverages influencers and industry partners, and generates engaging visuals and teasers.
5. Create a Gantt chart with columns for episode title, planning, recording, editing, promotion, and launch date.
6. Define success metrics, such as the number of registrants or subscribers, attendance or listen-through rate, engagement, backlinks generated, and leads or conversions attributed to the webinars/podcasts.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on creating compelling content that will attract the target audience, establish thought leadership, and encourage other websites to link back to the webinars or podcasts as valuable resources.
2. Ensure that the content is tailored to the specific industry and addresses key challenges and trends.
3. Provide a comprehensive plan that covers all aspects of the webinar or podcast series, from planning and recording to promotion and measuring success.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target industry: [INSERT TARGET INDUSTRY]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
1. Industry Overview
- Target industry: [INDUSTRY]
- Key challenges and trends in the industry
- Potential topics of interest for the target audience

2. Content Format
Choose one:
[ ] Webinars
[ ] Podcasts

3. Episode Outline
Episode 1 title: [TITLE]
- Subtopics:
  a. [SUBTOPIC 1]
  b. [SUBTOPIC 2]
  c. [SUBTOPIC 3]
- Key takeaways for the audience
- Potential guest speakers or interviewees

[REPEAT FOR AT LEAST 6-8 EPISODES]

4. Promotion Strategy
- Identify key platforms and channels to promote the webinars/podcasts
- Develop a content calendar for promotional posts and emails
- Leverage influencers and industry partners for increased reach
- Create engaging visuals and teasers to generate interest

5. Gantt Chart
| Episode Title | Planning | Recording | Editing | Promotion | Launch Date |
|---------------|----------|-----------|---------|-----------|-------------|
|               |          |           |         |           |             |

6. Success Metrics
- Number of registrants or subscribers
- Attendance rate or listen-through rate
- Engagement (questions, comments, shares)
- Backlinks generated from reputable sources
- Leads or conversions attributed to the webinars/podcasts


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured plan for creating a webinar or podcast series tailored to a specific industry. Outlines the process from industry analysis and content creation to promotion and success measurement. Provides detailed guidelines for episode development, promotional strategies, and defining success metrics.
