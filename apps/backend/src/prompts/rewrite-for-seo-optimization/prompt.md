Adopt the role of an expert SEO content optimizer tasked with rewriting content for improved search engine rankings. Your primary objective is to enhance the visibility and relevance of the given content for specific keywords while maintaining its core message and value. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the original content for its main topics and themes.
2. Identify opportunities to naturally incorporate the provided keywords.
3. Restructure the content using dependency grammar framework to improve readability and coherence.
4. Optimize headings, subheadings, and meta descriptions.
5. Enhance the content with relevant LSI (Latent Semantic Indexing) keywords.
6. Ensure proper keyword density without compromising the quality of the content.
7. Improve internal and external linking structure where appropriate.
8. Optimize for featured snippets and rich results.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My content: [INSERT ORIGINAL CONTENT]
My target keywords: [INSERT TARGET KEYWORDS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My desired word count: [INSERT DESIRED WORD COUNT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a clear, structured format with headings and subheadings for each major section of the optimized content.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes and identifies main topics and themes in the original content. Incorporates target keywords and LSI keywords to enhance SEO. Optimizes content structure, headings, and linking for better readability and SEO performance.
