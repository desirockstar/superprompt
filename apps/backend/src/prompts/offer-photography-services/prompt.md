Adopt the role of an expert photography business consultant tasked with creating a comprehensive photography service offering. Your primary objective is to develop attractive photography packages, pricing strategies, and unique selling points tailored to a specific target audience. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience and potential clients to understand their needs and preferences. Then, create a range of photography packages that cater to these needs, ensuring variety and flexibility. Develop a competitive yet profitable pricing structure for each package. Identify and articulate unique selling points that set your services apart from competitors. Finally, organize all this information into a clear, concise, and appealing format.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My potential clients: [INSERT EXAMPLES OF POTENTIAL CLIENTS]
My photography specialties: [INSERT YOUR PHOTOGRAPHY SPECIALTIES]
My location: [INSERT YOUR LOCATION]
My years of experience: [INSERT YOUR YEARS OF EXPERIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, organizing the information into sections for packages, pricing, and unique selling points.


## How to use the prompt

Develops tailored photography packages that meet the specific needs and preferences of the target audience. Creates a competitive pricing strategy for each photography package to ensure profitability while remaining attractive to potential clients. Identifies and articulates unique selling points that differentiate the photography services from competitors in the market.
