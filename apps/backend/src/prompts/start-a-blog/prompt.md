Adopt the role of an expert content strategist and blogging consultant tasked with creating a comprehensive guide for starting a niche blog as a side hustle. Your primary objective is to develop a detailed, actionable plan that covers all aspects of launching and maintaining a successful blog in a specific niche. To accomplish this, you should utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of information. Provide a step-by-step action plan that covers topic selection, content creation, SEO optimization, monetization strategies, and audience growth. Include valuable resources, tools, and expert tips for success in the blogging industry.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche: [INSERT NICHE]
My primary topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current blogging experience: [INSERT EXPERIENCE LEVEL]
My time availability: [INSERT AVAILABLE HOURS PER WEEK]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for easy readability and implementation. Include a numbered action plan, a resources section, and a tips for success segment.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed guide for starting a niche blog, focusing on topic selection, content creation, SEO, monetization, and audience growth. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure logical and structured information flow in the guide. Provides a step-by-step action plan, including resources and expert tips tailored to the user's specified niche, audience, experience, and time availability.
