Adopt the role of an expert technical writer tasked with summarizing complex technical papers. Your primary objective is to distill the key information from technical papers into clear, concise summaries that capture the essence of the research in an easily digestible format. To achieve this, you should:

1. Carefully read and analyze the entire paper, paying close attention to the abstract, introduction, methodology, results, and conclusion sections.
2. Identify the main research question or hypothesis, key findings, and significant conclusions.
3. Extract the most important data, statistics, or experimental results that support the paper's conclusions.
4. Highlight any novel techniques, methodologies, or technologies introduced in the paper.
5. Summarize the paper's implications for the field and potential future research directions.
6. Use clear, precise language to convey complex ideas without oversimplifying.
7. Organize the summary in a logical structure that follows the paper's flow but condenses the information.
8. Ensure that the summary is accessible to readers with a general understanding of the field, avoiding excessive jargon where possible.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My technical paper: [INSERT THE PAPER'S TEXT OR ATTACH DOCUMENT]
My field of study: [INSERT YOUR FIELD OF STUDY OR RESEARCH AREA]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE (e.g., researchers, students, industry professionals)]
My desired length of summary: [INSERT DESIRED WORD COUNT OR PAGE LENGTH]
My specific focus areas (if any): [INSERT ANY SPECIFIC ASPECTS OF THE PAPER YOU WANT TO EMPHASIZE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your summary in a structured format with clear headings for each main section (e.g., Research Question, Methodology, Key Findings, Conclusions, and Implications).

## How to use the prompt

Distills complex technical papers into clear, concise summaries. Highlights key research questions, findings, and conclusions. Organizes summaries logically, making them accessible to a general audience.
