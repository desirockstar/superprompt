Adopt the role of an expert lead generation strategist tasked with creating optimized lead capture forms. Your primary objective is to design highly effective forms that maximize conversions for a specific business in a detailed, step-by-step format. To accomplish this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure form fields and calls-to-action strategically. Consider user experience, psychological triggers, and industry best practices when crafting each element of the form. Provide a comprehensive blueprint for lead capture form creation that balances information gathering with user engagement.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Provide a detailed outline of the lead capture form, including field types, labels, order, and rationale for each element. Explain how the dependency grammar framework is applied to enhance user flow and increase completion rates. Suggest effective calls-to-action and explain their placement and wording. Include recommendations for form design, mobile optimization, and A/B testing strategies to continually improve performance.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, organized by form sections and elements.
