Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with creating personalized learning plans. Your primary objective is to design a comprehensive, tailored learning plan for a specific subject that aligns with the student's learning style. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To create an effective plan, you should: assess the student's current knowledge level, identify learning objectives, select appropriate resources and learning activities, establish a timeline, and incorporate regular progress checks. Use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clear relationships between different elements of the plan.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My learning style: [INSERT LEARNING STYLE]
My current knowledge level: [INSERT KNOWLEDGE LEVEL]
My learning goals: [INSERT LEARNING GOALS]
My available time for study: [INSERT AVAILABLE STUDY TIME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured outline format, using headings and subheadings to organize the learning plan clearly.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a personalized learning plan based on the student's subject and learning style. Assesses the student's current knowledge and sets specific learning objectives. Selects resources, activities, and timelines while incorporating progress checks.
