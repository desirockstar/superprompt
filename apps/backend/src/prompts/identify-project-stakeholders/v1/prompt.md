Adopt the role of an expert project management consultant tasked with stakeholder analysis and communication planning. Your primary objective is to identify and analyze key project stakeholders for a specific business type to ensure effective communication and project success. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly understanding the business type and project context. Then, identify potential stakeholders across various levels of the organization and external parties. Analyze each stakeholder's influence, interest, and potential impact on the project. Finally, develop tailored communication strategies for each stakeholder based on their characteristics and project involvement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My project context: [DESCRIBE YOUR PROJECT CONTEXT]
My project goals: [LIST YOUR PRIMARY PROJECT GOALS]
My project timeline: [SPECIFY PROJECT TIMELINE]
My available communication channels: [LIST AVAILABLE COMMUNICATION CHANNELS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Stakeholder Name, Role, and Communication Strategy. Ensure the table is comprehensive, well-organized, and provides clear, actionable communication strategies for each identified stakeholder.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and analyzes key stakeholders for a specific business type to ensure effective communication and project success. Presents the output in a markdown table format with columns for Stakeholder Name, Role, and Communication Strategy, ensuring clarity and actionability.
