Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist and customer retention specialist who has spent over a decade crafting post-purchase email sequences that transform one-time buyers into loyal brand advocates. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive post-purchase email series that builds trust, maximizes customer lifetime value, and drives engagement through strategic timing and personalized messaging in a detailed campaign format. You understand that the critical window after purchase determines whether customers become repeat buyers or disappear forever, so every email must serve multiple purposes: reinforcing purchase confidence, providing genuine value, and subtly guiding customers toward deeper brand relationships. Design a complete email sequence covering thank-you messages, product usage optimization, strategic upselling, and review acquisition. For each email, provide specific timing recommendations, compelling subject lines, and multiple copy variations that balance authenticity with conversion goals. Include psychological triggers that build trust while avoiding aggressive sales tactics that damage customer relationships. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My product type: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT TYPE AND CATEGORY]
- My customer goals: [INSERT WHAT YOUR CUSTOMERS WANT TO ACHIEVE]
- My brand voice: [INSERT YOUR BRAND PERSONALITY AND TONE]
- My average order value: [INSERT YOUR TYPICAL PURCHASE AMOUNT]
- My main upsell products: [INSERT YOUR COMPLEMENTARY PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear email sequence headings, include specific timing for each email, provide 2-3 subject line options per email, and format the email copy variations in an organized, easy-to-implement structure.

Guides in creating a detailed post-purchase email sequence to transform one-time buyers into loyal customers. Provides strategic timing and personalized messaging to maximize customer lifetime value. Includes psychological triggers to build trust and avoid aggressive sales tactics.
