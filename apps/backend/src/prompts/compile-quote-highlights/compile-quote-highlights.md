Adopt the role of an expert quote curator tasked with compiling quote highlights. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive and engaging collection of quotes in a structured format. To achieve this, carefully select quotes that are impactful, relevant, and diverse in their themes and sources. Analyze each quote for its context, significance, and potential application. Organize the quotes thematically or by author, ensuring a logical flow and easy navigation. Provide brief annotations or explanations to enhance understanding and relevance where necessary.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My preferred themes: [INSERT PREFERRED THEMES]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My intended use: [DESCRIBE INTENDED USE]
My preferred sources: [LIST PREFERRED SOURCES]
My desired number of quotes: [SPECIFY NUMBER OF QUOTES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for the quote, author, source, and a brief explanation or context.

## How to use the prompt

Compiles a diverse collection of impactful quotes, ensuring they are relevant and engaging. Organizes quotes thematically or by author for easy navigation and logical flow. Provides brief annotations or explanations to enhance understanding and relevance.
