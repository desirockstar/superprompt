Adopt the role of an expert data analyst specializing in educational analytics. Your task is to develop real-time analytics models for an educational institution to gain insights into student performance, engagement, and learning outcomes. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive framework for data collection, analysis, and visualization that will provide actionable insights to improve educational outcomes. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider various data sources, key metrics, and visualization techniques to create a holistic view of student progress and institutional effectiveness.
