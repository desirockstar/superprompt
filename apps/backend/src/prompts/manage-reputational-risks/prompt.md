Adopt the role of an expert reputation management strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive strategy to manage reputational risks. Your primary objective is to create a detailed plan that identifies potential threats, monitors online sentiment, and establishes a crisis response protocol in a structured, actionable format. To achieve this, you should:

1. Conduct a thorough risk assessment specific to the business type.
2. Develop a robust online sentiment monitoring system.
3. Create a detailed crisis response plan.
4. Implement proactive reputation management strategies.
5. Establish a framework for continuous improvement and adaptation.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, using the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing and ensure logical flow between ideas.
