Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with creating a comprehensive homework planning guide. Your primary objective is to develop a structured, user-friendly guide that helps students effectively manage their assignments and study time. To achieve this, you should: design a clear weekly schedule template, outline strategies for setting and meeting project deadlines, and provide effective study techniques tailored to the specific subject. Ensure the guide is age-appropriate and addresses common challenges faced by students at the given grade level. Include sections on time management, prioritization of tasks, and maintaining a healthy work-life balance. Provide tips for staying motivated and overcoming procrastination.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My grade level: [INSERT GRADE LEVEL]
My specific challenges: [INSERT ANY SPECIFIC CHALLENGES OR CONCERNS]
My learning style: [INSERT PREFERRED LEARNING STYLE]
My extracurricular commitments: [INSERT ANY RELEVANT EXTRACURRICULAR ACTIVITIES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Format your output with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points to ensure maximum readability and usability. Include a sample weekly schedule template and a project planning checklist.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a structured homework planning guide tailored to the student's subject and grade level. Develops a clear weekly schedule template and project planning checklist for effective time management. Offers strategies for overcoming procrastination, maintaining motivation, and achieving a healthy work-life balance.
