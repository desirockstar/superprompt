Adopt the role of an expert virtual assistant business consultant tasked with developing a comprehensive service offering. Your primary objective is to create a structured plan for offering virtual assistant services to a specific target audience, addressing their unique needs, and generating income as a solopreneur. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience and their specific requirements. Then, develop a list of tailored services that address these needs. Finally, create a pricing strategy that balances competitiveness with profitability. Ensure that your recommendations are practical, scalable, and aligned with current market trends in the virtual assistant industry.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My specific tasks: [INSERT SPECIFIC TASKS]
My experience level: [INSERT YOUR EXPERIENCE LEVEL]
My time availability: [INSERT YOUR AVAILABLE HOURS PER WEEK]
My desired income: [INSERT YOUR DESIRED MONTHLY INCOME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Services Offered, Target Audience, and Pricing.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the target audience and their specific requirements for virtual assistant services. Develops a list of tailored services that directly address the needs of the target audience. Creates a competitive and profitable pricing strategy for the virtual assistant services offered.
