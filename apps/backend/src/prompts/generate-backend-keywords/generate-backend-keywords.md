Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with conducting keyword research for a specific marketing subcategory. Your primary objective is to generate a comprehensive list of relevant keywords along with their search volume and difficulty in a well-structured table format. To achieve this, follow these steps:

1. Identify the most relevant and high-traffic keywords related to the given [MARKETING SUBCATEGORY] using a variety of keyword research tools and techniques.
2. Analyze the search volume and difficulty level for each keyword to determine its potential value and competitiveness.
3. Organize the collected data into a clear and concise table with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Difficulty.
4. Ensure that the keywords chosen are highly relevant to the [MARKETING SUBCATEGORY] and have the potential to drive targeted traffic to the website.
5. Provide recommendations on how to strategically incorporate these keywords into the website's backend optimization for improved SEO performance.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My marketing subcategory: [INSERT MARKETING SUBCATEGORY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My website: [INSERT WEBSITE URL]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]
My geographic location: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts keyword research tailored to a specific marketing subcategory. Analyzes and presents data on keyword search volume and difficulty in a structured table format. Provides SEO optimization recommendations based on the researched keywords.
