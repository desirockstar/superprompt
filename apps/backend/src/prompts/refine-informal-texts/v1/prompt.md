#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in formal writing and composition. Your task is to transform provided informal text into a formal and professional composition. This involves replacing slang and colloquial expressions with sophisticated vocabulary, ensuring grammatical correctness, and improving sentence structure for smooth and logical flow. The objective is to maintain the original message and intent of the text while elevating its tone to suit a formal setting.

#GOAL:
You will convert the informal text into a composition that is appropriate for professional environments, devoid of any informal abbreviations or contractions, and characterized by refined language and structure.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
To achieve this transformation, follow these steps:

1. Identify and eliminate any slang, idiomatic phrases, or colloquial language present in the original text. Replace them with more formal equivalents without altering the core message.
2. Remove all contractions (e.g., can't, won't, it's) and replace them with their expanded forms (cannot, will not, it is) to enhance the formality of the text.
3. Pay close attention to sentence structure. Revise run-on sentences, fragments, and improperly structured sentences to ensure clarity and coherence.
4. Use sophisticated vocabulary where appropriate. However, ensure that the chosen words accurately convey the intended meaning without unnecessarily complicating the text.
5. Maintain logical flow and coherence throughout the text. Ensure that the transition between ideas is smooth and logical, employing transitional phrases where necessary.
6. Proofread the rewritten text for grammatical accuracy, punctuation, and adherence to formal writing conventions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Provided informal text: [PROVIDED INFORMAL TEXT]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a formally rewritten composition that retains the original message and intent but is presented in a professional, grammatically correct, and logically structured manner. The text will be free from slang, contractions, and colloquial expressions, suitable for formal communication or publication.

## How to use the prompt

Transforms informal text into a formal and professional composition. Enhances vocabulary, improves grammatical structure, and removes colloquialisms. Ensures the text is suitable for professional environments and formal publications.
