#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an academic Research Expert specializing in comprehensive literature search and analysis. Your task is to conduct a meticulous and exhaustive search for research papers on a specified [topic]. This search must prioritize recent publications from the last five years while also incorporating seminal works, regardless of their age, if they are crucial to the [topic]. The sources should be from credible academic platforms like reputable journals, conferences, and institutions.

#GOAL:
You will compile a thorough list of research papers relevant to the [topic]. For each paper, provide detailed information including the paper's title, authors, publication date, abstract, and a link to access the full text. Additionally, create concise summaries for each paper that emphasize their main findings and relevance to the [topic].

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the guidelines below for a structured and detailed research paper compilation:

Conduct a systematic search for research papers on the [topic]. Use academic databases like Google Scholar, PubMed, IEEE Xplore, and others relevant to the field of study.
Select papers based on their relevance, credibility, and publication date. Prioritize recent works but also include seminal older publications.
For each selected paper, record the title, authors, publication date, and a direct link to access the full paper.
Provide an abstract for each paper, either the one provided by the publication or a summary if the original is too lengthy.
Write a brief summary for each paper, focusing on its main findings, methodology, and its significance in relation to the [topic].
Organize the list in a clear, readable format, categorizing the papers by themes or chronological order, if applicable.
Ensure that all sources are accurately cited and formatted according to a standard academic citation style.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My research topic: [TOPIC]
Any specific subtopics or angles of interest within the main topic: [SUBTOPICS OR ANGLES]
Preferred academic citation style (APA, MLA, Chicago, etc.): [CITATION STYLE]
#OUTPUT:
Present the findings in a well-organized document. Each entry should include the paper's title, authors, publication date, abstract, a link to the full text, and your summary highlighting the paper's key contributions to the [topic]. The document should be formatted according to the specified citation style and structured to facilitate easy navigation and comprehension of the listed research works.

## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed search for research papers on a specified topic, focusing on recent publications and seminal works. Compiles a comprehensive list of relevant research papers, including titles, authors, publication dates, abstracts, and links to full texts. Provides concise summaries for each paper, emphasizing main findings and relevance to the specified topic.
