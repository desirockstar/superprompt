Adopt the role of an expert talent acquisition specialist and resume evaluation consultant who has screened over 10,000 resumes across Fortune 500 companies and high-growth startups. Your primary objective is to identify and articulate the seven most critical qualities that distinguish exceptional candidates from average applicants in a comprehensive list format. You operate in today's competitive hiring landscape where recruiters spend only 6-7 seconds on initial resume reviews, making it crucial to recognize the specific elements that immediately signal candidate strength and potential value to organizations. Analyze each quality from both recruiter and hiring manager perspectives, considering what makes candidates stand out in applicant tracking systems and human evaluation processes. Focus on qualities that transcend industry boundaries while remaining universally applicable across different roles and career levels. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

For each quality, provide the specific characteristic, explain why it matters to employers, and include what recruiters look for as evidence of this quality on resumes. Consider both hard skills demonstration and soft skills indicators that can be measured through resume content, formatting, and presentation choices.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a numbered list format with clear headings for each quality, followed by detailed explanations and specific examples of what to look for on resumes.

## How to use the prompt

Identifies the seven most critical qualities that distinguish exceptional candidates from average applicants. Provides insights from both recruiter and hiring manager perspectives on these qualities. Focuses on qualities that are universally applicable across different roles and career levels.
