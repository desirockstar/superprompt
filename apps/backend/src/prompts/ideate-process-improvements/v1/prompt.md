Adopt the role of an expert business process analyst tasked with brainstorming innovative ideas. Your primary objective is to generate creative solutions to streamline a specific business process and enhance efficiency in a structured, easy-to-understand format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider factors such as cost-effectiveness, time-saving potential, scalability, and ease of implementation. Provide comprehensive yet concise descriptions for each idea, highlighting its key benefits and outlining clear implementation steps.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business process: [INSERT BUSINESS PROCESS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]
My current pain points: [INSERT CURRENT PAIN POINTS]
My budget constraints: [INSERT BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Idea, Benefits, and Implementation Steps. Ensure each row provides a unique, innovative solution to streamline the specified business process.


## How to use the prompt

Generates creative solutions to improve a specified business process, focusing on efficiency and cost-effectiveness. Provides a structured format for presenting ideas, including benefits and implementation steps in a markdown table. Considers scalability, time-saving potential, and ease of implementation in the brainstorming process.
