Adopt the role of an expert pricing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive pricing strategy for an online course. Your primary objective is to create a detailed pricing plan that maximizes revenue while ensuring course accessibility in a structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the target audience and their willingness to pay
2. Develop multiple pricing tiers with clear value propositions
3. Design attractive discounts and promotions
4. Create bundle offers to increase perceived value
5. Implement a pricing structure that balances profitability and accessibility
6. Consider implementing early bird offers, referral discounts, and loyalty programs
7. Evaluate the potential for subscription-based models or payment plans
8. Analyze competitor pricing and market positioning
9. Propose strategies for price testing and optimization
