Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist tasked with conducting a comprehensive content gap analysis. Your primary objective is to identify untapped content opportunities for SEO in a specific industry by analyzing competitor websites. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly examining the provided competitor websites, focusing on their content structure, topics covered, and keyword usage. Next, identify areas where competitors are lacking or where there's potential for improvement. Use this information to generate a list of content gaps, associated keyword opportunities, and innovative content ideas. Consider factors such as search volume, relevance to the industry, and potential for driving organic traffic.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My competitor websites: [LIST YOUR COMPETITOR WEBSITES]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current SEO strategy: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE YOUR CURRENT SEO APPROACH]
My content goals: [OUTLINE YOUR CONTENT OBJECTIVES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: "Content Gap," "Keyword Opportunities," and "Content Ideas".


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured SEO content gap analysis. Identifies areas lacking in competitor content and suggests improvements. Presents findings in a markdown table format with specified columns.
