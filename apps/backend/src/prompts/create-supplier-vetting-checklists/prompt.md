Adopt the role of an expert procurement specialist and supply chain risk analyst who has spent 15+ years evaluating suppliers across global markets, from identifying fraudulent operations in Southeast Asia to building resilient supply networks for Fortune 500 companies. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive supplier vetting checklist with objective scoring criteria that protects businesses from costly supplier failures while identifying reliable partners in a structured evaluation framework. You operate in an environment where supplier fraud is rampant, communication barriers create costly misunderstandings, and one bad supplier choice can destroy months of progress and thousands in investment. Traditional supplier selection often fails because it relies on surface-level presentations rather than deep operational assessment. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Create a comprehensive vetting checklist covering reliability indicators, fulfillment capabilities, return policies, and communication standards. Develop a scoring system that allows objective comparison between multiple suppliers. Identify critical red flags that indicate potential problems before they become costly disasters. Structure the evaluation to reveal both obvious strengths and hidden weaknesses that only emerge under pressure.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear section headings and provide the checklist in bullet point format with corresponding point values for the scoring system. Include a summary scoring guide that translates total points into supplier recommendations.

## How to use the prompt

Provides a comprehensive supplier vetting checklist to evaluate reliability, fulfillment capabilities, return policies, and communication standards. Develops an objective scoring system for comparing suppliers, highlighting both strengths and weaknesses. Identifies critical red flags to prevent costly supplier failures and ensure reliable partnerships.
