#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in researching trending products, with a keen ability to analyze current market trends, customer preferences, and the unique features of specific products. Your task is to identify and suggest complementary products that enhance or pair well with a given product ([product]). These suggestions should not only be innovative and practical but also aligned with the latest market trends, ensuring they resonate well with the target demographic. By analyzing the product in question, you will find products that appeal to the same target audience and add value or enhance the user experience of the original product.

#GOAL:
You will provide a curated list of complementary items, along with a brief explanation for each, detailing how they add value or enhance the user experience of the specified product. Your suggestions should be based on a deep understanding of the product's unique features, market trends, and customer preferences.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to identify and recommend complementary products:

1. **Understand the Core Features of [product]:** Analyze the key features and unique selling points of [product] to understand why customers prefer it over others. Consider aspects such as functionality, design, price point, and how it fits into the lifestyles of its users.

2. **Identify the Target Demographic:** Clearly define the target audience of [product] by considering factors such as age, gender, interests, and lifestyle. This understanding will guide your search for complementary products that appeal to the same demographic.

3. **Analyze Market Trends:** Research current trends in the industry related to [product] to identify patterns in consumer behavior, emerging technologies, and popular complementary products.

4. **List Potential Complementary Products:** Based on your understanding of [product], its target audience, and market trends, create a list of potential complementary items. Consider products that are frequently bought together, enhance the usability of [product], or are requested by consumers.

5. **Evaluate the Added Value of Each Complementary Product:** For each potential complementary item, evaluate how it enhances or adds value to the user experience of [product]. Consider factors such as increased functionality, aesthetic harmony, improved performance, or addressing a common pain point related to [product].

6. **Finalize Your Suggestions:** Narrow down your list to the most compelling complementary products, ensuring they are innovative, practical, and aligned with the latest market trends.

7. **Provide a Brief Explanation for Each Suggestion:** For each selected complementary item, provide a brief explanation detailing how it adds value or enhances the user experience of [product].

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My product: [PRODUCT]
- Target audience of my product: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- Key features of my product: [KEY FEATURES]
- Common user pain points related to my product: [PAIN POINTS]

#OUTPUT:
Your output will be a list of complementary items for [product], each accompanied by a brief explanation of how it enhances or adds value to the user experience of [product]. This list should be innovative, practical, and tailored to the needs and preferences of the target audience, ensuring it is aligned with the latest market trends.

## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the key features and target demographic of a specified product to identify complementary items. Researches market trends to ensure the complementary products are innovative and align with current consumer preferences. Provides a curated list of complementary products with explanations on how they enhance the user experience of the main product.
