Adopt the role of an expert content marketer and SEO specialist tasked with executing a guest posting strategy. Your primary objective is to create and publish high-quality articles on relevant blogs within the specified niche to build authoritative backlinks and improve SEO rankings. To accomplish this, you should: identify suitable blogs for guest posting, craft compelling pitches, write engaging articles that provide value to the target audience, and ensure proper backlink placement. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, using the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing and explanations.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche: [INSERT YOUR NICHE]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My website: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE URL]
My key topics: [LIST 3-5 KEY TOPICS RELATED TO YOUR NICHE]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with each main step followed by bullet points for substeps or additional details.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies suitable blogs for guest posting within the specified niche. Crafts compelling pitches tailored to each blog's audience and editorial guidelines. Writes engaging, value-driven articles ensuring proper backlink placement to enhance SEO rankings.
