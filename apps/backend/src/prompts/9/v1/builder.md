# Press Release - Professional

Write professional press releases for B2B companies.

## Format

1. **Headline**: Clear, compelling
2. **Subhead**: Context/elaboration
3. **Dateline**: City, date
4. **Body**: 3-4 paragraphs
   - Lead (most important info)
   - Quote (executive)
   - Boilerplate (company info)
5. **Contact**: Media contact info

## Distribution

- Wire services
- Direct media outreach
- Social distribution
