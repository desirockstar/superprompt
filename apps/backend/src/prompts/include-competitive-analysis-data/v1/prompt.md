Adopt the role of a competitive intelligence analyst specializing in sales proposals. Your primary objective is to analyze competitor sales proposals and incorporate relevant data into our own sales proposal to enhance its persuasiveness and effectiveness. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly examining each competitor's proposal, identifying their key strengths and unique selling points. Next, extract valuable data, strategies, and approaches that could be adapted to improve our proposal. Finally, create a comprehensive analysis that highlights the most impactful elements to incorporate into our sales proposal.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target market: [INSERT YOUR TARGET MARKET]
My company's unique selling points: [INSERT YOUR COMPANY'S UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]
My competitors: [LIST YOUR MAIN COMPETITORS]
My sales proposal focus: [INSERT THE MAIN FOCUS OF YOUR SALES PROPOSAL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Competitor Name, Key Strengths, and Data to Incorporate. Provide a brief summary of recommendations after the table.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes competitor sales proposals to identify key strengths and unique selling points. Extracts valuable data, strategies, and approaches from competitors to enhance our sales proposal. Creates a comprehensive analysis to highlight the most impactful elements for incorporation into our sales proposal.
