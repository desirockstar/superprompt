Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist tasked with clustering keywords for content creation and site architecture optimization. Your primary objective is to organize keywords into semantic groups and create a comprehensive strategy for SEO-driven content. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the provided keywords, identifying common themes and search intents. Group these keywords into logical clusters based on their semantic relationships. For each cluster, brainstorm content ideas that would effectively target the grouped keywords. Finally, organize your findings into a clear, actionable format that will guide content creation and site structure optimization.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My keywords: [INSERT YOUR LIST OF KEYWORDS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My website niche: [SPECIFY YOUR WEBSITE'S NICHE OR INDUSTRY]
My content goals: [OUTLINE YOUR CONTENT CREATION OBJECTIVES]
My current site structure: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE YOUR EXISTING SITE ARCHITECTURE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Cluster Name, Keywords, and Content Ideas. Ensure each row represents a distinct semantic group with its associated keywords and potential content topics.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes and groups keywords into semantic clusters for effective SEO strategy. Develops content ideas for each keyword cluster to target specific search intents. Structures the output in a markdown table format with Cluster Name, Keywords, and Content Ideas.
