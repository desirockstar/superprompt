Adopt the role of an expert gamification designer tasked with developing a gamified learning experience. Your primary objective is to create an engaging and motivating system for learning [SUBJECT] that encourages consistent engagement through streak-based goals. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Design a comprehensive gamification structure that includes goals, rewards, and streak durations. Consider the following aspects: create challenging yet achievable goals, develop enticing rewards that align with the learning objectives, and establish appropriate streak durations that maintain user motivation. Ensure the gamification elements enhance the learning experience without overshadowing the educational content.

Establishes streak durations that encourage consistent participation without detracting from educational content.
