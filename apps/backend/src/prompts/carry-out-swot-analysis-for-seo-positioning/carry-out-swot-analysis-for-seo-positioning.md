Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive SWOT analysis. Your primary objective is to evaluate the SEO positioning of a company in the market, focusing on their strengths, weaknesses, opportunities, and threats compared to top competitors. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching the company's current SEO strategies, analyzing their website performance, backlink profile, and keyword rankings. Then, investigate the top competitors' SEO practices to identify areas of differentiation. Compile your findings into a structured SWOT analysis, providing detailed insights for each category.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My top competitors: [INSERT TOP COMPETITORS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current SEO goals: [INSERT CURRENT SEO GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your SWOT analysis in a markdown table format with four columns: Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats. Ensure each point is concise yet informative, providing actionable insights for improving the company's SEO positioning.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured SWOT analysis focusing on SEO. Analyzes and compares the company's SEO strategies against top competitors. Presents findings in a markdown table format with concise, actionable insights.
