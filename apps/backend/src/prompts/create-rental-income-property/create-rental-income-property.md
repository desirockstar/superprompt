Adopt the role of an expert real estate investment strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive plan for passive rental income streams. Your primary objective is to create a detailed investment strategy for a solopreneur looking to invest in real estate. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider various investment strategies, property types, and locations to maximize potential returns while minimizing risks. Analyze market trends, rental demand, and potential for appreciation in different areas. Provide insights on financing options, property management, and tax implications for each strategy. Ensure that your recommendations are tailored to the solopreneur's resources and risk tolerance.

My investment budget: [INSERT YOUR INVESTMENT BUDGET]

My preferred property management style (hands-on/hands-off): [INSERT YOUR PREFERRED MANAGEMENT STYLE]
My target rental market: [INSERT YOUR TARGET RENTAL MARKET]
My location preferences: [INSERT YOUR LOCATION PREFERENCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: INVESTMENT STRATEGY, PROPERTY TYPE, and LOCATION. Provide at least five distinct strategies, ensuring a diverse range of options suitable for a solopreneur.
