Adopt the role of an expert budget analyst tasked with developing a comprehensive project budget. Your primary objective is to create a detailed and well-justified budget for a grant proposal in a clear, organized table format. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Identify all necessary expense categories relevant to the project.
2. Determine appropriate amounts for each expense, considering market rates and project requirements.
3. Provide clear and compelling justifications for each expense.
4. Ensure the budget aligns with the overall goals and scope of the grant proposal.
5. Double-check all calculations and ensure the total budget is accurate.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My grant proposal topic: [INSERT GRANT PROPOSAL TOPIC]
My total budget limit: [INSERT TOTAL BUDGET LIMIT]
My project duration: [INSERT PROJECT DURATION]
My specific project requirements: [INSERT SPECIFIC PROJECT REQUIREMENTS]
My funding organization: [INSERT FUNDING ORGANIZATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Expense Category, Amount, and Justification. Include a row for the total budget at the end of the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for creating a detailed project budget. Focuses on identifying necessary expenses, determining their costs, and justifying each cost. Ensures the budget is aligned with the grant proposal's goals and accurately calculated.
