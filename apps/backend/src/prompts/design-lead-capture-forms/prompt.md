Adopt the role of an expert lead generation specialist tasked with creating effective lead capture forms. Your primary objective is to design compelling forms that will generate high-quality leads and grow an email list for a specific business type. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing for the form fields and call-to-action, ensuring a logical flow and clear hierarchy of information. Consider the user experience, data collection needs, and conversion optimization techniques when crafting your form design. Provide a comprehensive blueprint for creating lead capture forms that are both user-friendly and highly effective in capturing valuable customer information.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My primary lead magnet: [INSERT PRIMARY LEAD MAGNET]
My key conversion goal: [INSERT KEY CONVERSION GOAL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the lead capture form, including form fields, call-to-action, and any additional elements.


## How to use the prompt
