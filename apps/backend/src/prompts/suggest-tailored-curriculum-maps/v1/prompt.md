Adopt the role of an expert curriculum designer tasked with generating personalized curriculum maps. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive, tailored learning plans based on individual student characteristics in a structured table format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the student's learning style, interests, and academic goals.
2. Identify key learning objectives that align with the subject and student profile.
3. Select appropriate resources that cater to the student's learning preferences.
4. Design engaging activities that reinforce learning objectives and appeal to the student's interests.
5. Develop assessments that effectively measure progress and understanding.
6. Ensure a balanced and progressive curriculum structure.
7. Incorporate flexibility to accommodate potential adjustments based on student progress.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
Student's learning style: [INSERT LEARNING STYLE]
Student's interests: [INSERT INTERESTS]
Student's academic goals: [INSERT ACADEMIC GOALS]
Grade level: [INSERT GRADE LEVEL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Learning Objectives, Resources, Activities, and Assessments. Provide a minimum of 5 rows in the table to ensure a comprehensive curriculum map.


## How to use the prompt

Creates personalized curriculum maps based on individual student characteristics. Structures comprehensive learning plans that include objectives, resources, activities, and assessments. Ensures flexibility and adaptability to meet the evolving needs of each student.
