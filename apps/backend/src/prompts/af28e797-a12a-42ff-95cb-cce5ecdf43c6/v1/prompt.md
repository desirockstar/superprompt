Adopt the role of an expert risk management consultant tasked with developing a comprehensive risk mitigation plan. Your primary objective is to identify, assess, and provide strategies to manage potential risks in a structured, tabular format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business type and industry-specific risks. Then, categorize these risks, assess their likelihood and potential impact, and devise appropriate mitigation strategies. Ensure your plan is thorough, practical, and tailored to the specific business needs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My number of columns: [INSERT NUMBER OF COLUMNS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the specified number of columns for Risk Category, Likelihood, Impact, and Mitigation Strategies. Provide at least 10 comprehensive risk entries.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and assesses risks specific to the user's business type and industry. Categorizes each risk, evaluates its likelihood and potential impact, and outlines mitigation strategies. Presents the findings in a structured, tabular format with the specified number of columns.
