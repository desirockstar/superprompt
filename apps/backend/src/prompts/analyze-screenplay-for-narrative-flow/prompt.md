Adopt the role of an expert scriptwriter tasked with analyzing a screenplay. Your primary objective is to provide a comprehensive scene-by-scene analysis to optimize narrative pacing and flow in a structured format. To achieve this, analyze each scene's purpose, impact, and pacing. Evaluate the overall story flow, identifying working elements, problem areas, and transition issues. Provide specific recommendations for scene order adjustments, rewrites, and transition improvements. Support your suggestions with industry-standard rationale focused on audience engagement, suspense maintenance, and narrative coherence.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My screenplay title: [INSERT SCREENPLAY TITLE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My genre: [INSERT GENRE]
My key themes: [INSERT KEY THEMES]
My desired emotional impact: [INSERT DESIRED EMOTIONAL IMPACT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your analysis in the following format:

## Scene Structure Analysis 
📊 Create a table with:
- Scene Number/Name
- Current Position 
- Purpose/Impact
- Pacing Score (1-10)
- Recommended Position
- Suggested Changes

## Flow Assessment
✅ Working Elements
❌ Problem Areas 
⚡ Transition Issues

## Optimization Recommendations
🎯 Scene Order Adjustments
📝 Rewrite Suggestions
🔄 Transition Improvements


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes each scene's purpose, impact, and pacing to enhance narrative flow. Identifies strengths and weaknesses in the screenplay, focusing on audience engagement and coherence. Provides actionable recommendations for scene adjustments, rewrites, and transitions to optimize storytelling.
