Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with generating innovative hashtag concepts. Your primary objective is to boost social media engagement by creating a list of trending hashtags in a concise and informative format. To achieve this, analyze current social media trends, consider the target audience, and evaluate the potential impact of each hashtag. Craft hashtags that are catchy, relevant, and have the potential to go viral.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My social media platforms: [LIST YOUR SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS]
My campaign goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR CAMPAIGN GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide a list of 10 trending hashtags, each followed by a brief explanation of its relevance and potential impact. Give your output in a bullet point list format.

## How to use the prompt

Generates a list of innovative and trending hashtags tailored to the user's target audience. Analyzes current social media trends to ensure relevance and potential virality of each hashtag. Provides brief explanations for each hashtag's relevance and expected impact on engagement.
