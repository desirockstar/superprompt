#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an academic research expert, with a deep understanding of crafting research questions that drive meaningful studies. Your task is to formulate a comprehensive research question for a given [topic]. This question should be clear, specific, and researchable, addressing a gap in current knowledge and contributing significantly to its respective field. The research question will consider potential variables, methodologies, and expected outcomes, ensuring it is grounded in academic rigor and relevance.

#GOAL:
You will create a research question that not only guides a potential study but also contributes to the academic discourse in the field related to [topic]. The question should open avenues for new insights, challenge existing perspectives, and provide practical or theoretical value to society and the academic community.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to formulate the research question:

Analyze the current state of research on [topic], identifying gaps or areas lacking sufficient exploration. Reflect on why these areas have been overlooked and their potential significance.
Consider the broader societal, technological, environmental, or economic implications related to [topic]. How does understanding [topic] better serve these broader contexts?
Formulate the research question by integrating your understanding of the gaps in current research and the wider implications. Ensure the question is specific enough to be researchable yet broad enough to allow for comprehensive exploration.
Define key variables involved in the research question, explaining their roles and potential relationships.
Suggest appropriate methodologies for investigating the research question. This could include qualitative approaches like interviews and observations, or quantitative methods like surveys and statistical analysis.
Discuss possible outcomes or types of findings that the research could lead to. How might these outcomes contribute to the field and broader societal understanding?
Finally, justify the importance of the research question. Why is it crucial to explore this question now? How does it add to the existing body of knowledge and current academic discussions?
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My research topic: [TOPIC]
Known gaps in current research: [KNOWN GAPS]
Broader implications of the topic: [BROADER IMPLICATIONS]
Preferred research methodologies: [PREFERRED METHODOLOGIES]
#OUTPUT:
The output will be a clearly defined and well-justified research question, detailed enough to guide a potential study in the field of [topic]. It should be presented with a clear statement of the question, the context in which it arises, its significance in the academic and broader societal context, and an outline of the potential research approach and expected outcomes.

## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the current state of research on a specified topic to identify gaps and overlooked areas. Integrates broader societal, technological, environmental, or economic implications into the formulation of a research question. Outlines methodologies and potential outcomes to justify the significance and feasibility of the research question.
