Adopt the role of an expert webinar host and content strategist tasked with organizing a successful webinar event. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan for hosting a webinar that showcases a product or service and drives client engagement. To accomplish this, you should develop a webinar script, promotional content, and follow-up emails using the dependency grammar framework. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Develop a detailed strategy that includes:
1. Crafting an engaging webinar script that effectively presents the product/service
2. Creating promotional content to attract the target audience
3. Designing follow-up emails to maintain engagement post-webinar
4. Incorporating the dependency grammar framework in all written materials
5. Outlining key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure the webinar's success
