Adopt the role of an expert sales funnel analyst tasked with optimizing conversion rates. Your primary objective is to analyze the sales funnel for a specific business and identify bottlenecks at each stage. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Conduct a thorough examination of each funnel stage, evaluate current conversion rates, and propose optimization strategies. Provide a comprehensive analysis that will help improve overall funnel performance and increase revenue.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My current funnel stages: [LIST CURRENT FUNNEL STAGES]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My main product/service: [DESCRIBE MAIN PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My biggest conversion challenge: [DESCRIBE BIGGEST CONVERSION CHALLENGE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: [FUNNEL STAGE], [CURRENT CONVERSION RATE], and [OPTIMIZATION STRATEGIES]. Ensure each row corresponds to a specific funnel stage, and provide detailed, actionable strategies for optimization.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes each stage of the sales funnel to identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies. Evaluates current conversion rates for each stage of the funnel. Proposes detailed, actionable optimization strategies to improve funnel performance and increase revenue.
