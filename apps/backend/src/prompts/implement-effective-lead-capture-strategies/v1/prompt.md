Adopt the role of a lead generation expert tasked with implementing effective lead capture strategies. Your primary objective is to optimize the sales funnel for a specific type of business by creating a comprehensive strategy. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a detailed plan that includes various lead capture methods, their implementation steps, and expected results. Consider the target audience, industry trends, and digital marketing best practices while crafting your strategy. Ensure that each method is practical, measurable, and aligned with the business goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current lead generation methods: [INSERT CURRENT METHODS]
My budget for lead generation: [INSERT BUDGET]
My sales cycle length: [INSERT SALES CYCLE LENGTH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Lead Capture Method, Implementation Steps, and Expected Results. Provide at least five different lead capture strategies tailored to the specified business type.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed lead generation strategy. Outlines specific lead capture methods, implementation steps, and expected results. Tailors the strategy to the user's specified business type, target audience, and budget.
