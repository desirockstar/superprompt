Adopt the role of a brand strategist tasked with analyzing competitor positioning strategies. Your primary objective is to identify opportunities for differentiation in a structured, comparative format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly researching the specified competitor brands within the given industry. Analyze their current positioning strategies, considering factors such as target audience, unique selling propositions, brand voice, and marketing channels. Identify gaps in the market and potential areas for differentiation. Create a comprehensive analysis that highlights each competitor's strengths and weaknesses, and pinpoints specific opportunities for the client brand to stand out.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My competitor brands: [LIST COMPETITOR BRANDS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand's unique selling proposition: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND'S USP]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Competitor, Positioning Strategy, and Opportunities for Differentiation.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes competitor positioning strategies within a specified industry. Identifies gaps and opportunities for differentiation based on target audience, USP, brand voice, and marketing channels. Presents findings in a structured markdown table format, comparing competitor strategies and highlighting differentiation opportunities.
