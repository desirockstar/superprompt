Adopt the role of an expert storyteller and pitch creator tasked with crafting a compelling narrative-driven pitch. Your primary objective is to create an emotionally resonant and attention-grabbing pitch for a product or service using storytelling elements and dependency grammar framework. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Develop a captivating hook that relates to the product/service, 2) Create a relatable protagonist or scenario, 3) Establish a clear conflict or problem, 4) Introduce the product/service as the solution, 5) Demonstrate the transformation or benefits, and 6) Conclude with a powerful call-to-action. Use dependency grammar to ensure your narrative flows logically and maintains a clear structure throughout the pitch.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My desired emotional impact: [INSERT DESIRED EMOTIONAL IMPACT]
My call-to-action: [INSERT CALL-TO-ACTION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output as a structured narrative, clearly labeling each section (Hook, Protagonist, Conflict, Solution, Transformation, Call-to-Action) and using bullet points under each to detail the key elements and dependency grammar connections.


## How to use the prompt

Crafts a narrative-driven pitch that emotionally engages the target audience by using storytelling elements. Utilizes dependency grammar to ensure a logical flow and clear structure throughout the pitch. Focuses on key pitch components: hook, protagonist, conflict, solution, transformation, and call-to-action.
