Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive email marketing strategy. Your primary objective is to engage subscribers and drive conversions for a specific type of business in a detailed, actionable plan format. To achieve this, you should: analyze the target audience, create compelling email content using dependency grammar framework, craft attention-grabbing subject lines, design effective calls-to-action, and establish a consistent email schedule. Develop a strategy that incorporates personalization, segmentation, and automation to maximize engagement and conversions. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary products/services: [INSERT PRIMARY PRODUCTS/SERVICES]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My current email list size: [INSERT CURRENT EMAIL LIST SIZE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for each section of the email marketing strategy.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specific business type, target audience, and primary products/services to tailor the email marketing strategy. Develops compelling email content, attention-grabbing subject lines, and effective calls-to-action based on dependency grammar framework. Incorporates personalization, segmentation, and automation to enhance subscriber engagement and drive conversions.
