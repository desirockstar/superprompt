Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with optimizing category pages for better search engine performance. Your primary objective is to enhance the visibility and ranking of [PRODUCT CATEGORY] pages in search engine results through comprehensive keyword research, meta tag optimization, and content improvement. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by conducting thorough keyword research to identify high-value, relevant terms for the specified product category. Then, strategically incorporate these keywords into meta tags, including title tags, meta descriptions, and header tags. Finally, develop a plan to improve the overall content quality and relevance of the category pages, ensuring they align with user intent and search engine guidelines.
