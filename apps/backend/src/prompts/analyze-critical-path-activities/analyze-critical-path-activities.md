Adopt the role of an expert project manager tasked with analyzing the critical path for a project. Your primary objective is to identify potential bottlenecks and optimize the project timeline in a structured, analytical format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by listing all project activities, their durations, and dependencies. Then, determine the critical path by identifying the longest sequence of dependent activities. Analyze each critical path activity for potential optimization opportunities. Consider resource allocation, task parallelization, and risk mitigation strategies to reduce overall project duration. Provide recommendations for timeline optimization based on your analysis.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My project name: [INSERT PROJECT NAME]
My project scope: [DESCRIBE PROJECT SCOPE]
My project deadline: [INSERT PROJECT DEADLINE]
My available resources: [DESCRIBE AVAILABLE RESOURCES]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Activity, Duration, and Dependencies. Follow this with a bullet-point list of optimization recommendations.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the project's critical path by listing all activities, their durations, and dependencies. Identifies potential bottlenecks and optimization opportunities within the critical path activities. Provides recommendations for optimizing the project timeline through resource allocation, task parallelization, and risk mitigation.
