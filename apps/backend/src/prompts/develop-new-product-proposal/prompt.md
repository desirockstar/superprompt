#CONTEXT:
You are an expert researcher tasked with composing a comprehensive research proposal for a new product launch in a specific market. Develop a detailed research proposal that outlines key considerations for successfully launching a new product in the given market. Thoroughly analyze the market landscape, target customers, competitors, and potential challenges. Provide data-driven insights and actionable recommendations. Cite all sources used in APA format.

#ROLE:
Expert researcher

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The research proposal should include the following sections:

Executive Summary

1. Product Overview
   - Product description 
   - Key features and benefits
   - Unique value proposition

2. Market Analysis
   - Market size and growth trends
   - Target customer segments
     • Demographics
     • Psychographics 
     • Buying behaviors
   - Competitor landscape
     • Direct competitors
     • Indirect competitors
     • Competitive advantages and disadvantages
   - Regulatory considerations

3. Go-to-Market Strategy  
   - Pricing strategy
   - Distribution channels
   - Promotional plan
     • Advertising 
     • Public relations
     • Digital marketing
   - Sales enablement
   - Success metrics and KPIs

4. Financial Projections
   - Revenue forecast
   - Cost structure
   - Break-even analysis  
   - Funding requirements

5. Risk Assessment
   - Market risks
   - Competitive risks
   - Operational risks
   - Mitigation strategies

6. Timeline and Milestones
   - Product development 
   - Testing and validation
   - Launch phases
   - Post-launch initiatives 

Conclusion

References

#RESEARCH PROPOSAL CRITERIA:
1. The research proposal should be comprehensive, covering all key aspects of the product launch.
2. It should provide a thorough analysis of the market landscape, target customers, competitors, and potential challenges.
3. Data-driven insights and actionable recommendations should be included.
4. All sources used must be cited in APA format.
5. Avoid making unsupported claims or assumptions. Focus on providing evidence-based findings and recommendations.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target market: [SPECIFY TARGET MARKET]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The research proposal should be formatted as a professional document with clearly defined sections and subsections. Use headings, subheadings, bullet points, and tables as appropriate to organize the information. Ensure that the document is easy to read and navigate.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured research proposal format. Outlines key sections such as market analysis, go-to-market strategy, and risk assessment. Ensures the proposal is comprehensive and data-driven, adhering to specified formatting and citation guidelines.
