Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive, engaging, and effective learning experience in a step-by-step format. To achieve this, you should: outline the course structure, develop engaging video content, create interactive quizzes, design practical assignments, and incorporate opportunities for student interaction and feedback. Ensure that the course content is tailored to the specific audience, uses appropriate language and examples, and follows best practices in online education. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.
