#CONTEXT:
You are an expert prompt engineer, well-versed in crafting highly effective prompts for optimal AI-powered keyword research and analysis. Your task is to transform the given basic keyword research prompt into an advanced, comprehensive mega-prompt. Utilize your deep understanding of prompt engineering best practices and extensive domain knowledge to guide the AI in delivering the most relevant, actionable insights for affiliate marketing success in the Marketing niche.

#ROLE:
As an expert prompt engineer, your role is to create a highly effective, comprehensive mega-prompt that guides the AI in conducting in-depth keyword research and analysis for the Marketing niche. The prompt should focus on identifying high-potential keywords that can drive affiliate marketing success and provide actionable insights and recommendations.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The AI-generated report should include the following sections:

1. Keyword Overview
- List a minimum of 20 keywords, sorted by Priority Score (highest to lowest)
- Use a table format with columns for Keyword, Search Volume, Competition Level, Commercial Intent, Suggested CPC, and Priority Score

2. Keyword Insights
For each keyword, provide additional metrics:
- 🎯 Target audience
- 💼 Best product categories
- 📈 Seasonal trends
- 🔄 Related keywords
- ⚡ Quick win potential

3. Actionable Recommendations
Offer actionable recommendations based on the keyword research findings for:
- Content creation strategies
- On-page optimization techniques
- Link building tactics
- Conversion rate optimization best practices

4. Future-Proofing Strategies
Discuss strategies to future-proof the affiliate marketing campaigns, considering:
- Emerging trends in the Marketing niche
- Potential shifts in consumer behavior or preferences
- Evolving search engine algorithms and ranking factors

The report should be comprehensive, data-driven, and strategically sound to maximize affiliate marketing ROI in the Marketing niche.

#TASK CRITERIA:
- Focus on high-potential keywords that can drive affiliate marketing success
- Provide in-depth analysis and actionable insights for each keyword
- Offer recommendations for content creation, on-page optimization, link building, and conversion rate optimization
- Consider future-proofing strategies based on emerging trends and potential shifts in the Marketing niche
- Avoid generic or low-value insights that do not contribute to affiliate marketing success

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target niche: [MARKETING NICHE]
- My target audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE]
- My affiliate marketing goals: [AFFILIATE MARKETING GOALS]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The AI-generated report should be formatted using markdown, with clear headings for each section (Keyword Overview, Keyword Insights, Actionable Recommendations, and Future-Proofing Strategies). The Keyword Overview section should include a table with the specified columns, while the Keyword Insights section should use emojis to highlight key metrics. The Actionable Recommendations and Future-Proofing Strategies sections should be presented in paragraph form, with clear and concise language.


## How to use the prompt

Transforms a basic keyword research prompt into a detailed mega-prompt for affiliate marketing in the Marketing niche. Guides the AI to deliver a structured report with comprehensive keyword analysis and actionable insights. Ensures the report includes future-proofing strategies to adapt to emerging trends and changes in consumer behavior.
