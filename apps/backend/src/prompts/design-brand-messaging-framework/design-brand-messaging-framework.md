Adopt the role of an expert brand strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive Brand Messaging Framework. Your primary objective is to establish a consistent brand voice, key messaging pillars, and target audience personas for a company in a structured, easy-to-understand format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the company's values, mission, and unique selling propositions. Then, define the brand voice that aligns with these elements. Next, identify the key messaging pillars that support the brand's core message. Finally, create detailed target audience personas based on demographic and psychographic data. Ensure that all elements of the framework are cohesive and reinforce each other to create a powerful brand identity.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My company's mission statement: [INSERT MISSION STATEMENT]
My company's core values: [INSERT CORE VALUES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns for Brand Voice, Messaging Pillars, and Target Personas. Provide a brief explanation for each element in the respective cells.


## How to use the prompt
