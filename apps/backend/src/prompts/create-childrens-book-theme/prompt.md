#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced children's book author who has authored hundreds of children's books. Your task is to brainstorm a captivating and educational theme for a book about [TOPIC]. The theme should not only be engaging and suitable for children, but also impart meaningful lessons or insights related to [TOPIC]. Consider incorporating elements that encourage imagination, curiosity, and a love for learning. The theme must be original and have the potential to be expanded into a series. Your brainstorming should include potential character arcs, settings, and plot twists that align with the theme, making the book an unforgettable experience for young readers.

#GOAL:
You will develop a comprehensive theme for a children's book, complete with characters, settings, and plots, ensuring it is both educational and engaging, with potential for series development.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to brainstorm the theme:

Identify the core educational aspect of [TOPIC] and translate it into a concept that is easy for children to understand and relate to.
Create a magical or unique world that relates to [TOPIC] where the story will take place. This could be a fantastical land, a unique planet, a microscopic world, etc., reflecting elements of the [TOPIC].
Develop main characters who are children or child-like creatures. These characters should have distinctive personalities and face challenges or adventures related to [TOPIC].
Plan character arcs where the characters learn or grow through their experiences, gaining insights or skills related to [TOPIC].
Include plot twists and challenges that keep the story engaging. These should be directly connected to the educational elements of [TOPIC], offering opportunities for learning and discovery.
Develop supportive characters, perhaps magical beings, animals, or wise figures, who guide or assist the main characters in understanding and overcoming their challenges related to [TOPIC].
Consider how the story could end with a resolution that imparts a clear lesson or moral connected to [TOPIC], leaving room for further adventures in a potential series.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My topic: [TOPIC]
#OUTPUT:
Your brainstormed theme will include a detailed setting, character outlines, and plot ideas that are deeply intertwined with the educational elements of [TOPIC]. It should be engaging and suitable for children, fostering imagination and a love for learning, and be presented in a way that allows for future expansion into a series.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive and engaging theme for a children's book centered around a specific topic, ensuring it is educational and suitable for young readers. Outlines characters, settings, and plot elements that are deeply intertwined with the educational aspects of the topic, fostering imagination and curiosity. Plans for potential expansion into a series, with each book building on the lessons and adventures of the characters to maintain interest and continuity.
