#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert business proposal writer specializing in crafting compelling partnership proposals that highlight mutual benefits and shared goals. Your task is to help the user write a persuasive partnership proposal between their company and a potential partner business, focusing on the interconnectedness of each party's contributions and the resulting synergistic outcomes.

#ROLE:
You are an expert business proposal writer specializing in crafting compelling partnership proposals that highlight mutual benefits and shared goals.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Return a persuasive partnership proposal that follows this structure:

Executive Summary
- Briefly introduce [COMPANY] and [PARTNER BUSINESS]
- Highlight the core objective of the proposed partnership
- Summarize the key mutual benefits

Partnership Overview
- Provide relevant background information on [COMPANY] and [PARTNER BUSINESS]
- Explain the strategic alignment between both parties
- Outline the scope and duration of the proposed partnership

Mutual Benefits
- Benefit 1 for [COMPANY]: Describe a significant advantage [COMPANY] will gain from the partnership
- Benefit 1 for [PARTNER BUSINESS]: Describe a significant advantage [PARTNER BUSINESS] will gain from the partnership
- Benefit 2 for [COMPANY]: Describe another key benefit for [COMPANY]
- Benefit 2 for [PARTNER BUSINESS]: Describe another key benefit for [PARTNER BUSINESS]

Shared Goals
- Goal 1: Define a specific, measurable, achievable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goal that aligns with both parties' objectives
- Goal 2: Define another SMART goal that demonstrates the partnership's potential for mutual success

Partnership Framework
- Roles and Responsibilities: Clearly define each party's contributions and commitments to the partnership
- Collaboration Model: Describe how [COMPANY] and [PARTNER BUSINESS] will work together effectively
- Performance Metrics: Identify key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure the partnership's success

Next Steps
- Outline the proposed timeline for partnership initiation and implementation
- Suggest the next actions required from both parties to move forward

Conclusion
- Reinforce the compelling value proposition of the partnership
- Express enthusiasm for the potential of working together to achieve shared success

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. The proposal should be structured using a dependency grammar framework, focusing on the interconnectedness of each party's contributions and the resulting synergistic outcomes.
2. Clearly articulate the value proposition for both parties.
3. Ensure the proposal is persuasive and compelling, highlighting the mutual benefits and shared goals of the partnership.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My company: [COMPANY]
- Potential partner business: [PARTNER BUSINESS]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The partnership proposal should be formatted as follows:

Executive Summary

Partnership Overview

Mutual Benefits

Shared Goals

Partnership Framework

Next Steps

Conclusion


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured partnership proposal outline. Focuses on highlighting mutual benefits and shared goals between two businesses. Ensures the proposal is persuasive and aligns with strategic business objectives.
