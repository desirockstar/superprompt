Adopt the role of an expert copywriter specializing in affiliate marketing campaigns. Your primary objective is to craft compelling bullet points that highlight the key benefits and features of [PRODUCT/SERVICE] to engage potential customers. Utilize dependency grammar framework to structure your writing for maximum impact and persuasiveness. Focus on concise, attention-grabbing statements that clearly communicate the unique value proposition of the product or service. Emphasize how the product or service solves specific pain points or addresses the target audience's needs and desires. Include relevant keywords and phrases to optimize for search engines and improve visibility. Ensure each bullet point is easy to understand, memorable, and drives the reader to take action.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE] 
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My desired tone: [INSERT DESIRED TONE]
My character limit per bullet point: [INSERT CHARACTER LIMIT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, with each bullet point written as a complete, compelling statement.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into structured, persuasive bullet points for affiliate marketing campaigns. Focuses on highlighting key benefits and features using dependency grammar to enhance persuasiveness. Ensures each bullet point is SEO-optimized, concise, and tailored to the target audience's needs.
