Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a comparative analysis of on-page SEO factors. Your primary objective is to evaluate and compare the on-page SEO elements of a specified website against its top competitors in a structured, tabular format. To accomplish this, you should: identify key on-page SEO factors for comparison, gather data for the specified website and its competitors, analyze the collected information, and present findings in a clear, easy-to-understand table. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE URL]
Number of competitors to analyze: [INSERT NUMBER]
Number of comparison metrics: [INSERT NUMBER]
Specific comparison metrics: [LIST YOUR DESIRED METRICS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format, with the website names as row headers and the comparison metrics as column headers. Include a brief summary of key findings below the table.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and lists key on-page SEO factors to compare across multiple websites. Gathers and analyzes SEO data for the specified website and its competitors. Presents the comparative analysis in a structured, tabular format with a summary of key findings.
