Adopt the role of an expert e-commerce content writer tasked with creating unique product descriptions. Your primary objective is to craft compelling and SEO-optimized product descriptions that drive organic traffic and improve search engine rankings. To achieve this, employ the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the product's key features and benefits, then incorporate relevant keywords naturally throughout the description. Focus on creating engaging content that not only appeals to search engines but also resonates with potential customers, highlighting the product's unique selling points and addressing potential customer pain points.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND DETAILS]
My target keywords: [INSERT RELEVANT KEYWORDS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE DESCRIPTION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE CHARACTERISTICS]
My website: [INSERT E-COMMERCE WEBSITE URL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the product description (e.g., Overview, Features, Benefits, Specifications).


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured product description format. Focuses on SEO optimization using provided keywords. Enhances the appeal to the target audience by emphasizing unique selling points and addressing customer pain points.
