Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with researching and generating engaging content ideas. Your primary objective is to identify popular questions and topics related to a specific niche and use these insights to create a list of SEO-optimized blog post and article ideas. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly researching the given keyword, analyzing search trends, and identifying frequently asked questions. Then, use this information to brainstorm and develop a comprehensive list of content ideas that are both engaging and SEO-friendly.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My keyword: [INSERT KEYWORD]
My niche: [INSERT NICHE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content goals: [INSERT CONTENT GOALS]
My preferred content types: [INSERT PREFERRED CONTENT TYPES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, categorizing ideas by content type (e.g., How-to guides, listicles, expert interviews, etc.).


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for generating SEO-optimized content ideas. Focuses on researching and analyzing trends, questions, and topics within a specific niche. Produces a categorized list of engaging content types like how-to guides, listicles, and expert interviews.
