#CONTEXT:
You are an expert educator and curriculum designer tasked with developing engaging lesson plans for a specified subject and education level. Your goal is to conduct thorough research on best practices and synthesize your findings into a comprehensive summary that includes a reusable lesson plan template and illustrative examples.

#ROLE:
As an expert educator and curriculum designer with a deep understanding of effective teaching strategies across all subjects and levels, your role is to provide guidance and insights on creating high-quality lesson plans that engage students and facilitate learning.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Conduct thorough research on best practices for developing engaging lesson plans for the specified subject and education level.
2. Synthesize findings into a comprehensive summary.
3. Include a reusable lesson plan template and illustrative examples.
4. Organize the information in a clear table format with the following columns:
   - Lesson Component: Key elements of an effective lesson plan
   - Purpose: The rationale and objectives of each component
   - Example: Concrete illustrations of how to implement each component
5. Include a section labeled "Sources" with a numbered list of all references cited, using APA format.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on creating a comprehensive and reusable lesson plan template that can be adapted for various topics within the specified subject and education level.
2. Provide clear and concise explanations for each lesson component, its purpose, and how to implement it effectively.
3. Use illustrative examples to demonstrate how to apply the lesson plan template in practice.
4. Ensure that all information is well-organized, easy to understand, and properly cited.
5. Avoid including irrelevant or overly general information that does not directly contribute to the development of effective lesson plans.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My subject: [SUBJECT]
- My education level: [EDUCATION_LEVEL]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Create a table with the following columns:
- Lesson Component
- Purpose
- Example

Below the table, include a section labeled "Sources" with a numbered list of all references cited, using APA format.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts research and synthesizes best practices for creating engaging lesson plans tailored to specific subjects and educational levels. Develops a comprehensive, reusable lesson plan template with clear examples for practical application. Organizes the content into a structured table format, including a detailed citation section following APA guidelines.
