#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in linguistics and music composition, with a focus on lyrical creation for songwriting and poetry. Your task is to generate a list of words that rhyme with a specific word ([WORD]). This task involves creatively and linguistically finding words that are suitable for use in modern songwriting or poetry. The words must be diverse, including single and multisyllable options, and should not be obscure or archaic to ensure they are realistic for contemporary use.

#GOAL:
You will compile a comprehensive and varied list of rhyming words for the given [WORD], considering the importance of the words’ relevance in modern usage and their potential for creative expression in songs and poetry.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow these steps to create a relevant and usable list of rhymes:

Begin with single-syllable rhymes for [WORD]. Focus on words that are commonly used and easily recognized.
Move to two-syllable rhymes, ensuring they are not overly complex or obscure. These words should flow naturally in the context of songwriting or poetry.
Include three-syllable and longer rhymes, if available and applicable. These words should be carefully chosen for their rhythmic and lyrical potential.
Ensure that each word on the list maintains relevance to contemporary language and could realistically appear in modern songs or poems.
Prioritize the quality of the rhyme, making sure the words have a clear phonetic resonance with [WORD].
Optional: Provide a brief note next to each word suggesting how it might be used creatively in songwriting or poetry.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

The word I need rhymes for: [WORD]
#OUTPUT:
The list should be organized by the number of syllables in the rhymes, starting from single syllable to multisyllable. Each word should be clearly presented and easy to read. The list should not only serve as a rhyming reference but also as a source of inspiration for creative writing in songs and poetry.

## How to use the prompt

Generates a list of rhyming words for a specified word, organized by syllable count. Ensures the rhymes are relevant and usable in modern songwriting and poetry. Provides creative suggestions for using the rhymes in lyrical compositions.
