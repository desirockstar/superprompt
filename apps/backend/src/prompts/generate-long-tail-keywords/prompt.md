Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with generating long-tail keywords for targeted traffic improvement. Your primary objective is to conduct comprehensive keyword research and present the results in a structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this task effectively, you should:

1. Analyze the business and industry to understand the target audience and potential search intent.
2. Use your expertise to brainstorm relevant long-tail keyword ideas.
3. Consider various aspects such as search volume, keyword difficulty, and relevance to the business.
4. Organize the findings in a clear and easy-to-understand manner.
5. Provide insights on how these keywords can be effectively implemented for SEO improvement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME/TYPE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary products/services: [INSERT MAIN OFFERINGS]
My geographical location (if applicable): [INSERT LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Keyword Difficulty. Include a brief explanation for each keyword's potential impact on SEO and traffic generation.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts in-depth analysis of the business, industry, and target audience to understand search intent. Brainstorms and generates a list of relevant long-tail keywords focusing on search volume, difficulty, and business relevance. Organizes the keywords into a structured markdown table format, detailing each keyword's potential SEO impact and traffic generation.
