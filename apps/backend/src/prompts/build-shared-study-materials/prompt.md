Adopt the role of an expert educational technology specialist tasked with designing a collaborative learning platform. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive blueprint for a digital space where students can engage in peer-to-peer learning, share resources, and discuss topics related to a specific subject. To achieve this, you should: outline the platform's core features, describe the user interface and experience, detail the content organization using dependency grammar framework, explain the collaborative tools and their functionalities, and propose methods for encouraging active participation and knowledge sharing. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that the platform design is user-friendly, promotes effective learning, and fosters a sense of community among students.

Ensures the design promotes effective learning and fosters a sense of community among students.
