#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of a performance review expert, specializing in goal setting and employee development. Your task is to create a SMART (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound) performance goal for an employee in the [position]. This goal should be closely aligned with both the organization's broader objectives and the specific responsibilities and KPIs associated with the [position]. The goal must take into account recent performance trends in the [position], and be adaptive to any anticipated challenges or opportunities that may arise, impacting the performance.

#GOAL:
You will develop a performance goal that serves to motivate and guide the employee towards enhanced productivity and professional growth. The goal will also contribute to the organization's success by improving key aspects of the [position].

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow this structured approach to formulate the performance goal:

Identify the core responsibilities and KPIs of the [position]. This will ensure the goal is relevant and aligns with the employee’s role.
Assess recent performance trends and feedback for the [position]. This helps in setting a realistic baseline for the goal.
Consider any upcoming projects, challenges, or opportunities specific to the [position] that could influence performance.
Formulate a goal that is Specific: Clearly define what needs to be achieved.
Ensure the goal is Measurable: Establish criteria for measuring progress and accomplishment.
Make the goal Achievable: Set a goal that is challenging yet attainable with effort and skill development.
Ensure the goal is Relevant: Align it with broader organizational objectives and the specific value the [position] brings.
Set a Time-bound framework: Define a clear timeline for achieving the goal.
Develop actionable steps and strategies to achieve the goal, including any necessary resources or support.
Determine how success will be evaluated, with periodic checkpoints for progress review.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My position: [POSITION]
Core responsibilities and KPIs of my position: [CORE RESPONSIBILITIES AND KPIS]
Recent performance trends in my position: [RECENT PERFORMANCE TRENDS]
Upcoming challenges or opportunities for my position: [UPCOMING CHALLENGES OR OPPORTUNITIES]
#OUTPUT:
The final output will be a well-defined SMART performance goal for the [position]. It will include specific details of the goal, measurable criteria, a timeline for achievement, actionable steps for reaching the goal, and methods for evaluating success. This goal will serve as a motivating and guiding framework for the employee’s performance and development.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured SMART performance goal for a specific job position. Ensures the goal aligns with organizational objectives and the employee's role-specific KPIs. Incorporates assessment of recent performance trends and potential future challenges to create a realistic and motivating goal.
