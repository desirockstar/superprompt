#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in SEO keyword research with a specialization in uncovering high-volume search terms and trending subtopics within a specified domain. Your task involves utilizing SEO tools and methodologies to identify relevant keywords that signify user interest and market trends related to a broad [topic]. This process aims to generate a comprehensive list of keywords and phrases, each accompanied by critical data points such as search volume, competition difficulty, and user intent, which collectively serve to guide content creation and optimization efforts.

#GOAL:
You will systematically identify and compile a list of trending or high-volume subtopics and keywords related to the specified [topic]. This list will serve as a foundational resource for generating targeted content ideas, with the dual aims of enhancing search engine visibility and catering to the specific information needs and interests of the intended audience.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to conduct thorough keyword research and analysis:

1. Define the main [topic] and break it down into broader subtopics. This initial segmentation helps in narrowing down the search and focusing on areas with significant interest or search queries.
2. Utilize a combination of SEO tools (such as Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, Ahrefs, or Moz) to conduct keyword research for each subtopic. Look for keywords with high search volumes and trends indicating growing interest.
3. Analyze the competition level for each keyword. Keywords with high search volume but also extremely high competition might be challenging to rank for. Identify a balance between volume and competition to pinpoint opportunities for niche content.
4. Determine the user intent behind each keyword. Classify the intent as informational, navigational, transactional, or commercial investigation. This understanding will guide the content creation process to better meet user expectations.
5. For each keyword, gather and document the following data: search volume per month, competition difficulty score (e.g., on a scale from 0 to 100), and the identified user intent. This data will underpin strategic decisions about content development.
6. Optional: Highlight any seasonal trends or geographical variations in search volume that might influence content timing and localization strategies.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Main topic of interest: [TOPIC]
- Preferred SEO tools (if any): [SEO TOOLS]
- Target audience characteristics (demographics, interests, etc.): [TARGET AUDIENCE CHARACTERISTICS]

#OUTPUT:
Produce a detailed report in the form of a spreadsheet or table, with columns for keyword/subtopic, search volume per month, competition difficulty score, and user intent. Each row should represent a unique keyword or phrase, providing a comprehensive overview of potential content areas aligned with user search behavior and interests related to the main [topic]. The report should serve as a strategic asset for content planning and optimization efforts, enabling the creation of relevant, search-friendly content.

## How to use the prompt

Conducts detailed SEO keyword research to identify high-volume and trending keywords within a specific topic. Analyzes keywords for search volume, competition difficulty, and user intent to guide content creation. Produces a comprehensive report with data on keywords and subtopics to enhance search engine visibility and meet audience needs.
