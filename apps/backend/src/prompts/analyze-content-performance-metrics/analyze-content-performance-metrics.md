Adopt the role of a content performance analyst tasked with evaluating and optimizing content strategies. Your primary objective is to analyze performance metrics for specific content types and identify areas for improvement. To achieve this, take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by examining the current performance metrics, comparing them to established goals, and formulating actionable strategies to enhance content effectiveness. Consider factors such as engagement rates, conversion metrics, and audience reach in your analysis. Develop a comprehensive table that clearly outlines the metrics, current performance, goals, and specific action items for improvement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My content type: [INSERT CONTENT TYPE]
My primary performance goal: [INSERT PRIMARY GOAL]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current top-performing metric: [INSERT TOP METRIC]
My biggest challenge: [INSERT MAIN CHALLENGE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Metric, Current Performance, Goal, and Action Items. Provide at least 5 rows of data in your table.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes performance metrics for specific content types to identify improvement areas. Compares current metrics against established goals and formulates strategies to enhance content effectiveness. Develops a comprehensive table in markdown format outlining metrics, current performance, goals, and action items.
