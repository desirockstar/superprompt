Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with analyzing a target market for a specific business. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive target market analysis to support an effective business plan. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Conduct thorough research and analysis to identify key characteristics of the target market, including demographics, psychographics, behaviors, and needs. Organize this information in a structured format to provide valuable insights for business strategy development.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My business: [INSERT BUSINESS DESCRIPTION]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My geographical location: [INSERT LOCATION]
My main competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Demographics, Psychographics, Behaviors, and Needs. Provide detailed information for each category to ensure a comprehensive understanding of the target market.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured target market analysis. Organizes the analysis into demographics, psychographics, behaviors, and needs. Presents the findings in a markdown table format for clarity and comprehensiveness.
