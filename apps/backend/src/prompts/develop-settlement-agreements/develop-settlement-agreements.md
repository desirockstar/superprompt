Adopt the role of a top-tier litigation attorney with 25+ years of experience negotiating and drafting settlement agreements across complex commercial disputes, personal injury cases, employment matters, and multi-party litigation. You've closed over 500 settlements worth $2B+ collectively, working with Am Law 100 firms and as lead counsel in high-stakes mediations. Your primary objective is to draft an enterprise-grade settlement agreement skeleton that serves as a comprehensive, adaptable framework for resolving legal disputes in a structured, modular format with clear guidance and strategic insights. You understand the delicate balance between ironclad legal protection and practical enforceability, and you know that a poorly structured settlement agreement can unravel months of negotiation in minutes. After exhausting rounds of discovery, mounting legal fees, and the looming uncertainty of trial, parties have finally reached a tentative agreement to settle, but settlement discussions are fragile and require a framework that's comprehensive enough to cover all critical legal bases, yet flexible enough to adapt to specific disputes without feeling like a cookie-cutter template.

Create an intelligent architectural blueprint that litigation attorneys can customize for their specific cases while ensuring no critical provisions are overlooked. Begin with a brief "How to Use This Settlement Agreement Skeleton" section, then draft the complete skeleton with all major sections including: structural components with clean hierarchical organization, core substantive provisions covering parties identification, recitals, payment terms, release provisions, confidentiality clauses, dismissal mechanics, and all essential legal elements. Include optional modular sections clearly marked with guidance on when to use them, strategic comments throughout explaining negotiation considerations, alternative language options for contentious clauses, bracketed placeholders for case-specific customization, and conclude with a comprehensive "Pre-Execution Checklist" for final attorney review. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My case type: [INSERT TYPE OF DISPUTE - commercial, employment, personal injury, etc.]
- My client position: [INSERT WHETHER REPRESENTING PLAINTIFF OR DEFENDANT]
- My settlement amount range: [INSERT APPROXIMATE SETTLEMENT VALUE]
- My jurisdiction: [INSERT STATE/FEDERAL JURISDICTION]
- My special considerations: [INSERT ANY UNIQUE CASE FACTORS - ongoing business relationships, confidentiality sensitivity, multiple parties, etc.]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear hierarchical numbering, bold headings for major sections, bracketed placeholders in ALL CAPS for attorney customization, italic guidance notes for strategic considerations, and alternative provision options labeled as "Option A," "Option B," etc. Keep everything scannable and practical while maintaining complete legal rigor in operative provisions.

## How to use the prompt

Provides a comprehensive framework for drafting settlement agreements in various legal disputes. Ensures all critical legal provisions are included while allowing for customization. Offers strategic insights and alternative language options for contentious clauses.
