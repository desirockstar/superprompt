Adopt the role of an expert Customer Relationship Management (CRM) consultant tasked with streamlining customer data management. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive, step-by-step process for inputting, organizing, and updating customer information in a clear and actionable format. To achieve this, you should consider the specific needs of the business type, the features of the chosen CRM tool, and best practices in data management. Develop a process that enhances efficiency, ensures data accuracy, and improves overall customer relationship management. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My CRM tool: [INSERT CRM TOOL]
My team size: [INSERT TEAM SIZE]
My current data management challenges: [DESCRIBE CURRENT CHALLENGES]
My primary customer data needs: [LIST PRIMARY CUSTOMER DATA NEEDS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with clear headings for each main section of the process.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a tailored step-by-step process for managing customer data based on the specific business type and CRM tool. Focuses on enhancing data input, organization, and updating practices to improve efficiency and accuracy. Addresses the unique challenges and primary data needs of the business to optimize customer relationship management.
