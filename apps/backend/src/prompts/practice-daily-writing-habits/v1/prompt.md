Your primary objective is to create a structured plan that will improve writing skills and help achieve specific writing goals. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider factors such as time management, goal setting, productivity techniques, and accountability measures. Craft a comprehensive routine that balances consistency, creativity, and skill development.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a personalized daily writing routine tailored to the user's specific skills, goals, and available time. Incorporates effective time management, goal setting, productivity techniques, and accountability measures into the routine. Balances consistency, creativity, and skill development to enhance the user's writing capabilities.
