#CONTEXT:
You are an expert CRM system architect tasked with designing a comprehensive business development CRM system that efficiently manages leads, tracks progress, automates workflows, and provides insightful analytics. The system should consider the key stages of the business development process, essential features needed, data models, and integrations required.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert CRM system architect with deep knowledge in business development processes and best practices for managing leads and tracking progress.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
The response should be organized into the following sections:

1. Key Stages and Requirements: Outline the key stages of the business development process and the requirements for each stage.

2. Data Model: Describe the entities, attributes, and relationships required for the CRM system.

3. Essential Features: List and describe the essential features needed for the CRM system.

4. Workflows and Automations: Outline the workflows and automations that should be implemented to streamline processes.

5. Integrations: Identify the necessary integrations with other systems and their purposes.

6. Analytics and Reporting: Describe the reports, KPIs, insights, and real-time metrics that should be provided by the CRM system.

7. Security and Compliance: List the security measures and compliance standards that must be met by the CRM system.

8. Scalability and Performance: Discuss the scalability considerations and performance optimizations required for the CRM system.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on providing a comprehensive and detailed design for the CRM system, considering all aspects of the business development process.
2. Ensure that the essential features, workflows, and automations are practical and aligned with best practices for managing leads and tracking progress.
3. Avoid discussing implementation details or specific technologies; focus on the high-level design and requirements.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
● My business development process: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT PROCESS]
● My current CRM pain points: [LIST YOUR CURRENT CRM PAIN POINTS]
● My CRM integration requirements: [SPECIFY YOUR CRM INTEGRATION REQUIREMENTS]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The response should be formatted using markdown, with clear headings for each section and bullet points for lists.  or any other formatting that may hinder readability.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for creating a business analytics system. Focuses on tracking and analyzing key performance indicators (KPIs). Ensures the output remains under 2000 characters.
