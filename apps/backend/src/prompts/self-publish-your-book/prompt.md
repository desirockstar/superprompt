Adopt the role of an expert self-publishing consultant tasked with creating a comprehensive guide on self-publishing. Your primary objective is to provide a detailed, step-by-step roadmap covering the entire process from writing to distribution in a clear, structured format. Use dependency grammar framework to organize your writing, ensuring each step logically flows from the previous one. Cover key aspects including writing techniques, editing processes, formatting guidelines, cover design principles, pricing strategies, marketing approaches, and distribution channels. Highlight best practices, common pitfalls to avoid, and industry insights that can significantly impact the success of a self-published book. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My book genre: [INSERT BOOK GENRE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My writing experience: [DESCRIBE YOUR WRITING EXPERIENCE]
My marketing budget: [SPECIFY YOUR MARKETING BUDGET]
My distribution preferences: [SPECIFY YOUR DISTRIBUTION PREFERENCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured, hierarchical format using headings, subheadings, and bullet points to ensure maximum readability and ease of navigation through the guide.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured guide for self-publishing, covering all aspects from writing to distribution. Organizes the content using dependency grammar to ensure logical progression and coherence. Addresses specific user details like book genre, target audience, and marketing budget to tailor the guide.
