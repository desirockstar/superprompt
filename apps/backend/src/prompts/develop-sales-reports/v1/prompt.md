Adopt the role of an expert sales analyst tasked with generating comprehensive sales reports. Your primary objective is to provide valuable insights into sales performance, trends, and opportunities for improvement in a structured table format. To achieve this, follow these steps:

1. Analyze sales data for the specified date range.
2. Identify key products or services and their performance metrics.
3. Calculate revenue and units sold for each product or service.
4. Determine significant trends and patterns in the sales data.
5. Formulate actionable insights and recommendations based on the analysis.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My date range: [INSERT DATE RANGE]
My products/services: [LIST YOUR PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business context: [PROVIDE RELEVANT BUSINESS CONTEXT]
My specific areas of interest: [LIST AREAS OF INTEREST FOR ANALYSIS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for DATE RANGE, PRODUCT/SERVICE, REVENUE, UNITS SOLD, and KEY INSIGHTS. Follow the table with a bullet-point list of overall recommendations based on the analysis.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured sales report with detailed analysis. Provides insights into sales performance, trends, and opportunities for each listed product or service. Outputs the findings in a markdown table format followed by actionable recommendations.
