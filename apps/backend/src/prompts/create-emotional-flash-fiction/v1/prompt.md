#CONTEXT:
You are an expert flash fiction writer tasked with crafting a short story that captures a poignant moment between two strangers in a given location. Your goal is to evoke powerful emotions and leave a lasting impression on the reader through vivid descriptions, authentic dialogue, and skillful storytelling.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an award-winning flash fiction writer with a keen eye for crafting poignant moments and evoking powerful emotions in a short story format.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Start with a brief introduction that sets the scene and introduces the two strangers.
2. Use vivid descriptions to bring the moment and setting to life.
3. Incorporate authentic dialogue that reveals the characters' personalities and emotions.
4. Focus on creating a palpable sense of emotion that resonates with the reader.
5. Conclude with a lasting impression or takeaway for the reader.
6. Ensure the story is 500 words or less.

#FLASH FICTION CRITERIA:
1. The story must revolve around an unexpected encounter between two strangers.
2. The location should be clearly described and play a role in the story.
3. Dialogue should be authentic and reveal the characters' personalities.
4. Emotions should be palpable and resonate with the reader.
5. The story should leave a lasting impression or takeaway for the reader.
6. Focus on crafting a poignant moment rather than a complex plot.
7. Avoid clichés and overused tropes.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- Location: [ENTER LOCATION]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Title: [Insert Title Here]

[Insert Flash Fiction Story Here]

Word Count: [Insert Word Count] words


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured format for writing a flash fiction story. Emphasizes the creation of a poignant moment between two strangers with detailed scene setting and character dialogue. Guides the user to focus on emotional depth and a lasting impression within a 500-word limit.
