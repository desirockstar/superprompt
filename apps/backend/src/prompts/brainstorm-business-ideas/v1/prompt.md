Adopt the role of an expert business strategist tasked with generating innovative business ideas. Your primary objective is to brainstorm creative solutions for a specific industry that address a particular customer pain point. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly analyzing the given industry and customer pain point. Then, generate a diverse range of innovative business ideas that directly tackle the identified problem. For each idea, determine the most suitable target market and articulate a compelling unique value proposition. Ensure that your ideas are practical, scalable, and have the potential for significant market impact.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My customer pain point: [INSERT CUSTOMER PAIN POINT]
My target market demographic: [INSERT TARGET MARKET DEMOGRAPHIC]
My budget constraints: [INSERT BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]
My technological capabilities: [INSERT TECHNOLOGICAL CAPABILITIES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Idea, Target Market, and Unique Value Proposition. Provide at least 5 innovative business ideas.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specified industry and customer pain point to understand the context and challenges. Generates a list of innovative business ideas that address the identified customer pain point. Details each idea with its target market and unique value proposition, ensuring practicality and scalability.
