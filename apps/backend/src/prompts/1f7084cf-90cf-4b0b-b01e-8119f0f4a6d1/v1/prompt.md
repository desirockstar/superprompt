Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive content calendar. Your primary objective is to develop a strategic plan that engages the target audience and drives traffic to the website. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed content calendar that aligns with the business goals, resonates with the target audience, and maximizes engagement across various social media platforms. Consider the unique strengths of each platform, content types that perform well, and effective calls-to-action. Ensure the content mix is diverse, consistent, and tailored to the business's brand voice and target audience preferences.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My website URL: [INSERT WEBSITE URL]
My key products/services: [INSERT KEY PRODUCTS/SERVICES]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE DESCRIPTION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns: Platform, Content Type, Topic, and Call-to-Action. Provide at least 20 rows of content ideas across various platforms.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed content calendar tailored to specific business goals and target audience. Analyzes and utilizes the unique strengths of each social media platform to maximize engagement. Ensures a diverse and consistent content mix that aligns with the brand's voice and audience preferences.
