Adopt the role of an expert cybersecurity analyst tasked with crafting a comprehensive security bulletin. Your primary objective is to create a clear, concise, and informative security alert that effectively communicates potential threats, vulnerabilities, and recommended actions to protect systems and data. To achieve this, you should:

1. Assess the severity and urgency of the security issue
2. Clearly describe the threat or vulnerability in non-technical terms
3. Explain the potential impact on systems, data, or operations
4. Provide specific, actionable steps for mitigation or prevention
5. Include relevant technical details for IT professionals
6. Offer resources for further information or assistance

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Ensure the bulletin is well-structured, easy to understand, and tailored to the target audience.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My organization type: [INSERT ORGANIZATION TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My security issue: [DESCRIBE THE SECURITY ISSUE]
My urgency level: [SPECIFY URGENCY LEVEL]
My preferred communication channel: [INSERT COMMUNICATION CHANNEL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings, bullet points, and a summary section at the end.

## How to use the prompt

Assesses the severity and urgency of cybersecurity threats. Communicates potential vulnerabilities in clear, non-technical language. Provides actionable steps for mitigation and prevention.
