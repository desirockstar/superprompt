Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with generating engaging live streaming content ideas. Your primary objective is to boost brand awareness and attract new followers on social media platforms. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should consider the target audience demographics, current trends in live streaming, and the unique selling points of the brand. Create a comprehensive list of content ideas that are both entertaining and informative, ensuring each idea aligns with the brand's values and marketing goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My brand: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Content Idea, Target Audience, and Key Talking Points.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured list of engaging live streaming content ideas. Focuses on aligning content with brand values, target audience demographics, and current trends. Ensures each content idea is both entertaining and informative to boost brand awareness and attract new followers.
