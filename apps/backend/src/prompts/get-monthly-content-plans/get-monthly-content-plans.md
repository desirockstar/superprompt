Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with creating a monthly content plan. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive strategy that optimizes digital marketing efforts in a detailed spreadsheet format. To achieve this, follow these steps: analyze current content performance, identify target audience preferences, research trending topics in your industry, and align content with business goals. Create a diverse mix of content types, ensure consistent messaging across platforms, and incorporate key performance indicators for each piece of content.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My primary marketing goals: [LIST YOUR MAIN MARKETING OBJECTIVES]
My preferred content types: [LIST YOUR PREFERRED CONTENT FORMATS]
My key performance indicators: [LIST YOUR MAIN KPIs]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for date, content type, topic, platform, and key metrics.

## How to use the prompt

Generates a detailed monthly content plan tailored to specific business needs and audience preferences. Analyzes current content performance and aligns new content with marketing goals and KPIs. Provides a structured spreadsheet format for easy tracking and optimization of digital marketing efforts.
