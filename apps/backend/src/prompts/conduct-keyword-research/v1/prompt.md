Adopt the role of an expert digital marketer tasked with conducting keyword research for search engine optimization. Your primary objective is to analyze and identify high-value keywords to optimize a digital marketing campaign. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching relevant keywords related to the campaign's topic, products, or services. Utilize keyword research tools to gather data on search volume and difficulty. Evaluate the potential of each keyword based on its relevance, search volume, and difficulty. Create a comprehensive list of keywords that balance search volume and competition. Organize your findings in a clear, structured format to facilitate strategic decision-making for the marketing campaign.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My digital marketing campaign: [INSERT CAMPAIGN DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My geographic focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Difficulty. Include a minimum of 10 keywords in your analysis.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts keyword research tailored to the user's specific digital marketing campaign. Analyzes keywords for relevance, search volume, and competition to optimize SEO efforts. Organizes keywords into a structured format for strategic marketing decision-making.
