#CONTEXT:
As an experienced Etsy seller, you are stepping into the shoes of a customer service expert who specializes in e-commerce platforms, particularly Etsy. Your task is to create a comprehensive list of Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) for an Etsy shop that sells [product]. These FAQs should address common queries and concerns that customers might have about the product itself, shipping processes, return policies, handling custom orders, and any other relevant topics that would apply to your shop's operations and offerings.

#GOAL:
You will craft clear, concise, and customer-friendly FAQs that not only provide valuable information to potential buyers but also help to build trust and confidence in your Etsy shop and its products. The ultimate aim is to ensure that these FAQs address the most common customer concerns in a way that encourages them to make a purchase.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the step-by-step approach below to create your list of FAQs:

Product Information:

Detail the unique features of your product, materials used, and any customization options available. Include any care instructions or usage tips to enhance customer satisfaction.
Ordering Process:

Explain how customers can place an order, customize their product (if applicable), and what they should do if they encounter any issues during the ordering process.
Shipping:

Provide information on shipping options, estimated delivery times, tracking order status, and handling charges or free shipping conditions, if any.
Returns and Exchanges:

Clearly outline your return policy, including conditions under which returns or exchanges are accepted, the process for initiating a return, and any restocking fees or shipping costs that the customer may be responsible for.
Custom Orders:

If you offer custom orders, describe how customers can request them, the process for approval of custom designs, expected production times, and any additional costs involved.
Feedback and Reviews:

Encourage customers to leave feedback and explain how you address any negative reviews or concerns. Mention any customer satisfaction guarantees or follow-up services you offer.
Safety and Compliance:

For relevant products, include information about compliance with safety standards, certifications, and any warnings or age restrictions.
Promotions and Discounts:

Detail any ongoing promotions, how customers can find out about future sales, and if you offer discounts on bulk purchases or to repeat customers.
Contact Information:

Provide clear instructions on how customers can contact you for further inquiries, the typical response time, and the preferred channels of communication (e.g., Etsy messaging, email).

#OUTPUT:
The FAQs should be structured as a list of questions and answers, covering all the relevant aspects mentioned above. Each FAQ should be concise and to the point, aiming for clarity and helpfulness. The tone should be friendly and reassuring, designed to address customer concerns proactively and encourage them to feel confident in making a purchase from your shop.

## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured list of FAQs for an Etsy shop, addressing product details, ordering, and shipping. Outlines return policies, custom order processes, and customer feedback mechanisms. Includes safety, compliance, promotions, and contact information to enhance customer trust and satisfaction.
