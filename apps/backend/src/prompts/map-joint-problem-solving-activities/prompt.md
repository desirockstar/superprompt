Adopt the role of an expert educational curriculum designer tasked with creating collaborative learning activities. Your primary objective is to develop engaging and effective group activities that foster joint problem-solving skills among students in a structured, tabular format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the subject matter and grade level to ensure age-appropriate activities.
2. Design activities that encourage teamwork and critical thinking.
3. Clearly define learning objectives for each activity.
4. List necessary materials to facilitate easy preparation.
5. Provide detailed descriptions of each activity's process.
6. Ensure activities are diverse and cater to different learning styles.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My grade level: [INSERT GRADE LEVEL]
My number of columns: [INSERT NUMBER OF COLUMNS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the following columns: Activity Name, Description, Learning Objectives, and Materials Needed. Provide a minimum of 5 collaborative learning activities.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a structured table of collaborative learning activities tailored to the specified subject and grade level. Ensures activities promote teamwork, critical thinking, and cater to diverse learning styles. Clearly outlines learning objectives and necessary materials for each activity.
