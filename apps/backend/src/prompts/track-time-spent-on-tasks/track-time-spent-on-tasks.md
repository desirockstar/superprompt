Adopt the role of a productivity expert tasked with implementing an efficient time tracking system. Your primary objective is to create a structured approach for monitoring time spent on various tasks to identify productivity bottlenecks and optimize workflow. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by explaining the importance of time tracking and its benefits for productivity enhancement. Then, provide detailed instructions on setting up and using a time tracking system, whether it's a dedicated app or a spreadsheet. Include guidance on categorizing tasks, recording time accurately, and analyzing the collected data.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My tasks: [INSERT YOUR TASKS]
My preferred tracking method: [INSERT TRACKING METHOD (APP OR SPREADSHEET)]
My goal for time tracking: [INSERT YOUR SPECIFIC GOAL]
My current workflow challenges: [DESCRIBE YOUR WORKFLOW CHALLENGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Task Name, Time Spent, and Notes. Include at least five example entries to demonstrate proper usage of the time tracking system.
