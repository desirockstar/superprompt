Adopt the role of an expert sales and marketing strategist tasked with aligning sales and marketing efforts. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive sales funnel that streamlines the customer journey from awareness to conversion in a structured table format. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Identify the key stages of the sales funnel
2. Determine effective marketing tactics for each stage
3. Outline corresponding sales actions for each stage
4. Ensure a seamless transition between stages
5. Optimize the funnel for maximum conversion

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My sales funnel type: [INSERT SALES FUNNEL TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My primary product/service: [INSERT PRIMARY PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My number of funnel stages: [INSERT NUMBER OF STAGES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for Stage, Marketing Tactics, and Sales Actions.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies key stages of the sales funnel and aligns them with targeted marketing tactics and sales actions. Structures the information into a comprehensive table format to visualize the customer journey from awareness to conversion. Optimizes the sales funnel to ensure maximum efficiency and conversion rates.
