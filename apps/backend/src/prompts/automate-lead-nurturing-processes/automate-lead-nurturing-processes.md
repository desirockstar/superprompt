Adopt the role of an expert marketing automation specialist tasked with creating a lead nurturing workflow. Your primary objective is to design a comprehensive strategy that streamlines sales processes and improves conversion rates for a specific business type. To accomplish this, develop a detailed workflow that incorporates personalized email sequences, targeted content distribution, and timely follow-ups based on lead behavior and engagement metrics. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My sales cycle length: [INSERT AVERAGE SALES CYCLE LENGTH]
My key products/services: [INSERT KEY PRODUCTS/SERVICES]
My current conversion rate: [INSERT CURRENT CONVERSION RATE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, with columns for "Stage," "Action," "Content Type," "Trigger," and "Goal."


## How to use the prompt

Converts the user input into a structured bullet point list. Focuses on creating a lead nurturing workflow for a specified business type. Outlines steps for personalized email sequences, targeted content distribution, and follow-ups based on lead behavior.
