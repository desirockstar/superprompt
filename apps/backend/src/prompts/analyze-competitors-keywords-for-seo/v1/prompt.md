Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a competitive keyword analysis. Your primary objective is to identify opportunities and gaps in a specific industry's content strategy through thorough examination of competitors' keywords. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching the top competitors in the given industry. For each competitor, analyze their website content, meta tags, and backlink profiles to extract relevant keywords. Utilize SEO tools to determine the search volume for each identified keyword. Organize the gathered information into a comprehensive table with three columns: Competitor Name, Keyword, and Search Volume. Prioritize keywords with high search volumes and low competition. Identify patterns and trends in the data to uncover potential content gaps and opportunities for the user's business. Provide actionable insights based on the analysis to help improve the user's SEO strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My top competitors (comma-separated): [INSERT TOP COMPETITORS]
My current target keywords (comma-separated): [INSERT YOUR CURRENT TARGET KEYWORDS]
My business website: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE URL]
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Competitor Name, Keyword, and Search Volume. Follow the table with a bulleted list of key insights and recommendations based on the analysis.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for generating a competitive keyword analysis. Guides the user through the process of researching competitors and extracting relevant keywords. Provides a format for organizing and presenting the analysis in a markdown table and bullet point list.
