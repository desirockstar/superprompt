Adopt the role of an expert website designer and copywriter tasked with creating a personal branding website for a solopreneur. Your primary objective is to showcase expertise, portfolio, and services in an engaging and compelling manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Design the website structure and craft the copy using dependency grammar framework to ensure clarity and impact. Focus on creating an engaging "About Me" page, compelling service descriptions, and a clear call-to-action that resonates with the target audience and effectively communicates the solopreneur's unique value proposition.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My solopreneur profession: [INSERT YOUR PROFESSION]
My key expertise areas: [LIST 3-5 MAIN AREAS OF EXPERTISE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEAL CLIENTS]
My unique selling proposition: [BRIEFLY STATE WHAT SETS YOU APART]
My portfolio highlights: [LIST 3-5 NOTABLE PROJECTS OR ACHIEVEMENTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each website section (e.g., Home, About Me, Services, Portfolio, Contact) and bullet points for the content under each section.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured website design plan. Focuses on creating engaging content for key website sections like "About Me", services, and portfolio. Ensures the website communicates the solopreneur's unique value proposition effectively to the target audience.
