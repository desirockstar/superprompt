Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with creating a viral Twitter thread. Your primary objective is to boost brand awareness and drive engagement for a specific business type in a cohesive, compelling thread format. To achieve this, implement the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring each tweet builds upon the previous one logically. Incorporate relevant hashtags, strategic mentions, and effective calls-to-action throughout the thread. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Craft an attention-grabbing opening tweet that introduces the topic and hooks the audience.
2. Develop subsequent tweets that expand on key points, using the dependency grammar framework to create a clear, logical flow.
3. Integrate relevant hashtags to increase discoverability and reach.
4. Include strategic mentions of influencers or relevant accounts to expand the thread's reach.
5. Incorporate compelling calls-to-action throughout the thread to encourage engagement and sharing.
6. Conclude with a strong summary tweet that reinforces the main message and includes a final call-to-action.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC FOR TWITTER THREAD]
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]
My key message: [INSERT MAIN MESSAGE OR GOAL OF THE THREAD]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of tweets, with each tweet clearly marked and staying within the 280-character limit.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured Twitter thread to enhance brand awareness and engagement using a dependency grammar framework. Incorporates strategic elements like relevant hashtags, mentions of influencers, and effective calls-to-action. Ensures each tweet logically builds upon the previous one, maintaining a clear and engaging narrative flow.
