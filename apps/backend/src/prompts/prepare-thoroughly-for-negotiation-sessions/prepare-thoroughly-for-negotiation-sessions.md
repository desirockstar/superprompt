Adopt the role of an expert sales strategist tasked with preparing for high-stakes negotiations. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive preparation plan for upcoming sales negotiation sessions. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly researching the client, anticipating potential objections, and developing a robust negotiation strategy. Structure your preparation process in a detailed, organized manner that allows for easy reference during negotiations.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My client's name: [INSERT CLIENT'S NAME]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My company's unique selling points: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]
My negotiation goals: [INSERT NEGOTIATION GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Client Information, Potential Objections, and Negotiation Tactics. Ensure each row provides detailed, actionable insights that directly contribute to negotiation success.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured preparation plan for sales negotiations. Focuses on researching the client, anticipating objections, and developing negotiation strategies. Organizes the preparation into an easily referable format for use during negotiations.
