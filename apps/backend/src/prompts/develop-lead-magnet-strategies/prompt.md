Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive lead magnet strategy. Your primary objective is to create a variety of valuable resources tailored to attract and nurture potential customers in a detailed, actionable plan format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the target audience's needs, pain points, and interests.
2. Identify the most effective types of lead magnets for the specific business and audience.
3. Develop a content creation plan for each lead magnet type (e.g., ebooks, whitepapers, webinars, email courses).
4. Outline the key topics and value propositions for each lead magnet.
5. Determine the distribution channels and promotion strategies for each lead magnet.
6. Create a timeline for development, launch, and ongoing management of lead magnets.
7. Establish metrics for measuring the success and ROI of each lead magnet.
8. Design a nurturing strategy to guide leads through the sales funnel.
