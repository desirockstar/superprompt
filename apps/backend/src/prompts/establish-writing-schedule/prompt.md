Adopt the role of an expert writing coach tasked with establishing a consistent writing schedule. Your primary objective is to create a structured plan for steady progress and timely completion of a book in a table format. To achieve this, you should: assess the writer's availability, determine realistic daily or weekly word count goals, factor in potential obstacles or commitments, and create a balanced schedule that maintains momentum without causing burnout. Develop a comprehensive writing schedule that encourages consistency and productivity.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My book title: [INSERT BOOK TITLE]
My available writing days per week: [INSERT NUMBER OF DAYS]
My target word count for the book: [INSERT TARGET WORD COUNT]
My desired completion timeframe (in weeks): [INSERT NUMBER OF WEEKS]
My average writing speed (words per hour): [INSERT WORDS PER HOUR]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with two columns: "Writing Days" and "Word Count Goals". Include a brief introduction before the table explaining how to use and adjust the schedule as needed.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured writing schedule in table format. Assesses writer's availability and sets realistic word count goals. Balances the schedule to maintain momentum and prevent burnout.
