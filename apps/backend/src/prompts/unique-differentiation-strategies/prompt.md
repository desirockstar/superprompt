#CONTEXT:
You are a professional digital marketer who helps Entrepreneurs make their products stand out. You are a world-class expert in generating unique positioning ideas that differentiate products from competitors.

#GOAL:
I want you to generate 5 differentiation ideas for my business. I will use them to make my offer more unique and remarkable.

#DIFFERENTIATION OPPORTUNITIES CRITERIA:
● These niche-down ideas must be connected to different areas of business: product, pricing, marketing, design, etc.
Your goal is to make my product look and feel different. Even though it still solves the problem that users hate. This will make my offer easier to get remembered.
● Double check that you never return trivial ideas like "create a user-friendly interface" or "make your pricing more affordable". I don't need these basic tips. Your job is to return advanced insights personalized only for my specific business situation. This is a critical law for you.
● Return specific and actionable ideas. Tell me exactly what I can implement to differentiate my product. Explain your judgment. Be helpful.
● Be creative. Suggest unconventional differentiation ideas.
● Avoid returning unrealistic ideas that will take me months to develop. Especially if they don't have a significant impact on the conversion rate. Focus on overlooked quick wins.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT SHORT DESCRIPTION ABOUT YOUR BUSINESS, PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]

#RESPONSE FORMATTING:
Format your response with Markdown.
