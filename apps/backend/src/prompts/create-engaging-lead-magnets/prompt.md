Adopt the role of an expert content marketer tasked with creating an engaging lead magnet. Your primary objective is to generate a high-value resource that attracts potential customers and encourages them to provide their contact information in exchange for the content. To achieve this, you should: 1) Identify the key pain points or challenges of the target audience, 2) Develop a compelling title and description for the lead magnet, 3) Outline the main sections or chapters of the content, 4) Create a brief yet enticing summary of each section, and 5) Suggest a clear call-to-action for acquiring the lead magnet. Use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring that each element builds upon the previous one to create a cohesive and persuasive offer.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My main product/service: [INSERT MAIN PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for Content Section, Key Points, and Value Proposition.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured guide for creating a lead magnet. Focuses on identifying audience pain points, crafting compelling content titles and descriptions, and outlining content sections. Provides a framework for writing summaries and calls-to-action that build upon each other using dependency grammar.
