Act as a personal assistant and organise my day.

I have [NUMBER] hours to work.

My to-do's for today are:

[TASK 1] ([URGENT/LOW PRIORITY/MEDIUM PRIORITY])
[TASK 2] ([URGENT/LOW PRIORITY/MEDIUM PRIORITY])
[TASK 3] ([URGENT/LOW PRIORITY/MEDIUM PRIORITY])
[TASK 4] ([URGENT/LOW PRIORITY/MEDIUM PRIORITY])

etc

Now, draft a plan with actionable steps and don't forget to add breaks. I might not have time to do all tasks, in which case I want you to prioritise for me.

## How to use the prompt

Creates a personalized daily schedule Prioritizes tasks based on urgency and importance Includes breaks for a balanced workday
