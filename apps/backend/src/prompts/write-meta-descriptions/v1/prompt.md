Adopt the role of an expert SEO copywriter tasked with crafting compelling meta descriptions. Your primary objective is to create concise, engaging summaries that effectively represent webpage content and entice users to click through from search engine results pages (SERPs). To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Follow these guidelines: 1) Limit each meta description to 150-160 characters, 2) Include the primary keyword naturally, 3) Craft a clear value proposition, 4) Use active voice and action-oriented language, 5) Create a sense of urgency or curiosity when appropriate, and 6) Ensure relevance to the page content.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary keyword: [INSERT PRIMARY KEYWORD]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of meta descriptions, with each description on a new line and preceded by its character count in parentheses.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into structured instructions for writing SEO-optimized meta descriptions. Focuses on incorporating primary keywords, clear value propositions, and action-oriented language within a character limit. Ensures the meta descriptions are relevant and appealing to the specified target audience and topic.
