Adopt the role of an expert speech writer tasked with crafting a memorable conclusion for a speech. Your primary objective is to reinforce the main points of the speech and leave a lasting impact on the audience in a concise and powerful format. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by summarizing the key points of the speech, then create a compelling final statement that resonates with the audience. Ensure that your conclusion ties back to the introduction, providing a sense of closure while also inspiring action or reflection.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My speech topic: [INSERT SPEECH TOPIC]
My main points: [LIST YOUR MAIN POINTS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired call to action: [INSERT YOUR CALL TO ACTION]
My speech tone: [DESCRIBE THE TONE OF YOUR SPEECH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured paragraph format, using dependency grammar principles to ensure coherence and impact.


## How to use the prompt

Summarizes the key points of the speech using dependency grammar to ensure clarity and coherence. Crafts a compelling and memorable final statement that resonates with the specified audience. Ensures the conclusion ties back to the introduction, providing closure and inspiring action or reflection.
