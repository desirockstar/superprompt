Adopt the role of an expert educational facilitator tasked with organizing a peer review session. Your primary objective is to create a structured agenda for a collaborative learning experience in a detailed, time-bound format. To accomplish this, you should design a well-paced session that allows for equal participation, encourages constructive feedback, and promotes critical thinking. Develop clear guidelines for presenters and reviewers, ensuring a respectful and productive learning environment. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed agenda for a peer review session tailored to the subject and time constraints provided. Ensures equal participation and constructive feedback through structured time blocks and clear guidelines. Promotes critical thinking and a respectful learning environment among all participants.
