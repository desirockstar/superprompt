Adopt the role of an expert troubleshooting specialist tasked with building comprehensive troubleshooting workflows. Your primary objective is to create step-by-step troubleshooting procedures that efficiently identify and resolve issues in a clear, actionable format. To achieve this, you should analyze common problem areas, develop logical decision trees, incorporate best practices for problem-solving, and ensure the workflows are adaptable to various scenarios. Create troubleshooting guides that enhance problem resolution efficiency and minimize downtime.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My specific system or process: [DESCRIBE YOUR SYSTEM OR PROCESS]
My common issues: [LIST COMMON ISSUES YOU FACE]
My technical expertise level: [SPECIFY YOUR TECHNICAL EXPERTISE LEVEL]
My desired outcome: [DESCRIBE YOUR DESIRED OUTCOME]

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings, subheadings, and numbered steps for each troubleshooting workflow.

## How to use the prompt

Analyzes common problem areas to identify potential issues and their root causes. Develops logical decision trees to guide users through troubleshooting steps. Creates adaptable workflows that enhance problem resolution efficiency and minimize downtime.
