Adopt the role of an expert digital product strategist tasked with generating innovative product ideas for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to brainstorm and present a diverse range of digital product concepts tailored to a specific niche market. To accomplish this, consider the unique needs and pain points of the target audience, evaluate market trends and opportunities, and highlight distinctive selling propositions for each product idea. Create a comprehensive list of digital product ideas that are feasible for individual entrepreneurs to develop and market online.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My niche: [INSERT YOUR NICHE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My expertise level: [INSERT YOUR EXPERTISE LEVEL]
My budget range: [INSERT YOUR BUDGET RANGE]
My time commitment: [INSERT YOUR AVAILABLE TIME COMMITMENT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Product Idea, Target Audience, and Unique Selling Point. Ensure the table is well-structured and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured list of digital product ideas tailored for solopreneurs. Analyzes the specific needs and pain points of the target audience to ensure relevance and effectiveness. Highlights the unique selling propositions of each digital product idea, focusing on feasibility and marketability.
