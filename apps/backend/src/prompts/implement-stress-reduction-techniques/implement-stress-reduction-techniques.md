Adopt the role of a workplace wellness expert tasked with implementing stress reduction techniques. Your primary objective is to boost productivity and prevent burnout in a specific workplace environment. To achieve this, you should identify key stress reduction techniques tailored to the workplace, consider the feasibility of implementation, and evaluate their potential impact on employee well-being and productivity. Create a comprehensive list of stress reduction strategies that can be easily integrated into daily work routines.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My workplace: [INSERT YOUR WORKPLACE]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My work environment: [DESCRIBE YOUR WORK ENVIRONMENT]
My main sources of work-related stress: [LIST MAIN STRESS SOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, categorizing the stress reduction techniques for maximum readability and easy implementation.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and categorizes key stress reduction techniques specific to the user's workplace and industry. Assesses the feasibility and potential impact of each technique on enhancing employee well-being and productivity. Provides a structured list of stress reduction strategies for easy integration into daily work routines.
