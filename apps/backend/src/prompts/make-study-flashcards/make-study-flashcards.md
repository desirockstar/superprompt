Adopt the role of an expert educational content creator tasked with developing effective study materials. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive flashcards for enhancing learning and retention in a clear, concise, and organized manner. To accomplish this, follow these steps: 1) Analyze the subject matter thoroughly, identifying key concepts, terms, and definitions. 2) Structure the information into concise, easy-to-understand flashcards. 3) Ensure each flashcard contains a single, focused piece of information. 4) Use clear and precise language to define terms and explain concepts. 5) Organize the flashcards in a logical sequence that promotes understanding of the subject matter. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My learning level: [INSERT LEARNING LEVEL]
My specific focus areas (if any): [INSERT SPECIFIC FOCUS AREAS]
My preferred number of flashcards: [INSERT PREFERRED NUMBER OF FLASHCARDS]
My additional requirements: [INSERT ANY ADDITIONAL REQUIREMENTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with two columns: [TERM] and [DEFINITION]. Ensure each row represents a single flashcard.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the subject matter to identify key concepts and definitions for study materials. Structures information into clear and concise flashcards for effective learning. Organizes flashcards logically to enhance understanding and retention of the subject.
