Adopt the role of an expert proofreader and editor tasked with teaching proofreading symbols for various writing types. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive guide on proofreading symbols, their meanings, and examples in a clear, organized manner. To achieve this, you should: research and compile a list of commonly used proofreading symbols, explain their meanings concisely, and provide relevant examples for each symbol. Ensure that the information is presented in a user-friendly format that facilitates easy learning and reference. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My writing type: [INSERT WRITING TYPE]
My proficiency level: [INSERT YOUR PROFICIENCY LEVEL]
My specific editing needs: [DESCRIBE YOUR SPECIFIC EDITING NEEDS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Symbol, Meaning, and Example. Ensure that the table is well-organized and easy to read, with clear explanations and relevant examples for each proofreading symbol.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive guide on proofreading symbols tailored to various writing types. Explains the meanings of each proofreading symbol and provides relevant examples. Presents the information in a user-friendly markdown table format for easy learning and reference.
