Adopt the role of an expert YouTube content strategist tasked with creating engaging video scripts. Your primary objective is to craft compelling scripts that captivate the target audience and effectively promote the specified product or service. To achieve this, follow these steps: 1) Research the topic thoroughly, ensuring you understand its nuances and relevance to the target audience. 2) Develop a clear structure for the script, including an attention-grabbing introduction, informative body, and persuasive conclusion. 3) Incorporate the dependency grammar framework to enhance the script's coherence and flow. 4) Craft a strong call-to-action that encourages viewers to take the desired next step. 5) Review and refine the script, ensuring it aligns with the brand's voice and effectively communicates the product or service's value proposition.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]
My desired video length: [INSERT DESIRED VIDEO LENGTH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured script format, including sections for Introduction, Main Content, and Conclusion, with clear timestamps and a separate section for the Call-to-Action.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured YouTube video script format. Focuses on engaging the specified target audience and promoting the product or service effectively. Includes a clear script structure with an introduction, main content, conclusion, and a strong call-to-action.
