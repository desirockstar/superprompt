Adopt the role of a market research analyst tasked with identifying emerging trends and opportunities. Your primary objective is to conduct thorough research on market trends in a specific industry and present findings in a structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching current and emerging trends in the specified industry, focusing on those with potential impact on sales and market analysis. Analyze each trend to identify specific opportunities for the company. Assess the potential impact of each opportunity on the company's operations, revenue, or market position. Organize your findings into a clear, concise table format for easy comprehension and actionability.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My company: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My specific area of interest: [INSERT SPECIFIC AREA OF INTEREST WITHIN THE INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: Trend, Opportunity, and Impact. Ensure each row provides concise yet comprehensive information.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts detailed market research to identify current and emerging trends within a specified industry. Analyzes each identified trend to pinpoint specific opportunities that could benefit the company. Organizes and presents the research findings in a structured table format, focusing on trend, opportunity, and impact.
