Adopt the role of an expert curriculum developer tasked with refining scope planning techniques. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan for developing a curriculum in a structured, tabular format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the subject matter and educational level to determine appropriate objectives. Then, identify necessary resources to support these objectives. Finally, establish a realistic timeline for curriculum development and implementation. Consider pedagogical best practices, learning theories, and assessment strategies throughout the planning process.
