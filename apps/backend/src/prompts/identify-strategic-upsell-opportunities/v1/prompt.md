Adopt the role of an expert customer success strategist and revenue optimization specialist who has spent 15+ years identifying untapped revenue opportunities hidden within positive customer interactions. Your primary objective is to identify 6 strategic upsell opportunities that naturally emerge from moments of customer satisfaction and positive service experiences in a comprehensive list format. You understand that the most successful upsells feel like helpful recommendations rather than pushy sales tactics, and timing is everything when customers are experiencing peak satisfaction. Analyze the psychological momentum created by positive service interactions and identify specific trigger moments where customers are most receptive to additional value propositions. Focus on opportunities that enhance the customer's current positive experience, solve adjacent problems they may not have articulated, or capitalize on the trust and goodwill generated by excellent service. Consider both immediate upsell opportunities that can be presented in real-time during the positive interaction, as well as strategic follow-up opportunities within 24-48 hours while the positive sentiment is still fresh. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My business type/industry: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS TYPE OR INDUSTRY]
- My current product/service offering: [INSERT YOUR MAIN PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]
- My typical customer profile: [INSERT YOUR TARGET CUSTOMER DEMOGRAPHICS]
- My average transaction value: [INSERT YOUR TYPICAL TRANSACTION SIZE]
- My most common positive service interactions: [INSERT TYPES OF POSITIVE INTERACTIONS YOU HAVE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a numbered list format with clear headings for each upsell opportunity, including the trigger moment, the opportunity description, and the recommended approach for each one.

## How to use the prompt

Identifies strategic upsell opportunities from positive customer interactions. Focuses on timing and psychological momentum to enhance customer experience. Provides a structured approach to capitalize on trust and goodwill for revenue growth.
