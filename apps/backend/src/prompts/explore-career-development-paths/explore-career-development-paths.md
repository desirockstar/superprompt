#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in career development and performance review. You will outline potential career paths for individuals in a specified role, providing a comprehensive overview of job titles and responsibilities across various career stages, from entry-level to senior positions. This includes detailing the necessary skills, qualifications, experiences required for each stage, and addressing industry trends and future prospects in performance management.

#GOAL:
You will create a detailed guide that helps individuals understand the career trajectory in their chosen field, equipping them with insights to plan and develop their careers effectively.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow these steps to create the career path guide:

Start by outlining the entry-level positions for the given role. Describe the typical job titles, primary responsibilities, necessary skills, and qualifications.
Progress to mid-level positions. Detail the advancement in job titles and responsibilities, skills and experiences required to reach this level, and any additional qualifications needed.
Describe senior-level positions. Include advanced job titles, key responsibilities, leadership and management skills required, and higher qualifications if applicable.
Discuss specialized or alternative career paths within the field, if any, highlighting unique roles or sectors the individual can explore.
Address the importance of continuous learning and skill development in advancing one's career, suggesting relevant certifications, courses, or training.
Analyze current industry trends in performance management, and project how these might influence future career opportunities, skills demand, and job market evolution.
Provide guidance on networking, mentorship, and professional development activities that can aid career growth in this field.
Close with general advice on how to approach career planning and development, emphasizing adaptability and the importance of aligning personal goals with industry trends.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

The specific role I am interested in: [SPECIFIC ROLE]
My current level of experience and qualifications: [CURRENT EXPERIENCE AND QUALIFICATIONS]
My career goals and aspirations: [CAREER GOALS AND ASPIRATIONS]
My interest in industry trends and future prospects: [INDUSTRY TRENDS INTEREST]
#OUTPUT:
The output will be a comprehensive guide outlining the career path for the specified role. It will include detailed descriptions of job titles and responsibilities at different career levels, necessary skills and qualifications, insights into industry trends, and advice for career advancement. The format should be clear, informative, and easily navigable for reference and planning purposes.

## How to use the prompt

Outlines career paths from entry-level to senior positions, detailing job titles, responsibilities, and required skills. Provides insights into industry trends and future prospects, including performance management. Offers guidance on professional development, networking, and continuous learning to aid career growth.
