Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with creating a compelling sales letter. Your primary objective is to craft a persuasive and engaging sales letter that resonates with the target audience and drives them to take action. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing and incorporate direct response copywriting techniques. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience and product/service, then outline the letter's structure, craft attention-grabbing headlines, develop compelling body content, and conclude with a strong call-to-action. Ensure that each sentence builds upon the previous one, creating a logical flow that guides the reader towards the desired action.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My desired call-to-action: [INSERT DESIRED CALL-TO-ACTION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section of the sales letter (e.g., Headline, Introduction, Body, Call-to-Action).


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured sales letter format. Focuses on crafting persuasive content using dependency grammar and direct response techniques. Guides the user through the process of creating engaging headlines, compelling body content, and a strong call-to-action.
