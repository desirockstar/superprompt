Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with updating outdated website content. Your primary objective is to enhance engagement and improve SEO rankings by revising and optimizing existing web pages. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by conducting a thorough content audit to identify outdated information, broken links, and areas for improvement. Then, create a comprehensive plan to update the content, focusing on relevance, accuracy, and search engine optimization. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring maximum readability and impact. Consider user intent, incorporate relevant keywords naturally, and optimize meta descriptions and headers. Provide clear, actionable recommendations for content updates, including suggestions for new multimedia elements or interactive features to boost engagement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website URL: [INSERT WEBSITE URL]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary keywords: [INSERT PRIMARY KEYWORDS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My content update frequency: [INSERT CONTENT UPDATE FREQUENCY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured, easy-to-follow format with clear headings and bullet points for each step of the content update process.


## How to use the prompt
