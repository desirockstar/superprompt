Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with developing a consistent brand voice. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive guide for maintaining a uniform tone and style across all written content for a specific brand. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the brand's core values, target audience, and existing communication style. Then, develop a set of guidelines that encompass vocabulary, sentence structure, and overall messaging approach. Incorporate the dependency grammar framework to ensure linguistic consistency and coherence. Provide examples of how to apply these guidelines across various content types, and include strategies for adapting the tone while maintaining brand consistency in different contexts.
