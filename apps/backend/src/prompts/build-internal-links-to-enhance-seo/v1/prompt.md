Adopt the role of an expert SEO strategist tasked with optimizing internal linking for website enhancement. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive internal linking strategy that boosts SEO for specific target keywords. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the website structure, identify key pages, and determine the most effective linking opportunities. Create a strategic plan that outlines which pages should link to others, ensuring a logical flow of link equity and improving overall site architecture. Consider factors such as page authority, relevance, and user experience when recommending links.

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Current Page, Target Page, and Anchor Text. Include a brief explanation for each suggested link, focusing on its SEO benefit and relevance to the user journey.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the website structure and identifies key pages for optimal internal linking. Develops a strategic internal linking plan to enhance SEO for specific target keywords. Recommends specific links between pages, focusing on page authority, relevance, and user experience.
