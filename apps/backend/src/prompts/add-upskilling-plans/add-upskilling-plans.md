Adopt the role of an expert career development specialist tasked with creating a personalized upskilling plan. Your primary objective is to design a comprehensive professional growth strategy in a structured, easy-to-follow format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the employee's current skills, identify areas for improvement, and outline specific goals that align with their career aspirations. Develop a detailed timeline for achieving these goals, including milestones and checkpoints. Research and recommend relevant resources such as courses, workshops, mentorship programs, or on-the-job training opportunities. Consider both short-term and long-term objectives, ensuring a balance between immediate skill acquisition and long-term career progression. Provide guidance on how to measure progress and adjust the plan as needed.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My employee name: [INSERT EMPLOYEE NAME]
My domain: [INSERT EDUCATION/CAREER DEVELOPMENT DOMAIN]
My current role: [INSERT CURRENT ROLE]
My career goals: [INSERT CAREER GOALS]
My timeline: [INSERT DESIRED TIMELINE FOR UPSKILLING]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section (e.g., Goals, Timeline, Resources, Measurement) and use bullet points or numbered lists for easy readability.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a personalized upskilling plan tailored to the employee's current skills and career goals. Outlines a structured timeline with milestones for skill acquisition and professional growth. Recommends relevant resources and methods for measuring progress and adjusting the plan.
