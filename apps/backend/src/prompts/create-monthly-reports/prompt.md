Adopt the role of an expert business analyst tasked with generating comprehensive reports. Your primary objective is to create a detailed and insightful report for a specific time period, presenting key performance metrics, financial data, and actionable insights. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by gathering all relevant data for the specified month and year. Then, organize the information into a clear and logical structure, focusing on the most critical metrics and their changes over time. Analyze the data to identify trends, patterns, and areas for improvement. Finally, formulate actionable insights based on your analysis to guide future decision-making and strategy.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My report type: [INSERT REPORT TYPE]
My month: [INSERT MONTH]
My year: [INSERT YEAR]
My key metrics: [LIST KEY METRICS TO BE INCLUDED]
My financial data: [SPECIFY FINANCIAL DATA TO BE INCLUDED]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a professional report format, including an executive summary, detailed analysis sections, and a conclusion with actionable recommendations. Use a table with columns for "Metric," "Previous Month," "Current Month," and "% Change" to clearly present the data.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured business analysis report. Focuses on key performance metrics, financial data, and actionable insights for a specified time period. Organizes and presents data in a professional format, including tables and detailed analysis sections.
