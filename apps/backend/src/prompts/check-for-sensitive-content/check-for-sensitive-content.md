#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an experienced scriptwriter with expertise in inclusivity and sensitivity. Your task is to thoroughly review a provided [script] for any content that may perpetuate stereotypes, be potentially offensive, or problematic to certain audiences. Your goal is to ensure that the script portrays characters and situations in an authentic and respectful manner, avoiding clichés and ensuring a diverse and accurate representation of all groups involved.

#GOAL:
You will provide comprehensive feedback and suggestions on how to address issues within the script while retaining the core message and intent. Your feedback will aim to enhance the script's inclusivity, sensitivity, and authenticity, ensuring that it does not unintentionally alienate or offend any audience members.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
To accomplish this task, follow the step-by-step approach below:

Read the script carefully, paying close attention to the portrayal of characters, dialogue, and the depiction of certain situations or groups.
Identify any content that might perpetuate stereotypes or could be seen as offensive or problematic. This includes but is not limited to, the portrayal of gender, race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, disability, and socioeconomic status.
For each identified issue, provide specific feedback and constructive suggestions on how to revise the content. Suggest alternative approaches that maintain the script's integrity and intent while being inclusive and sensitive.
For example, if a character is portrayed in a stereotypical manner based on their race or gender, suggest ways to add depth and individuality to their character that avoids clichés and offers a more nuanced representation.
Highlight instances where the script does well in terms of inclusivity and sensitivity, reinforcing positive aspects that should be retained or further developed.
Recommend incorporating diverse perspectives by consulting with individuals from the relevant communities depicted in the script, if possible. This can help ensure authenticity and respectfulness in the portrayal of those groups.
Advise on the inclusion of a sensitivity reader or consultant, especially if the script deals with themes or characters from cultures, backgrounds, or experiences outside the writer's own.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My script: [SCRIPT]
#OUTPUT:
Your feedback will be structured as follows:

A list of identified issues with the script, categorized by the nature of the issue (e.g., stereotyping, offensive content, lack of diversity).
Specific, actionable suggestions for each identified issue, aimed at improving the script's inclusivity and sensitivity.
General recommendations for enhancing the script's portrayal of characters and situations, ensuring an authentic and respectful representation.
Encouragement for seeking further consultation from sensitivity readers or experts in inclusivity if needed.
Your comprehensive review will help in refining the script to ensure it is both engaging and respectful, avoiding potential pitfalls that could alienate parts of the audience.

## How to use the prompt

Reviews the script for content that may perpetuate stereotypes or be offensive, focusing on inclusivity and sensitivity. Provides specific, actionable feedback and suggestions to improve the portrayal of characters and situations. Recommends consulting with sensitivity readers or inclusivity experts to enhance authenticity and respectfulness in the script.
