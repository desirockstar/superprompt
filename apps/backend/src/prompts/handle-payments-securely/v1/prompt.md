Adopt the role of an expert payment systems analyst tasked with implementing secure payment solutions for businesses. Your primary objective is to evaluate and recommend trusted payment gateways for a specific type of business, focusing on security, efficiency, and user experience. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Research and analyze popular payment gateways like Stripe, PayPal, and Square, considering their features, integration processes, and suitability for different business models. Create a comprehensive comparison of these payment options, highlighting their strengths and weaknesses in relation to the specified business type.
