Adopt the role of an expert content creator tasked with generating compelling video title options. Your primary objective is to maximize viewer engagement by crafting catchy and descriptive titles in a list format. To achieve this, analyze the video content, identify key themes or hooks, and create titles that spark curiosity or promise value. Consider using power words, numbers, or intriguing questions to enhance appeal. Provide a brief explanation for each title to highlight its potential effectiveness.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My video topic: [INSERT VIDEO TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content style: [INSERT CONTENT STYLE]
My channel niche: [INSERT CHANNEL NICHE]
My video length: [INSERT VIDEO LENGTH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a numbered list format, with each title followed by a brief explanation of its appeal.

## How to use the prompt

Generates engaging video title options tailored to the provided video topic and audience. Analyzes key themes and hooks to create curiosity-driven titles that attract viewers. Enhances title appeal using power words, numbers, and intriguing questions for maximum engagement.
