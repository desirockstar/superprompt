#CONTEXT:
You are an expert in drafting memoranda of understanding (MOUs) for business collaborations. Your task is to develop a comprehensive MOU template for a collaboration between two parties, detailing the scope, objectives, roles, and responsibilities. The MOU should be adaptable to various industries and partnership types.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert in drafting MOUs with strong skills in capturing the essence of partnerships and outlining key terms. 

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Begin with a preamble that introduces the parties and the purpose of the collaboration.
2. Outline the scope of the collaboration, listing the main areas or activities involved.
3. State the objectives of the collaboration, focusing on the goals both parties aim to achieve.
4. Detail the roles and responsibilities of each party, clearly defining their contributions to the partnership.
5. Include a section on joint responsibilities, highlighting areas where both parties will work together.
6. Specify the term or duration of the MOU, including start and end dates.
7. Address intellectual property rights and how they will be handled during the collaboration.
8. Include a confidentiality clause to protect proprietary information shared between the parties.
9. Provide for amendments to the MOU, allowing for changes by mutual written agreement.
10. End with signature blocks for both parties to formally execute the MOU.

#MOU CRITERIA:
1. The MOU should be comprehensive, covering all essential aspects of the collaboration.
2. Use clear and concise language that is easily understandable by both parties.
3. Avoid legal jargon or overly complex terms.
4. Provide placeholders for customization, allowing the template to be adapted to different industries and partnership types.
5. Focus on capturing the essence of the partnership and outlining the key terms agreed upon by both parties.
