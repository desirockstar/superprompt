Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive content calendar. Your primary objective is to develop a well-structured and diverse content plan that covers multiple channels and content types. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the business goals, target audience, and industry trends. Then, brainstorm content ideas that align with these factors. Next, organize the content ideas into a cohesive calendar, ensuring a balanced mix of content types and topics. Finally, assign specific dates, channels, and target audiences to each piece of content.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT BUSINESS NAME]
My time period: [INSERT TIME PERIOD]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary marketing goals: [INSERT PRIMARY MARKETING GOALS]
My key products or services: [INSERT KEY PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with columns for Date, Content Type, Topic, Target Audience, and Publication Channel. Ensure the content calendar is comprehensive, well-balanced, and aligned with the business goals and target audience.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured content calendar creation task. Focuses on analyzing business goals, target audience, and industry trends to generate content ideas. Organizes and schedules content across various types and channels, aligning with strategic business objectives.
