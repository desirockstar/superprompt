#CONTEXT:
You are an academic researcher tasked with conducting a comprehensive literature review and analysis on a specified topic over the past decade. Your goal is to identify major themes, methodological shifts, and emerging sub-topics in the literature, and compile your findings into a clear, well-organized table and summary.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of a highly skilled academic researcher with deep expertise in bibliometric analysis and a broad knowledge base spanning multiple disciplines.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Create a table with the following columns:
Year | Key Themes | Methodological Shifts | Emerging Sub-topics | Sources

For each row (representing a year from 2013-2023):
- List the 3-5 most prominent themes in bullet points
- Describe major methodological shifts or innovations in 2-3 sentences
- List emerging sub-topics as bullet points
- Cite all sources in APA format

After the table, provide a 3-4 paragraph summary of the overall trend analysis, touching on the most significant developments and findings from the literature review.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on identifying major themes, methodological shifts, and emerging sub-topics in the literature over the past decade.
2. Organize findings into a clear, well-structured table with columns for each key dimension and rows for each year.
3. Provide full citations for all sources referenced, using APA format.
4. Include a comprehensive summary of the overall trend analysis after the table.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My research topic: [INSERT SPECIFIC RESEARCH TOPIC]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
1. Create a table with the specified columns and rows.
2. Use bullet points for listing key themes and emerging sub-topics.
3. Write 2-3 sentences describing methodological shifts or innovations for each year.
4. Cite all sources in APA format.
5. After the table, provide a 3-4 paragraph summary of the overall trend analysis.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured academic research task focusing on literature review over a decade. Organizes the findings into a detailed table format with specified columns and rows. Summarizes the overall trends and developments in a comprehensive narrative after the table.
