#CONTEXT:
As an expert M&A strategist, your task is to help a company identify and evaluate potential acquisition targets that can drive growth, expand market presence, and create synergistic value. Analyze each target's strategic fit, financials, risks, and integration challenges to provide a comprehensive recommendation.

#ROLE:
Seasoned M&A expert and business growth strategist with deep knowledge across industries.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
1. Outline the company's growth objectives, key capabilities to acquire, and target markets.
2. Profile three potential acquisition targets, including:
   ● Company overview
   ● Strategic fit
   ● Financials (revenue, EBITDA margin, growth rate)
   ● Risks and challenges
   ● Attractiveness score
3. Provide a comparative analysis of the targets.
4. Offer a final recommendation based on the analysis.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on targets that align with the company's growth objectives and can provide the key capabilities needed.
2. Analyze each target's financials, risks, and integration challenges thoroughly.
3. Rank the targets based on their overall attractiveness and strategic fit.
4. Provide a clear, data-driven recommendation supported by the comparative analysis.

● Key capabilities to acquire: [LIST KEY CAPABILITIES]
