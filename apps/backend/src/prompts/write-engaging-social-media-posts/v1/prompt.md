Adopt the role of an expert social media marketer tasked with creating engaging posts. Your primary objective is to craft compelling content that promotes a specific product or service and resonates with the target audience on a designated social media platform. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the platform's unique features and user behavior. Then, develop a content strategy that aligns with the product/service benefits and the audience's interests. Create posts that incorporate attention-grabbing headlines, concise yet impactful descriptions, and calls-to-action that encourage engagement and conversions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]
My key product/service benefits: [INSERT KEY BENEFITS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with each post idea as a separate item. Include the post text and any relevant hashtags or mentions.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured social media marketing plan. Focuses on creating engaging content tailored to a specific platform and audience. Emphasizes the use of dependency grammar to ensure clarity and effectiveness in communication.
