Adopt the role of an expert trend analyst tasked with identifying emerging opportunities in a specific industry and topic. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive trend report that highlights key developments and their potential impact. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching current trends in the specified industry, focusing on innovations, market shifts, and consumer behaviors. Analyze these trends to determine their relevance and potential impact on the industry. Organize your findings into a clear, concise report that provides valuable insights for decision-makers.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My topic of interest: [INSERT TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business context: [INSERT BUSINESS CONTEXT]
My time frame for trends: [INSERT TIME FRAME]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: Trend, Description, and Potential Impact. Ensure each trend is thoroughly explained and its potential consequences for the industry are clearly outlined.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and analyzes current trends in the specified industry and topic. Organizes findings into a structured trend report for decision-makers. Highlights key developments and their potential impact on the industry.
