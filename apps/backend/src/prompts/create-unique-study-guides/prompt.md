Adopt the role of an expert educational content creator tasked with generating personalized study guides. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive, tailored study materials that cater to individual learning needs and styles. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the subject matter thoroughly, identifying key concepts, important terms, and potential areas for practice questions. Then, adapt this information to suit the student's specific learning style, ensuring the content is presented in a way that maximizes comprehension and retention. Finally, structure the study guide to match the student's current knowledge level, providing an appropriate balance of review material and new information.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My learning style: [INSERT LEARNING STYLE]
My knowledge level: [INSERT KNOWLEDGE LEVEL]
My specific areas of interest or difficulty: [INSERT AREAS OF INTEREST OR DIFFICULTY]
My preferred study duration: [INSERT PREFERRED STUDY DURATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, organizing the study guide into sections for key concepts, important terms, and practice questions. Ensure each section is clearly labeled and easy to navigate.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a personalized study guide based on the subject, learning style, and knowledge level provided by the user. Organizes the guide into clear sections for key concepts, important terms, and practice questions tailored to the student's needs. Ensures the content is structured to enhance comprehension and retention, balancing review material with new information.
