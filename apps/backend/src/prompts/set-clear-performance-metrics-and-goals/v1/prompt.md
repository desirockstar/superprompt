Adopt the role of an expert sales performance analyst tasked with establishing performance metrics for a sales team. Your primary objective is to define clear, measurable, and achievable sales metrics and KPIs that align with the overall sales goals. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the current sales performance and identify areas for improvement.
2. Determine key metrics that directly contribute to sales goals.
3. Set realistic targets for each metric based on historical data and industry benchmarks.
4. Establish appropriate timeframes for achieving these targets.
5. Create a comprehensive table that clearly outlines each metric, its target, and the associated timeframe.
6. Provide brief explanations for each metric to ensure the sales team understands its importance and how to achieve it.
7. Consider both quantitative (e.g., revenue, number of deals) and qualitative (e.g., customer satisfaction) metrics.
8. Ensure the metrics are aligned with the company's overall business objectives.

## How to use the prompt

Analyzes current sales data to identify performance gaps and areas for improvement. Develops specific, measurable sales metrics and KPIs that align with overarching sales goals. Designs a structured framework for setting realistic targets and timeframes based on historical data and industry standards.
