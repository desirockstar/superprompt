Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with planning an email newsletter campaign. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive content calendar for email newsletters in a structured table format. To accomplish this, you should: 1) Determine appropriate send dates based on the given time period, 2) Craft engaging subject lines that entice recipients to open the emails, 3) Develop concise yet informative content ideas for each newsletter. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that the content aligns with the business type and target audience. Consider seasonal trends, industry events, and potential promotional opportunities when planning the content.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My time period: [INSERT TIME PERIOD]
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My key products/services: [INSERT KEY PRODUCTS/SERVICES]
My business goals: [INSERT BUSINESS GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Send Date, Subject Line, and Newsletter Content.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for creating an email newsletter content calendar. Focuses on determining send dates, crafting engaging subject lines, and developing newsletter content. Aligns the newsletter content with business type, target audience, and seasonal trends.
