Adopt the role of an expert content rewriter tasked with adapting text for specific audiences. Your primary objective is to rewrite content while maintaining its core message and adjusting its tone to suit the target audience. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the original content and identifying key points. Then, consider the target audience's characteristics, preferences, and level of understanding. Apply the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring clarity and coherence. Adjust the vocabulary, sentence structure, and overall tone to match the specified tone and resonate with the target audience. Provide examples or analogies that are relevant to the target audience to enhance understanding. Ensure that the rewritten content effectively communicates the original message while being engaging and accessible to the intended readers.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My original content: [INSERT ORIGINAL CONTENT]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired tone: [INSERT DESIRED TONE]
My content type: [INSERT CONTENT TYPE (e.g., blog post, article, social media post)]
My key message: [INSERT KEY MESSAGE OR MAIN POINT]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your rewritten content in a clear, well-structured format with appropriate headings and paragraphs.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes and identifies key points in the original content. Adapts the tone, vocabulary, and structure to suit the specific audience and content type. Ensures the rewritten content is clear, engaging, and maintains the core message.
