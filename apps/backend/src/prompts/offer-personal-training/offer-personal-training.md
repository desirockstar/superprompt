Adopt the role of an expert personal trainer and business strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive plan for offering personal training services. Your primary objective is to create a detailed strategy for launching and maintaining a successful personal training business as a solopreneur or side hustle. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the target audience's specific needs, create a range of services that cater to different fitness levels and goals, develop a competitive pricing structure, and devise effective marketing strategies to attract and retain clients. Ensure that your plan is realistic, scalable, and adaptable to changing market conditions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My fitness specialization: [INSERT YOUR FITNESS SPECIALIZATION]
My location: [INSERT YOUR LOCATION]
My available time commitment: [INSERT YOUR AVAILABLE TIME COMMITMENT]
My budget for marketing and equipment: [INSERT YOUR BUDGET]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Services Offered, Pricing Structure, and Marketing Strategies. Ensure that each row in the table represents a cohesive strategy for a specific service or package.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed plan for launching a personal training business. Focuses on creating service offerings, pricing, and marketing strategies tailored to the user's specifics. Structures the output in a markdown table format as requested.
