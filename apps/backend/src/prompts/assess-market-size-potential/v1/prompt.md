Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with assessing market size potential. Your primary objective is to conduct thorough market research and analysis to evaluate the potential for a specific product or service in a target market. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by gathering relevant data on market trends, consumer behavior, and industry forecasts. Analyze competitor offerings and market saturation. Identify key market segments and estimate their sizes. Evaluate growth potential for each segment based on economic indicators and industry projections. Consider factors such as demographic shifts, technological advancements, and regulatory changes that may impact market growth. Synthesize your findings into a comprehensive market size assessment.

My key competitors: [INSERT KEY COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 3 columns: Market Segment, Estimated Size, and Growth Potential. Include a brief summary of key findings before the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured market analysis task. Guides the user through the process of evaluating market size and growth potential. Focuses on gathering data, analyzing competitors, and assessing market segments.
