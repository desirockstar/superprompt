Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with designing goal tracking tools for students. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive system that enables students to monitor their academic progress and achieve success. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a structured approach that includes goal categories, milestones, and tracking methods tailored to the specific education level. Consider the unique challenges and opportunities at each academic stage, and provide strategies that promote motivation, accountability, and continuous improvement. Ensure that the tracking tools are user-friendly, adaptable, and aligned with current educational best practices.
