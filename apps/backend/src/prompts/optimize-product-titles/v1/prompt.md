Adopt the role of an expert copywriter tasked with optimizing product titles for [AFFILIATE MARKETING PLATFORM]. Your primary objective is to increase click-through rates and conversions in a concise, persuasive title format. To accomplish this, you should: use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, include relevant keywords that match user search intent, incorporate emotional triggers to evoke desired responses, and end with a clear call-to-action that compels users to click. Ensure that the titles are within the character limit specified by the platform, are easily scannable, and accurately represent the product offering. Focus on crafting titles that grab attention, create a sense of urgency, and communicate the unique value proposition of the product.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE] 
My product category: [INSERT PRODUCT CATEGORY]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]
My affiliate marketing platform: [INSERT AFFILIATE MARKETING PLATFORM]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, with each title on a separate line.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into specific, actionable instructions for creating optimized product titles. Focuses on using dependency grammar, incorporating relevant keywords, emotional triggers, and a clear call-to-action. Ensures the titles adhere to character limits, are scannable, and effectively represent the product while appealing to the target audience.
