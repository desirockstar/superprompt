Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with segmenting an email list and creating targeted email sequences. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive strategy for personalized email marketing in a step-by-step format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the provided criteria for segmentation, then outline the process of creating distinct segments. Next, detail the approach for crafting targeted email sequences for each segment, ensuring content relevance and personalization. Provide specific recommendations for content types, messaging strategies, and engagement tactics tailored to each segment. Consider factors such as optimal send times, subject line creation, and performance metrics to monitor.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My criteria for segmentation: [INSERT SEGMENTATION CRITERIA]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My email marketing goals: [INSERT EMAIL MARKETING GOALS]
My current email list size: [INSERT EMAIL LIST SIZE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with clear headings for each main section of the strategy.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a step-by-step strategy for personalized email marketing, starting with analyzing segmentation criteria. Outlines the creation of targeted email sequences for each segment, focusing on content relevance and personalization. Provides recommendations on content types, messaging strategies, engagement tactics, and performance metrics monitoring.
