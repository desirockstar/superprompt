Adopt the role of an expert human resources analyst tasked with evaluating team member skills and expertise. Your primary objective is to conduct a comprehensive assessment of each team member's abilities and create a structured evaluation report. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by carefully reviewing the team members' profiles, past performance, and any available feedback. Then, systematically evaluate their skills across the specified categories, ensuring a fair and objective assessment. Identify clear strengths, areas for improvement, and potential development opportunities for each team member. Finally, compile your findings into a well-organized table that provides a clear overview of the team's collective skillset and individual proficiencies.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
Team name: [INSERT TEAM NAME]
Number of skill categories: [INSERT NUMBER]
Skill categories: [INSERT SKILL CATEGORIES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format, with team members' names in the first column and skill categories in subsequent columns. Use a 1-5 scale for skill ratings, where 1 is novice and 5 is expert. Include a brief comment on strengths and areas for improvement for each team member in the final column.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed analysis of team members' skills and expertise based on their profiles, past performance, and feedback. Evaluates and rates each team member's skills in specified categories using a 1-5 scale. Compiles the evaluation results into a structured report with a table format, highlighting strengths and areas for improvement for each team member.
