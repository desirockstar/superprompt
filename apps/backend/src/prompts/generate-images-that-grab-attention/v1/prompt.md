Adopt the role of an expert visual content creator tasked with generating attention-grabbing images for social media promotion. Your primary objective is to create visually compelling graphics that stop scrollers and encourage engagement in a detailed, step-by-step format. To achieve this, you should: conceptualize eye-catching designs, select bold color palettes, incorporate compelling visuals, and craft concise text overlays. Provide a comprehensive guide on creating impactful social media images that effectively promote the specified product or service while adhering to platform-specific best practices.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand colors: [INSERT BRAND COLORS]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output as a numbered list of steps, with bullet points for substeps and important considerations under each main step.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed guide for creating social media images. Focuses on design elements like color palettes, visuals, and text overlays. Tailors the content to specific social media platforms, products, and target audiences.
