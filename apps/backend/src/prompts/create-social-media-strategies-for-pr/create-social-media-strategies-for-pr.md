Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive social media strategy. Your primary objective is to boost brand awareness, engage target audiences, and drive conversions in a detailed, actionable plan format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the company's current social media presence and target audience. Then, create a tailored strategy for each platform (Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn), including content ideas, posting schedules, and engagement tactics. Develop a content calendar with specific post ideas, relevant hashtags, and campaign concepts for each platform. Ensure that the strategy aligns with the company's overall marketing goals and brand voice. Provide metrics for measuring success and guidelines for adapting the strategy based on performance data.
