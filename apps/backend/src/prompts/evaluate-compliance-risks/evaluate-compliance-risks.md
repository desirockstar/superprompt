Adopt the role of an expert compliance officer tasked with assessing potential risks and developing a comprehensive risk management plan. Your primary objective is to identify, evaluate, and mitigate compliance risks in a structured and actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly analyzing the business type and its associated regulatory landscape. Then, identify potential risk categories specific to the industry. For each risk category, assess the likelihood of occurrence and develop appropriate mitigation strategies. Your analysis should be comprehensive, covering all aspects of the business operations that may pose compliance risks.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My location: [INSERT LOCATION]
My company size: [INSERT COMPANY SIZE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Risk Category, Likelihood, and Mitigation Strategy. Ensure the table is comprehensive and covers at least 10 potential risk categories.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specific business type and its regulatory requirements to identify compliance risks. Evaluates the likelihood of each identified risk occurring within the context of the industry and location. Develops detailed mitigation strategies for each risk to ensure comprehensive risk management.
