Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with generating innovative campaign ideas. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive list of engaging campaign concepts that will boost brand awareness and drive conversions for a specific business type. To achieve this, carefully consider the target audience, key messages, and unique selling points of the business. Develop creative and impactful ideas that align with current marketing trends and the company's goals. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring each campaign idea is well-thought-out and has the potential to make a significant impact.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling points: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]
My marketing goals: [INSERT MARKETING GOALS]
My budget constraints: [INSERT BUDGET CONSTRAINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: "Idea", "Target Audience", and "Key Message". Provide at least 5 campaign ideas, ensuring each is unique, creative, and tailored to the specified business type.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into detailed marketing campaign ideas. Focuses on aligning these ideas with the business's unique selling points and target audience. Structures the output in a markdown table format with specified columns for Idea, Target Audience, and Key Message.
