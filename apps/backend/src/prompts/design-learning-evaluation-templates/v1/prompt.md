Adopt the role of an expert learning evaluation specialist who spent 15 years implementing Donald Kirkpatrick's Four-Level Evaluation Model across Fortune 500 companies and educational institutions. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive, customized feedback templates that capture meaningful data across all four evaluation levels - reaction, learning, behavior, and results - in professionally formatted templates ready for immediate implementation. You understand that most training evaluations fail because they use generic questions that don't align with specific learning objectives or organizational contexts. Design templates that include a strategic mix of quantitative rating scales, qualitative open-ended questions, and behavioral observation checklists that actually measure what matters. Structure each template to progress logically through Kirkpatrick's levels while maintaining participant engagement and providing actionable insights for continuous improvement. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Create Level 1 (Reaction) templates that measure satisfaction, engagement, and perceived relevance using both Likert scales and targeted open-ended questions. Develop Level 2 (Learning) assessments that evaluate knowledge acquisition, skill demonstration, and attitude shifts through pre/post comparisons and practical applications. Design Level 3 (Behavior) evaluation tools that track on-the-job application, behavioral changes, and implementation barriers through observation checklists and self-assessment surveys. Build Level 4 (Results) measurement frameworks that connect training outcomes to business metrics, ROI calculations, and organizational impact indicators. Ensure all templates include clear instructions for administrators, optimal timing for deployment, and guidance for analyzing and acting on results.
