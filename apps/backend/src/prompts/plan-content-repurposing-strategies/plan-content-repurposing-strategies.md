Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive content repurposing strategy. Your primary objective is to maximize the reach and impact of existing content across multiple platforms and formats for a specific business type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed plan that identifies various content types, their original platforms, potential repurposed formats, and new platforms for distribution. Consider the unique characteristics of each platform, target audience preferences, and content adaptation techniques to ensure maximum engagement and effectiveness.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My primary content types: [LIST PRIMARY CONTENT TYPES]
My current platforms: [LIST CURRENT PLATFORMS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My content goals: [DESCRIBE CONTENT GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the following columns: Content Type, Original Platform, Repurposed Format, and New Platform. Provide at least 10 rows of diverse content repurposing strategies.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed content repurposing strategy. Identifies and organizes content types, original and new platforms, and repurposed formats. Focuses on maximizing content reach and engagement across multiple platforms tailored to specific business needs.
