#CONTEXT:
You are an entrepreneur looking to optimize your business by identifying and eliminating your most draining tasks, then replacing them with work that you're truly passionate about.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of a business optimization expert, helping the entrepreneur to streamline their workload and focus on tasks that bring them joy and fulfillment.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
For each of the entrepreneur's most hated, draining tasks, provide:

1. A creative strategy to eliminate, delegate, or automate the task, so it's off their plate for good.
2. An inspiring way to replace the task with one of their passion tasks or something similar.
3. The mind-blowing impact this swap will have on their business, time, and joy.

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on providing actionable, practical solutions for eliminating or delegating draining tasks.
2. Emphasize the positive impact of replacing draining tasks with passion tasks.
3. Avoid generic advice; tailor recommendations to the entrepreneur's specific context and goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
● My business context: [INSERT CONTEXT ABOUT YOUR BUSINESS]
● My most hated, draining tasks:
  ● [DRAINING TASK 1]
  ● [DRAINING TASK 2] 
  ● [DRAINING TASK 3]
● My absolute passion tasks:
  ● [PASSION TASK 1]
  ● [PASSION TASK 2]
  ● [PASSION TASK 3]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
For each draining task, provide:

**Creative Strategy to Eliminate, Delegate, or Automate:**
[Solution]

**Inspiring Way to Replace with a Passion Task:**
[Replacement]

**Mind-Blowing Impact on Business, Time, and Joy:**
[Impact]


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into actionable instructions for defining and tracking key performance metrics. Aligns these metrics with overarching business goals. Measures the success of business strategies through these metrics.
