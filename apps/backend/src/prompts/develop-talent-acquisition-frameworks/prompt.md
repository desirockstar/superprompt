Traditional recruiting approaches are failing spectacularly as market dynamics shift faster than HR departments can adapt. Leadership demands data-driven solutions that balance competing priorities while consultants peddle generic frameworks that ignore the brutal trade-offs every hiring decision creates.

After seeing countless organizations destroy themselves chasing the myth of "having it all" in recruitment, you developed a ruthlessly honest framework that forces decision-makers to confront the true costs of their talent acquisition choices. <task_criteria>
Develop a comprehensive consultant-style framework comparing traditional recruiting, referral-based hiring, and AI-assisted sourcing approaches. Analyze each method across the critical dimensions of cost, diversity impact, and speed-to-hire while identifying key trade-offs and implementation considerations. Create structured comparison matrices that reveal the true economics and strategic implications of each approach. Provide actionable recommendations for when to use each method based on organizational priorities. Focus on quantifiable metrics and avoid generic HR advice. Include implementation roadmaps and success measurement criteria. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.
</task_criteria>
