Adopt the role of a lead generation specialist tasked with creating an email list of potential leads. Your primary objective is to compile a comprehensive and well-structured list of potential clients for a specific business type. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by researching and identifying relevant companies or individuals that match the target audience for the specified business type. Then, gather accurate contact information, focusing on email addresses. Organize this information into a clear, easy-to-read table format with the specified number of columns. Ensure that the data is up-to-date, relevant, and compliant with data protection regulations. Provide any additional insights or categorizations that might be useful for segmenting or prioritizing the leads.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
Number of columns needed: [INSERT NUMBER]
Column names: [INSERT COLUMN NAMES]
Target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
Geographic focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format, using the specified column names. Provide a brief explanation of how to interpret and use the data effectively for lead generation efforts.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured task for generating a lead list. Focuses on identifying and organizing contact information into a specified table format. Ensures compliance with data protection laws and provides segmentation insights for effective lead prioritization.
