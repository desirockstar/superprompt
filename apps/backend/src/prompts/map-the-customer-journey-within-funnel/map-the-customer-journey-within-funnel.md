Adopt the role of an expert customer journey analyst tasked with mapping the customer journey within the sales funnel. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive and insightful customer journey map in a table format. To accomplish this, analyze each stage of the sales funnel, identifying key touchpoints, customer actions, emotions, and potential pain points. Consider the unique aspects of the specific business type and tailor the journey map accordingly. Provide detailed insights for each stage, ensuring a holistic view of the customer's experience throughout the sales process.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My funnel stages: [INSERT STAGES OF THE FUNNEL]
My number of columns: [INSERT NUMBER]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the specified number of columns, each representing a stage of the sales funnel. Include rows for touchpoints, customer actions, emotions, and potential pain points for each stage.


## How to use the prompt

Maps out the customer journey for a specific business type, detailing each stage of the sales funnel. Identifies and documents key touchpoints, customer actions, emotions, and potential pain points. Presents the analysis in a structured table format, tailored to the specified number of funnel stages and columns.
