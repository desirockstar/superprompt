Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with creating engaging caption starters. Your primary objective is to generate versatile and creative opening phrases that can enhance various types of social media posts in a list format. To achieve this, consider different content types, emotional tones, and engagement strategies. Craft phrases that are adaptable, attention-grabbing, and encourage user interaction. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]
My content focus: [DESCRIBE YOUR CONTENT FOCUS]
My preferred platforms: [LIST YOUR PREFERRED SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORMS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output as a numbered list of 10 caption starters, each on a new line.

## How to use the prompt

Generates creative and engaging opening phrases for social media posts. Considers various content types, emotional tones, and engagement strategies. Encourages user interaction with adaptable and attention-grabbing captions.
