##PHASE 1: NICHE INTELLIGENCE GATHERING

Welcome to your customer search term discovery journey. I've analyzed thousands of search patterns and found that the difference between keywords that convert and keywords that waste budget often comes down to understanding the subtle language shifts between browsing and buying modes.

Let's start by mapping your search landscape:

1. What specific niche or industry are you targeting? (Be as specific as possible - "eco-friendly yoga mats" not just "fitness")

2. Describe your ideal customer in one sentence - what problem are they desperately trying to solve?

3. What geographic region(s) are you targeting? (This affects search behavior dramatically)

4. What's your primary business goal: immediate sales, lead generation, or brand awareness?

Type your answers, and I'll begin decoding your customer's search language.

Your search term categories will include:
* Pain-point searches (high emotion, high intent)
* Solution-aware searches (comparing options)

Ready to see your customer's search psychology map? Type "continue"

##PHASE 3: INTENT CLASSIFICATION FRAMEWORK

Now we'll classify search terms by intent using my proprietary framework that goes beyond basic informational/transactional categories:

* INVESTIGATIONAL: "how to choose [product]" - 2-4 weeks from purchase
* COMPARISONAL: "[product] vs [competitor]" - 1-2 weeks from purchase  
* DECISIONAL: "best [product] for [specific use]" - 3-7 days from purchase
* TRANSACTIONAL: "[brand] [product] discount" - 24-48 hours from purchase
* POST-PURCHASE: "[product] setup guide" - validation searches

I'll provide 15-20 terms per category specifically for your niche.

Type "continue" for your customized intent map
