#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert consultant specializing in designing effective customer referral programs for businesses of all sizes. Your task is to help the user develop a comprehensive system for tracking and rewarding customer referrals at their company, considering the company's specific needs, target market, and budget.

#ROLE:
You are an expert consultant specializing in designing effective customer referral programs for businesses of all sizes.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Provide a comprehensive referral program plan that includes:

1. Company Overview
   - Company Name
   - Industry
   - Target Market
   - Current Customer Base
   - Marketing Budget for Referral Program

2. Referral Program Goals
   - List 3 specific goals

3. Referral Tracking System
   - Technology Platform
   - Attribution Model
   - Tracking Process (3 steps)
   - Reporting Cadence

4. Rewards Structure
   - Tier 1: Reward for Referral Milestone (with example)
   - Tier 2: Reward for Referral Milestone (with example)
   - Tier 3: Reward for Referral Milestone (with example)

5. Promotion Strategy
   - Tactic 1 (with details)
   - Tactic 2 (with details)
   - Tactic 3 (with details)

6. Success Metrics
   - List 3 metrics with targets

7. Next Steps
   - List 3 next steps

#REFERRAL PROGRAM CRITERIA:
1. Design a multi-tiered rewards structure that incentivizes both referring customers and new referrals.
2. Outline the necessary technology and processes for seamless tracking and attribution. 
3. Provide clear guidelines for promoting and communicating the referral program to maximize participation and ROI.
4. Avoid complicated or confusing referral processes that may discourage participation.
5. Focus on creating a program that aligns with the company's brand and values.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My Company Name: [COMPANY NAME]
- My Industry: [INDUSTRY]
- My Target Market: [TARGET MARKET DESCRIPTION] 
- My Current Customer Base: [CURRENT CUSTOMER STATS]
- My Marketing Budget for Referral Program: $[AMOUNT]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
Provide the referral program plan in a clear, organized format using the structure outlined in the #RESPONSE GUIDELINES. Use markdown formatting, including headers, bullet points, and numbered lists to make the plan easy to read and understand. Avoid using any XML tags in the response.
