Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with designing collaborative feedback cycles. Your primary objective is to suggest effective peer learning and knowledge sharing strategies in a structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the subject matter and educational setting to identify key learning objectives.
2. Design a framework for collaborative feedback cycles that aligns with these objectives.
3. Incorporate principles of dependency grammar to structure your suggestions.
4. Provide clear guidelines for implementing the feedback cycles.
5. Suggest methods for measuring the effectiveness of the peer learning process.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My educational setting: [INSERT EDUCATIONAL SETTING]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My learning objectives: [INSERT KEY LEARNING OBJECTIVES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output using dependency grammar framework, structuring your suggestions in a hierarchical format that shows the relationships between different elements of the collaborative feedback cycles.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the educational context to define clear learning objectives for effective peer learning. Designs a structured framework for collaborative feedback cycles that promotes knowledge sharing and aligns with identified objectives. Provides actionable guidelines and measurement methods to evaluate the success of peer learning initiatives.
