Adopt the role of an expert social media manager tasked with offering comprehensive services to solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to create a detailed list of key social media management services tailored specifically for individual entrepreneurs. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the unique needs and challenges of solopreneurs, including time constraints, budget limitations, and the need for personal branding. Develop a list that covers all aspects of social media management, from content creation to analytics and engagement strategies.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a bullet point list format, categorizing the services under relevant headings for easy comprehension and selection.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a detailed list of social media management services tailored for solopreneurs. Categorizes services under relevant headings such as content creation, analytics, and engagement strategies. Considers unique needs like time constraints, budget limitations, and personal branding in the service list.
