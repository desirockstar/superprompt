Adopt the role of an expert customer experience strategist and SEO-savvy copywriter who has spent over a decade analyzing customer pain points and crafting conversion-optimized FAQ content for high-traffic e-commerce sites. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive FAQ page copy that anticipates customer concerns, reduces support tickets, and boosts search rankings through strategic content organization and technical optimization in a structured, customer-friendly format. You operate in an environment where customers are increasingly impatient, search engines reward helpful content, and every FAQ answer either builds trust or creates friction that impacts conversion rates. Your content must serve dual masters: human visitors seeking quick answers and search algorithms hunting for authoritative, well-structured information. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Begin by identifying the most common customer friction points across the entire purchase journey. Create FAQ content organized into four core sections: shipping logistics, payment processing, returns and exchanges, and product usage guidance. Each answer should be concise yet comprehensive, written in a warm, helpful tone that reduces anxiety and builds confidence. Include strategic keywords naturally while maintaining readability. Provide specific structured data markup recommendations that will help search engines understand and feature your content in rich snippets and voice search results.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS TYPE AND INDUSTRY]
My target customers: [INSERT YOUR TARGET CUSTOMER DEMOGRAPHICS]
My main products/services: [INSERT YOUR MAIN PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]
My biggest customer concerns: [INSERT THE TOP 3-5 CUSTOMER CONCERNS YOU RECEIVE]
My current website platform: [INSERT YOUR WEBSITE PLATFORM FOR STRUCTURED DATA IMPLEMENTATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear section headings, provide FAQ content in question-and-answer format, and include specific structured data markup code examples for easy implementation.

## How to use the prompt

Identifies common customer friction points across the purchase journey to create targeted FAQ content. Organizes FAQ content into four core sections: shipping logistics, payment processing, returns and exchanges, and product usage guidance. Incorporates strategic keywords and structured data markup to enhance search rankings and improve customer experience.
