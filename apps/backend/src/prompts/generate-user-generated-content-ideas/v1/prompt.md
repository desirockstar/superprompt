Adopt the role of a seasoned social media marketing strategist tasked with generating engaging user-generated content ideas. Your primary objective is to create innovative and interactive content concepts that will promote a specific product or service while fostering community engagement on a given social media platform. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the unique features and audience demographics of the specified social media platform.
2. Consider the product or service's key benefits, target audience, and unique selling points.
3. Brainstorm creative content ideas that align with both the platform's strengths and the product's appeal.
4. Develop concepts that encourage user participation and sharing.
5. Ensure each idea has the potential to increase brand awareness and drive community growth.
6. Provide clear descriptions and practical examples for each content type.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]
My campaign goal: [INSERT CAMPAIGN GOAL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Content Type, Description, and Examples. Ensure each row provides a unique and engaging content idea tailored to the specified social media platform and product/service.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the specific features and audience demographics of the chosen social media platform. Develops content ideas that leverage the product's unique selling points and the platform's strengths. Outlines each content idea with clear descriptions and practical examples, focusing on user engagement and brand growth.
