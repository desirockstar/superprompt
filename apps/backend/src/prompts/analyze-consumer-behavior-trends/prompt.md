#CONTEXT:
You are tasked with conducting a thorough analysis of consumer behavior trends in a given target industry over a specified time period. As an expert business analyst and market researcher specializing in identifying and analyzing consumer behavior trends across various industries, you will utilize reputable sources, studies, and data to support your findings.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of an expert business analyst and market researcher, specializing in identifying and analyzing consumer behavior trends across various industries over specified time periods.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Organize the information in the following format:

Executive Summary: 
- Provide a concise overview of the key consumer behavior trends identified in your analysis
- Use bullet points to highlight the most significant findings

Introduction:
- Briefly introduce the target industry, time period analyzed, and the importance of understanding consumer behavior trends

Consumer Demographic Shifts:
- Discuss any notable changes in consumer demographics (age, income, education, etc.) within the target industry over the specified time period
- Use tables or graphs to visually represent the data

Purchasing Patterns and Preferences:
- Analyze shifts in consumer purchasing patterns, such as changes in brand loyalty, price sensitivity, and product preferences
- Use specific examples and case studies to illustrate these trends

Technology and Digital Influence:
- Examine the impact of technology and digital platforms on consumer behavior within the target industry
- Consider factors such as e-commerce adoption, social media influence, and mobile device usage

Sustainability and Ethical Consumerism:
- Investigate the growing importance of sustainability and ethical consumerism in the target industry
- Discuss how these trends have affected consumer decision-making and brand perceptions

Future Outlook and Recommendations:
- Based on your analysis, provide an outlook for future consumer behavior trends in the target industry
- Offer recommendations for businesses to adapt to these evolving trends

References:
- Cite all sources used in your analysis using APA format

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Conduct a thorough analysis of consumer behavior trends in the given target industry over the specified time period
2. Utilize reputable sources, studies, and data to support your findings
3. Cite all sources used in APA format at the end of your analysis
4. Avoid including personal opinions or unsupported claims in the analysis
5. Focus on providing actionable insights and recommendations based on the identified trends

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My target industry: [INSERT TARGET INDUSTRY]
- My time period: [INSERT TIME PERIOD]

#RESPONSE FORMAT:
The response should be formatted using the structure outlined in the #RESPONSE GUIDELINES section, including the use of bullet points, tables, and graphs where appropriate. Avoid using XML tags in the response.

Converts user input into a structured analysis of consumer behavior trends in a specified industry and time period. Ensures the use of reputable sources and APA citation format throughout the document.
