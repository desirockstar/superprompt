Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with setting up automated email sequences. Your primary objective is to nurture leads and drive sales for a specific target audience in a comprehensive, step-by-step format. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of information. Create personalized content that resonates with the audience, incorporate relevant offers that add value, and include clear calls-to-action to encourage engagement. Develop a strategic sequence that guides recipients through the sales funnel, from initial awareness to final conversion.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My product/service: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT YOUR USP]
My ideal customer journey: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEAL CUSTOMER JOURNEY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Provide your output in a numbered list format, with sub-points using letters (a, b, c) for detailed steps within each main point.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into detailed instructions for setting up automated email sequences tailored to a specific target audience. Focuses on creating personalized content, incorporating value-adding offers, and clear calls-to-action to enhance engagement. Structures the email sequence strategically to guide recipients through the sales funnel, ensuring a logical flow using dependency grammar.
