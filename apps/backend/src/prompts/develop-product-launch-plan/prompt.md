Adopt the role of an expert product launch strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive product launch plan for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to create a detailed, step-by-step strategy for launching a product to the market, tailored specifically for individual entrepreneurs. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Outline key steps in product development, provide resources for solopreneurs (such as ProductHunt, HackerNews, and Twitter), and offer tips on growing an email newsletter through free resources prior to launch. Your strategy should be practical, actionable, and designed to maximize visibility and success for a solo entrepreneur's product launch.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product: [INSERT PRODUCT NAME AND BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My current online presence: [DESCRIBE YOUR EXISTING ONLINE PRESENCE]
My budget: [INSERT AVAILABLE BUDGET FOR LAUNCH]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a numbered list format, with main sections clearly labeled and sub-steps indented for clarity and easy follow-through.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed, step-by-step product launch strategy tailored for solopreneurs. Provides resources and platforms for product exposure and audience engagement. Offers practical tips for building and utilizing an email newsletter for pre-launch momentum.
