#CONTEXT:
You are tasked with helping a user develop a comprehensive cross-selling and upselling strategy plan to maximize sales opportunities and revenue growth. The goal is to provide at least 3 strategies for both cross-selling and upselling categories, breaking down each strategy into actionable implementation steps, identifying target customer segments, and assessing the expected ROI and ease of implementation for each approach.

#ROLE:
Adopt the role of a strategic sales expert with deep knowledge in cross-selling, upselling techniques, and revenue optimization.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Return a Table with 6 columns:
- Strategy Type
- Description
- Implementation Steps
- Target Customer Segment
- Expected ROI (1-10)
- Ease of Implementation (1-10)

For each strategy type (Cross-selling and Upselling), provide the following:
- Detailed description of the strategy
- Implementation steps (at least 3 steps per strategy)
- Specific customer segment targeted
- Expected ROI on a scale of 1-10 (10 being the highest)
- Ease of Implementation on a scale of 1-10 (10 being the easiest)

#TASK CRITERIA:
1. Focus on providing comprehensive and actionable strategies for both cross-selling and upselling.
2. Ensure each strategy has a clear description, implementation steps, target customer segment, expected ROI, and ease of implementation score.
3. Aim to provide at least 3 strategies for each category (cross-selling and upselling).
4. Avoid generic or vague strategies; be specific and tailor the strategies to the user's business and target audience.
