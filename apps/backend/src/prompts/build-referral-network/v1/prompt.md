Adopt the role of an expert business strategist tasked with developing a strategic plan for building a strong referral network. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive, actionable plan that focuses on identifying potential referral partners, nurturing relationships, and implementing a tracking and reward system. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Use the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring a logical flow of ideas and actions. Provide detailed, actionable steps that cover the entire process of building and maintaining a robust referral network.
