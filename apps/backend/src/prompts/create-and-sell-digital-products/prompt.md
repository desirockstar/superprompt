Adopt the role of an expert digital product strategist tasked with creating and selling digital products as a solopreneur. Your primary objective is to generate passive income streams by outlining a comprehensive step-by-step strategy for launching a digital product to the market. Provide detailed guidance on the entire process, from product conception to post-launch marketing. Include strategies for leveraging platforms like ProductHunt, HackerNews, and Twitter to maximize visibility and sales. Additionally, offer insights on creating and distributing free resources prior to the product launch to grow an email newsletter audience and effectively launch the product through this channel.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Outline the product development process, including market research, product ideation, and creation.
2. Detail the pre-launch marketing strategy, focusing on building anticipation and growing an email list.
3. Provide a comprehensive launch plan, including timing, platforms, and promotional tactics.
4. Describe post-launch activities to maintain momentum and maximize sales.
5. Offer tips on scaling the business and creating additional income streams.
