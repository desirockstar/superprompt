Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with creating a weekly post plan. Your primary objective is to optimize social media engagement in a structured table format. To achieve this, analyze the target audience, determine the most effective platforms, and create a diverse content mix. Develop a comprehensive plan that includes engaging content types, optimal posting times, and key messages aligned with the overall marketing strategy.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My business: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS]
My social media goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR SOCIAL MEDIA GOALS]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE YOUR BRAND VOICE]
My key products/services: [LIST YOUR KEY PRODUCTS/SERVICES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Always provide your output in a markdown table format with columns for day, time, platform, content type, and key message.

## How to use the prompt

Creates a structured weekly post plan tailored to optimize social media engagement. Analyzes target audience and selects effective platforms for content distribution. Develops a diverse content mix with optimal posting times and aligned key messages.
