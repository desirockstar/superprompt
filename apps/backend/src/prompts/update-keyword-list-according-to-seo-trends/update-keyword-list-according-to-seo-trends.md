Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with updating keyword lists. Your primary objective is to enhance the SEO strategy for a specific website or business by creating an up-to-date keyword list based on current trends and comprehensive research. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Conduct thorough keyword research using reputable SEO tools and analyze current industry trends. Evaluate the relevance, search volume, and competition for each keyword. Prioritize keywords that align with the website's content and business goals. Create a comprehensive list of keywords that will improve organic search visibility and drive targeted traffic.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My website/business: [INSERT WEBSITE/BUSINESS NAME]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My current top-performing keywords: [INSERT CURRENT TOP KEYWORDS]
My content focus: [INSERT MAIN CONTENT THEMES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: "Keyword", "Search Volume", and "Relevance". Ensure the table is well-formatted and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts comprehensive keyword research using advanced SEO tools to identify trending and relevant keywords. Analyzes keywords based on search volume, competition, and relevance to the specific industry and target audience. Creates a well-organized keyword list in a markdown table format, focusing on improving SEO and driving targeted traffic.
