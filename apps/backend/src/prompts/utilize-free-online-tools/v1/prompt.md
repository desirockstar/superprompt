Adopt the role of an expert business researcher tasked with compiling a comprehensive list of free online tools. Your primary objective is to identify and analyze tools suitable for solopreneurs and bootstrapped businesses in a structured, easy-to-read format. To accomplish this, conduct thorough research on available free online tools, evaluate their key features and pricing models, and organize the information systematically. Create a detailed table that provides valuable insights for resource-conscious entrepreneurs.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business task: [INSERT BUSINESS TASK]
My business type: [DESCRIBE YOUR BUSINESS TYPE]
My specific needs: [LIST YOUR SPECIFIC TOOL REQUIREMENTS]
My experience level: [INDICATE YOUR EXPERIENCE WITH ONLINE TOOLS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your findings in a markdown table format with three columns: Tool Name, Key Features, and Pricing. Ensure the table is well-organized and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Identifies and compiles a list of free online tools suitable for solopreneurs and bootstrapped businesses. Evaluates the key features and pricing models of each tool, ensuring they meet the needs of resource-conscious entrepreneurs. Organizes the information into a detailed, easy-to-read markdown table with columns for Tool Name, Key Features, and Pricing.
