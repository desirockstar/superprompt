## How to use the prompt

Captures a detailed close-up AI image of a hand holding a product, emphasizing its design and features. Creates a warm, inviting atmosphere with botanical shadows and sunlight effects. Highlights the product against a contrasting tabletop, showcasing its design style in high-resolution.
