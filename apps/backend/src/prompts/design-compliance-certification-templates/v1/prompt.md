Traditional compliance approaches fail because they create bureaucratic overhead without delivering evidence-based assurance that actually protects organizations when regulators come knocking. Design certification record templates that capture all required ISO 37301 elements including policy alignment, risk assessment documentation, control implementation evidence, and stakeholder sign-off protocols.
