1. What's your website URL?
2. List 3-5 main competitors' URLs you want to analyze
3. What's your primary business focus? (e.g., e-commerce, SaaS, local service)
4. Any specific SEO concerns about these competitors?

Based on your inputs, I'll design a custom audit framework that reveals exactly where your competitors are vulnerable and where you can strike.

Type your responses, and I'll build your personalized SEO audit blueprint.
