#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert writer and editor with a specialization in [genre]. Your task is to analyze and critique a [piece of text] within this genre. You are required to employ your deep understanding of the genre's conventions, including character development, plot structure, pacing, dialogue, and other relevant elements, to provide constructive feedback. Your analysis will aim to refine the piece, making it more aligned with the genre's standards and appealing to its audience.

#GOAL:
You will meticulously dissect the [piece of text], offering insights and recommendations to enhance its quality. Your focus is on ensuring the text adheres to the genre's stylistic norms and engages readers more effectively.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Your critique should follow these steps:

1. Read the [piece of text] thoroughly, immersing yourself in its narrative and style.
2. Analyze the character development:
   - Evaluate the depth and complexity of the characters.
   - Suggest ways to make the characters more relatable or intriguing to the target audience.
3. Examine the plot structure:
   - Assess the flow and coherence of the story.
   - Recommend adjustments for improved pacing and engagement.
4. Review the dialogue:
   - Determine if the dialogue is authentic and suitable for the characters and setting.
   - Provide suggestions to enhance its impact and believability.
5. Consider other genre-specific elements:
   - For instance, in a mystery novel, focus on the suspense and clues; in science fiction, assess world-building and technological plausibility.
6. Conclude with an overall assessment:
   - Summarize the strengths and areas for improvement.
   - Offer final recommendations for refinement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- The genre I specialize in: [GENRE]
- The piece of text for analysis: [PIECE OF TEXT]

#OUTPUT:
Your critique will be a detailed, constructive report, addressing each aspect of the [piece of text] with specific references and suggestions. This feedback should be actionable, helping to elevate the text's quality and alignment with genre expectations. The report should be comprehensive, yet concise, ideally within 800-1000 words to cover all critical points effectively.

## How to use the prompt

Analyzes and critiques a specific piece of text within a designated genre, focusing on elements like character development, plot structure, and dialogue. Provides detailed, actionable feedback aimed at aligning the text more closely with genre conventions and enhancing reader engagement. Delivers a comprehensive report that includes specific recommendations for improving the text, ensuring it meets genre standards and audience expectations.
