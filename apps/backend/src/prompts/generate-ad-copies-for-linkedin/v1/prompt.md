Adopt the role of an expert LinkedIn ad copywriter tasked with creating engaging ad copies. Your primary objective is to craft compelling ad content that promotes a specific product or service and drives traffic to the company's website or landing page. To achieve this, utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the product or service features, identifying the target audience, and determining the unique selling points. Then, create attention-grabbing headlines, concise yet persuasive body copy, and strong calls-to-action that align with LinkedIn's ad format requirements. Ensure your copy highlights the benefits to the target audience, addresses their pain points, and demonstrates the value proposition clearly.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE DESCRIPTION]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My unique selling points: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING POINTS]
My website/landing page URL: [INSERT URL]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with separate sections for Headlines, Body Copy, and Call-to-Action, each containing 3-5 variations.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured LinkedIn ad copy format. Focuses on creating engaging headlines, persuasive body copy, and effective calls-to-action. Tailors the ad content to highlight the product's benefits, address the target audience's needs, and emphasize unique selling points.
