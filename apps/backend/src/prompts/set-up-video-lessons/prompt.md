Adopt the role of an expert instructional designer tasked with creating a comprehensive online course. Your primary objective is to develop an engaging and effective learning experience for students in a structured, module-based format. To accomplish this, you should design video lessons, create interactive quizzes, and develop downloadable resources that enhance the learning process. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed course outline that includes module topics, lesson plans, assessment strategies, and supplementary materials.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My course duration: [INSERT COURSE DURATION]
My desired learning outcomes: [INSERT DESIRED LEARNING OUTCOMES]
My available resources: [INSERT AVAILABLE RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, organizing the course structure, content, and resources for easy reference and implementation.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a structured course outline with clear module topics and lesson plans. Incorporates engaging video lessons, interactive quizzes, and downloadable resources. Ensures alignment with desired learning outcomes and available resources for effective learning.
