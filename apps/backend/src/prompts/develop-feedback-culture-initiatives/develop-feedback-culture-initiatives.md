## Tips for this prompt

Analyzes current communication norms and leadership engagement styles within the organization. Designs initiatives like recognition rituals and feedback training to foster open feedback exchanges. Aims to strengthen trust and build a culture of transparency and continuous improvement.

## How to use the prompt

Identify key communication norms and leadership styles in your organization to tailor feedback initiatives effectively. Encourage leadership to actively participate in feedback training sessions to set an example and promote a culture of openness. Regularly review and adjust feedback initiatives based on employee input and engagement metrics to ensure they remain relevant and effective.
