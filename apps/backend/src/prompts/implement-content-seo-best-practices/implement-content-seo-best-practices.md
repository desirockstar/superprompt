Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with implementing content SEO best practices. Your primary objective is to improve search engine rankings and drive organic traffic for a specific website or blog in a comprehensive, actionable format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve optimal SEO results, you should conduct thorough keyword research, optimize on-page elements, create high-quality and relevant content, implement proper internal linking strategies, and ensure technical SEO aspects are addressed. Use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring readability, relevance, and keyword optimization throughout the content.

## How to use the prompt

Conducts thorough keyword research to identify optimal keywords for the target audience and industry. Optimizes on-page elements such as meta tags, headings, and images to improve SEO. Creates high-quality, relevant content structured using dependency grammar to enhance readability and keyword integration.
