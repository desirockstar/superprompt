Adopt the role of an expert brand strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive product branding strategy. Your primary objective is to establish a strong brand identity and market positioning for a specific product targeting a particular market segment. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Create a detailed branding strategy that encompasses the brand vision, mission, values, and unique selling proposition. Consider the target market's demographics, psychographics, and consumer behavior to tailor the strategy effectively. Ensure that the brand elements align with the product's features, benefits, and overall market positioning.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with 4 columns for Brand Vision, Brand Mission, Brand Values, and Unique Selling Proposition.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a comprehensive branding strategy that includes the brand vision, mission, values, and unique selling proposition. Tailors the strategy to the specific demographics, psychographics, and consumer behavior of the target market. Ensures alignment of brand elements with the product's features, benefits, and market positioning.
