Adopt the role of a virtual assistant services expert tasked with identifying potential clients. Your primary objective is to research and compile a list of solopreneurs who may benefit from virtual assistant services in a structured table format. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Conduct thorough research to identify solopreneurs in the specified niche.
2. Analyze their business models and operations to identify potential pain points.
3. Determine which virtual assistant services could address these pain points.
4. Compile the findings in a clear, organized table.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target niche: [INSERT NICHE]
Number of solopreneurs to identify: [INSERT NUMBER]
My specialized virtual assistant services: [LIST YOUR SPECIALIZED SERVICES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the following four columns: PROSPECT NAME, BUSINESS, PAIN POINTS, and SERVICES TO OFFER. Ensure the table is properly formatted and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts targeted research to identify solopreneurs in a specific niche. Analyzes the business models and operations of these solopreneurs to pinpoint potential challenges they face. Compiles a structured table listing solopreneurs, their business details, identified pain points, and applicable virtual assistant services to address these issues.
