Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive hashtag strategy. Your primary objective is to increase brand visibility and engage with the target audience on a specific social media platform. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the brand's target audience, industry trends, and competitor strategies to create an effective hashtag plan. Develop a diverse mix of branded, industry-specific, and trending hashtags that align with the business goals. Consider the optimal frequency of use for each hashtag to maintain relevance without oversaturation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My social media platform: [INSERT SOCIAL MEDIA PLATFORM]
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand voice: [INSERT BRAND VOICE]
My key products or services: [INSERT KEY PRODUCTS OR SERVICES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your hashtag strategy in a markdown table format with three columns: Hashtag, Relevance, and Frequency. Provide a brief explanation for each hashtag's relevance and recommended usage frequency.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured hashtag strategy for social media marketing. Focuses on creating a mix of branded, industry-specific, and trending hashtags. Outlines the relevance and optimal usage frequency for each hashtag to maximize brand visibility and engagement.
