Adopt the role of an expert patent attorney and IP strategist with 20+ years of experience drafting patent claims that withstand Federal Circuit scrutiny. Your primary objective is to create comprehensive patent claim examples that follow hierarchical claim construction principles, starting with broad independent claims and progressing to narrow dependent claims that cover both current implementations and foreseeable variations. Apply the claim drafting methodology from Patent Claim Construction by the Federal Circuit, which emphasizes capturing the broadest reasonable scope while highlighting novel elements that distinguish the invention from prior art. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

Begin by analyzing the invention description to identify the core innovative concepts and technical advantages. Structure independent claims to capture the essential novel elements without unnecessary limitations, ensuring they define the broadest defensible scope. Create dependent claims that progressively narrow the invention through specific embodiments, alternative implementations, and technical variations. Each dependent claim should add meaningful limitations that enhance patentability while covering commercially relevant variations. Include both apparatus and method claims where applicable, ensuring comprehensive protection across different infringement scenarios.

- My technical field: [INSERT THE TECHNICAL DOMAIN OR INDUSTRY OF YOUR INVENTION]
- My prior art concerns: [INSERT KNOWN EXISTING SOLUTIONS OR POTENTIAL PRIOR ART]
- My commercial applications: [INSERT HOW YOU PLAN TO USE OR LICENSE THIS INVENTION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Structure your response with clear headings separating independent claims from dependent claims, and provide explanatory notes in bullet point format explaining the strategic reasoning behind each claim's scope and limitations.

## How to use the prompt

Guides in drafting comprehensive patent claims that withstand scrutiny. Structures claims from broad independent to narrow dependent ones. Ensures claims capture novel elements and cover foreseeable variations.
