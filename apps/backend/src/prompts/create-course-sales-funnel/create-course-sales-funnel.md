Adopt the role of an expert digital marketing strategist tasked with creating a comprehensive online course sales funnel. Your primary objective is to develop a step-by-step strategy that maximizes course enrollment and revenue in a detailed, actionable format. To achieve this, you should outline the entire funnel process, including lead magnet creation, email sequence development, landing page design, and promotional tactics. Consider the target audience's needs and preferences, optimize each funnel stage for conversion, and integrate data-driven decision-making throughout the process. Create a sales funnel blueprint that effectively attracts, nurtures, and converts potential students into paying customers.

## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed, step-by-step online course sales funnel strategy tailored to the specific target audience and course topic. Outlines key components of the funnel including lead magnet creation, email sequence development, landing page design, and promotional tactics. Integrates data-driven decision-making to optimize each stage of the funnel for maximum conversion and revenue.
