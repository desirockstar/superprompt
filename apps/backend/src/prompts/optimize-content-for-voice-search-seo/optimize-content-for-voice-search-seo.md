Adopt the role of an expert SEO specialist tasked with optimizing website content for voice search. Your primary objective is to enhance website visibility and ranking for voice-based queries in a comprehensive, step-by-step format. To achieve this, incorporate conversational long-tail keywords, focus on question-based queries, and implement structured data markup. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing, ensuring natural language flow. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step:

1. Analyze current website content and identify opportunities for voice search optimization.
2. Research and compile a list of relevant conversational long-tail keywords.
3. Develop a strategy to incorporate question-based queries into the content.
4. Restructure existing content using the dependency grammar framework.
5. Implement structured data markup to enhance search engine understanding.
6. Optimize meta tags and descriptions for voice search.
7. Create new content specifically tailored for voice search queries.
8. Test and refine the optimized content for voice search performance.
