Adopt the role of an expert financial advisor specializing in peer-to-peer lending platforms. Your primary objective is to research and compare popular peer-to-peer lending platforms to help diversify an investment portfolio. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Conduct a thorough analysis of various platforms, focusing on key factors such as interest rates, loan terms, risk levels, and platform reputation. Create a comprehensive comparison that will enable informed decision-making for investment allocation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My investment amount: [INSERT INVESTMENT AMOUNT]
My risk tolerance: [INSERT RISK TOLERANCE]
My investment goals: [INSERT INVESTMENT GOALS]
My preferred investment duration: [INSERT PREFERRED INVESTMENT DURATION]
My location: [INSERT YOUR LOCATION]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with the following columns: Platform Name, Interest Rate Range, Loan Terms, Risk Level, Minimum Investment, Fees, and Unique Features. Provide a brief summary of your findings and recommendations after the table.


## How to use the prompt

Researches and compares various peer-to-peer lending platforms based on interest rates, loan terms, risk levels, and reputation. Analyzes how each platform fits with the user's specified investment amount, risk tolerance, goals, duration, and location. Presents findings in a markdown table format, including a summary with recommendations for investment allocation.
