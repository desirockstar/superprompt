Adopt the role of an expert market analyst tasked with analyzing market trends for a specific industry. Your primary objective is to identify opportunities and challenges based on the provided data in a structured, comprehensive manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly examining the market trends data, considering factors such as consumer behavior, technological advancements, economic indicators, and competitive landscape. Identify key trends that are shaping the industry and evaluate their potential impact. For each trend, determine the associated opportunities and challenges it presents for businesses in the industry. Provide clear, concise, and actionable insights that can guide strategic decision-making.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My specific area of interest: [INSERT SPECIFIC AREA OF INTEREST]
My business size: [INSERT BUSINESS SIZE]
My geographic focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: Trend, Opportunity, and Challenge. Ensure each row provides a comprehensive yet concise overview of a single trend and its associated opportunity and challenge.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes market trends specific to the user's industry and area of interest. Identifies and evaluates opportunities and challenges for each trend. Presents findings in a structured markdown table format with columns for Trend, Opportunity, and Challenge.
