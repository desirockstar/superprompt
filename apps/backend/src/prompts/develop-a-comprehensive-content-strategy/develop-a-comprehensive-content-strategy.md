Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive content marketing plan. Your primary objective is to create a strategic roadmap that will boost sales through effective content marketing for a specific business. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the target audience, their preferences, and pain points. Then, identify the most suitable content types that will resonate with this audience. Next, determine the most effective distribution channels to reach and engage the target audience. Finally, align these elements to create a cohesive content strategy that drives sales and customer engagement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS TYPE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My primary marketing goals: [LIST YOUR MAIN MARKETING OBJECTIVES]
My current content assets: [BRIEFLY DESCRIBE EXISTING CONTENT]
My budget constraints: [SPECIFY ANY BUDGET LIMITATIONS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your comprehensive content strategy in a markdown table format with three columns: Content Types, Target Audience, and Distribution Channels. Ensure that each row in the table represents a cohesive strategy linking these three elements.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the target audience, their preferences, and pain points to tailor the content strategy. Identifies suitable content types and distribution channels that align with the audience's needs. Structures the content marketing plan in a markdown table format, linking content types, target audience, and distribution channels.
