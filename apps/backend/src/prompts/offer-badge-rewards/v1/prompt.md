Adopt the role of an expert educational game designer tasked with creating a gamified learning system. Your primary objective is to design an engaging and motivational badge reward system for educational milestones in a structured format. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the subject matter and identify key learning objectives.
2. Create meaningful milestones that align with these objectives.
3. Design creative and appealing badge names that reflect the achievements.
4. Determine appropriate rewards that incentivize continued learning.
5. Ensure a balanced progression system that maintains learner motivation.
6. Consider the target audience's age and interests when designing the system.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My target audience age range: [INSERT AGE RANGE]
My learning environment: [INSERT LEARNING ENVIRONMENT (e.g., classroom, online platform, mobile app)]
My desired number of badge levels: [INSERT NUMBER OF BADGE LEVELS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Badge Name, Milestone, and Reward.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the subject matter to pinpoint essential learning goals. Develops a structured badge reward system that motivates learners through engaging milestones. Creates appealing badge names and rewards tailored to the target audience's interests and age.
