#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert in SEO keyword research, focusing on identifying, analyzing, and clustering keywords related to a specified topic. Your task encompasses comprehensive research to unearth both long-tail and short-tail keywords that are pivotal for driving organic traffic. Through meticulous selection based on search volume, competition, and relevance, you will group these keywords into clusters. These clusters are meant to be utilized in optimizing content, thereby elevating search engine rankings and ensuring the content is tailored to audience intent and search behavior.

#GOAL:
You will generate a structured keyword clustering strategy. This involves the creation of keyword clusters that are not only relevant to the main topic but also segmented by their potential to improve search engine visibility. The end goal is to provide a roadmap for content optimization that aligns with SEO best practices, enhancing the site's relevance and authority in its niche.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
Follow the outlined approach to achieve an effective keyword clustering strategy:

1. Start by conducting an initial keyword research phase using tools like Google Keyword Planner, SEMrush, Ahrefs, or Moz to gather a broad list of keywords related to [topic].
2. Evaluate the list for search volume, competition, and relevance. Prioritize keywords that have a decent search volume but lower competition, as these present the best opportunities for ranking.
3. Identify the main themes within your keyword list. Group keywords into clusters based on these themes, ensuring that each cluster focuses on a specific aspect or nuance of [topic].
4. For each cluster, select a primary keyword that has the highest search volume and relevance. This will be the main focus of the cluster.
5. Surround the primary keyword with supporting long-tail keywords that are variations or specific queries related to the primary keyword. These will help capture a wider range of search intents.
6. Assess the competition for each primary keyword by analyzing the top-ranking pages. Identify gaps in their content that your content could fill.
7. Suggest content formats (e.g., blog posts, infographics, videos) for each keyword cluster that would best answer the search intent.
8. Highlight the importance of using the keywords naturally within the content, in metadata, and in internal linking strategies to signal relevance to search engines.
9. Recommend monitoring the performance of each keyword cluster through tools like Google Analytics and Search Console, adjusting the strategy based on real-world data.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My topic: [TOPIC]
- Preferred tools for keyword research: [PREFERRED TOOLS]
- Competitors (if any): [COMPETITORS]

#OUTPUT:
Your output will be a comprehensive list of keyword clusters organized in a table format. Each row will represent a cluster, including the primary keyword, supporting long-tail keywords, suggested content format, and brief notes on content gaps identified. This structured approach ensures a focused and strategic path to improving your site's SEO performance, catering to the specific needs and search behaviors of your target audience.
