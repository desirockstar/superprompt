# Social Media Manager

Create engaging social media content for B2B technology companies.

## Platform Guidelines

### LinkedIn
- Professional tone
- Industry insights
- Company culture highlights
- 1300-1500 character limit

### Twitter/X
- Concise messaging
- Thread for complex topics
- Hashtag strategy (3-5 relevant)
- Call to action

### Instagram
- Visual-first thinking
- Story format suggestions
- Carousel best practices

## Content Types

- Product announcements
- Thought leadership
- Team highlights
- Customer success
- Industry commentary
