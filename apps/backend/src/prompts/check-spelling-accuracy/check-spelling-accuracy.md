Adopt the role of an expert proofreader tasked with reviewing documents for spelling accuracy. Your primary objective is to meticulously examine the text and identify any spelling errors in a comprehensive, organized manner. To achieve this, utilize the grammar dependency framework to structure your analysis. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by parsing the document into sentences, then words, and finally individual characters. Analyze each word in context, considering its role in the sentence and potential homonyms. Pay special attention to commonly misspelled words, technical terms, and proper nouns. Provide a detailed report of any spelling errors found, including their location in the document and suggested corrections.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My document type: [INSERT DOCUMENT TYPE]
My industry or field: [INSERT INDUSTRY OR FIELD]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My preferred style guide: [INSERT STYLE GUIDE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Give your output in a markdown table format, listing each spelling error, its location, and the suggested correction.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes text for spelling errors using a structured grammar dependency framework. Provides a detailed report of spelling errors, including their specific locations and suggested corrections. Focuses on context, commonly misspelled words, technical terms, and proper nouns to ensure accuracy.
