#CONTEXT:
Act as an experienced essay writer tasked with crafting a compelling introduction for an essay. The introduction must be engaging, thought-provoking, and clearly articulate the essay's main argument. It should utilize a hook to captivate the reader's attention and include a precise thesis statement. The goal is to entice the reader to continue exploring the essay. Adherence to academic writing standards and formatting guidelines is essential to ensure clarity, conciseness, and appropriateness.

#GOAL:
You will create an introductory paragraph that sets the stage for the essay's argument, drawing the reader in with a captivating opening and presenting a clear thesis statement that outlines the essay's main points.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
To achieve this, follow the step-by-step approach below:

1. **Identify the Essay Topic**: Start by clearly understanding the [topic] of the essay. This involves comprehending the scope and the specific angle you intend to explore within this theme.
   
2. **Craft a Hook**: Begin the introduction with a compelling hook that grabs the reader's attention. This could be a surprising statistic, a provocative question, an anecdote, or a quote related to the [topic].
   
3. **Provide Background Information**: Briefly introduce the background or context necessary for understanding the essay's main argument. This should be relevant and concise, setting the stage without delving too deep into the details.
   
4. **Develop a Thesis Statement**: Formulate a clear and concise thesis statement that presents the main argument or perspective of the essay. This statement should encapsulate the essence of the essay and guide the reader on what to expect.
   
5. **Outline the Essay's Structure**: Briefly mention the main points that will be discussed in the essay, following the thesis statement. This gives the reader a roadmap of the argument's progression.

6. **Transition to the Body**: Conclude the introduction with a smooth transition that connects the introductory ideas to the body paragraphs of the essay.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
- My essay topic: [TOPIC]

#OUTPUT:
The output will be a well-structured introduction that includes:
- A compelling hook that engages the reader.
- Necessary background information providing context to the topic.
- A clear and concise thesis statement articulating the essay's main argument.
- A brief outline of the essay's structure, giving the reader an idea of what to expect.
- A transition sentence leading into the body of the essay.

The introduction will adhere to academic standards of writing, being clear, concise, and formatted appropriately for an academic essay.

## How to use the prompt

Crafts a compelling introduction for an academic essay, including a hook and a clear thesis statement. Outlines the structure of the essay and provides necessary background information. Ensures the introduction adheres to academic writing standards and formatting guidelines.
