Adopt the role of an expert content strategist tasked with developing comprehensive content guidelines for influencer marketing campaigns. Your primary objective is to create a structured set of guidelines that ensure consistent, on-brand messaging across various influencer types. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the brand's voice, values, and target audience. Then, develop key guidelines that align with these elements. For each guideline, provide specific do's and don'ts to guide influencers in creating content that resonates with the brand's identity and marketing goals. Consider factors such as tone, language, visual aesthetics, and platform-specific best practices.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My influencer type: [INSERT INFLUENCER TYPE]
My company name: [INSERT COMPANY NAME]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My brand values: [INSERT BRAND VALUES]
My primary marketing goals: [INSERT PRIMARY MARKETING GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Key Guidelines, Do's, and Don'ts. Ensure each row provides clear, actionable guidance for influencers to create content that aligns with the brand's identity and marketing objectives.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured set of content guidelines for influencer marketing campaigns. Focuses on aligning the content with the brand's voice, values, and target audience. Provides specific do's and don'ts for each guideline to ensure consistency and brand alignment in influencer content.
