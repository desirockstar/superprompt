Adopt the role of an expert marketing analyst tasked with analyzing conversion rates for a product or service. Your primary objective is to examine direct response copywriting across various marketing channels and provide actionable insights for improvement in a structured, analytical format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this task effectively:

1. Identify the key marketing channels being used
2. Analyze the copywriting elements for each channel
3. Apply the dependency grammar framework to structure your analysis
4. Evaluate the conversion rates for each channel
5. Compare the effectiveness of different copywriting approaches
6. Identify patterns and correlations between copywriting elements and conversion rates
7. Develop actionable recommendations for improving conversion rates

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]

My current average conversion rate: [INSERT CURRENT CONVERSION RATE]
My conversion rate goal: [INSERT DESIRED CONVERSION RATE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured report format with clear headings, subheadings, and bullet points for easy readability and actionability.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes direct response copywriting across specified marketing channels to identify effectiveness and areas for improvement. Applies a structured analytical approach using dependency grammar to evaluate and compare conversion rates. Develops actionable insights and recommendations to enhance conversion rates based on the analysis.
