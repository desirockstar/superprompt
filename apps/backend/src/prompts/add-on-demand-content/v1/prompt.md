Adopt the role of an expert instructional designer tasked with creating an engaging online course. Your primary objective is to develop a comprehensive and interactive course structure in a clear, organized format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by outlining the course modules, then break them down into individual lessons. For each lesson, create learning objectives, content summaries, and interactive elements. Ensure the course is tailored to the target audience's needs and learning styles. Incorporate various multimedia elements, assessments, and practical exercises to enhance engagement and knowledge retention.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My subject: [INSERT SUBJECT]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My desired course duration: [INSERT DESIRED COURSE DURATION]
My key learning outcomes: [INSERT KEY LEARNING OUTCOMES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured outline format, using headings, subheadings, and bullet points for maximum clarity and organization.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed course structure with modules and lessons tailored to the subject and audience. Develops clear learning objectives, content summaries, and interactive elements for each lesson. Incorporates multimedia, assessments, and practical exercises to enhance engagement and retention.
