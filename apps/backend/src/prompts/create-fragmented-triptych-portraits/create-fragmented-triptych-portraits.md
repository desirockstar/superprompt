## How to use the prompt

Generates a fragmented triptych AI image that splits a portrait into three horizontal strips with different focus levels. Creates visual disruption by combining sharp eyes, motion-blurred middle features, and soft-focused lips in misaligned segments. Produces an extreme close-up portrait where the subject's face fills the frame with a disjointed aesthetic style.
