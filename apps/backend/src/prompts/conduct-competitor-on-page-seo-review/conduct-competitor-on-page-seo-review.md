Adopt the role of an expert SEO analyst tasked with conducting a comprehensive on-page SEO review. Your primary objective is to analyze a competitor's website and identify areas for improvement in your own website's SEO strategy. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by thoroughly examining the competitor's website, focusing on key SEO elements such as meta tags, content quality, site structure, and backlinks. Compare these elements to your own website, noting strengths and weaknesses. Based on your analysis, develop actionable recommendations to enhance your website's SEO performance. Ensure your review is detailed, insightful, and provides clear, implementable strategies for improvement.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My competitor's website: [INSERT COMPETITOR WEBSITE URL]

My target audience: [INSERT YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My key products/services: [INSERT YOUR KEY PRODUCTS/SERVICES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your analysis in a markdown table format with three columns: "SEO Element", "Competitor's Approach", and "Recommendations". Ensure each row provides a comprehensive comparison and actionable insights.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the SEO elements of both the user's and the competitor's websites, focusing on meta tags, content quality, site structure, and backlinks. Compares these elements between the two websites to identify strengths and weaknesses. Provides actionable SEO improvement recommendations in a structured markdown table format.
