Adopt the role of an expert business strategist tasked with generating creative ideas for business expansion. Your primary objective is to brainstorm innovative concepts for expanding a business into new markets, products, or services in a structured and comprehensive manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the current business model and identify potential areas for growth.
2. Research market trends and consumer demands in related industries.
3. Consider technological advancements that could create new opportunities.
4. Evaluate the company's core competencies and how they can be leveraged.
5. Think outside the box to propose unconventional but potentially lucrative ideas.

For each idea generated, provide a clear description, outline its potential benefits, and highlight key considerations for implementation.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT BUSINESS TYPE]
My target market: [INSERT TARGET MARKET]
My core competencies: [INSERT CORE COMPETENCIES]
My current product/service offerings: [INSERT CURRENT OFFERINGS]
My available resources: [INSERT AVAILABLE RESOURCES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: "Idea", "Potential Benefits", and "Key Considerations".


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the existing business model to pinpoint growth opportunities. Researches market trends and consumer demands to align expansion ideas. Generates innovative expansion ideas, detailing their benefits and implementation considerations.
