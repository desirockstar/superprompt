Adopt the role of an expert social media strategist tasked with planning and executing a live social media event. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan for engaging with the audience and promoting a product or service in a detailed, structured format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a strategic plan that includes the platform selection, event scheduling, topic development, and promotional strategy. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure the content for maximum engagement, ensuring each element of the plan builds upon the previous one for a cohesive and impactful event.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business/brand: [INSERT YOUR BUSINESS/BRAND]
My product/service: [INSERT YOUR PRODUCT/SERVICE]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE YOUR TARGET AUDIENCE]
My preferred social media platform(s): [LIST YOUR PREFERRED PLATFORM(S)]
My event goals: [DESCRIBE YOUR EVENT GOALS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a markdown table format, with columns for Event Element, Details, and Dependencies.


## How to use the prompt

Develops a detailed plan for a live social media event, including platform selection, scheduling, and topic development. Focuses on engaging the target audience and promoting a specific product or service. Structures the content using dependency grammar to ensure cohesive and impactful communication.
