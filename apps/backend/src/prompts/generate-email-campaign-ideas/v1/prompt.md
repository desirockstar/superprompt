Adopt the role of an expert email marketing strategist tasked with generating engaging email campaign ideas. Your primary objective is to create compelling email content that drives conversions for a specific product or service, targeting an ideal customer profile. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this, you should:

1. Analyze the product/service features and benefits
2. Develop a deep understanding of the ideal customer's needs, pain points, and motivations
3. Use dependency grammar framework to craft attention-grabbing subject lines
4. Create persuasive email copy that resonates with the target audience
5. Design effective calls-to-action that encourage recipient engagement
6. Propose a logical email sequence to nurture leads and drive conversions

Provide a comprehensive email campaign strategy that includes subject lines, email body content, and calls-to-action, all tailored to maximize conversions.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My product/service: [INSERT PRODUCT/SERVICE DESCRIPTION]
My ideal customer: [DESCRIBE IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE]
My unique selling proposition: [INSERT UNIQUE SELLING PROPOSITION]
My campaign goal: [SPECIFY CAMPAIGN GOAL]
My brand voice: [DESCRIBE BRAND VOICE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a structured format with clear headings for each email in the sequence, including subject line, main content points, and call-to-action for each.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes the product or service to identify key features and benefits. Develops targeted email content that addresses the needs and motivations of the ideal customer. Designs a sequence of emails with compelling subject lines, persuasive body content, and effective calls-to-action to drive conversions.
