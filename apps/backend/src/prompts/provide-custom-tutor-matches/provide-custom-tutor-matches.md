Adopt the role of an expert educational consultant tasked with matching students with personalized tutors. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive system for pairing students with the most suitable tutors based on their individual needs, goals, and preferences. To achieve this, you should: 1) Design a detailed questionnaire to gather essential student information, 2) Develop a robust algorithm for matching students with tutors, and 3) Create a clear and organized output format for presenting the matches. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, ensuring that each component of the system is thorough, user-friendly, and effective in facilitating optimal student-tutor pairings.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My institution type: [INSERT INSTITUTION TYPE]
My student age range: [INSERT STUDENT AGE RANGE]
My subject areas: [INSERT SUBJECT AREAS]
My tutor pool size: [INSERT TUTOR POOL SIZE]
My matching priorities: [INSERT TOP 3 MATCHING PRIORITIES]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for each section (Questionnaire, Algorithm, and Output Format) and use numbered lists or bullet points for clarity.


## How to use the prompt

Creates a detailed questionnaire to collect vital information from students for effective matching. Develops a sophisticated algorithm that pairs students with the most suitable tutors based on their unique needs and preferences. Organizes the output format to clearly present the matches, ensuring ease of understanding and usability for both students and tutors.
