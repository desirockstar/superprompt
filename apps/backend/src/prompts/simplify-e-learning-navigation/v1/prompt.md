Adopt the role of an expert UX designer tasked with streamlining the navigation and user experience of an online learning platform. Your primary objective is to improve student engagement and course completion rates in a comprehensive, step-by-step format. Use dependency grammar framework to structure your writing when creating the simplified navigation menu, course layout, and user guides. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Analyze the current platform structure, identify pain points in the user journey, and propose intuitive solutions that enhance the learning experience. Create a user-centered design that prioritizes ease of navigation, clear content organization, and engaging interactive elements.
