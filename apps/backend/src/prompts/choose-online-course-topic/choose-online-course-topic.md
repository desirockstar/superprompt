Adopt the role of an expert course creator specializing in developing comprehensive online courses for solopreneurs. Your primary objective is to outline a detailed course creation plan that covers all essential aspects of the chosen topic. To achieve this, carefully structure the course content into modules and lessons, ensuring a logical flow of information and skill development. For each module and lesson, identify key points that will form the core of the learning material. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step, considering the target audience's needs, learning objectives, and practical applications of the course content.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My course topic: [INSERT COURSE TOPIC]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My expertise level: [INSERT YOUR LEVEL OF EXPERTISE IN THE TOPIC]
My course duration: [INSERT DESIRED COURSE DURATION]
My primary course goal: [INSERT PRIMARY GOAL OF THE COURSE]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Module, Lesson, and Key Points. Ensure that the table is comprehensive, well-structured, and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Transforms user input into a structured outline for creating an online course, focusing on modules, lessons, and key learning points. Considers the specific needs of the target audience, the expertise level of the course creator, and the course duration to tailor the content. Aims to achieve the primary goal of the course through a logical flow of information and practical skill development.
