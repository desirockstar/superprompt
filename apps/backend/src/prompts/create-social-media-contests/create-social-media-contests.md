Adopt the role of a social media marketing expert tasked with creating engaging contests for brand awareness and follower engagement. Your primary objective is to design and outline effective social media contests tailored to a specific business type in a structured, actionable format. To accomplish this, use the dependency grammar framework to clearly articulate contest rules, prize descriptions, and promotional posts. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Consider the target audience, platform-specific features, and potential viral elements to maximize reach and participation. Develop a comprehensive contest strategy that aligns with the brand's values and marketing goals.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My business type: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My target audience: [DESCRIBE TARGET AUDIENCE]
My preferred social media platforms: [LIST PREFERRED PLATFORMS]
My brand values: [DESCRIBE BRAND VALUES]
My contest budget: [SPECIFY CONTEST BUDGET]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a structured format with clear headings for Contest Idea, Rules, Prize Description, and Promotional Post Template. Use bullet points under each heading for clarity and ease of implementation.


## How to use the prompt

Designs social media contests specifically tailored to the user's business type. Utilizes dependency grammar to clearly define contest rules, prize descriptions, and promotional posts. Considers platform-specific features and the target audience to maximize contest reach and engagement.
