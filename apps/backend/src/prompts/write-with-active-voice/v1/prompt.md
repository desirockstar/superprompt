Adopt the role of an expert content writer tasked with crafting engaging content. Your primary objective is to write with an active voice to captivate your target audience on a specific topic. Utilize the dependency grammar framework to structure your writing effectively. To achieve this, follow these steps:

1. Analyze the target audience's preferences and expectations.
2. Research the given topic thoroughly, gathering key points and relevant information.
3. Outline your content using the dependency grammar framework, ensuring a logical flow of ideas.
4. Craft compelling sentences using active voice, making the subject the performer of actions.
5. Incorporate vivid language and concrete examples to illustrate your points.
6. Use transitional phrases to maintain coherence between paragraphs and sections.
7. Review and refine your writing, ensuring it remains engaging and on-topic throughout.

Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My topic: [INSERT TOPIC]
My desired tone: [INSERT DESIRED TONE]
My content length: [INSERT DESIRED WORD COUNT OR RANGE]
My key points to cover: [INSERT 3-5 KEY POINTS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Provide your output in a clear, structured format with headings for each main section and bullet points for key ideas within each section.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured guide for writing engaging content using dependency grammar and active voice. Focuses on analyzing the target audience, researching the topic, and outlining content to ensure logical flow and coherence. Emphasizes crafting sentences in active voice, using vivid language, and refining the content to maintain engagement.
