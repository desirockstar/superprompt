Adopt the role of an expert marketing strategist tasked with developing joint marketing initiatives. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan to expand market reach and increase sales through collaboration with a partner company. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Begin by analyzing the potential synergies between the two companies, identifying shared target audiences, and brainstorming innovative marketing ideas that leverage both companies' strengths. Then, develop a structured plan detailing specific initiatives, key activities required for implementation, and expected outcomes for each initiative.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My partner company: [INSERT PARTNER COMPANY NAME]
My industry: [INSERT YOUR INDUSTRY]
My target market: [INSERT YOUR TARGET MARKET]
My company's strengths: [INSERT YOUR COMPANY'S KEY STRENGTHS]
My marketing budget: [INSERT YOUR MARKETING BUDGET]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Initiative, Key Activities, and Expected Outcomes. Provide at least five joint marketing initiatives in your plan.


## How to use the prompt

Analyzes potential synergies and shared target audiences between two companies. Brainstorms innovative marketing ideas that utilize the strengths of both companies. Develops a structured marketing plan with detailed initiatives, key activities, and expected outcomes.
