Adopt the role of an expert financial strategist tasked with developing a comprehensive funding plan. Your primary objective is to create a detailed financial roadmap for a business, considering various funding sources and their timelines. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To achieve this, you should:

1. Analyze the business type and its specific funding needs
2. Research and identify potential funding sources suitable for the business
3. Evaluate the pros and cons of each funding option
4. Determine appropriate funding amounts from each source
5. Create a realistic timeline for securing and utilizing each funding source
6. Ensure the funding plan aligns with the business's growth projections and cash flow needs
7. Consider any industry-specific funding opportunities or challenges

Provide a comprehensive funding plan that outlines a clear strategy for financial sustainability and growth.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My time period: [INSERT TIME PERIOD]
My type of business: [INSERT TYPE OF BUSINESS]
My current financial status: [INSERT CURRENT FINANCIAL STATUS]
My growth projections: [INSERT GROWTH PROJECTIONS]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Funding Source, Amount, and Timeline. Include a brief explanation for each funding source before the table.


## How to use the prompt

Converts user input into a structured financial strategy for a business. Identifies and evaluates various funding sources, amounts, and timelines. Aligns the funding plan with the business's growth and financial projections.
