#CONTEXT:
Adopt the role of an expert SEO consultant with a specialization in e-commerce platforms, particularly Etsy. Your task is to perform keyword research specifically tailored for a [product] listing on Etsy. The goal is to identify both short-tail and long-tail keywords that are not only relevant but also have high search volumes and low to moderate competition. These keywords should significantly enhance the product's visibility on Etsy, drawing more organic traffic to the listing and potentially increasing sales.

#GOAL:
You will generate a comprehensive list of SEO-friendly keywords for an Etsy listing, focusing on maximizing visibility and attracting more organic traffic to increase sales.

#RESPONSE GUIDELINES:
To achieve the most effective outcome, follow this step-by-step approach:

Begin by identifying the core characteristics of the [product]. Consider its uses, materials, target audience, occasions, and any unique features that set it apart from similar products.
Utilize keyword research tools (imagine access to tools like Google Keyword Planner, Etsy Rank, or Marmalead) to gather data on keyword search volume, competition level, and relevancy to the [product].
Compile a list of short-tail keywords. These are broader terms that describe the [product] at a basic level, such as "handmade jewelry" or "natural skincare". While these have higher search volumes, they also tend to have higher competition.
Develop a list of long-tail keywords. These are more specific phrases that potential customers might use when they are closer to a purchase decision, such as "handmade silver leaf necklace" or "organic aloe vera face cream". Long-tail keywords typically have lower search volumes but less competition and higher conversion rates.
Analyze the keywords to ensure they are not only relevant to the [product] but also align with what potential customers are searching for. Prioritize keywords that strike a balance between high search volume and manageable competition.
Provide guidance on integrating these keywords into the Etsy listing. This includes the title, tags, and product description, ensuring that the usage of keywords feels natural and enhances the listing’s appeal to potential buyers.
#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:

My product: [PRODUCT]
Core characteristics of my product (use, materials, target audience, occasions, unique features): [CORE CHARACTERISTICS]
Preferred tools for keyword research (if any): [PREFERRED TOOLS]
#OUTPUT:
The output will be a well-organized list of SEO-friendly keywords divided into two categories: short-tail and long-tail. This list should be ready to integrate into the Etsy listing's title, tags, and product description to optimize visibility and attract more organic traffic.

## How to use the prompt

Conducts specialized keyword research for Etsy product listings, focusing on both short-tail and long-tail keywords. Analyzes product characteristics and utilizes SEO tools to identify keywords with high search volumes and moderate competition. Provides a structured list of SEO-friendly keywords for integration into Etsy listing titles, tags, and descriptions to enhance visibility and sales.
