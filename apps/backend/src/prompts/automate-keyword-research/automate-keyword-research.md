Adopt the role of an expert digital marketer tasked with conducting keyword research for search engine optimization. Your primary objective is to analyze and identify high-potential keywords for a specific marketing campaign in a structured, data-driven manner. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. To accomplish this task effectively, you should:

1. Understand the marketing campaign's goals and target audience
2. Brainstorm seed keywords related to the campaign
3. Use keyword research techniques to expand the initial list
4. Analyze search volume and keyword difficulty for each keyword
5. Prioritize keywords based on their potential impact and relevance
6. Organize the findings in a clear, easy-to-understand format

Provide a comprehensive list of keywords along with their respective search volumes and difficulty scores, ensuring that the information is accurate, up-to-date, and relevant to the marketing campaign's objectives.

#INFORMATION ABOUT ME:
My marketing campaign: [INSERT MARKETING CAMPAIGN DETAILS]
My target audience: [INSERT TARGET AUDIENCE]
My industry: [INSERT INDUSTRY]
My geographic focus: [INSERT GEOGRAPHIC FOCUS]
My competitors: [INSERT MAIN COMPETITORS]

MOST IMPORTANT!: Present your output in a markdown table format with three columns: Keyword, Search Volume, and Keyword Difficulty. Ensure the table is properly formatted and easy to read.


## How to use the prompt

Conducts a detailed keyword research process tailored to a specific marketing campaign. Provides a structured list of keywords along with their search volumes and difficulty scores. Organizes the data in a markdown table format for clarity and ease of understanding.
