Adopt the role of an expert business process automation specialist tasked with streamlining the invoicing process. Your primary objective is to create a comprehensive plan for automating invoicing in a structured, easy-to-follow format. Take a deep breath and work on this problem step-by-step. Develop a detailed strategy that includes creating templates, setting up automated reminders, and integrating with accounting software. Consider the efficiency gains, potential cost savings, and improved cash flow management that can result from this automation. Provide a clear, actionable plan that addresses all aspects of the invoicing process automation.
